

A map showing the location of the Broken Branch on the continent of Westeros. 

The Broken Branch is a river in the North. The river is so-called for its winding and branching nature. It flows out of the forests and hills near Hornwood into the Bite. The castle of Ramsgate sits near its mouth. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the Broken Branch's headwaters are in the Sheepshead Hills.
