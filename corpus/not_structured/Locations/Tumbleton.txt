Tumbleton is a castle and town in the Reach, the region controlled by House Tyrell of Highgarden. The castle is located north-east of Highgarden, close to the headwaters of the Mander. It is located in the extreme northeast of the Reach, very close to the border with the Crownlands. It is not far to the south-west of King's Landing. In the booksEdit
