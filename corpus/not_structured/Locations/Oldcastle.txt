Oldcastle is a castle in the North, the region ruled by House Stark of Winterfell. Oldcastle is located on the northern coast of the Bite, south-east of White Harbor and not far from the mouth of the White Knife river. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 4Edit 

Oldcastle is seen on a map of the North that Roose Bolton has in the Dreadfort, when he is pointing at Moat Cailin to the west. In the booksEdit
