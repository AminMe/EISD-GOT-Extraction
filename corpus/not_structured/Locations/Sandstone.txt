A map showing the location of Sandstone on the continent of Westeros. 

Sandstone is a castle in Dorne, the region controlled by House Martell of Sunspear, and the seat of House Qorgyle. The castle is located in the south-west of Dorne, between the Brimstone and the Torentine in the deepest part of the Dornish desert. It is west of the Hellholt. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Sandstone is the seat of House Qorgyle. Prince Oberyn Martell was a ward at Sandstone for a time.
