A map showing the location of Pinkmaiden on the continent of Westeros. 

Pinkmaiden is a castle in the Riverlands, the area controlled by House Tully of Riverrun. The castle is located south of Riverrun, near the border with the Westerlands and a tributary of the Red Fork of the Trident. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels Pinkmaiden is the seat of House Piper. It was one of the settlements sacked by Gregor Clegane at the beginning of the War of the Five Kings.
