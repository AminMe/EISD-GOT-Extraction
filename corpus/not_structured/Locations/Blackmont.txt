A map showing the location of Blackmont on the continent of Westeros. 

Blackmont is a castle in Dorne, the region controlled by House Martell of Sunspear. The castle is located in the far west of Dorne near the border with the Reach. It is north of Starfall and east of Skyreach, on the Torentine River in the foothills of the Red Mountains. 

It is the seat of the eponymous House Blackmont. In the booksEdit
