A map showing the location of Ramsgate on the continent of Westeros. 

Ramsgate is a castle in the North, the region ruled by House Stark of Winterfell. Ramsgate is located south-east of Winterfell, near the mouth of the Broken Branch river. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 4Edit 

Ramsgate is seen on a map of the North that Roose Bolton has in the Dreadfort, when he is pointing at Moat Cailin to the west. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Ramsgate is in the same area. The rulers of Ramsgate have not been mentioned, but they are sworn to House Manderly of White Harbor.
