

A map showing the location of the Ghiscari Strait off the coast of Essos. 

The Ghiscari Strait is a narrow body of water separating the islands of New Ghis from the mainland of Essos. It is located in Ghiscari territory. The strait links the Gulf of Grief with the Summer Sea. In the booksEdit
