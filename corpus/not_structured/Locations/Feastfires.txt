A map showing the location of Feastfires on the continent of Westeros 

Feastfires is a castle in the Westerlands, the region controlled by House Lannister of Casterly Rock. The castle is located on the tip of a peninsula west of Casterly Rock. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Feastfires is the seat of House Prester. It is located close to a town called Kayce.
