

The Plaza of Pride is a location in Astapor. It is one of the major locations of the city, where the Good Masters take their customers to deliver their purchases. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 3Edit 

Kraznys mo Nakloz and the rest of the Good Masters deliver their Unsullied to Daenerys Targaryen on the Plaza of Pride. Behind the scenesEdit 

The Plaza of Pride is finally mentioned by name by Grey Worm in "The Mountain and the Viper". In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the Plaza of Pride is is an open market where the city's slavers take their customers to view their purchases. In the center of the plaza stands a red brick fountain with a monstrous hammered bronze harpy rearing 20 foot tall in its middle. Yellow water smelling of brimstone gushes from her heavy breasts. 

In A Storm of Swords Daenerys inspects the Unsullied at the Plaza of Pride, but when she decides to purchase all the Unsullied of the city, including the half-trained who have not yet won their caps, the number turns out to be so large that the purchase is completed at the Plaza of Punishment, which in the show was replaced with the Walk of Punishment.
