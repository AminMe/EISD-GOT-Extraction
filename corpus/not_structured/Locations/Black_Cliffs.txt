

The Black Cliffs are a series of cliffs on the northwest coast of Slaver's Bay. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 4Edit 

When Daenerys Targaryen asks for the whereabouts of her largest dragon, Drogon, Grey Worm informs he was last seen flying over the Black Cliffs three days earlier. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the Black Cliffs are a series of cliffs that are situated on the northwest coast of Slaver's Bay, south of the Painted Mountains. They lie between Tolos and the ruined city of Bhorash, south of the Demon Road.
