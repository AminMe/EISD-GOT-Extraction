Ar Noy is a ruined city on the continent of Essos. It is located on the banks of the River Qhoyne, not far north from where it joins the Rhoyne. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Ar Noy was once a great city of the Rhoynar kingdom ruled by Nymeria. Like most of the other Rhoynar cities, it was destroyed by the dragons of Valyria approximately one thousand years ago.
