Tormund: "Most of them are at Hardhome. You know where that is?" Jon Snow: "Up at Storrold's Point." — Jon Snow making peace between the Free Folk and the Night's Watch.[src] 

Storrold's Point is a large peninsula in the southeast of the lands beyond the Wall and on the shores of the Shivering Sea. 

Hardhome, a large settlement of wildlings, is located in a sheltered cove at the tip of the peninsula.
