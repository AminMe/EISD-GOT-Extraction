A map showing the location of Antlers on the continent of Westeros. 

Antlers is a castle in the Crownlands, the region controlled directly by the Iron Throne in King's Landing. The castle is located north of King's Landing near the border with the Riverlands. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels Antlers is the seat of House Buckwell. It is alternatively called "the Antlers".
