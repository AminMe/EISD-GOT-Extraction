A map showing the location of Wickendon on the continent of Westeros. 

Wickendon is a town in the Vale of Arryn, the region controlled by House Arryn of the Eyrie. The town is on the southern coast of the Vale, just across the bay from Maidenpool. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the town's name is spelled Wickenden. It is the seat of House Waxley and is on the northern edge of the Bay of Crabs.
