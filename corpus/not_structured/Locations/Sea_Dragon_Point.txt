

A map showing the location of Sea Dragon Point on the continent of Westeros. 

Sea Dragon Point is a large peninsula in the North. It extends northwards, separating the Bay of Ice to the east from the Sunset Sea in the west. It is located west of the Wolfswood. The nearest major castle is Deepwood Motte. Contents[show] HistoryEdit 

During the Greyjoy Rebellion, ironborn moored on Sea Dragon Point, although they were beaten back. In the booksEdit
