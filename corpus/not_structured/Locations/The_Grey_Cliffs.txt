

A map showing the location of the Grey Cliffs on the continent of Westeros. 

The Grey Cliffs are located along the coastline of northern Westeros, east and north-east of Karhold in the North. The cliffs form the southern shoreline of the Bay of Seals and part of the western shore of the vast Shivering Sea. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the Grey Cliffs are in the same location. They are controlled by House Karstark. ReferencesEdit ↑ HBO viewers guide, Season 1 map, Karhold entry ↑ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Karhold entry See alsoEdit Grey Cliffs on A Wiki of Ice and Fire
