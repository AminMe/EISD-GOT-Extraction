A map showing the location of Silverhill on the continent of Westeros. 

Silverhill is a castle in the Westerlands, the region controlled by House Lannister of Casterly Rock. The castle is located in the woodlands south-east of Casterly Rock and south of Deep Den. It is ruled by House Serrett. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Silverhill is in the same location. It is assumed to be named for veins of silver located in the nearby hills.
