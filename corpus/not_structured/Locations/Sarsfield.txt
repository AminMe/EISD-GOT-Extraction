Sarsfield is a castle in the Westerlands and the seat of House Sarsfield, a noble house sworn to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. It is located southwest to Golden Tooth and northeast of Casterly Rock, being connected to the two castles through the River Road, and northeast of Hornvale. In the booksEdit
