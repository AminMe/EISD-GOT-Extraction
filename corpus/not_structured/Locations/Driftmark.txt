Driftmark is an island in Blackwater Bay near Dragonstone. It is the seat of House Velaryon. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 1Edit 

The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms mentions that the firstborn son of Orys Baratheon married Monica Velaryon at Driftmark when he was seventeen.
