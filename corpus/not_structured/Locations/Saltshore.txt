Saltshore, sometimes rendered as Salt Shore, is a castle in Dorne, the region controlled by House Martell of Sunspear. The castle is located on the southern coast of Dorne, on the Summer Sea. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 1Edit 

Joffrey Baratheon claims that lords will come from all of Westeros, from the Last Hearth in the north to the Salt Shore in the south, to his and Sansa's wedding. In the booksEdit
