A map showing the location of Blackcrown on the continent of Westeros. 

Blackcrown is a castle in the Reach, the region controlled by House Tyrell of Highgarden. The castle is located south-west of Highgarden and Oldtown, on the northern side of Whispering Sound, the estuary of the River Honeywine. The castle overlooks the Redwyne Straits. In the booksEdit
