Crakehall is a stronghold in the Westerlands. It is the seat of House Crakehall, bannermen to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. Located on the Searoad, close to the shores of the Sunset Sea, south of Casterly Rock and Lannisport and close to the border of the Westerlands and the Reach. In the booksEdit
