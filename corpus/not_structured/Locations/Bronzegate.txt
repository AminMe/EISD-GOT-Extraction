A map showing the location of Bronzegate on the continent of Westeros. 

Bronzegate is a castle in the Stormlands, the region controlled by House Baratheon of Storm's End. The castle is located north of Storm's End just off the Kingsroad, southwest of Haystack Hall. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Bronzegate is the seat of House Buckler. It is located south-east of the Wendwater.
