A map showing the location of Rain House on the continent of Westeros. 

Rain House is a castle in the Stormlands, the region controlled by House Baratheon of Storm's End. It is the seat of House Wylde. The castle is located south-east of Storm's End, near the eastern tip of a peninsula extending into the Narrow Sea. It overlooks the southern coastline of Shipbreaker Bay, south of the island of Tarth. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Rain House is the seat of House Wylde. It lies on the far eastern edge of Cape Wrath.
