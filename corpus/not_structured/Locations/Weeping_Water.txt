A map showing the location of the Weeping Water on the continent of Westeros. "I'm going to sail up the Narrow Sea, all the way to the Weeping Water. I'm going to march on the Dreadfort. I'm going to find my little brother. And I'm going to bring him home" ―Yara Greyjoy[src] 

The Weeping Water is a river in the North. It runs past the Dreadfort and through the lands of House Bolton into the Narrow Sea. 

Yara Greyjoy and her crew paddling up the Weeping Water. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 4Edit 

Yara Greyjoy sails from Pyke to lead an assault on the Dreadfort to rescue her captured brother Theon. She and her crew paddle up the Weeping Water. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Ramsay Snow was raised by his mother at a mill along the Weeping Water up river from the Dreadfort.
