A map showing the location of Honeyholt on the continent of Westeros. 

Honeyholt is a castle in the Reach, the region controlled by House Tyrell of Highgarden, and the seat of House Beesbury. The castle is located between Oldtown and Brightwater Keep, on the banks of the Honeywine river. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Honeyholt is the seat of House Beesbury, a vassal of the Tyrells.
