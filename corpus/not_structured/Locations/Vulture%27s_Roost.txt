A map showing the location of Vulture's Roost on the continent of Westeros. 

Vulture's Roost is a ruined castle in Dorne, the region controlled by House Martell of Sunspear. The castle is located in the north-west of Dorne on the border with the Stormlands. It is located east of Nightsong and west of Wyl, within the Red Mountains. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Vulture's Roost is in the same location, near the source of the Wyl river, deep in the Red Mountains. The name of the house that owned the castle has not been given.
