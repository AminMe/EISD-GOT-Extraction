A map showing the location of Hornvale on the continent of Westeros. 

Hornvale is a castle in the Westerlands, the region controlled by House Lannister of Casterly Rock. The castle is located in the center of the region, among the mountains east of Casterly Rock. It is south of Golden Tooth, near the headwaters of the Red Fork. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Hornvale is the seat of House Brax, a loyal vassal house of the Lannisters.
