

The Saltspear is an inlet in the North. It is located in Blazewater Bay and west of Moat Cailin. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Saltspear is fed by the Fever River, a river in the Neck. It opens into Blazewater Bay.
