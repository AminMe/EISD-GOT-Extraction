

A map showing the location of the Sea of Myrth off the coast of Essos. 

The Sea of Myrth is a large inlet of the Narrow Sea, located in south-western Essos where it separates the Disputed Lands from the lands further north. The Free City of Myr lies on the eastern coast of the Sea of Myrth, while Tyrosh lies on an island at the mouth of the sea. In the booksEdit
