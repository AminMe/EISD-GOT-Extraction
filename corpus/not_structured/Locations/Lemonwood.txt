A map showing the location of Lemonwood on the continent of Westeros. 

Lemonwood is a castle in Dorne, the region controlled by House Martell of Sunspear, and the seat of House Dalt. The castle is located on the east coast of Dorne, south of the mouth of the Greenblood. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Lemonwood is the seat of House Dalt, loyal vassals of the Martells. It lies just south of the Planky Town.
