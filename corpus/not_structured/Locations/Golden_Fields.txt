

A map showing the location of the Golden Fields on the continent of Essos. 

The Golden Fields is a region in western Essos. This area is located between Dagger Lake and a tributary of the Rhoyne. The Flatlands lie to the northwest and the Disputed Lands and the Free City of Myr to the south-west. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the Golden Fields are located in the same area. The tributary of the Rhoyne that lies along their south-western edge is called the Lhorulu.
