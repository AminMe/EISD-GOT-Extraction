House Wells is a noble house. They are situated in Stoney Sept. Contents[show] Known MembersEdit Corlys Wells, a member of the Night's Watch. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there are two separate houses that bear the name "House Wells": one from the North and the other from Dorne. The fact that they are situated in Stoney Sept in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series makes it difficult to determine which one it is intended to be.
