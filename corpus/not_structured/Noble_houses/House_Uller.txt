House Uller is a vassal house sworn to House Martell of Sunspear. Their seat is Hellholt. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 4Edit 

Ellaria Sand, a bastard daughter of House Uller and paramour of Prince Oberyn Martell, accompanies him to King's Landing. Known members Edit Ellaria Sand, the bastard daughter of Lord Uller and paramour of Prince Oberyn Martell. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Uller is one of House Martell's most powerful vassals, and reputed to be impulsive and unpredictable. There's a saying in Dorne that goes "Half of the Ullers are half-mad, and the other half are worse." Their arms are a rayonne yellow over crimson while their words are unknown.
