House Greenfield is a vassal house from the Westerlands. They are a knightly house sworn to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. Contents[show] MembersEdit Ser Preston Greenfield, a knight of the Kingsguard. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Greenfield is a house of landed knights sworn to Casterly Rock. Their seat is Greenfield, though its location has not yet been revealed. Neither their arms or their words appear in the books, but according to semi-canon sources they blazon their sigil is as a double tressure white on green.
