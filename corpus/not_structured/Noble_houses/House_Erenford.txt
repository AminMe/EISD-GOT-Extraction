House Erenford is a knightly house of the Riverlands. They are sworn to House Frey of the Twins. Contents[show] Known membersEdit Lady {Joyeuse Erenford}, the eighth wife of Lord Walder Frey, killed by Catelyn Stark during the Red Wedding. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Erenford is a lesser knightly house sworn to House Frey. Their sigil is a golden heron, beaked and gammed black, standing with a silver fish in its beak, on pink.
