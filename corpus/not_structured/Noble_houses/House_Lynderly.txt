House Lynderly is a noble house from the Vale of Arryn, sworn to House Arryn, the Lords of the Eyrie and Lords Paramount of the Vale. Contents[show] Known members Edit Knight of House Lynderly In the books Edit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Lynderly is a house sworn to the Arryns. Their seat is Snakeswood and their sigil is eleven green snakes on a field of black.
