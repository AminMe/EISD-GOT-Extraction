House Haigh is a knightly house from the Riverlands sworn to House Frey. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 4Edit 

Davos Seaworth reports to Stannis Baratheon he has managed to secure the support of some noble houses, among them House Haigh. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Haigh is a loyal vassal of House Frey. Their sigil is a black pitchfork on a golden bar sinister on russet. They participate in the Red Wedding, fighting on the side of their liege lords.
