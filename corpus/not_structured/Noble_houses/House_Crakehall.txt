House Crakehall is a vassal house that holds fealty to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. Their lands are located at the Searoad, at the south of the Westerlands, between the sea and a large forest. The current head of the family is Desmond Crakehall. The Crakehall sigil is a black and white brindled boar on brown.
