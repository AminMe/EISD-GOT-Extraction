House Lorch is a knightly house of the Westerlands. They are sworn to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. Contents[show] MembersEdit Ser {Amory Lorch}, a knight in the service of Lord Tywin Lannister. Poisoned by Jaqen H'ghar at Arya Stark's behest. HistoryEdit Season 2Edit 

Ser Amory Lorch fights for House Lannister during the War of the Five Kings. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the only known members of House Lorch are Ser Amory and Ser Lorent Lorch. Image GalleryEdit Ser Amory Lorch in "What is Dead May Never Die."
