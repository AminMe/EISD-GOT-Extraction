House Blount is a vassal house from the Crownlands. It holds fealty to House Baratheon of King's Landing. MembersEdit Ser Boros Blount, a knight of the Kingsguard. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Blount is a vassal house sworn to the Iron Throne. They blazon their arms with a red bend sinister between two black porcupines on green.
