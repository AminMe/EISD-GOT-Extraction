House Santagar is a vassal house sworn to House Martell of Sunspear. They are established in Dorne. Contents[show] Members Edit Ser Aron Santagar, the master-at-arms of the Red Keep. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Santagar is a house of landed knights. Neither their arms or their motto appear in the books. In a semi-canon source they blazon their arms with a spotted leopard with a golden axe, over a per bend sinister blue and white field. MembersEdit Ser Symon Santagar, the Knight of Spottswood.
