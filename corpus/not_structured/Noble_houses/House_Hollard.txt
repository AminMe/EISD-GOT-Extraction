House Hollard is an extinct vassal house of the Crownlands, holding fealty to House Baratheon of King's Landing. The house was once powerful, but over the years it fell into disgrace. Known members Edit 

Ser Dontos Hollard's breast-plate displays the heraldry of House Holllard {Dontos Hollard}, called "Dontos the Red," the last living member of the family. He was formerly a&nbsp;knight, but served at the Red Keep as King Joffrey Baratheon's fool since Joffrey's nameday tourney, to which Dontos showed up drunk. Killed by crossbowmen serving Lord Petyr Baelish&nbsp;after helping Sansa Stark escape the Purple Wedding at Baelish's command (and promise of payment), marking the extinction of the House. Ser&nbsp;{Symon Hollard}, a knight. Killed by Ser Barristan Selmy in revenge for the death of Ser Gwayne Gaunt during the Defiance of Duskendale. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Hollard was an ancient house sworn to House Darklyn, having three daughters wedded to Darklyn kings during the Age of Heroes. Following the Defiance of Duskendale, the entire House was executed at Aerys II's command, except for the infant Dontos Hollard, who was allowed to live at Ser Barristan Selmy's request.
