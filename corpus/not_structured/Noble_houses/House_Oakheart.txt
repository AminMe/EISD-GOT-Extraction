House Oakheart is a vassal house from the Reach that holds fealty to House Tyrell. Its seat is Old Oak. MembersEdit Ser Arys Oakheart, a member of the Kingsguard. Served as Princess Myrcella Baratheon's escort to Dorne. Later returned to King's Landing after the death of King Joffrey Baratheon. In the booksEdit 

In A Song of Ice and Fire, House Oakheart is an old and powerful family from the Reach. They are among the noble houses from the Reach that can trace their descent from Garth Greenhand. The house is headed by Lady Arwyn Oakheart, a loyal vassal of Mace Tyrell and Ser Arys' mother. The Oakheart sigil is three green leaves on a golden plain.
