House Cargyll was a vassal house from the Crownlands. Known membersEdit Ser Arryk Cargyll, a knight of the Kingsguard who declared for King Aegon II during the Dance of the Dragons. Erryk's twin Ser Erryk Cargyll, a knight of the Kingsguard who declared for Queen Rhaenyra during the Dance of the Dragons. Arryk's twin. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Cargyll is extinct. However, their sigil mentioned in The Hedge Knight, as a member of the house participates in the Ashford Tourney.
