House Selmy is a vassal house from the Stormlands that holds fealty to House Baratheon of Storm's End. Known members Edit {Ser Barristan Selmy}, known as "Barristan the Bold", former Lord Commander of the Kingsguard. &nbsp;Since being dismissed by King Joffrey Baratheon,&nbsp;he tracked down Daenerys Targaryen and swore his loyalty to her. Slain when fighting the Sons of the Harpy. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Selmy is a house sworn to Storm's End. Their seat is Harvest Hall and their sigil is three stalks of yellow wheat on brown.
