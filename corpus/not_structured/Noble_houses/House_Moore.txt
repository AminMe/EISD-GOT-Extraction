House Moore is a vassal house from the Vale. It holds fealty to House Arryn. MembersEdit Ser {Mandon Moore}, a knight of the Kingsguard. Killed by Podrick Payne during the Battle of the Blackwater for attempting to murder Tyrion Lannister. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Moore is a vassal house sworn to the Eyrie. Their sigil is three bronze spearheads on white with a bronze border embattled.
