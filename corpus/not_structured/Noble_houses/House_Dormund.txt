House Dormund is a vassal house that holds fealty to House Stark of Winterfell. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 2Edit 

During the War of the Five Kings a letter detailing the infantry movements of House Lannister's forces was accidentally sent to Lord Marlin of House Dormund, instead of Lord Damon Marbrand, due to the illiteracy of Amory Lorch.
