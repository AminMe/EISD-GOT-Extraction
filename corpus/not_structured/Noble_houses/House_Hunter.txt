In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Hunter are loyal bannermen of House Arryn. Their seat is Longbow Hall and their sigil is five silver arrows, fanned, on a brown field. MembersEdit Lord Eon Hunter, Lord of Longbow Hall. An old man with gouty legs, who has been ruling his house for sixty years, known as Old Lord Hunter. Ser Gilwood Hunter, his first son and heir. Ser Eustace Hunter, his second son. Ser Harlan Hunter, his third son.
