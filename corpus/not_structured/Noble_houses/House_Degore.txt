House Degore is a noble house from the North. They are unique to Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. Contents[show] HistoryEdit BackgroundEdit 

House Degore fought against the Ironborn during the Greyjoy Rebellion, where the majority of the house was killed. The lone survivor, Royland Degore, was awarded a knighthood by King Robert Baratheon for his service during the Siege of Pyke. "Iron From Ice"Edit 

The Degores are mentioned in Royand's codex entry, detailing what happened during the rebellion. MembersEdit Ser Royland Degore, the last surviving member of the house and master-at-arms for Ironrath. In the booksEdit 

House Degore has no analogue in the novels, being an invention of Telltale. ReferencesEdit
