House Serrett is a vassal house that holds fealty to House Lannister of Casterly Rock. Their lands are located in the woodlands southeast of Casterly Rock and south of Deep Den. Contents[show] HistoryEdit Season 2Edit 

Lord Serrett is mentioned in a letter on Tywin Lannister's table at Harrenhal on troop movements. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the House Serrett are the Lords of Silverhill. Their sigil is a male peacock in his pride on a field of cream. Their words are I Have No Rival.
