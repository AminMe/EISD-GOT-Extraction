Nail is a background character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is a member of the Second Sons. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "Sons of Winter"Edit 

After showing Asher Forrester and Beskha to their tent, Croft informs Nail that they have two more soldiers in their ranks. In the books Edit 

In the&nbsp;A Song of Ice and Fire&nbsp;novels, Nail is a member of the Second Sons, similarly to his role in the game series. He serves as an apprentice to the drunken&nbsp;blacksmith and armorer known as&nbsp;Hammer. 

Nail is introduced in the fifth novel "A Dance with Dragons". After Tyrion, Jorah and their dwarf companion escape from Yezzan's camp and join the Second Sons, they are ordered to go to Hammer for equipment. Since Hammer is too drunk, Nail takes them to the weapons and armor supply. He tells them that most of it is crap iron, but that they're welcome to anything they can use.
