Joby is an unidentified background character in the first season. He is a newly made Steward of the Night's Watch, of the same group of recruits as Jon Snow. Joby is assigned to the stables, it is assumed that he is the person who readies the horses for the rangers.&nbsp; Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

Joby is present during the speech Lord Commander Jeor Mormont gave to the "graduating" recruits. Joby is assigned to the stables.
