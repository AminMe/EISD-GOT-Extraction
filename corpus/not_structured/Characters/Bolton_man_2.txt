A&nbsp;Bolton soldier&nbsp;is one of the men holding Theon Greyjoy prisoner. He's a torturer serving House Bolton under the command of Ramsay Snow, the bastard son of Roose Bolton. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 3Edit 

When Ramsay leads Theon&nbsp;back into the Dreadfort, this man and his comrade grab Theon and tie him back onto the cross. 

They later hold Theon down as Ramsay castrates him. AppearancesEdit Season Three appearances Valar Dohaeris Dark Wings, Dark Words Walk of Punishment And Now His Watch is Ended Kissed by Fire The Climb The Bear and the Maiden Fair Second Sons The Rains of Castamere Mhysa In the booksEdit
