Loreza Sand is an unseen character in the fourth season. She is alluded to in "First of His Name". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Loreza Sand is the last of the eight bastard daughters of Prince Oberyn Martell, known as the "Sand Snakes". Her mother is Oberyn's paramour, Ellaria Sand. Season 4Edit 

Oberyn mentions his eight bastard daughters to Queen Cersei Lannister.
