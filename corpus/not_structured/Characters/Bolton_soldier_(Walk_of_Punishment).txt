A Bolton soldier is an unnamed minor character in the third season. He is played by guest star Gary Lightbody and only appears in "Walk of Punishment". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

This soldier is fighting for House Bolton in the War of the Five Kings. Season 3Edit 

He is a member of the force led by Locke and is present when they capture Jaime Lannister and Brienne of Tarth. As they ride through the woods, he leads the other soldiers in singing a rendition of "The Bear and the Maiden Fair".
