A Fury gunnery officer is an unnamed background character in the second season. He is played by an unidentified extra and only appears in "The Prince of Winterfell." Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is serving aboard the Fury, Stannis Baratheon's flagship, fighting for House Baratheon of Dragonstone in the War of the Five Kings. Season 2Edit 

He is seen drilling men firing the ship's ballista as the fleet approaches King's Landing.
