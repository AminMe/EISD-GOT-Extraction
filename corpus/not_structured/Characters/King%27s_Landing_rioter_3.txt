A King's Landing rioter is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Rea Separovic and only appears in "The Old Gods and the New." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He shouts at King Joffrey Baratheon just before the Riot of King's Landing.
