Craster's wife is a minor character in the third season. She is played by Michelle Costello and debuted in "Walk of Punishment". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

She is one of Craster's wives who he has married. It is unstated whether or not she's his daughter. Season 3Edit 

Along with another of Craster's wives, she delivers Gilly's baby, Sam, while Samwell Tarly watches.
