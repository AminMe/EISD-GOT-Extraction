A Night's Watch Officer is an unnamed background character in the second season. He is played by an unidentified actor and debuts in the "The North Remembers." He is part of the ranging north of the Wall. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He is part of the ranging north of the Wall and is forced to give his axe to Craster. The Night's Watch are forced to move on when Jon Snow is caught spying on Craster leaving a newborn son in the woods.
