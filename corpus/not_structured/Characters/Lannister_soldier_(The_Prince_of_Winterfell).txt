A Lannister Soldier is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Ciaran McMahon and only appears in "The Prince of Winterfell." Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

This soldier is fighting for House Lannister in the War of the Five Kings. Season 2Edit 

He bumps into Arya Stark while crossing the courtyard at Harrenhal, making her waste the water she is carrying. He cuffs her round the head and warns her to be more careful.
