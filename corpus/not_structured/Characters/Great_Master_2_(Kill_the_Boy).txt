A Great Master is a character appearing fifth season episode "Kill the Boy". He is played by Bobby Asghar. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is the head of one of the ancient slaving families of Meereen. Season 5Edit 

After Ser Barristan Selmy is murdered by the Sons of the Harpy, Daenerys has the heads of the great families of Meereen arrested. She forces them into the catacombs were Viserion and Rhaegal are kept and then has the two dragons kill and devour one of the prisoners while the others watch in horror.
