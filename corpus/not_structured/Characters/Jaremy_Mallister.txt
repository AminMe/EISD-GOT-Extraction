Jaremy Mallister was a knight&nbsp;and member of House Mallister. He was a loyal bannerman of House Tully. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 3Edit 

Ser Jaremy was among the prisoners of the joint Northern-Riverlands army held captive at Harrenhal. Before abandoning the castle, Ser Gregor Clegane had all the prisoners put to the sword. When the Northern army, led by Robb Stark, discovered the corpses, Catelyn Stark recognizes Ser Jaremy.
