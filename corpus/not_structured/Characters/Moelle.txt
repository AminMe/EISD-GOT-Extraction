Moelle&nbsp;is a recurring character in the fifth season. She is portrayed by Maggie Hayes&nbsp;and debuts in "The Gift".&nbsp; Contents[show] BiographyEdit Background Edit 

Moelle is a septa of the Faith of the Seven stationed at the Great Sept of Baelor in King's Landing. Season 5 Edit 

Moelle is present along with Septas Unella and Scolera during the arrest of Cersei Lannister. She watches as Unella throws the Queen into her cell under the orders of the High Sparrow. In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Moelle is described as having stiff white hair, small eyes, and lips twisted in perpetual disapproval.
