Portan Season(s) 2 Appeared in "The North Remembers" Status Presumably Alive Allegiance House Stark Portrayed by Gordon Fulton "Lord Portan?" ―Maester Luwin[src] 

Portan is a minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Gordon Fulton and only appears in "The North Remembers." He is a Northern Lord who visits Winterfell to speak before Prince Bran Stark.
