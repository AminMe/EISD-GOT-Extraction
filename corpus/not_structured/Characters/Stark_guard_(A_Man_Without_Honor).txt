A Stark guard is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star David Sheehan and only appears in "A Man Without Honor." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He is guarding the recaptured Jaime Lannister when he is dismissed by Catelyn Stark. 

He is assigned to guard Catelyn after she betrays Robb by releasing Jaime.
