Kieran is a lieutenant of the Second Sons. He appears in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "Sons of Winter"Edit 

Kieran is present at Daenerys Targaryen's war council along with Asher Forrester, Beskha, Croft, Therrond and Penn. On Croft's orders, he leads a stealth attack on Meereen's western beacon. "A Nest of Vipers"Edit 

Kieran returns to Daenerys' camp after the Siege of Meereen.
