Symon Hollard is an unseen character in Game of Thrones. He is deceased at the start of the series, and thus not expected to appear. He was a knight of House Hollard and an uncle to Dontos Hollard. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 4Edit 

He is mentioned within the entry of Ser Gwayne Gaunt, as recorded in the Book of Brothers. During the Defiance of Duskendale he killed Ser Gwayne. For this he was later killed by Ser Barristan Selmy. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Symon Hollard was a key accomplice in the Defiance of Duskendale, when Lord Denys Darklyn of Duskendale defied King Aerys II and captured him, imprisoning him within the Dun Fort. 

Ser Symon slew Ser Gwayne Gaunt of the Kingsguard during the taking of the King. When Ser Barristan Selmy of the Kingsguard entered the Dun Fort and escaped with the King, he avenged Ser Gwayne by killing Ser Symon.
