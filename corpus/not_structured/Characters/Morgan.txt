Morgan is an minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Chris Reily and appears in "Two Swords." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 4Edit 

He visits Littlefinger's whore house with his friend. While there singing the Rains of Castemere, they are confronted by the foreigner, Oberyn Martell, who threatens Morgan and then stabs a dagger through his hand when he attempted to draw his sword at the table. He and his friend are forced to escape and treat the wound before it becomes fatal.
