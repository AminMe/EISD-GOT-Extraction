Lobor is a minor character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is a newly made Steward of the Night's Watch, of the same group of recruits as Gared Tuttle. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "The Sword in the Darkness"Edit 

Lobor is present during the speech Frostfinger gives to the "graduating" recruits. Lobor is assigned to the Stewards.
