A Tourney Herald is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Marko Cindric and only appears in "The North Remembers." The Tourney Herald announces the participants in King Joffrey Baratheon's name day celebration. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

The herald announces the participants in King Joffrey Baratheon's name day tourney. He claps when the Hound knocks his opponent over the walls of the Red Keep. When Joffrey asks who is next to fight, he&nbsp;announces&nbsp;Lothor Brune and Ser Dontos Hollard. However, the latter fails to appear at his first call, and the herald has to summon him again.
