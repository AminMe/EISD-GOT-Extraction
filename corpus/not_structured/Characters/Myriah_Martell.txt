Myriah Martell is an unseen character in Game of Thrones. She is not expected to appear in the series, being long dead by the time it begins. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Myriah Martell was a Dornish Princess of the House Martell of Sunspear. She married King Daeron II Targaryen and became the mother of Baelor, Aerys, Rhaegel, and Maekar; and one daughter, Aelinor. Season 1Edit 

Queen Myriah, along with her progenie, is mentioned in House Targaryen's entry of the book The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms. Deceased In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Queen Myriah married Daeron II as part of the marriage pact that brought Dorne under the rule of the Iron Throne. Though originall named Myriah in the novels, her name has been retconned as Mariah Martell in The World of Ice and Fire.
