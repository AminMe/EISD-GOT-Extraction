Beric Dayne&nbsp;was the&nbsp;Lord of Starfall and the father of Ser Arthur Dayne, the Sword of the Morning. Season 4Edit 

He is mentioned in his son Arthur's entry in the Book of Brothers. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Ser Arthur Dayne's father is never named or mentioned. Ser Arthur's nephew Edric Dayne is the Lord of Starfall during the events of the series.
