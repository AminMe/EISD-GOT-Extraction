The Night's Watch archer is a minor character in the fourth season.&nbsp;He is a member of the Night's Watch and appears in the episode "The Watchers on the Wall". He is played by Neil Marshall, the director of this episode.&nbsp; Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 4Edit 

He along with his brothers participated in the defense of the Wall from the attacks of the wilding army and attacked them with flaming arrows on the orders of Jon Snow.
