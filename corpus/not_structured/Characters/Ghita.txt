Ghita is a minor character in the fifth season. She was played by Hattie Gotobed. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Ghita is a ten year old terminally ill girl. Season 5Edit 

Ghita is brought to the House of Black and White by her father after various treatment attempts have failed. Arya Stark puts the girl out of her misery by giving her the poisoned water from the pool in the central sanctuary. 

Arya wears Ghita's face and pretends to be a child prostitute in order to ambush and murder Ser Meryn Trant in a brothel. Once she completes this task, she returns the face to the Hall of Faces. In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels there is no mention of a character named Ghita. However, many people go to the House of Black and White to seek an end to their misery or that of their loved ones.
