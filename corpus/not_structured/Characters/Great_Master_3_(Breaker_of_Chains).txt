A Great Master is an unnamed minor character in the fourth season. He is a slave-trader from the city of Meereen, on the coast of Slaver's Bay. He is played by uncredited extra Laurence Doherty and appears in "Breaker of Chains". Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 4Edit 

He is one of the many Great Masters that gather over the walls of Meereen when Daenerys Targaryen and her army appear before the city gates to lay siege to the city.
