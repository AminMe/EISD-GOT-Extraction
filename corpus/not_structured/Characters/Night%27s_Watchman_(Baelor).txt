A Night's Watchman is an unnamed minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Marcus Lamb and only appears in "Baelor." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

He congratulates Jon Snow for saving the life of Lord Commander Jeor Mormont&nbsp;and says that he earned the sword Mormont had given him, Longclaw.
