Niko is an unidentified background character in the first season. He is a newly made Ranger of the Night's Watch, of the same group of recruits as Jon Snow. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

Niko is present during the speech Lord Commander Jeor Mormont gave to the "graduating" recruits. Niko is assigned to the Rangers.
