Lady Stork is a recurring character in the sixth season. Lady Stork is the leading actress in Izembaro's Braavosi theater troupe. Lady Stork is played by Essie Davis. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Lady Stork is an elegant, fun, charismatic, rum-drinking actress in Izembaro's traveling theater company. Season 6Edit In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Lady Stork is the leading actress in Izembaro's theater troupe in Braavos. Just as in the show, she is a heavy drinker and portrays Cersei in the troupe's current performance.
