A young bravo is a character in the fifth season. He is played by Jack Hickey and appears in "The Dance of Dragons" Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

This young Braavosi is a bravo, a swordsman and practitioner of the Water Dance. Season 5Edit 

He's present at the brothel were Lhara practices her trade when "Lanna" shows up, following Meryn Trant. At Lhara's request he buys half a dozen oysters and pays Lanna a silver because Lhara likes her.
