Casper is a minor character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is a newly made Builder of the Night's Watch, of the same group of recruits as Gared Tuttle. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "The Lost Lords"Edit 

Casper is seen dueling Finn, and Finn defeats him. "The Sword in the Darkness"Edit 

Casper is present during the speech Frostfinger gives to the "graduating" recruits. Casper is assigned to the Builders.
