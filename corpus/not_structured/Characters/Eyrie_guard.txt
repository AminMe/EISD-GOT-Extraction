An Eyrie Guard is an unnamed minor character in the fifth season. He is played by guest star Paul Kennedy and only appears in "The House of Black and White". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is a guard in service to House Arryn who serves them at the Eyrie. Season 5Edit 

He accompanies Lord Petyr Baelish and Sansa Stark from Runestone. During their stay at an inn, he stands guard as Brienne of Tarth approaches them and tries to convince Sansa to allow her to be her protector. He attempts to stop her when she turns to leave, and she promptly knocks him out. In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there are several knights, hedge knights, and men-at-arms in Littlefinger's service once he becomes Lord Protector of the Vale.
