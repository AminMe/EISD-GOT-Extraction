A Lannister captain is an unnamed recurring character in the second season. He is played by guest star Aiden Condron and debuts in "The Old Gods and the New." He is stationed at Harrenhal during the War of the Five Kings. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He announces the arrival of Lord Petyr Baelish to Lord Tywin Lannister at Harrenhal. When Tywin marches from Harrenhal he orders Rorge and Biter to fall in.
