A Tortured Lannister is an unseen, unnamed background character in the second season. He is played by an unidentified extra and only appears in "The Prince of Winterfell." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He can be heard being tortured for information about the murder of Ser Amory Lorch as Arya Stark passes the gallows at Harrenhal.
