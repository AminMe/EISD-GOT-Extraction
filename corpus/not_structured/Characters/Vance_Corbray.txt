Vance Corbray is a minor character in the fourth season, played by Richard Doubleday. Ser&nbsp;Vance Corbray&nbsp;is a knight of House Corbray, a noble house sworn to House Arryn of the Eyrie. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 4Edit 

Ser Vance, along with Lord Yohn Royce and Lady Anya Waynwood, sits on a tribunal to investigate the death of Lysa Arryn. Like the other judges, Ser Vance assumes foul play on Littlefinger's part. During the tribunal, Ser Vance is made aware that Petyr Baelish's niece, "Alayne", is actually Sansa Stark, but he agrees to keep her secret and accepts her testimony that Lady Arryn committed suicide. In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there is no character named Ser Vance Corbray. There are two known Corbray knights, brothers to Lord Lyonel Corbray, Ser Lyn and Ser Lucas.
