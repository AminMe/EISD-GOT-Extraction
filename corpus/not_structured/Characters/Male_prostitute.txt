A male prostitute is an unnamed recurring character in the second season. He is played by guest star Dennis Stokes and debuts in "The North Remembers." He works for Petyr Baelish in King's Landing. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He helps Ros to train a new prostitute named Marei by sleeping with her. He sleeps with Marei again for the enjoyment of a voyeuristic customer.
