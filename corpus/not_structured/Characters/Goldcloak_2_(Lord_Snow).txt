A Goldcloak is a minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Seamus Kelly and only appears in "Lord Snow." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

He stops Catelyn Stark on her arrival in King's Landing and escorts her to Littlefinger's brothel.
