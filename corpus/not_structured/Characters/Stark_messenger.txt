A Stark Messenger is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Moe Dunford and only appears in "The Prince of Winterfell." Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

This soldier is a messenger for House Stark. Season 2Edit 

While Robb and Talisa are walking back from The Crag, he rides out to Robb, to announce that Jaime Lannister has escaped.
