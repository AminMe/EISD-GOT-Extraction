Eon Hunter is a minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Barrington Cullen and only appears in "A Golden Crown." Eon Hunter is the Lord of Longbow Hall and the head of House Hunter, a vassal family to House Arryn of the Eyrie. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

Lord Hunter is present when Tyrion Lannister demands a trial by combat for the charges of murdering Jon Arryn and conspiring to murder Bran Stark. After a young knight offers to champion for Lady Lysa Arryn, Lord Hunter asks for the chance to avenge the death of Lord Arryn as well. Ultimately, another knight champions for Lady Arryn, and is defeated by Bronn. AppearanceEdit Season One appearances Winter is Coming The Kingsroad Lord Snow Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things The Wolf and the Lion A Golden Crown You Win or You Die The Pointy End Baelor Fire and Blood In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Eon Hunter is the head of House Hunter of Longbow Hall and has been so for nearly sixty years. Half-crippled by gout, Lord Eon is older than Jon Arryn and has three quarrelsome sons: Gilwood, Eustace and Harlan. He's one of the many suitors of Lady Lysa.
