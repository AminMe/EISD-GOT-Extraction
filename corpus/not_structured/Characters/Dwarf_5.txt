A&nbsp;dwarf is an unnamed minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Krysten Coombs and&nbsp;only appears in "The Lion and the Rose". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

A dwarf, he makes a living by performing for other peoples' amusement. Season 4Edit 

Along with a group of other dwarfs, he takes part in a mock "reenactment" of the War of the Five Kings at King Joffrey's wedding. He dresses as Balon Greyjoy and rides a kraken.
