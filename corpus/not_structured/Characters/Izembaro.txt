Izembaro is a recurring character in the sixth season. Izembaro is the manager of a Braavosi troupe of actors, in which he also acts. Izembaro is played by Richard E. Grant. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Izembaro is a larger-than-life actor who specializes in portraying drunken aristocrats. He is a huge presence on stage and rather nasty to his company when off-stage. Season 6Edit In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Izembaro is also the leader of a theater troupe in Braavos. Not unlike in the show, he has a penchant for portraying kings.
