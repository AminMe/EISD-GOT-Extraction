Gelmarr is a background character in the second season. He is played by an unidentified extra and debuts in "Valar Morghulis". Gelmarr is an ironborn raider and member of the Sea Bitch crew. He takes part in the seizing of Winterfell during the War of the Five Kings. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

Gelmarr is present when Theon Greyjoy gives a speech encouraging his crew to resist the Northmen led by Ramsay Snow and die a glorious death. 

After handing Theon over, Gelmarr and the other ironborn are flayed alive by Ramsay Snow and the soldiers of House Bolton. AppearancesEdit Season Two appearances The North Remembers The Night Lands What is Dead May Never Die Garden of Bones The Ghost of Harrenhal The Old Gods and the New A Man Without Honor The Prince of Winterfell Blackwater Valar Morghulis In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Gelmarr's background is the same. He's also known as "Gelmarr the Grim" and took part in the raid of Stony Shore, the assault on Winterfell and the hunt for Bran and Rickon. Alongside Aggar&nbsp;and&nbsp;Gynir Rednose, he took part in the abduction and killing of the children whose corpses were passed off as Bran's and Rickon's. He, Gelmarr, and Gynir were secretly killed at Theon's command, to prevent them from revealing the truth about the slain children. Farlen was falsely accused by Theon of the killing and was executed.
