Wyllis Bracken is an unseen character in the fifth season. He is a knight from the Riverlands and a member of House Bracken, Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 5Edit 

Jaime Lannister arranges the betrothal of Lollys Stokeworth to Ser Wyllis, thus breaking the arrangement made by Queen Cersei Lannister that betrothed Lollys to Ser Bronn of the Blackwater. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels there is no mention of a character named Ser Wyllis Bracken, and Bronn is the one who marries Lollys Stokeworth.
