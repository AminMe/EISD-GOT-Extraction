Olly - son Portrayed by Jem Wall "No one boils a potato better than your mum. She got -" ―His last words[src]Guymon is a minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Jem Wall and only appears in "Breaker of Chains". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He lives with his wife and son in a village close to the Wall. Season 4Edit 

When he and his son are on their way home for dinner, their village is attacked by a group of wildlings. He is killed by an arrow to the head from Ygritte. His body is later eaten by Styr.
