Tregar Ormollen is an unseen character in Game of Thrones, indirectly mentioned in "Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things". He is a Lysene merchant lord. Lynesse Hightower, the estranged wife of Jorah Mormont, is his concubine. In the booksEdit
