Lord Thorren Forrester ​is an unseen character in&nbsp;Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is long dead by the time the series begins, he is also the father of&nbsp;Gregor Forrester. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Lord Thorren seized back the river valley from House Whitehill. 

He later fought in Robert's Rebellion with the rebels and died at the Battle of the Trident. He was succeeded by his son Gregor. "Iron From Ice"Edit 

Maester&nbsp;Ortengryn&nbsp;mentions Lord Thorren to Ethan that he was the one who seized back the river valleys from the Whitehills and that they called him Thorren the Bold. Family TreeEdit &nbsp; &nbsp; Thorren Forrester
