Drunk Man Season(s) 1 Appeared in "Lord Snow" Status Alive Portrayed by Paul Portelli 

A drunk man is an unnamed minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Paul Portelli and only appears in "Lord Snow." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

He stumbles into Littlefinger's room at the brothel on the arm of a King's Landing whore. Littlefinger is meeting with Catelyn Stark and waves them away.
