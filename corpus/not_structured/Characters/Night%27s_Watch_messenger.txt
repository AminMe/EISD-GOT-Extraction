A Night's Watch messenger is an unnamed minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Frank O'Sullivan and only appears in "The Pointy End." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

He announces the arrival of a raven to Lord Commander Jeor Mormont. He congratulates Jon Snow for protecting Mormont from an attack by a wight the next day.
