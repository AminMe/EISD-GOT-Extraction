A Yunkai'i slave is a minor unnamed character first appearing in Season 3. She's played by Mustapha Mekanassi and only appeared in "Mhysa". BiographyEdit Season 3Edit 

She is one of the slaves that are freed by Daenerys Targaryen following the Sack of Yunkai. When Daenerys greets the slaves of Yunkai and tells them that it is their own choice to reach for their freedom, she is one of the slaves shouting "mhysa" at Daenerys. When Daenerys steps into the crowd, she is one of the slaves who embraces her.
