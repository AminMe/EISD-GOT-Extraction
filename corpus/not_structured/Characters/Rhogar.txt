Rhogar is a background character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is a member of the Lost Legion. BiographyEdit "The Sword in the Darkness"Edit 

As Asher Forrester, Beskha, and Malcolm Branfield are chased through the canyon, they enter a cave, with the Lost Legion right behind them. One of the members orders them to alert Rhogar that to gather the rest of the Legion as they follow the trio into the cave. It is unknown if he enters the cave and is killed by one of Asher's group or Drogon.
