Karl Whitehill is an unseen character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series.&nbsp;He is one of the three eldest sons of&nbsp;Lord Ludd Whitehill&nbsp;and Lady Whitehill, brother of&nbsp;Gryff, Ebbert, Torrhen,&nbsp;and&nbsp;Gwyn Whitehill. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "Sons of Winter"Edit 

While waiting for Ludd Whitehill to receive him in Highpoint, Rodrik Forrester notices a tapestry of the Whitehill family. Asking Gwyn about the three boys seen in it, Rodrik learns that Karl died of greyscale several years ago. Family TreeEdit &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;
