A Riverlands traveller is a minor character in the third season. He is portrayed by Joe Purcell and appears in "Dark Wings, Dark Words". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is a peasant who travels through the Riverlands to trade and sell. Season 3Edit 

He saw Jaime Lannister and Brienne of Tarth travel in the Riverlands, and tipped them off to Locke. Locke commands the Bolton men to give him the reward. AppearancesEdit Season Three appearances Valar Dohaeris Dark Wings, Dark Words Walk of Punishment And Now His Watch is Ended Kissed by Fire The Climb The Bear and the Maiden Fair Second Sons The Rains of Castamere Mhysa In the booksEdit 

There is no mention of a traveller in the books. The noise Brienne and Jaime made while fighting was what led to their capture by a dozen of the Brave Companions, a sellsword company led by Vargo Hoat, the book counterpart of Locke.
