A wife of Craster is an unnamed minor character in the fourth season. She is played by guest star Aeryn Walker and debuts in "Oathkeeper". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

She is one of Craster's daughters who he has married. Season 4Edit 

She's been kept by the Mutineers, who have repeatedly raped her.
