Ser Marlon of Tumblestone is an unseen character in Game of Thrones. He is a knight, and a patron of Littlefinger's brothels, where he is said to prefer amputees. His name is as reference to the Tumblestone, one of the tributaries of the Trident. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

Ser Marlon and his unusual tastes are mentioned by Varys when the latter attempts to intimidate Petyr Baelish with the knowledge of what transpires in his brothels. In the booksEdit
