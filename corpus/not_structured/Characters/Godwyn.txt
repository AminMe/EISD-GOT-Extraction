Godwyn an unseen character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "The Ice Dragon"Edit 

Tom discovers that Red Cloaks are searching for Mira Forrester on suspicion the she murdered Damien. He offers to help her escape from King's Landing, and tells her to meet him at a colliers shop by the River Gate on Muddy Way. When he notices how close the guards are, he tells Mira to get to the colliers shop and tell Godwyn he sent her, before allowing himself to be captured. However, Mira never reaches Godwyn, as she is abducted by Lord Rickard Morgryn and handed over to Lucan. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the only character named Godwyn is a guard loyal to House Lannister. He serves the royal family as one of the Red Cloaks station in King's Landing. 

Godwyn accompanies Princess Myrcella Baratheon and Prince Tommen Baratheon in the Red Keep when they encounter Arya Stark, though they do not recognise her and mistake her for a street child.
