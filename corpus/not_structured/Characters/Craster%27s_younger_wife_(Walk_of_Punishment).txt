Craster's younger wife is a minor character in the third season. She is played by Lisa Walsh and debuted in "Walk of Punishment". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

She is one of Craster's wives who he has married. Season 3Edit 

She helps another of Craster's wives with the delivering of Gilly's baby Sam, while Samwell Tarly watches.
