Penn is a lieutenant of the Second Sons. He appears in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "Sons of Winter"Edit 

Penn is present at Daenerys Targaryen's war council along with Asher Forrester, Beskha, Croft, Therrond and Kieran. On Croft's orders, he leads a stealth attack on Meereen's southern beacon. "A Nest of Vipers"Edit 

Penn returns to Daenerys' camp after the Siege of Meereen.
