Bharbo is an unseen character in Game of Thrones. He was a Dothraki Khal and the father of Khal Drogo. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Bharbo was a revered Khal amongst the Dothraki. He is the father of Drogo. Season 1Edit 

Rakharo tells Ser Jorah Mormont that his father was a bloodrider to Khal Bharbo and was a great warrior. When Khal Drogo vows to cross the Narrow Sea and conquer the Seven Kingdoms in response to the attempted assassination of his pregnant wife, Daenerys Targaryen, he swears by his father's name. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Bharbo is mentioned in the same way as in the TV series; Drogo refers to him when swearing to retake the Iron Throne&nbsp;for Daenerys and for their son Rhaego.
