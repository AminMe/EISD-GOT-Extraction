Orphan kid 2 is a background character in the third season who debuts in "Valar Dohaeris." Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

This child is an orphan living at an orphanage in King's Landing. Season 3Edit 

The orphanage he is living at is visited by Margaery Tyrell as she passes through the streets of Flea Bottom. Margaery asks him if his father was a soldier, in which he replies yes. Through King Joffrey's reign, Margaery explains how the soldiers helped saved the city in the Battle of the Blackwater.
