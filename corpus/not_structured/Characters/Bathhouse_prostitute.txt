A bathhouse prostitute is a character who appears in the fourth season. She is played by guest star Samantha McEwan and appears in "The Laws of Gods and Men". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

She is a prostitute living in Braavos. Season 4Edit 

She is at a bathhouse with Salladhor Saan when Davos Seaworth interrupts them and recruits Salladhor. Salladhor confuses her with another prostitute named Lhara who is also present.
