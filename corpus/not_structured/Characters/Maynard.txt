Ser Maynard is a knight from Westeros. He is mentioned in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Maynard is a good friend of Mace Tyrell's, and the two drink together frequently. "The Lost Lords"Edit
