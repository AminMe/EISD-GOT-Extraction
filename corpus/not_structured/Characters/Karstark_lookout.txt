A Karstark lookout is a character in the third season episode "Kissed by Fire". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is one of Rickard Karstark's men. Season 3Edit 

He acts as a lookout when Rickard and his men murdered Martyn and Willem Lannister. When Robb Stark orders that Karstark's accomplices be hanged, he protests that he was only the lookout. Robb, on the basis that the man knew perfectly well what Rickard intended to do and did nothing to stop it, then orders him to be executed last so that he may watch the others die.
