A man of the Night's Watch is an unnamed minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Patrick Molloy and appears in "Breaker of Chains". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is a member of the Night's Watch. Season 4Edit 

He brings a number of recruits to Castle Black, and lists their reasons for being sent to the Wall to one of his brothers.
