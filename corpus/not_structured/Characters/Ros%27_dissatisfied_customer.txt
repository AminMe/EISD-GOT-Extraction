Ros' dissatisfied customer is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Derek Reid and only appears in "The Night Lands." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He pays for the services of the prostitute Ros in the brothel owned by Petyr Baelish. She begins to cry as they are just beginning. He walks out, dissatisfied. Lord Petyr himself emerges to answer his complaints and arranges for him to see Armeca instead.
