Hobb is a minor character in the fourth season who had already been mentioned in the third season.&nbsp;He is a member of the Night's Watch Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Hobb is a steward of the Night's Watch. He serves as the cook of Castle Black. Season 3Edit 

On their way back to the Wall from the mutiny at Craster's Keep,&nbsp;Sam mentions&nbsp;to Gilly&nbsp;that Hobb makes a very tasty venison stew with onions. Season 4Edit 

Hobb prepares to butcher wildlings with his cleaver. 

Acting Commander Thorne warns Jon Snow that Hobb will serve his direwolf Ghost for supper if the creature isn't locked up. 

While staying at Castle Black,&nbsp;Gilly plucks geese and takes them to Hobb in the kitchens. 

During the Battle of Castle Black, a small wildling party comes from the south and breaches the walls of the keep, and some of them break into the kitchen.&nbsp;Hobb calmly walks up to the wildlings, still wearing his apron, and lands his cleaver into the chest of one of them. In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Hobb is also known as Three-Finger Hobb.&nbsp;In the show, he is never identified as such and seems to have all of his fingers.
