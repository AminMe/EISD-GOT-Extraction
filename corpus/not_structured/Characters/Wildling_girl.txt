A wildling girl is an unnamed minor character in the first season. She is played by Claire Wright and only appears in the series premiere. She is seen as a wight in the Haunted Forest. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

She was originally a member of the Free Folk before turning into a wight. Season 1Edit 

During his investigation of a wildling camp, Will of the Night's Watch observes her corpse nailed to a tree. A short time later, he sees her again, now standing and walking; she turns to him, and is revealed to have the blue eyes of a wight. Will flees before she reacts in any way.
