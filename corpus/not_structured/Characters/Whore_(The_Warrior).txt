A whore is a minor recurring character in the fourth and fifth seasons. She is a prostitute in the King's Landing&nbsp;brothel owned by Petyr Baelish. She's played by Xena Avramidis. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 4Edit 

She engages in an orgy with Oberyn Martell and Ellaria Sand in Littlefinger's brothel alongside Olyvar and Marei. She leaves along with the others when Tywin Lannister enters and requests a word with Oberyn. Season 5Edit 

The whore dressed as the Warrior She participates in a sexual roleplay for the High Septon, disguised as the Warrior of the Faith of the Seven. She's is not selected for "worship" by the leader of the religion and seconds later is scared away when a group of Sparrows break into the establishment to seize the High Septon.
