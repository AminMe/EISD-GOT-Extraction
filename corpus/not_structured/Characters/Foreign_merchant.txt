A foreign merchant is a character in the fifth season. He is portrayed by Allon Sylvain and appears in "Sons of the Harpy". Contents[show] BiographyEdit Background Edit 

This lesser merchant, presumably of Essosi origin, deals in idols and images of different religions, including representations of the fiery heart of the Lord of Light. Season 5Edit 

The merchant is in King's Landing when members of the the recently restored Faith Militant approach him and destroy his post and the idols he sells. He shouts for help, but a pair of members of the City Watch ignores him.
