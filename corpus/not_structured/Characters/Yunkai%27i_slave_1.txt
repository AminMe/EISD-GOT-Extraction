Yunkai'i slave Season(s) 3 Appeared in "Mhysa" Status Alive Origin Yunkai Allegiance Daenerys Targaryen Portrayed by Marin Tudor 

A Yunkai'i slave is a minor unnamed character first appearing in Season 3. He's played by Marin Tudor and only appeared in "Mhysa". BiographyEdit Season 3Edit 

He is one of the slaves that are freed by Daenerys Targaryen following the Sack of Yunkai. When Daenerys greets the slaves of Yunkai and tells them that it is their own choice to reach for their freedom, the slave stretches his hand towards Daenerys and calls out "mhysa". After a moment, another follows suit and soon the entire crowd is shouting "mhysa". When Daenerys steps into the crowd, he is one of the slaves who embraces her.
