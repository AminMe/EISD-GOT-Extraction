A Mallister supporter&nbsp;is a member of the Night's Watch from the Shadow Tower. He is played by guest star Derek Lord and only appears in "The House of Black and White". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is a brother of the Night's Watch stationed at the Shadow Tower. Season 5Edit 

He nominates Ser Denys Mallister to be the next Lord Commander of the Night's Watch.
