A Second Son is a character in the fifth season. He is portrayed by Slavko Sobin. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Background Edit 

This man is a member of the Second Sons, an Essosi mercenary company hired by Yunkai to fight against Daenerys Targaryen that went over to her. Season 5Edit 

The Second Son is flirting with a prostitute in Meereenese tavern/brothel, when he has his throat slit by a Son of the Harpy.
