Desmond Crakehall&nbsp;is a character who appears in the&nbsp;third season&nbsp;episode "Mhysa". He is played by&nbsp;Maro Drobnic. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Lord Desmond Crakehall is the Lord of Crakehall and the head of House Crakehall.
