A Frey guard is an unnamed minor character who appears in the third season episode "The Rains of Castamere". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is a soldier sworn to House Frey. Season 3Edit 

The Frey guard turns Sandor Clegane and Arya Stark away from the Twins when they attempt to enter under the guise of a hog farmer and his daughter, as the surrounding soldiers prepare for the Red Wedding massacre. After Sandor voices doubt in the feast being over, the guard asks if he is soft in the head and tells him to turn the cart around. By doing this, the guard actually saves Sandor's life by stopping him entering what is to become the Red Wedding.
