Scolera is a character in the fifth Season. She is played by Mary Jordan and appears in "The Gift". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Scolera is a septa of the Faith of the Seven serving at the Great Sept of Baelor in King's Landing Season 5Edit 

Scolera is present along with Septas&nbsp;Unella and Moelle during the arrest of&nbsp;Cersei Lannister. She watches as Unella throws the former Queen into her cell under the orders of the&nbsp;High Sparrow. 

Before Cersei Lannister's penance walk from the Sept of Baelor to the Red Keep, Scolera and Moelle escort the Queen and help remove her clothes.
