Aegon Targaryen, the son of Prince Rhaegar Targaryen, is an unseen character in Game of Thrones. He is deceased when the events of the series begin and is not expected to appear in the series. Contents[show] Biography Background 

Aegon is killed by Gregor Clegane Aegon Targaryen was the son of Prince Rhaegar Targaryen and Elia Martell. He had an older sister, Rhaenys Targaryen. House Targaryen was deposed as the ruling house of Westeros during Robert's Rebellion. Rhaegar was killed during the fighting and Elia and both their children were killed soon afterwards during the Sack of King's Landing.
