Bryan is an unseen character mentioned in the second season. Bryan was Jaime Lannister's squire on the day of Willem Frey's wedding tournament. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

While imprisoned by House Stark, Ser Alton and Jaime Lannister talk about Willem Frey's wedding. Ser Alton mentioned he filled in as Jaime's squire, when Bryan threw up on his horse. Jaime said it was Tyrion who made him so drunk. In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there is no mention of Jaime ever having a squire named Bryan. ReferencesEdit ↑ "A Man Without Honor"
