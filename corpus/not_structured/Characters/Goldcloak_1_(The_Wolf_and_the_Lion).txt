A Goldcloak is a minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Alan Paris and only appears in "The Wolf and the Lion." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

He and his partner stop Arya Stark at the entrance of the Red Keep but back down when she reveals her parentage.
