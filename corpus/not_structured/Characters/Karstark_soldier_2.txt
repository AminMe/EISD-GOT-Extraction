A Karstark soldier is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Duncan Lacroix and only appears in "A Man Without Honor." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He calls for justice for House Karstark when Jaime Lannister (whom he calls "sisterfucker") is recaptured, having killed Torrhen Karstark during an escape attempt.
