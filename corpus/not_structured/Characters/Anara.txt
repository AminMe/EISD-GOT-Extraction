Anara is a character in the fifth season. She is played by Ollie Kram and appears in "The Dance of Dragons". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Anara is a prostitute living in Braavos. She practices her trade in the same brothel as Lhara and Brea. Season 5Edit 

Anara is shown to Ser Meryn Trant after Brea is dismissed for being too old for Meryn's tastes. Even though the madam states Anara is the most expensive of he establishment's girls and assures Meryn she's worth the price, Meryn discards her, finding even her too old.
