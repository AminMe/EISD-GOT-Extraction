Namond is an unseen character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. Contents[show] HistoryEdit BackgroundEdit 

Namond was a friend of Rodrik Forrester and Meghan. The three of them would play together on Red Rob's farm when they were children. "The Sword in the Darkness"Edit 

Rodrik travels to Red Rob's abandoned farm to meet in secret with Gwyn Whitehill. He examines an old well and remembers hiding in there while playing Monsters-and-maidens with Namond and Meghan.
