A brothel guard is a character in the fifth season. He is played by Mishael Lopes Cardozo and appears in "High Sparrow". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He guards the door at a brothel located in the Long Bridge of Volantis. He just speaks the Low Valyrian of Volantis. Season 5Edit 

When Tyrion Lannister and Varys are about to enter the brothel this man is guarding, the guard rubs Tyrion's head, citing in that it is good luck to rub a dwarf's head. Tyrion quips that it is better luck to "suck his cock."
