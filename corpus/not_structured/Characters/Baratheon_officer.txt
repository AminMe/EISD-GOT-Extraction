A Baratheon Officer is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Kieran Cunningham and only appears in "Blackwater." Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

This man is an officer in the army of House Baratheon of Dragonstone. Season 2Edit 

During the Battle of the Blackwater he commands troops attacking the Mud Gate. He yells to his soldiers to get up the ladders. His leg is chopped off by Tyrion Lannister, after which&nbsp;he collapses to the ground and Tyrion finishes him with a blow to the head.
