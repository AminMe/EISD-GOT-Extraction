A Lannister guardsman is an unnamed minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Conor Delaney and only appears in "The Kingsroad." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

He leads the Lannister guardsmen who accompany King Robert's party to Winterfell and back. On the return journey Prince Joffrey is bitten by the direwolf Nymeria. He leads a search for the wolf and reports his inability to find her to the King. When Queen Cersei demands that Sansa's direwolf Lady be killed in her place he reports that Lady is chained up outside the Inn at the Crossroads.
