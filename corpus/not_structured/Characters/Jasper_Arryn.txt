Jasper Arryn is an unseen character in Game of Thrones. He is deceased when the events of the series begin and is not expected to appear in the series. He has only appeared in the House Arryn family tree issued by HBO. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Jasper was the Head of House Arryn and the Lord of the Eyrie. He had three children:&nbsp;Jon, Alys, and Ronnel. Family treeEdit
