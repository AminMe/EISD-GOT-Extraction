Talia Branfield&nbsp;was the mother of&nbsp;Malcolm Branfield and Elissa Forrester.&nbsp;She is mentioned in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Lady Talia was the mother of Elissa, Malcolm, and several other children. She passed away during or after Robert's Rebellion. "Iron From Ice"Edit
