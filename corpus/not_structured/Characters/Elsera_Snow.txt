Elsera Snow is the bastard daughter of Lord Gregor Forrester and the sister of Josera Snow, both of whom live in the North Grove. She is a witch capable of performing blood magic rituals. She also controls an army of wildling warriors, that are heavily implied to be undead. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

Elsera and Josera, as the bastard children of Gregor, were his hidden shame. Because of this, he sent them beyond the Wall to the North Grove to protect it. "The Ice Dragon"Edit
