Ebbert Whitehill is an unseen character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is one of the three eldest sons of Lord Ludd Whitehill and Lady Whitehill, brother of Gryff, Gwyn, Karl and Torrhen. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "Sons of Winter"Edit 

Ebbert is mentioned when Rodrik Forrester visits Highpoint and talks with Ebbert's sister, Gwyn, regarding a tapestry of the Whitehill family. Ebbert, as a second born son, has been sent to The Citadel to train to become a maester. Family TreeEdit &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;
