Endrew Tarth is a minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Dez McMahon and only appears in "Two Swords". He is a knight of House Tarth and a sworn brother of the Night's Watch, serving as the Master-at-arms at Castle Black. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 4Edit 

Ser Endrew oversees archery training 

Ser Endrew oversees the archery training of new recruits of the Watch who have arrived in Castle Black along with Ser Alliser Thorne and Janos Slynt. AppearancesEdit Season Four appearances Two Swords The Lion and the Rose Breaker of Chains Oathkeeper First of His Name The Laws of Gods and Men Mockingbird The Mountain and the Viper The Watchers on the Wall The Children In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Ser Endrew Tarth is summoned from the Shadow Tower to serve as the new Master-at-arms of Castle Black after Lord Commander Jeor Mormont decides to send Alliser Thorne to King's Landing. 

During the Battle of Castle Black, Bowen Marsh chases three hundred wildlings led by the Weeper to the Shadow Tower, fights them at the Bridge of Skulls and defeats them, but at great cost: more than hundred brothers are killed, among them Ser Endrew. 

His relation with the main branch of House Tarth is not mentioned. See alsoEdit Endrew Tarth on A Wiki of Ice and Fire (spoilers from the books) ReferencesEdit ↑ "Two Swords"
