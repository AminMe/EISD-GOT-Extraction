A Lannister torturer is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Mark Byatt and only appears in "The Prince of Winterfell." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 2Edit 

He can be heard torturing Lannister soldiers for information about the murder of Ser Amory Lorch as Arya Stark passes the gallows at Harrenhal.
