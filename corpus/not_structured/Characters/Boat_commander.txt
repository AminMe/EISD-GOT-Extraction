An unidentified boat commander is a character in the third season. He is played by Michael Power. Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

This man is a member of Salladhor Saan's crew. Season 3Edit 

When Davos Seaworth is spotted atop a rocky skerry in Blackwater Bay, he commands a ship to investigate. After Davos identifies himself and acknowledges his loyalty to Stannis Baratheon, the commander smiles and takes Davos back to Saan's ship.
