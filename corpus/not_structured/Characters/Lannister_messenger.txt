A Lannister messenger is an unnamed minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Simon Stewart and only appears in "The Pointy End." Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

He is part of the Lannister army in the Riverlands and delivers news of the movements of the Stark army to Tywin Lannister.
