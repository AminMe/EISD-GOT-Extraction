Gordy is a minor character in the fifth season. He is played by Tristan McConnell. Contents[show] Biography Edit Background Edit 

Gordy is a soldier in service to House Bolton. Season 5 Edit 

Gordy takes part in the Battle of Winterfell. After the battle, he and another Bolton soldier encounter a wounded Stannis Baratheon in the forest. They attack him but are promptly slain.
