A King's Landing whore is an unnamed recurring character in the first season. She is played by guest star Emily Diamond and debuts in "Lord Snow." She is a whore in the brothel of Littlefinger in King's Landing. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

She stumbles into a meeting between Littlefinger and Catelyn Stark and is waved away. Later on she attracts the attention of Jory Cassel during Lord Eddard Stark's visit to the brothel.
