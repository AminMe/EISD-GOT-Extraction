The first mate is an unnamed minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Rhodri Miles and debuts in "The Laws of Gods and Men". Contents[show] BiographyEdit BackgroundEdit 

He is one of the fifty best ironborn fighters and follows Yara Greyjoy after she chooses him to go with her to the Dreadfort to rescue Theon Greyjoy. Season 4Edit 

He enters the Dreadfort under the command of Yara Greyjoy as they try to take Theon Greyjoy back to the Iron Islands. They fight fiercely and kill many Bolton men, but when Ramsay Snow sets his hunting dogs free, they are forced to retreat to their boat.
