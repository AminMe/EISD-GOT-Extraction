Contents[show] BackgroundEdit 

Calla&nbsp;is the seven-year-old daughter of&nbsp;Master&nbsp;Mighdal, one of the Great Masters of Meereen. She was tutored by a slave named Fennesz before Daenerys Targaryen abolished slavery in the city. ​BiographyEdit Season 4Edit 

When&nbsp;Fennesz explains his background to Daenerys Targaryen, he mentions that Calla has come to admire her after learning about her family's history and her accomplishments. Behind the ScenesEdit 

The name Calla ​was selected by G.O.T writer D.B. Weiss in honor of (Carla). Carla is the wife of his beloved childhood friend David Migdal (Master Mighdal). In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there is no equivalent to this character. 

The only other person named "Calla" is "Calla Blackfyre", a daughter of Daemon Blackfyre. ​ReferencesEdit ↑ 1.0 1.1 "The Children" Retrieved from "http://gameofthrones.wikia.com/wiki/Calla?oldid=193643"
