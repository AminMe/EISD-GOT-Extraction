Wendel is a minor character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is a brother of the Night's Watch. Contents[show] BiographyEdit "The Sword in the Darkness"Edit 

At Castle Black, Jon Snow orders Wendel to pack extra rations while preparing for his raid of Craster's Keep.
