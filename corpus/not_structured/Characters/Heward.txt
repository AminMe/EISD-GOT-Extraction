Heward is a character in the first season. He is a guardsman sworn to House Stark. Contents[show] BiographyEdit Season 1Edit 

Heward travels south with Lord Eddard Stark when the latter is appointed Hand of the King. He accompanies Lord Stark to one of Petyr Baelish's brothels, and is killed by Lannister guardsmen on the orders of Ser Jaime . In the booksEdit 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Heward is also a guardsman sworn to House Stark. He is killed by the same manner as in the show.
