
The House of Loraq is one of the ancient noble slaver families from the city of Meereen, known as the Great Masters.

 Known members 
The head of the House of Loraq, crucified under Daenerys Targaryen's orders.
Hizdahr zo Loraq, his son, and new head of the house of Loraq.


In the books
In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the House of Loraq is one of the ancient and most prestigious slaving families of Meereen, having produced famous figures. The family colors are purple, indigo, and lilac.
There is no mention in the novels that Hizdahr's father is one of the Great Masters who were executed by Daenerys.
