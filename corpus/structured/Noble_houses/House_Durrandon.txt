</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Durrandon</b> is a former <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great House","link":"Great Houses","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Houses|Great House]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Great_Houses" title="Great Houses" class="mw-redirect">Great House</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It ruled over <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stormlands","link":"the Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/The_Stormlands" title="The Stormlands">the Stormlands</a> until the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of Conquest","link":"War of Conquest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of Conquest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/War_of_Conquest" title="War of Conquest">War of Conquest</a>. It also held much of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a> until they lost them to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Hoare","link":"House Hoare","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Hoare]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/House_Hoare" title="House Hoare">House Hoare</a>. The head of House Durrandon held the title of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm King","link":"Storm King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Storm_King" title="Storm King">Storm King</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The House was founded by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Durran Godsgriefl","link":"Durran","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Durran|Durran Godsgriefl]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Durran" title="Durran">Durran Godsgriefl</a>, a legendary figure from the Age of Heores. The last King of the Durrandon dynasty, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Argilac the Arrogant","link":"Argilac Durrandon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Argilac Durrandon|Argilac the Arrogant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Argilac_Durrandon" title="Argilac Durrandon">Argilac the Arrogant</a> was killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Orys Baratheon","link":"Orys Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Orys Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Orys_Baratheon" title="Orys Baratheon">Orys Baratheon</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Last Storm","link":"Last Storm","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Last Storm]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Last_Storm" title="Last Storm">Last Storm</a>. Baratheon took Argilac's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"daughter","link":"Argalia Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Argalia Baratheon|daughter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Argalia_Baratheon" title="Argalia Baratheon">daughter</a> as his wife, as well as his sigil and words, taking over the Stormlands as vassal of his half-brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon the Conqueror","link":"Aegon the Conqueror","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon the Conqueror]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Aegon_the_Conqueror" title="Aegon the Conqueror" class="mw-redirect">Aegon the Conqueror</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Durrandon was the dynasty that claimed descent from Durran Godsgrief, the first Storm King and founder of Storm's End. King Arrec of House Durrandon was the last Storm King who ruled <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>, conquered by his ancestors three hundred years before, losing it to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> led by King Harwyn Hardhand of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Hoare","link":"House Hoare","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Hoare]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/House_Hoare" title="House Hoare">House Hoare</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Durrandon seemingly left no other known surviving branches, and is officially extinct, but its female bloodline lived on in the Baratheon dynasty.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Eventually, the bloodline of House Baratheon perished with the deaths of Shireen and Stannis in the TV series, thus, ending the legitimate Durrandon bloodline as well. The only known living descendants of the Baratheons or Durrandons are any surviving bastard children of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64512187556cca953ae19a" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>.
</p>
