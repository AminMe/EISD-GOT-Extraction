</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Glenmore</b> is a noble house from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rills","link":"The Rills","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Rills|Rills]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/The_Rills" title="The Rills">Rills</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"North","link":"The North","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The North|North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">North</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. They are unique to <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series","link":"Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Game_of_Thrones:_A_Telltale_Games_Series" title="Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series">Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series</a></i>. Their seat is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rillwater Crossing","link":"Rillwater Crossing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rillwater Crossing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Rillwater_Crossing" title="Rillwater Crossing">Rillwater Crossing</a>, and they hold fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Ryswell","link":"House Ryswell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Ryswell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/House_Ryswell" title="House Ryswell">House Ryswell</a> of the Rills.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Glenmores have significant ties to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a>, though according to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Elaena","link":"Elaena Glenmore","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elaena Glenmore|Elaena]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Elaena_Glenmore" title="Elaena Glenmore">Elaena</a>, she herself has never been personally written to by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady Margaery","link":"Margaery Tyrell","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Margaery Tyrell|Lady Margaery]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Margaery_Tyrell" title="Margaery Tyrell">Lady Margaery</a>. The Glenmores are also on relatively good terms with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Forrester","link":"House Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/House_Forrester" title="House Forrester">House Forrester</a>, and Elaena was betrothed to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Forrester","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik Forrester</a> before the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a> broke out. Their forces seem to have not been majority affected by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">A recently returned Rodrik meets with Elaena at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a> in order to secure the betrothal, largely so that the Glenmores' army will become oathbound to Rodrik, allowing the Forresters to retaliate against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Whitehill","link":"House Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/House_Whitehill" title="House Whitehill">House Whitehill</a>. However, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Glenmore","link":"Glenmore (Lord)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Glenmore (Lord)|Lord Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Glenmore_(Lord)" title="Glenmore (Lord)">Lord Glenmore</a> had asked Elaena to reconsider, given what had recently happened to the Forresters. Depending on player choice, Rodrik may either win or lose the betrothal.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Sword in the Darkness","link":"The Sword in the Darkness","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Sword in the Darkness]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/The_Sword_in_the_Darkness" title="The Sword in the Darkness">The Sword in the Darkness</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan Tuttle","link":"Duncan Tuttle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan Tuttle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Duncan_Tuttle" title="Duncan Tuttle">Duncan Tuttle</a> mentions that the Rodrik either gained the aid of the Glenmores or lost it. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Royland Degore","link":"Royland Degore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Royland Degore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Royland_Degore" title="Royland Degore">Royland Degore</a> also mentions if you gained their help that their forces aren't available to the Forresters until after the wedding.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sons of Winter","link":"Sons of Winter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sons of Winter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Sons_of_Winter" title="Sons of Winter">Sons of Winter</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Elaena and her brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arthur Glenmore","link":"Arthur Glenmore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arthur Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Arthur_Glenmore" title="Arthur Glenmore">Arthur Glenmore</a> visit Ironrath, to treat with Rodrik on matters of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gryff Whitehill","link":"Gryff Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gryff Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Gryff_Whitehill" title="Gryff Whitehill">Gryff Whitehill</a>, who is now betrothed to Elaena after Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ludd Whitehill","link":"Ludd Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ludd Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Ludd_Whitehill" title="Ludd Whitehill">Ludd Whitehill</a> threatened Lord Glenmore that unless Elaena marries Gryff, they would be slaughtered along with the Forresters, which Glenmore agreed to. Elaena asks that Rodrik aid them in taking down Gryff, and in exchange the Elite Guard of House Glenmore will be his to command. The guard helps Rodrik imprison the Whitehill Garrison of Ironrath. Rodrik can bring the Glenmores to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Highpoint","link":"Highpoint","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Highpoint]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Highpoint" title="Highpoint">Highpoint</a>, or leave them to defend Ironrath. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Upon returning to Ironrath, with the guard, Ironrath is found abandoned. Arthur begins a search for his sister. If Rodrik did not bring the guard to Ironrath he will instead find arrows and puddles of blood, indicating a battle.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Nest of Vipers","link":"A Nest of Vipers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Nest of Vipers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/A_Nest_of_Vipers" title="A Nest of Vipers">A Nest of Vipers</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-172850681056cca9601572e" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a> flays Arthur Glenmore and sends the guard home. Elaena becomes very distraught at this. Rodrik can comfort Elaena, either letting her rest by herself or join her. If you join her, later in the episode, Elaena will be in Rodrik's bed. If you let her rest, Rodrik states she can stay at Ironrath as long as she likes.
