 House Wylde is a vassal house which holds fealty to House Baratheon of Storm's End. Known members Ser Willem Wylde, a member of the Kingsguard of King Daeron II Targaryen. In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Wylde is a noble house from Rain House in the Stormlands. It is one of the principal houses sworn to House Baratheon.
