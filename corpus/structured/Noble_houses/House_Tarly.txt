<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201240216156cca952543f0" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Tarly is a loyal vassal of the Tyrells. Lord Randyll Tarly has a formidable reputation as a general, and has been named the finest battlefield commander in all of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201240216156cca952543f0" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>. During <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201240216156cca952543f0" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, Randyll Tarly was the only royalist general to win a decisive victory over <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201240216156cca952543f0" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>, defeating his army at the Battle of Ashford despite being outnumbered. Randyll's skills were later squandered during the fruitless <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Siege of Storm's End","link":"Siege of Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201240216156cca952543f0" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Storm's_End" title="Siege of Storm's End">Siege of Storm's End</a>, while Robert and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201240216156cca952543f0" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> won great victories further north.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Randyll is a hard, ruthless man. When his son Samwell showed a preference for songs, poetry, fine clothes and good food over martial valor and a love of warfare, Randyll disinherited him and sent him to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201240216156cca952543f0" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> to join the Night's Watch. His fiercer, younger son Dickon was made heir in Samwell's stead.
</p>
