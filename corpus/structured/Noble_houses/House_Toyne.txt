<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25003542356cca9a4ceadc" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Toyne fell from grace after Ser Terrence Toyne of the Kingsguard was beheaded under orders of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon IV Targaryen","link":"Aegon IV Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon IV Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25003542356cca9a4ceadc" href="/wiki/Aegon_IV_Targaryen" title="Aegon IV Targaryen">Aegon IV Targaryen</a> for having an affair with one of the King's mistresses. His brothers tried to avenge his death but were stopped by Prince Aemon the Dragonknight, though the latter lost his life in the process. Afterward the brothers Toyne were also executed for treason. Some members of House Toyne went into exile across the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Narrow Sea","link":"Narrow Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Narrow Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25003542356cca9a4ceadc" href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a> where they joined <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Blackfyre","link":"House Blackfyre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Blackfyre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25003542356cca9a4ceadc" href="/wiki/House_Blackfyre" title="House Blackfyre">House Blackfyre</a> in their repeated attempts to unseat <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25003542356cca9a4ceadc" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a> from the Iron Throne.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Their sigil is a winged heart, black on gold, within a black bordure embattled and their words are "Fly High, Fly Far".
</p>
