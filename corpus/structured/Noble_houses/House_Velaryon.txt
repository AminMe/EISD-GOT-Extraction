<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22781598556cca96e2ec42" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Velaryon is an ancient and proud house, with the blood of old <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyria","link":"Valyria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22781598556cca96e2ec42" href="/wiki/Valyria" title="Valyria" class="mw-redirect">Valyria</a> in its veins. Some members were married into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22781598556cca96e2ec42" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a> before and after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of Conquest","link":"War of Conquest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of Conquest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22781598556cca96e2ec42" href="/wiki/War_of_Conquest" title="War of Conquest">War of Conquest</a>. Velaryons, like the Targaryens are born with the Valyrian features of silver-blond hair and purple eyes. They rule over Driftmark, the largest island of the Blackwater Bay, and are sworn to Dragonstone. Their arms depict a silver seahorse on sea green and their titles are Lord of the Tides and Master of Driftmark. Their words do not appear in the books, but according to semi-canon sources they are "The Old, the True, the Brave".
</p>
