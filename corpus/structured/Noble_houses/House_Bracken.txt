<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>House Bracken</b> is a vassal house that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>. Their lands are in the heart of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>, located along the banks of the Red Fork of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Trident","link":"Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Trident" title="Trident" class="mw-redirect">Trident</a>. Their seat is known as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stone Hedge","link":"Stone Hedge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stone Hedge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Stone_Hedge" title="Stone Hedge">Stone Hedge</a>. The head of the house holds the title <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Stone Hedge","link":"Lord of Stone Hedge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Stone Hedge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Stone_Hedge" title="Lord of Stone Hedge">Lord of Stone Hedge</a>. Their current lord is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jonos Bracken","link":"Jonos Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jonos Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Jonos_Bracken" title="Jonos Bracken">Jonos Bracken</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Bracken's sigil is a red stallion on a gold field.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Brackens hold an ancient grudge against their neighbors, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackwoods","link":"House Blackwood","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Blackwood|Blackwoods]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/House_Blackwood" title="House Blackwood">Blackwoods</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Raventree Hall","link":"Raventree Hall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raventree Hall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Raventree_Hall" title="Raventree Hall">Raventree Hall</a>. The two houses, which hold the blood of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"First Men","link":"First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/First_Men" title="First Men">First Men</a> in their veins and merged with the invading <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Andals","link":"Andals","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Andals]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Andals" title="Andals">Andals</a> rather than being completely conquered by them, competed for the throne of the Riverlands when it was an independent kingdom. The enmity and competition continues to this day and was increased when House Bracken converted to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Faith of the Seven","link":"Faith of the Seven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Faith of the Seven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Faith_of_the_Seven" title="Faith of the Seven">Faith of the Seven</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season1\/#!\/locations\/stone-hedge\/ HBO viewers guide, season 1 map, Stone Hedge entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2>History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Season 1</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">At the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Crossroads Inn","link":"Crossroads Inn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crossroads Inn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Crossroads_Inn" title="Crossroads Inn" class="mw-redirect">Crossroads Inn</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> asks for the assistance of a man-at-arms of House Bracken in taking <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> prisoner. The man-at-arms, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kurleket","link":"Kurleket","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kurleket]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Kurleket" title="Kurleket">Kurleket</a>, agrees to aid her. She tells him that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"her father","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully|her father]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">her father</a> counts Lord Jonos as one of his most loyal bannermen.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> The banner of House Bracken flies at the tourney of the Hand in King's Landing. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kurleket","link":"Kurleket","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kurleket]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Kurleket" title="Kurleket">Kurleket</a> is killed in a clash with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"hill tribes","link":"hill tribes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[hill tribes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Hill_tribes" title="Hill tribes">hill tribes</a> on the eastern edge of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>. Tyrion uses his shield to batter to death one of the tribesmen attacking Catelyn Stark.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Jonos Bracken himself joins the army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> as it crosses the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>, following the victory at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood|Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Whispering Wood</a>. As Robb, the Northern lords and the Riverlords debate a course of action, Bracken suggests declaring for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>, to which Robb refuses as Renly is second in line to his older brother, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a>. After <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greatjon Umber","link":"Greatjon Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greatjon Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Greatjon_Umber" title="Greatjon Umber" class="mw-redirect">Greatjon Umber</a> interrupts and suggests they crown Robb as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King in the North","link":"King in the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King in the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/King_in_the_North" title="King in the North">King in the North</a>, Bracken joins the Northern lords and his fellow Riverlords in proclaiming Robb their King and seceding their lands from the authority of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Fire and Blood]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130732486856cca970d4153" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Bracken is a loyal vassal of the Tullys. The enmity between Brackens and Blackwoods was radically exacerbated when Ser Otho Bracken slew Lord Quentyn Blackwood in a tourney accident roughly a century ago.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The sigil of House Bracken is slightly different in the books: like the TV series, it consists of a red stallion on gold, however, in the books the outside edge of the gold field also has a brown border - though it was <i>briefly</i> shown surmounting a brown escutcheon at the tournament in Season 1.
