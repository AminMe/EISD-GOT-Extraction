<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-745589856cca9a59d335" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Greenfield is a house of landed knights sworn to Casterly Rock. Their seat is Greenfield, though its location has not yet been revealed. Neither their arms or their words appear in the books, but according to semi-canon sources they blazon their sigil is as a double tressure white on green.
</p>
