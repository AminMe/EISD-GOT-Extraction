</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Merlyn</b> is a vassal house from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52289643156cca99d2b743" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a> that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Greyjoy","link":"House Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52289643156cca99d2b743" href="/wiki/House_Greyjoy" title="House Greyjoy">House Greyjoy</a>. Their seat is located on the island of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Wyk","link":"Great Wyk","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Wyk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52289643156cca99d2b743" href="/wiki/Great_Wyk" title="Great Wyk">Great Wyk</a>, the largest of the Iron Isles.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Season 2 bluray: War of the Five Kings feature<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52289643156cca99d2b743" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52289643156cca99d2b743" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Merlyn is seated at Pebbleton, in Great Wyk. They blazon their arms with twining waterspouts, green on white. Their current lord is Meldred Merlyn, also known as "the Merlyn".
</p>
