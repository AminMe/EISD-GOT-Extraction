</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Towers</b> is an extinct noble house of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124806394456cca9941d928" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>. Their sigil was five black towers on white, surrounded by a double tressure red and black.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Their seat was the castle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124806394456cca9941d928" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>, which was granted to them after the extinction of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Qoherys","link":"House Qoherys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Qoherys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124806394456cca9941d928" href="/wiki/House_Qoherys" title="House Qoherys">House Qoherys</a>. However, the Towers also died out, and the castle was passed on to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Harroway","link":"House Harroway","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Harroway]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124806394456cca9941d928" href="/wiki/House_Harroway" title="House Harroway">House Harroway</a>.
</p>
