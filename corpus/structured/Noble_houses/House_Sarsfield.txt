 House Sarsfield is a noble house of the Westerlands. They are vassals to House Lannister. Members Ser Eldrick Sarsfield, a knight. In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Sarsfield is briefly mentioned and its only known members are: Ser Melwyn Sarsfield. Unnamed squire who accompanied Polliver and the Tickler when they encountered Arya Stark and the Hound at the Inn at the Crossroads. Killed by Arya during the ensuing brawl.
