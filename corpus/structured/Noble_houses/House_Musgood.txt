<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118084940456cca99c652a1" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Musgood has only been mentioned. Jack Musgood was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118084940456cca99c652a1" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118084940456cca99c652a1" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> during the invasion of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Raymun Redbeard","link":"Raymun Redbeard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raymun Redbeard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118084940456cca99c652a1" href="/wiki/Raymun_Redbeard" title="Raymun Redbeard">Raymun Redbeard</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King Beyond the Wall","link":"King Beyond the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King Beyond the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118084940456cca99c652a1" href="/wiki/King_Beyond_the_Wall" title="King Beyond the Wall" class="mw-redirect">King Beyond the Wall</a>. He didn't notice Raymun's invasion and was known after that as Sleepy Jack. Their sigil is a golden pavillion on blue and a green laurel crown on white, quartered.
</p>
