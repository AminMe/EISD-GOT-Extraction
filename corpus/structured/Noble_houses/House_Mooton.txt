</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Mooton</b> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maidenpool","link":"Maidenpool","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maidenpool]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/Maidenpool" title="Maidenpool">Maidenpool</a> is a vassal house that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>. They control Maidenpool, a port town near the border between the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Crownlands","link":"Crownlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crownlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/Crownlands" title="Crownlands" class="mw-redirect">Crownlands</a>, on the Bay of Crabs. Their sigil is a red salmon.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">To protect her identity, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> claims to be from Maidenpool while serving <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> as a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"cupbearer","link":"cupbearer","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[cupbearer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/Cupbearer" title="Cupbearer">cupbearer</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169521339356cca991c6537" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>.
