</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Fossoway</b> is a vassal house from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a> that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a>. Their seat is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cider Hall","link":"Cider Hall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cider Hall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Cider_Hall" title="Cider Hall">Cider Hall</a> and their sigil is a red apple on a gold field.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">A member of House Fossoway fought in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Trial by seven of Duncan the Tall","link":"Trial by seven of Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Trial by seven of Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Trial_by_seven_of_Duncan_the_Tall" title="Trial by seven of Duncan the Tall">Trial by seven of Duncan the Tall</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Justice of the Seven Kingdoms]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">House Fossoway follows their liege lords, House Tyrell, in pledging support to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>'s claim to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a>. Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"death","link":"Assassination of Renly I","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Assassination of Renly I|death]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Assassination_of_Renly_I" title="Assassination of Renly I">death</a> of Renly, however, the Fossoways do not follow their Tyrell overlords and instead go over to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a>. They participate in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Season 2 Bluray: War of the Five Kings feature<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Fossoway of Cider Hall is commonly referred as the red-apple Fossoways, to differentiate them from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"other branch","link":"Cadet branch","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cadet branch|other branch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Cadet_branch" title="Cadet branch">other branch</a> of the house, the "green apple" Fossoways of New Barrel, who split from the red apples in 208 AL. After Stannis' defeat at the Battle of the Blackwater, they bend the knee to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey","link":"Joffrey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Joffrey" title="Joffrey" class="mw-redirect">Joffrey</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Leonette Fossoway is married to Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Garlan Tyrell","link":"Garlan Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Garlan Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Garlan_Tyrell" title="Garlan Tyrell">Garlan Tyrell</a>, elder brother of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Margaery","link":"Margaery Tyrell","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Margaery Tyrell|Margaery]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Margaery_Tyrell" title="Margaery Tyrell">Margaery</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Loras Tyrell","link":"Loras Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Loras Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139690698256cca97b8bf24" href="/wiki/Loras_Tyrell" title="Loras Tyrell">Loras Tyrell</a>.
</p>
