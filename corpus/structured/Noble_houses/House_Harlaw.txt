<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36292932856cca9619e001" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Harlaw is a powerful family with strong ties to the Greyjoys. They have several branches along the island of Harlaw as well as several vassals. The sigil of House Harlaw is a silver scythe on black. The current head of the family is Lord Rodrik Harlaw, brother of Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Alannys Harlaw","link":"Alannys Harlaw","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Alannys Harlaw]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36292932856cca9619e001" href="/wiki/Alannys_Harlaw" title="Alannys Harlaw" class="mw-redirect">Alannys Harlaw</a>, called Rodrik the Reader for his passion for books (a habit that other ironmen find strange and unmanly).
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">As one of the wealthiest and most powerful families in the Iron Islands, House Harlaw has multiple <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"cadet branches","link":"Cadet branch","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cadet branch|cadet branches]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36292932856cca9619e001" href="/wiki/Cadet_branch" title="Cadet branch">cadet branches</a>:
