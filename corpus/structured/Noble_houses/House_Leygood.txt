House Leygood is a vassal house from the Reach that holds fealty to House Tyrell of Highgarden. History 

 Season 4 

Bronn mentions to Jaime Lannister he's having an affair with the wife of a knight from House Leygood. In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels no member of House Leygood has been mentioned by name. House Leygood is part of the Tyrell force that participates in the Battle of the Blackwater.
