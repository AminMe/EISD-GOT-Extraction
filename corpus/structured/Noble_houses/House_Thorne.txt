<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">As a vassal house sworn to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-24390952756cca96ebe5ff" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, House Thorne contributed troops to the defense of the city during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sack of King's Landing","link":"Sack of King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-24390952756cca96ebe5ff" href="/wiki/Sack_of_King's_Landing" title="Sack of King's Landing">Sack of King's Landing</a>. Ser Alliser Thorne was on the walls when the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister|Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-24390952756cca96ebe5ff" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">Lannister</a> troops stormed the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-24390952756cca96ebe5ff" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a>. He was wounded and captured. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-24390952756cca96ebe5ff" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> gave him the choice between taking the black or being executed. He chose the former.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Nothing else is known about the family save their arms - a silver flail on a red background - and the fact that they still exist.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">A historial member of the house is Ser Rickard Thorne, a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-24390952756cca96ebe5ff" href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight" class="mw-redirect">knight</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-24390952756cca96ebe5ff" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> who served during the reign of King Viserys I and chose to side with King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon II","link":"Aegon II Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon II Targaryen|Aegon II]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-24390952756cca96ebe5ff" href="/wiki/Aegon_II_Targaryen" title="Aegon II Targaryen">Aegon II</a> during the Dance of the Dragons. 
</p>
