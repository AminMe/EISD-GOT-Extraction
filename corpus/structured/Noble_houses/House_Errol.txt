House Errol is a vassal house from the Stormlands that holds fealty to House Baratheon. Their seat is Haystack Hall and their sigil is a yellow haystack on an orange field. History 

 Season 2 

House Errol, along with most noble houses of the Stormlands, pledge support to Renly Baratheon's claim to the Iron Throne.
