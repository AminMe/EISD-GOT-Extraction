His wife <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Marya","link":"Marya Seaworth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Marya Seaworth|Marya]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75081733556cca96b9fee8" href="/wiki/Marya_Seaworth" title="Marya Seaworth">Marya</a> and their youngest sons are usually found at their keep.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Seaworth came into existence after <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75081733556cca96b9fee8" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75081733556cca96b9fee8" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Davos Seaworth has seven sons: Dale, Allard, Matthos, Marc, Devan, Stannis, and Steffon. The oldest five serve in Stannis' army, though the younger two are children too young to serve. Dale, Allard, Matthos, and Marc are all killed in the Battle of the Blackwater. In the TV series, these four sons have been functionally condensed into Matthos. Till now only Matthos appeared and was mentioned after his death, suggesting that he was Davos' only son.
</p>
