</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Goodbrother</b> is a vassal house from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a> that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Greyjoy","link":"House Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/House_Greyjoy" title="House Greyjoy">House Greyjoy</a>. Their seat is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hammerhorn","link":"Hammerhorn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hammerhorn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/Hammerhorn" title="Hammerhorn">Hammerhorn</a>, a castle located on the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hardstone Hills","link":"Hardstone Hills","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hardstone Hills]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/Hardstone_Hills" title="Hardstone Hills">Hardstone Hills</a> of the island of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Wyk","link":"Great Wyk","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Wyk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/Great_Wyk" title="Great Wyk">Great Wyk</a>, the largest of the Iron Isles.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Season 2 bluray: War of the Five Kings feature<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Goodbrother is one of the most powerful houses from Great Wyk. Their keep of Hammerhorn is inland, in the Hardstone Hills, their wealth not coming from the sea but from their mines. Their blazon is a gold-banded black warhorn on red. Their current lord is Gorold Goodbrother.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Goodbrother also has three cadet branches: House Goodbrother of Downdelving, House Goodbrother of Crow Spike Keep, and House Goodbrother of Corpse Lake.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Goodbrother briefly ruled the Iron Islands as Iron Kings. After a King of House Greyiron died, his brother called a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"kingsmoot","link":"kingsmoot","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[kingsmoot]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/Kingsmoot" title="Kingsmoot">kingsmoot</a> while the King's son, Torgon, was away. However, Urragon Goodbrother was chosen instead. Urragon then killed all of the late King's family, becoming known as "Badbrother".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">However, Torgon returned after two years and declared the kingsmoot unlawful since he wasnt' present to put forth his candidacy, as was his right. Due to Urragon's cruelty, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drowned Men","link":"Drowned Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drowned Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79945033556cca994ab7d7" href="/wiki/Drowned_Men" title="Drowned Men">Drowned Men</a> rose against Urragon and raised Torgon to kingship.
</p>
