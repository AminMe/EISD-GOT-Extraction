</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Dayne</b> is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"noble house","link":"noble house","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[noble house]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-13982057056cca9590a82a" href="/wiki/Noble_house" title="Noble house">noble house</a> from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-13982057056cca9590a82a" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a>. They are vassals to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Martell","link":"House Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-13982057056cca9590a82a" href="/wiki/House_Martell" title="House Martell">House Martell</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sunspear","link":"Sunspear","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sunspear]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-13982057056cca9590a82a" href="/wiki/Sunspear" title="Sunspear">Sunspear</a>. Their seat is the castle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Starfall","link":"Starfall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Starfall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-13982057056cca9590a82a" href="/wiki/Starfall" title="Starfall">Starfall</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Their ancestral weapon is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dawn","link":"Dawn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dawn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-13982057056cca9590a82a" href="/wiki/Dawn" title="Dawn">Dawn</a>, a blade forged from the metal of a fallen star. The Dayne knight that bears the sword is granted the title of "the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sword of the Morning","link":"Sword of the Morning","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sword of the Morning]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-13982057056cca9590a82a" href="/wiki/Sword_of_the_Morning" title="Sword of the Morning">Sword of the Morning</a>".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Dayne's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"heraldry","link":"Heraldry","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Heraldry|heraldry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-13982057056cca9590a82a" href="/wiki/Heraldry" title="Heraldry">heraldry</a> consists of a sword and a falling star, on a lavender field. 
</p>
