<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">​In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">​In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Yronwood of Yronwood is the most powerful house in Dorne after the Martells. Yronwood Castle is the last fortress defending the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Boneway","link":"Boneway","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Boneway]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Boneway" title="Boneway">Boneway</a>: this is reflected in their title Warden of the Stone Way. The Yronwoods were kings in Dorne before the arrival of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhoynar","link":"Rhoynar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhoynar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Rhoynar" title="Rhoynar">Rhoynar</a>, and the head of the house continues to hold the self-styled title "The Bloodroyal".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Located in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Mountains","link":"Red Mountains","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Mountains]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Red_Mountains" title="Red Mountains">Red Mountains</a> along Dorne's western border, the Yronwoods are Stony Dornishmen - with hardly any <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhoynar","link":"Rhoynar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhoynar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Rhoynar" title="Rhoynar">Rhoynar</a> ancestry, and mostly resembling the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"First Men","link":"First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/First_Men" title="First Men">First Men</a>/<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Andals","link":"Andals","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Andals]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Andals" title="Andals">Andals</a> like the rest of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Yronwoods have been the Martells' main rivals in Dorne since they arrived, and only grudgingly submitted to their rule after they were defeated. During major civil wars in Westeros, the Yronwoods have frequently used the opportunity to choose the opposite side from the Martells, such as during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackfyre Rebellion","link":"Blackfyre Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackfyre Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Blackfyre_Rebellion" title="Blackfyre Rebellion">Blackfyre Rebellion</a>. King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daeron II Targaryen","link":"Daeron II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daeron II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Daeron_II_Targaryen" title="Daeron II Targaryen">Daeron II Targaryen</a> intermarried with the Martells to finally unite Dorne with the rest of the realm, leading to a pro-Martell/pro-Dorne faction developing at his court, while an anti-Dorne faction rallied around <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daemon Blackfyre","link":"Daemon Blackfyre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daemon Blackfyre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159045492356cca98c765f6" href="/wiki/Daemon_Blackfyre" title="Daemon Blackfyre">Daemon Blackfyre</a>, which later erupted into civil war. Even so, House Yronwood sided with the Blackfyres against the rest of Dorne and the Martells.
</p>
