<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194001323856cca97964706" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Rykker was raised to lordship over Duskendale after the purge of House Darklyn, following the Defiance of Duskendale. Renfred Rykker, Lord of Duskendale, rules over Duskendale from the Dun Fort, a castle overlooking Duskendale. Their sigil is two black warhammers crossed on a white saltire on blue.
