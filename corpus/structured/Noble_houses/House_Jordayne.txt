<h2>In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34030701256cca9a92fbad" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Jordayne are also vassals to House Martell. They are ruled by Lord Trebor Jordayne. Prince Oberyn is accompanied to King's Landing by Lord Trebor's daughter and heir Myria Jordayne.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The name of the house is an homage to Robert Jordan, author of <i>The Wheel of Time</i> novels and a friend of George R.R. Martin. The lord is called "Trebor" which is "Robert" backwards. "Jordayne" is similar to "Jordan" and "Tor" is Jordan's publisher.
</p>
