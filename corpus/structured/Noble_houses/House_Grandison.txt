House Grandison is a vassal house from the Stormlands that holds fealty to House Baratheon. Their sigil is a black sleeping lion on a yellow field. History 

 House Grandison was one of the three loyalist houses that defied their liege lord, Robert Baratheon, at the beginnings of Robert's Rebellion. Along with two other noble houses, House Grandison was defeated at the Battle of Summerhall in the course of a single day.The Stormlands (Histories &amp; Lore)\/ref} data-rte-instance130814-54669743656cca982d9815 classplaceholder placeholder-ext srcdata:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw type=ext / In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Grandison is a vassal house sworn to Storm's End. Their seat is Grandview and their words are Rouse Me Not. Ser Jaime Lannister's predecessor in the Kingsguard of Aerys II was Ser Harlan Grandison, an old knight who died in his sleep at the White Sword Tower. The current head of the family is Lord Hugh Grandison, an old man dubbed Greybeard. See also References
