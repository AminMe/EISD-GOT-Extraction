</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54669743656cca982d9815" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Grandison is a vassal house sworn to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54669743656cca982d9815" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>. Their seat is Grandview and their words are "Rouse Me Not". Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54669743656cca982d9815" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>'s predecessor in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54669743656cca982d9815" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys II","link":"Aerys II","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54669743656cca982d9815" href="/wiki/Aerys_II" title="Aerys II" class="mw-redirect">Aerys II</a> was Ser Harlan Grandison, an old <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54669743656cca982d9815" href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight" class="mw-redirect">knight</a> who died in his sleep at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Sword Tower","link":"White Sword Tower","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Sword Tower]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54669743656cca982d9815" href="/wiki/White_Sword_Tower" title="White Sword Tower">White Sword Tower</a>. The current head of the family is Lord Hugh Grandison, an old man dubbed Greybeard.
</p>
