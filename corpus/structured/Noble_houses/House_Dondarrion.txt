</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Dondarrion</b> is a vassal house that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>. Their lands are located within the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dornish Marches","link":"Dornish Marches","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dornish Marches]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Dornish_Marches" title="Dornish Marches">Dornish Marches</a>, a region of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormlands","link":"Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Stormlands" title="Stormlands" class="mw-redirect">Stormlands</a> that lies to the north of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a>. Their castle is called <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackhaven","link":"Blackhaven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackhaven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Blackhaven" title="Blackhaven">Blackhaven</a> and the head of the house is the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Blackhaven","link":"Lord of Blackhaven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Blackhaven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Blackhaven" title="Lord of Blackhaven">Lord of Blackhaven</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Dondarrion is ruled by Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Beric Dondarrion","link":"Beric Dondarrion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beric Dondarrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Beric_Dondarrion" title="Beric Dondarrion">Beric Dondarrion</a>, a charismatic and popular young lord, noted for his skill at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tourneys","link":"tourneys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[tourneys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Tourneys" title="Tourneys" class="mw-redirect">tourneys</a>. House Dondarrion's sigil is a forked purple lightning bolt on a black sky speckled with four-pointed stars.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Dondarrion was founded by a messenger who brought word of an impending attack by Dorne to the Storm King. The messenger was attacked by two Dornishmen, but a freak bolt of lightning killed both of them, allowing the messenger to complete his task.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">A century before the events of the series, House Dondarrion was charged by the King to root out a bandit warlord known as the Vulture King, who had established a hideaway in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Mountains","link":"Red Mountains","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Mountains]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Red_Mountains" title="Red Mountains">Red Mountains</a>. The Dondarrions, allied to the neighboring <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Caron","link":"House Caron","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Caron]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/House_Caron" title="House Caron">House Caron</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Nightsong","link":"Nightsong","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nightsong]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Nightsong" title="Nightsong">Nightsong</a>, took eight hundred horse and four thousand foot soldiers into the mountains to complete the mission. Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a> took part in this mission as a small boy and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"squire","link":"squire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[squire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Squire" title="Squire">squire</a> to Ser Arlan of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pennytree","link":"Pennytree","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pennytree]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171018641856cca965bf912" href="/wiki/Pennytree" title="Pennytree">Pennytree</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Beric Dondarrion is young, handsome and popular, known for his valor, steadfastness and reliability.
</p>
