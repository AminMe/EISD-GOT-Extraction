<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Qorgyle - along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Uller","link":"House Uller","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Uller]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/House_Uller" title="House Uller">House Uller</a> - are "Sandy Dornishmen", from the harsh central desert of Dorne. Their home is an oasis amidst the sands. When the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhoynar","link":"Rhoynar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhoynar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/Rhoynar" title="Rhoynar">Rhoynar</a> migrated to Dorne a thousand years ago, they settled primarily on the coasts and in the river valleys, and as a result this is where their impact upon Dorne's ethnic makeup was greatest. In contrast, the Rhoynar barely settled in the Red Mountains of western Dorne, where whose inhabitants are still mostly descended from the same <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"First Men","link":"First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/First_Men" title="First Men">First Men</a>/<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Andal","link":"Andal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Andal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/Andal" title="Andal" class="mw-redirect">Andal</a> stock as the rest of southern Westeros. The Rhoynar ethnic makeup therefore forms a gradient from east to west in Dorne, forming three loose categories: the "Salty Dornishmen" of the coasts are mostly descended from the Rhoynar, while the "Stony Dornishmen" of the mountains who have little or no Rhoynar ancestry. The third major group are the "Sandy Dornishmen" of the central deserts, who possess an intermediate mix of Rhoynar and First Men/Andal ancestry. Ironically, despite having less Rhoynar blood the Sandy Dornishmen actually have darker skin than the Salty Dornishmen, because their ancestors were burned and bronzed by the desert sun for hundreds of years.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Prince Oberyn Martell fostered as a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ward","link":"Ward","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ward]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/Ward" title="Ward">Ward</a> of House Qorgyle at Sandstone when he was a child. As a result, the Qorgyles are considered staunch allies of the Martells in the current generation.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">A member of House Qorgyle was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jeor Mormont","link":"Jeor Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jeor Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/Jeor_Mormont" title="Jeor Mormont">Jeor Mormont</a>'s immediate predecessor as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34546164756cca99816825" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>, though he has died by the time the novels begin.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The members of House Qorgyle at the time of the novels are:
