<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192626517256cca98a78156" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Allyrion is headed by Delonne Allyrion, the Lady of Godsgrace. Other members of the family include Ser Ryon, Delonne's son and heir, and Ryon's bastard son, Ser Daemon Sand.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Their blazon is a golden hand on gyronny red and black. Their words do not appear in the books, but according to semi-canon sources are "No Foe May Pass".
</p>
