<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170617870656cca97515528" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Tallhart is a masterly house sworn to the Starks of Winterfell as Masters of Torrhen's Square. Its members are:
</p>
<ul data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Ser {Helman Tallhart}, the head of the family and Master of Torrhen's Square. Slain in the battle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170617870656cca97515528" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a>.
<ul><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">{Benfred Tallhart}, Ser Helman's eldest son and heir. Slain by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170617870656cca97515528" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> in the battle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stony Shore","link":"Stony Shore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stony Shore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170617870656cca97515528" href="/wiki/Stony_Shore" title="Stony Shore">Stony Shore</a>.
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Eddara Tallhart, Ser Helman's only daughter. Held captive by the Ironborn at Torrhen's Square.
</li></ul>
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">{Leobald Tallhart}, Ser Helman's brother and Castellan of Torrhen's Square. Slain by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170617870656cca97515528" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sack of Winterfell","link":"Sack of Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170617870656cca97515528" href="/wiki/Sack_of_Winterfell" title="Sack of Winterfell">Sack of Winterfell</a>.
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Berena Hornwood, Leobald's wife. Held captive by the Ironborn at Torrhen's Square.
<ul><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Brandon Tallhart, Leobald's eldest son. Held captive by the Ironborn at Torrhen's Square.
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Beren Tallhart, Leobald's younger son. Held captive by the Ironborn at Torrhen's Square.
</li></ul>
</li></ul>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the novels, House Tallhart's heraldry consists of three green sentinel trees on a brown background. The TV series changed this to one green sentinel tree, on a white background.
</p>
