<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Graysons were loyal Stark bannermen, and prominent allies of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Forrester","link":"House Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/House_Forrester" title="House Forrester">House Forrester</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>. They fought alongside the Forresters for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>.
</p>
<h3>"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">During a meeting of Ironrath's small council, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Rodrik Forrester","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester|Lord Rodrik Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Lord Rodrik Forrester</a> may ask about the state of House Forrester's allies. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan Tuttle","link":"Duncan Tuttle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan Tuttle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/Duncan_Tuttle" title="Duncan Tuttle">Duncan Tuttle</a> responds that House Grayson, along with Houses <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Elliver","link":"House Elliver","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Elliver|Elliver]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/House_Elliver" title="House Elliver">Elliver</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brownbarrow","link":"House Brownbarrow","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Brownbarrow|Brownbarrow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/House_Brownbarrow" title="House Brownbarrow">Brownbarrow</a>, were wiped out at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Unlike the Forresters and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Whitehills","link":"House Whitehill","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Whitehill|Whitehills]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/House_Whitehill" title="House Whitehill">Whitehills</a>, House Grayson seems to be purely an invention of Telltale, much like Houses <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Branfield","link":"House Branfield","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Branfield|Branfield]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/House_Branfield" title="House Branfield">Branfield</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Glenmore","link":"House Glenmore","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Glenmore|Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51844032656cca98825058" href="/wiki/House_Glenmore" title="House Glenmore">Glenmore</a>.
