<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>

<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->For a time, House Reyne was the second wealthiest family in Westeros, behind House Lannister. They were not content with being the second wealthiest, however, and the last Lord Reyne built a castle as grand as Casterly Rock, and gave his wife diamonds to wear which were bigger than any that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a>'s wife <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joanna","link":"Joanna Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joanna Lannister|Joanna]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" href="/wiki/Joanna_Lannister" title="Joanna Lannister">Joanna</a> ever wore.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Second Sons (episode)|Second Sons]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Eventually, House Reyne <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"openly challenged","link":"Reyne Rebellion","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Reyne Rebellion|openly challenged]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" href="/wiki/Reyne_Rebellion" title="Reyne Rebellion">openly challenged</a> their liege-lords, feeling that House Lannister's rule over the Westerlands had grown weak under Tywin's ineffectual father <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tytos Lannister","link":"Tytos Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tytos Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" href="/wiki/Tytos_Lannister" title="Tytos Lannister">Tytos Lannister</a>. In response, however, the capable young Tywin Lannister marched his forces against the upstart Lord Reyne. At the end of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Reyne Rebellion","link":"Reyne Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Reyne Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" href="/wiki/Reyne_Rebellion" title="Reyne Rebellion">Reyne Rebellion</a>, Tywin's army put Castamere castle to the torch, and had all surviving members of House Reyne put to the sword - man, woman, and child - as an example to any vassals who would dare challenge the Lannisters in the future. Tywin subsequently had the corpses of the executed Reynes hung from the gates of Casterly Rock on public display, where he left them to rot for the entire summer (it was a long summer, which lasted many years).<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"Lannister featurette\">\"[[House Lannister (Complete Guide to Westeros)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Westerlands (Histories & Lore)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Second Sons (episode)|Second Sons]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The destruction of House Reyne was immortalized in the song "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Rains of Castamere","link":"The Rains of Castamere (song)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Rains of Castamere (song)|The Rains of Castamere]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" href="/wiki/The_Rains_of_Castamere_(song)" title="The Rains of Castamere (song)">The Rains of Castamere</a>."<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"Lannister featurette\"\/>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Reynes were one of the richest and most powerful families in the Westerlands after the Lannisters. During their defiance against Casterly Rock and Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tytos Lannister","link":"Tytos Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tytos Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29625969956cca950b53b7" href="/wiki/Tytos_Lannister" title="Tytos Lannister">Tytos Lannister</a>, the Reynes rose alongside the ancient House Tarbeck of Tarbeck Hall. Both houses were totally annihilated by Tywin Lannister.
</p>
