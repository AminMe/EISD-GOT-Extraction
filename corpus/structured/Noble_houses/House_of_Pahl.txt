<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The <b>House of Pahl</b> is one of the ancient noble <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"slaver","link":"Slavery","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slavery|slaver]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69355935856cca9b16a806" href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">slaver</a> families from the city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meereen","link":"Meereen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meereen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69355935856cca9b16a806" href="/wiki/Meereen" title="Meereen">Meereen</a>. Their family colors are yellow and black.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Known members </h2>
<ul><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">{<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oznak zo Pahl","link":"Oznak zo Pahl","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oznak zo Pahl]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69355935856cca9b16a806" href="/wiki/Oznak_zo_Pahl" title="Oznak zo Pahl">Oznak zo Pahl</a>}, a Meereenese warrior. Beheaded in duel by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daario Naharis","link":"Daario Naharis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daario Naharis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69355935856cca9b16a806" href="/wiki/Daario_Naharis" title="Daario Naharis">Daario Naharis</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Siege of Meereen","link":"Siege of Meereen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Meereen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69355935856cca9b16a806" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Meereen" title="Siege of Meereen">Siege of Meereen</a>.
</li></ul>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69355935856cca9b16a806" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the House of Pahl is one of the ancient slaving families of Meereen, known as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Masters","link":"Great Masters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Masters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69355935856cca9b16a806" href="/wiki/Great_Masters" title="Great Masters">Great Masters</a>. They own a pyramid made of pink and white bricks, the family's colors.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Oznak is not the only man of his house who gets killed at Daenerys's orders: his father dies defending the city gates during the siege, and his three uncles are among the one hundred and sixty three slavers Daenerys orders to be executed. As a result, while many of the Meereenese noble houses are hostile toward Daenerys, the house of Pahl hates her most of all.
</p>
