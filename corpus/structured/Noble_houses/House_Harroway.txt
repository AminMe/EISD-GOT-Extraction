</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Harroway</b> is an extinct noble house of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154019035556cca9a43cca6" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>. Their sigil was per bend sinister orange and black rayonne, a castle countercharged.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Their seat was the castle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154019035556cca9a43cca6" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>, which was granted to them after the extinction of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Towers","link":"House Towers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Towers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154019035556cca9a43cca6" href="/wiki/House_Towers" title="House Towers">House Towers</a>. However, the Harroways also died out, and the castle was passed on to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Strong","link":"House Strong","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Strong]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154019035556cca9a43cca6" href="/wiki/House_Strong" title="House Strong">House Strong</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154019035556cca9a43cca6" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Harroway's backstory is the same. There is a town, called Lord Harroway's Town, that is named after a former lord of the house.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maegor the Cruel","link":"Maegor Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maegor Targaryen|Maegor the Cruel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154019035556cca9a43cca6" href="/wiki/Maegor_Targaryen" title="Maegor Targaryen">Maegor the Cruel</a>, who kept a harem of wives, was married to Alys Harroway.
</p>
