<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10016596356cca99100763" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Tollett is a house sworn to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Royce","link":"House Royce","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10016596356cca99100763" href="/wiki/House_Royce" title="House Royce">House Royce</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Runestone","link":"Runestone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Runestone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10016596356cca99100763" href="/wiki/Runestone" title="Runestone">Runestone</a>. </p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Dolorous Edd is apparently a member of a lesser branch of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tollett","link":"House Tollett","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tollett]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10016596356cca99100763" href="/wiki/House_Tollett" title="House Tollett">House Tollett</a>, as he frequently makes comments indicating that he grew up in poverty, and his family were essentially living on the same level as peasants.
