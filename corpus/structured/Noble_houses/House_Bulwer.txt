 House Bulwer is a vassal house from The Reach sworn to House Hightower. Their seat is Blackcrown. Known members Jack Bulwer, nicknamed Black Jack Bulwer. A Builder of the Night's Watch. Killed in a wildling attack on Mole's Town. In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Bulwer is headed by Lady Alysanne Bulwer, a child of seven.Theirsigil is a bull's skull, bone over blood and their words are Death Before Disgrace. See also
