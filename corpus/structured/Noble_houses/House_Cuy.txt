<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169649220056cca9a704566" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Cuy is a vassal to House Hightower. Their sigil is six yellow flowers on blue and their seat is located near <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oldtown","link":"Oldtown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oldtown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169649220056cca9a704566" href="/wiki/Oldtown" title="Oldtown">Oldtown</a>. In the third and fourth novels, their castle is called "Sun House", but in the fifth book this is changed to "Sunflower Hall". On the TV series maps it remains "Sun House".
</p>
