House Cuy is a vassal house from the Reach that holds fealty to House Hightower. Its seat is Sun House. Members Ser Emmon Cuy, a member of Renly I's Kingsguard. Killed in self-defense by Brienne of Tarth. In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Cuy is a vassal to House Hightower. Their sigil is six yellow flowers on blue and their seat is located near Oldtown. In the third and fourth novels, their castle is called Sun House, but in the fifth book this is changed to Sunflower Hall. On the TV series maps it remains Sun House.
