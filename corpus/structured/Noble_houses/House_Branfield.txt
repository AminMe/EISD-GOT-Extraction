</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Branfield</b> is a minor noble house from the south of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. They were noted <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen|Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">Targaryen</a> loyalists. After the fall of House Targaryen, House Branfield lost all its lands and titles, with most of its members killed or exiled. Today, only one known member of House Branfield is still alive, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Malcolm Branfield","link":"Malcolm Branfield","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Malcolm Branfield]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" href="/wiki/Malcolm_Branfield" title="Malcolm Branfield">Malcolm Branfield</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">House Branfield is a minor southern house. They fought for House Targaryen during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, after which all of their lands and titles were taken from them. Elissa Branfield had married <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Forrester","link":"Gregor Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" href="/wiki/Gregor_Forrester" title="Gregor Forrester">Gregor Forrester</a>, heir to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Forrester","link":"House Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" href="/wiki/House_Forrester" title="House Forrester">House Forrester</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>, some time before the rebellion. She was torn between the loyalty of both houses, despite being married to Lord Gregor but ultimately chose House Forrester. Elissa's brother, Malcolm fought bravely along with his brothers during the war, but never married or fathered any children, ensuring the eventual extinction of the house. Since Elissa's daughter Mira is a lady in waiting to Margery Tyrell, it is possible that House Branfield was originally from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Reach","link":"The Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">The Reach</a> (especially as the Tyrells were Targaryen supporters during Robert's Rebellion). <img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>http:\/\/www.telltalegames.com\/blog\/discussion\/86265<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38949548156cca97807dc4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
