Their lands are located on the island of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Arbor","link":"The Arbor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Arbor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/The_Arbor" title="The Arbor">The Arbor</a>, southwest of Highgarden. Its <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"heraldry","link":"heraldry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[heraldry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Heraldry" title="Heraldry">heraldry</a> consists of a burgundy grape cluster on a white field.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Arbor is known for producing the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"finest wines","link":"Food and Drink#Wines","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Food and Drink#Wines|finest wines]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Food_and_Drink#Wines" title="Food and Drink">finest wines</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>. Due to their island location, their merchant ships require great protection at sea, so the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Redwyne Fleet","link":"Redwyne Fleet","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Redwyne Fleet]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Redwyne_Fleet" title="Redwyne Fleet">Redwyne Fleet</a> is one of the three largest in the realm. The Redwynes enjoy a particularly close relationship with its overlord, House Tyrell.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season-1\/episode-5\/map\/location\/50\/the-arbor HBO viewers guide, season 1: episode 5 map, The Arbor entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the current generation, Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Olenna Tyrell","link":"Olenna Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Olenna Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Olenna_Tyrell" title="Olenna Tyrell">Olenna Tyrell</a> was herself born Olenna Redwyne, before she married Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Luthor Tyrell","link":"Luthor Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Luthor Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Luthor_Tyrell" title="Luthor Tyrell">Luthor Tyrell</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Redwyne is a one of the most powerful noble families in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a>. A large portion of the wines exported across <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a> come from the Arbor, and the higher-quality wines produced on the Arbor are considered to be the best in Westeros. Due to their island location and heavy involvement in the wine trade, House Redwyne maintains a large fleet of ships. The Redwyne fleet forms a significant portion of the fleet of the Reach, of which Lord Paxter Redwyne serves as high admiral and commander.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Members of House Redwyne tend to have bright orange hair.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While most vassal Houses from the Reach joined House Tyrell in fighting for the royal <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a> during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, the Redwynes are noted for being particularly staunch Targaryen-loyalists.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Season 2 Blu-ray special features display the Redwyne sigil under a list of Renly Baratheon's supporters, but in the books House Redwyne does not declare for Renly. They wanted to, but during the early stages of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a> Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> holds the twin sons of Lord Paxter hostage at the royal court. After Renly dies, they join their Tyrell liege lords in aiding the Lannister army in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">A slight difference is that the sigil of House Redwyne in the books is a burgundy grape cluster on a blue field, but the TV series changed this to a burgundy grape cluster on a white field.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Members</b>

</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Ryam Redwyne is a past <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> who was considered to be the greatest knight of his day. For this he was named <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hand of the King","link":"Hand of the King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hand of the King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101652074156cca959d589a" href="/wiki/Hand_of_the_King" title="Hand of the King">Hand of the King</a>, but was a disaster. He is now considered to be one of the worst Hands the realm has ever seen and is now a warning to those who become Hands.
