House Degore is a noble house from the North. They are unique to Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. History 

 Background 

House Degore fought against the Ironborn during the Greyjoy Rebellion, where the majority of the house was killed. The lone survivor, Royland Degore, was awarded a knighthood by King Robert Baratheon for his service during the Siege of Pyke. Iron From Ice 

The Degores are mentioned in Royand's codex entry, detailing what happened during the rebellion. Members Ser Royland Degore, the last surviving member of the house and master-at-arms for Ironrath. In the books 

House Degore has no analogue in the novels, being an invention of Telltale. References
