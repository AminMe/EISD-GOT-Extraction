<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">House Degore fought against the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironborn","link":"Ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123585746856cca973d705c" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">Ironborn</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123585746856cca973d705c" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, where the majority of the house was killed. </p>
