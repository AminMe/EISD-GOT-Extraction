</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Hornwood</b> is a vassal house that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. Their lands are southeast of Winterfell, located in a forested region. Their stronghold is a castle also called <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hornwood","link":"Hornwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hornwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/Hornwood" title="Hornwood">Hornwood</a>. The Hornwood lands border the holdings of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Manderly","link":"House Manderly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Manderly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/House_Manderly" title="House Manderly">House Manderly</a> to the south and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bolton","link":"House Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/House_Bolton" title="House Bolton">House Bolton</a> to the northeast.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Hornwood is a loyal vassal of the Starks. House Bolton is said to have designs on their fertile lands.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> summoned his bannermen, Lord Halys Hornwood and his son Daryn went to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> and joined his army. Daryn became one of Robb's personal guards. Both of them were killed in the subsequent battles.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Once <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a> learned about the death of Halys and Daryn, he kidnapped Lady Donella, raped her, and forced her to marry him and to sign a will naming him the sole heir of the Hornwood lands. Then he locked her in a cell and did not bring her food. When Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Cassel","link":"Rodrik Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139670669456cca96ad3d72" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Cassel" title="Rodrik Cassel">Rodrik Cassel</a> arrived to save her, it was too late: she starved to death after chewing several of her fingers.
