<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">House Whitehill are bitter rivals of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Forrester","link":"House Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/House_Forrester" title="House Forrester">House Forrester</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>, and have served <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bolton","link":"House Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/House_Bolton" title="House Bolton">House Bolton</a> as bannermen for five centuries, despite their location north of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> far west of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Dreadfort","link":"The Dreadfort","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Dreadfort]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/The_Dreadfort" title="The Dreadfort">The Dreadfort</a>. Their sigil represents the barren hill on which their seat is situated - stripped bare of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironwood","link":"ironwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ironwood" title="Ironwood">ironwood</a> trees centuries ago.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>http:\/\/www.telltalegames.com\/blog\/discussion\/86265<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron From Ice","link":"Iron From Ice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron From Ice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Iron_From_Ice" title="Iron From Ice">Iron From Ice</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Whitehill banners are seen at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a> by Gared Tuttle, where they join their Bolton overlords in massacring the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark|Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">Stark</a> host. Later, on his way back home, he encounters a Bolton and two Whitehill men who murdered his family. He kills the Bolton and can kill one of the Whitehills, but the other Whitehill, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Britt Warrick","link":"Britt Warrick","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Britt Warrick]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Britt_Warrick" title="Britt Warrick">Britt Warrick</a>, escapes.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ludd Whitehill","link":"Ludd Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ludd Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ludd_Whitehill" title="Ludd Whitehill">Ludd Whitehill</a> arrives at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a> unannounced and unexpected, demanding an audience with the new Lord, Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ethan","link":"Ethan Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ethan Forrester|Ethan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ethan_Forrester" title="Ethan Forrester">Ethan</a>, to demand 'justice'. Lord Whitehill wants to deliver justice to Gared Tuttle, for his actions against the soldiers. Britt Warrick explains that they were attacked for nothing.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Whitehill returns with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a>, who demands Lord Ethan swear fealty and bend the knee to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bolton","link":"House Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/House_Bolton" title="House Bolton">House Bolton</a>. Lord Whitehill is instructed by Ramsay to install a garrison of twenty men inside the walls of Ironrath. House Whitehill take <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ryon Forrester","link":"Ryon Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ryon Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ryon_Forrester" title="Ryon Forrester">Ryon Forrester</a> as their ward, as insurance for the obedience of House Forrester. Ramsay also splits the ironwood forests 50/50 between the houses to see who fares better.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Forrester","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik Forrester</a> wakes up, he hears the sounds of the Whitehill soldiers, that Ramsay ordered to be placed within Ironrath. They are either drunk from wine served or angry because they are fed poor quality food, depending on your choice of Sentinel. As Rodrik exits his chambers, he comes across <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"a Whitehill soldier","link":"Whitehill soldier 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Whitehill soldier 1|a Whitehill soldier]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Whitehill_soldier_1" title="Whitehill soldier 1">a Whitehill soldier</a> blocking his path to the great hall. Rodrik intimidates the soldier to move out of his way.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Whitehill hires two merchant lords, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Andros","link":"Andros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Andros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Andros" title="Andros">Andros</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Morgryn","link":"Morgryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Morgryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Morgryn" title="Morgryn" class="mw-redirect">Morgryn</a>, to broker a deal to sell ironwood to the throne. Tyrion however dismisses the pair to deal with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mira Forrester","link":"Mira Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mira Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Mira_Forrester" title="Mira Forrester">Mira Forrester</a> instead.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Later in the episode, after talking with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Elaena Glenmore","link":"Elaena Glenmore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elaena Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Elaena_Glenmore" title="Elaena Glenmore">Elaena Glenmore</a>, Lord Whitehill comes to Ironrath, demanding obedience from Rodrik for ordering his men around. To make amends, Lord Whitehill gives Rodrik the choice of making him kiss his ring, in order to allow Ryon Forrester to attend the funeral of Ethan and Gregor Forrester. Before he leaves, he takes control of all the ironwood forests.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">If the player kisses his ring, Ryon does attend the funeral, managed by three Whitehill men. If the player refuses, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gwyn Whitehill","link":"Gwyn Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gwyn Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Gwyn_Whitehill" title="Gwyn Whitehill">Gwyn Whitehill</a> attends, informing Rodrik of Ryon's status.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Sword in the Darkness","link":"The Sword in the Darkness","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Sword in the Darkness]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/The_Sword_in_the_Darkness" title="The Sword in the Darkness">The Sword in the Darkness</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gryff Whitehill","link":"Gryff Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gryff Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Gryff_Whitehill" title="Gryff Whitehill">Gryff Whitehill</a> arrives at Ironrath, welcomed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harys","link":"Harys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Harys" title="Harys">Harys</a> and the rest of the garrison. At the small council meeting, Rodrik can decide to either save Ryon or exile Gryff. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan Tuttle","link":"Duncan Tuttle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan Tuttle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Duncan_Tuttle" title="Duncan Tuttle">Duncan Tuttle</a> also notes that the Whitehills have raided Forrester lands, burnt farms, and cut down trees from Ironrath to Highpoint. After the meeting, Duncan notifies Rodrik that Gwyn Whitehill wishes to meet with him. At the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wall","link":"Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Wall" title="Wall" class="mw-redirect">Wall</a>, Britt is among the new recruits. </p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the meeting, Gwyn tells Rodrik that her father is conspiring against the Forresters and waiting for an excuse to attack. She leaves as Whitehill men approach the meeting place.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Back at Ironrath, Rodrik enters the great hall, which lays in waste. He briefly talks to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his mother","link":"Elissa Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elissa Forrester|his mother]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Elissa_Forrester" title="Elissa Forrester">his mother</a> before several Whitehill soldiers enter the hall, mocking Ethan's demise. </p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Gryff drags <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talia","link":"Talia Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talia Forrester|Talia]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Talia_Forrester" title="Talia Forrester">Talia</a> out to Rodrik showing a bite on a man of the garrison inflicted by Talia, although she says she caught the man stealing from the kitchens. Rodrik takes the punishment Gryff would give Talia, and can either submit or keep trying to stand up.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sons of Winter","link":"Sons of Winter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sons of Winter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Sons_of_Winter" title="Sons of Winter">Sons of Winter</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Whitehills continue their occupation of Ironrath. Elaena and her brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arthur Glenmore","link":"Arthur Glenmore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arthur Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Arthur_Glenmore" title="Arthur Glenmore">Arthur Glenmore</a> along with the guard visit Ironrath. Elaena explains that Ludd threatened <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Glenmore","link":"Glenmore (Lord)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Glenmore (Lord)|Lord Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Glenmore_(Lord)" title="Glenmore (Lord)">Lord Glenmore</a> that unless Elaena marries Gryff, they would be slaughtered along with the Forresters.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rodrik enter the great hall to find Gryff and his men beating maester <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ortengryn","link":"Ortengryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ortengryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ortengryn" title="Ortengryn">Ortengryn</a> for letting the bite the soldier in the previous episode acquired fester. Rodrik can determinately order the guard to kill anyone that moves, which results in one of the Whitehill men being killed by Arthur. The guard holds Gryff done as Rodrik can beat him, and the garrison is sent to the cellars.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Whitehill sends a message, unaware that the garrison has been imprisoned, inviting Rodrik to Highpoint to discuss a truce. Lady Forrester, Rodrik, Duncan or Royland, and determinately the Glenmore soldiers can come.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Mira gains access to the royal wedding. She notices Morgryn and Andros arguing. Mira talks to Morgryn, who is no longer working for Ludd Whitehill. She also gains some information on Andros' new business partner <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lyman Lannister","link":"Lyman Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lyman Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Lyman_Lannister" title="Lyman Lannister">Lyman Lannister</a>. Using some information gained from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Garibald Tarwick","link":"Garibald Tarwick","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Garibald Tarwick]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Garibald_Tarwick" title="Garibald Tarwick">Garibald Tarwick</a>, she gets Lyman to spill Andros' secrets: he's building an army for Lord Whitehill. Lyman also disassociates with Andros, convinced that Andros spilled his secrets.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Gwyn welcomes the visitors to Highpoint, although she is upset if the Glenmore soldiers are present. Gwyn notes if Rodrik either submitted or defied Gryff. Gwyn address the group, saying their houses were close once, and gestures to a shield on the wall with both the Forrester and Whitehill sigils. Gwyn also explains to Rodrik her family with the painting of them.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Whitehill welcomes the group to Highpoint, and sits down at the table. He starts off noting that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Forrester","link":"Gregor Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Gregor_Forrester" title="Gregor Forrester">Gregor Forrester</a> once called Highpoint 'a keep for the gods'. He toasts to family. Ludd states the matter of their meeting is ironwood, and warns that the Forresters are headed for destruction but know more about ironwood than any other house in the realm, and that if the Forresters help the Whitehills craft ironwood for the Boltons , he'll make sure they survive. Ludd threatens Rodrik that if he doesn't agree to his terms, he'll have Gryff torch Ironrath and slaughter the smallfolk. Rodrik finally tells him that Gryff and the garrison has been imprisoned, angering Ludd severely. Rodrik then proposes a trade, Ryon for Gryff.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ludd has Ryon brought in front of his guests, and grabs him pulling a essential to his head. The situation escalates, with Ludd stating he doesn't care about Gryff. Ryon also bites Ludd's hands, before he grabs him again. Rodrik can accept Ludd's deal, call his buff, beg for mercy or attack, which results in a game over. Ludd then orders them out of the hall.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Nest of Vipers","link":"A Nest of Vipers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Nest of Vipers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/A_Nest_of_Vipers" title="A Nest of Vipers">A Nest of Vipers</a>"</h3>

</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Talia intercepts one the traitor's messages. The traitor is revealed to be whoever you did not select to be your Sentinel. They defend themselves, saying they had House Forrester's best interests at mind. Your Sentinel then comes in and informs you that Gryff and the garrison has been sent free by the traitor. With Talia, Lady Forrester, some guards and your Sentinel watching, you can choose to either execute Royland or Duncan, or spare them. The traitor asks to be spared, saying otherwise Asher is doomed. If spared, the traitor reveals that Ludd intends to ambush Asher and his pit fighters once he lands. Regardless, the Forresters ride for the coast.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Asher and Rodrik unite, and the Forrester forces are moving out of yard, a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Whitehill soldier","link":"Whitehill soldier 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Whitehill soldier 1|Whitehill soldier]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Whitehill_soldier_1" title="Whitehill soldier 1">Whitehill soldier</a> lowers the gate, trapping Rodrik, Asher, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Beast","link":"the Beast","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Beast]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/The_Beast" title="The Beast">the Beast</a>, and some of the Forrester men-at-arms and pit fighters with Gryff, Harys, and the Whitehill force, starting the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ambush at the Harbor","link":"Ambush at the Harbor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ambush at the Harbor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/Ambush_at_the_Harbor" title="Ambush at the Harbor">Ambush at the Harbor</a>. The Beast attempts to raise the gate, and is shot with crossbow bolts. He runs to the control, and knocks down two Whitehill men, and trys to raise the lever but Harys throws a spear and impales him, killing him. Asher and Rodrik attempt to raise the gate, while the remaining Forresters and pit fighters fight the Whitehill men. They raise the gate slightly, and some Forrester mens crawl under, but are cut down. The Whitehills approach the brothers, and one must stay behind for the other to escape. The one who stays behind kills several Whitehill men before being shot in the shoulder, stabbed by Harys, hit in the neck by a crossbow bolt, hit in the eye, and finally collapses and die. The ambush is a Whitehill victory.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">House Whitehill has not been mentioned in the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-112327175856cca95865f54" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels themselves, only in the semi-canon <i>A Song of Ice and Fire Campaign Guide</i> RPG book - which was released in 2010, <i>before</i> the TV series began. The guidebook presented lists of multiple minor Houses with new heraldry and sometimes a note or two about them. Therefore House Whitehill is <i>broadly</i> part of the "book continuity", and was not <i>entirely</i> invented from scratch by Telltale Games.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The only information provided about House Whitehill was their heraldry, their direct allegiance to House Stark (possibly at odds with the Bolton allegiance seen in the videogame), and that they are one of the few Northern families to follow the Faith of the Seven instead of the Old Gods. The heraldry of House Whitehill consists of a white pile (wedge) inverted on indigo, an arch of four-pointed stars above. Like House Forrester, House Whitehill's motto wasn't established in the novels but was developed for the video game.
</p>
