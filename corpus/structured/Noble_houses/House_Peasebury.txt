<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-98718832656cca99688578" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Peasebury is a noble house from the Stormlands, seated at the castle of Poddingfield. During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-98718832656cca99688578" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a> they declare for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-98718832656cca99688578" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a> and go over to Stannis after Renly's death. Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-98718832656cca99688578" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>, while several Stormlords <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bend the knee","link":"Bending the knee","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bending the knee|bend the knee]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-98718832656cca99688578" href="/wiki/Bending_the_knee" title="Bending the knee">bend the knee</a> to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey","link":"Joffrey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-98718832656cca99688578" href="/wiki/Joffrey" title="Joffrey" class="mw-redirect">Joffrey</a>, House Peasebury remains loyal to Stannis.
</p>
