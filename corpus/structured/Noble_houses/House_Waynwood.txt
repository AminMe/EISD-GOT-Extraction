 House Waynwood is a vassal house which holds fealty to House Arryn of the Vale. Its head is the widowed Lady Anya Waynwood. 

House Waynwood's heraldry consists of a black broken wheel on green. Members Lady Anya Waynwood, head of the House Ser Donnel Waynwood, the Knight of the Gate In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the Waynwoods are powerful vassals of House Arryn. Their seat is the castle of Ironoaks, east of the Eyrie on the coast of the Narrow Sea. They are known for their insistence on ceremony. 

The members of House Waynwood at the time of the novels are: Lady Anya Waynwood, Lady of Ironoaks. An elderly woman. Ser Morton Waynwood, her eldest son and heir. Ser Donnel Waynwood, her second son. Knight of the Gate. Wallace Waynwood, her youngest son.
