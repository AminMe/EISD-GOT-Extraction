</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Mallister</b> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seagard","link":"Seagard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seagard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Seagard" title="Seagard">Seagard</a> is a vassal house that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>. Their lands are north of Riverrun, along the west coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a> on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironman's Bay","link":"Ironman's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironman's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Ironman's_Bay" title="Ironman's Bay">Ironman's Bay</a>, and southwest of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Twins","link":"the Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">the Twins</a>. The castle is near the headwaters of the Blue Fork of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Trident","link":"Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Trident" title="Trident" class="mw-redirect">Trident</a> River.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Mallisters are longstanding enemies of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a>: Seagard was built as a defense against marauders from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season-3\/episode-1\/map\/location\/44\/seagard\/ HBO viewers guide, season 3: episode 1 map, Seagard entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Mallister's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Heraldry","link":"Heraldry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Heraldry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Heraldry" title="Heraldry">Heraldry</a> consists of a silver eagle on a blue field. Their motto is <b>"Above the Rest"</b>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Mallister is one of House Tully's most loyal vassals. Lord Jason Mallister is noted as both a loyal vassal and also a close personal friend and ally of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a>. During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, the Mallisters defended Seagard from an <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"assault","link":"Battle of Seagard","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Seagard|assault]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Seagard" title="Battle of Seagard">assault</a>. While the town and castle were damaged and severe casualties incurred, the ironborn suffered significant losses, with Lord Jason slaying <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Greyjoy","link":"Rodrik Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108363310256cca9626933a" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Greyjoy" title="Rodrik Greyjoy">Rodrik Greyjoy</a> in personal combat.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">A slight change is that in the books their sigil is a silver eagle on a purple field, but in the TV series the field is blue.
</p>
