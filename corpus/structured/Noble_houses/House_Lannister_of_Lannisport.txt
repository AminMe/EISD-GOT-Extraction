House Lannister of Lannisport is a vassal house that holds fealty to House Lannister of Casterly Rock, of which they are a cadet branch. Their seat is located at the city of Lannisport. Members Reginald Lannister, a bannerman of Lord Tywin Lannister. Reginald's wife. In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the Lannisters of Lannisport have the same blond hair as the main branch, however theirs is straight, unlike the curly hair of the main family. The onlyLannisters of Lannisport mentioned in the books are Rosamund, a handmaiden to Princess Myrcella Baratheon, and Maester Theomore, a maester in service at White Harbor.
