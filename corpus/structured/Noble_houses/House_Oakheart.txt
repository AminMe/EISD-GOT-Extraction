</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166881423256cca97fe0019" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, House Oakheart is an old and powerful family from the Reach. They are among the noble houses from the Reach that can trace their descent from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Garth Greenhand","link":"Garth Greenhand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Garth Greenhand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166881423256cca97fe0019" href="/wiki/Garth_Greenhand" title="Garth Greenhand" class="mw-redirect">Garth Greenhand</a>. The house is headed by Lady Arwyn Oakheart, a loyal vassal of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mace Tyrell","link":"Mace Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mace Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166881423256cca97fe0019" href="/wiki/Mace_Tyrell" title="Mace Tyrell">Mace Tyrell</a> and Ser Arys' mother. The Oakheart sigil is three green leaves on a golden plain.
</p>
