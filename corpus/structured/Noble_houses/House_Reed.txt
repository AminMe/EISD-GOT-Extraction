<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142955906856cca952e428c" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the crannogmen of the Neck are gifted and talented hunters and warriors. They have an ages-old feud with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Frey","link":"House Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142955906856cca952e428c" href="/wiki/House_Frey" title="House Frey">House Frey</a>, who rule the lands immediately to the south. The Reeds came to the attention of their liege lords, House Stark, when Howland Reed met <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142955906856cca952e428c" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> at a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tournament","link":"tournament","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[tournament]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142955906856cca952e428c" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">tournament</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142955906856cca952e428c" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>. Howland Reed and Eddard Stark became fast friends and allies. Aside from Eddard, Howland was the only survivor of the raid at the Tower of Joy, on the occasion of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lyanna Stark","link":"Lyanna Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lyanna Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142955906856cca952e428c" href="/wiki/Lyanna_Stark" title="Lyanna Stark">Lyanna Stark</a>'s death. The Reed stronghold at Greywater Watch is hard to find because it supposedly moves around on a floating island.
</p>
