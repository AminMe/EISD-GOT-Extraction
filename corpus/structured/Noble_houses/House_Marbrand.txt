</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Marbrand</b> is a vassal house that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Casterly Rock","link":"Casterly Rock","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Casterly Rock]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Casterly_Rock" title="Casterly Rock">Casterly Rock</a>. Their lands are in the hilly area where the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tumblestone","link":"Tumblestone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tumblestone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Tumblestone" title="Tumblestone">Tumblestone</a> begins and their castle is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ashemark","link":"Ashemark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ashemark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Ashemark" title="Ashemark">Ashemark</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The head of the family bears the title of Lord of Ashemark. The current head of the family is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Damon Marbrand","link":"Damon Marbrand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Damon Marbrand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Damon_Marbrand" title="Damon Marbrand">Damon Marbrand</a>. The Marbrand sigil is a burning tree, orange on smoke.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Marbrand's are loyal vassals of House Lannister. Ser Addam Marbrand is a childhood friend of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> and a key officer in the Lannister army.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Marbrand is also closely tied to Lannisters through marriage:  Jeyne Marbrand was the wife of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tytos Lannister","link":"Tytos Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tytos Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Tytos_Lannister" title="Tytos Lannister">Tytos Lannister</a> and mother of all five of his children, including Tywin and Kevan.  Thus all of the Lannister characters who appear in the TV series have some blood relationship with the Marbrands.  Tywin's younger brother Tygett also married Darlessa Marbrand - Tygett died of a pox before the novels begin, and Darlessa is his widow, as well as the mother of his son Tyrek Lannister, a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"squire","link":"squire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[squire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Squire" title="Squire">squire</a> of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon|Robert]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert</a> who was cut from the series.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">​A historical member of the house is Ser Lorent Marbrand, a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight" class="mw-redirect">knight</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> who served during the reign of King Viserys I and chose to side with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaenyra Targaryen","link":"Rhaenyra Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaenyra Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/Rhaenyra_Targaryen" title="Rhaenyra Targaryen">Rhaenyra Targaryen</a> during the Dance of the Dragons.  He later became Lord Commander of her Queensguard, succeeding Ser Steffon Darklyn, before dying in the riots in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179425339256cca973229f8" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>.
</p>
