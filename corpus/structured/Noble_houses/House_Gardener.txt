<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Garth's crown.png|thumb|left|250px|The crown of the Gardener kings.]]","title":"Garth's_crown.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Garth's crown","align":"left","caption":"The crown of the Gardener kings.","width":250,"captionParsed":"The crown of the Gardener kings."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-113737218656cca956cdd54" alt="" src="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/4/46/Garth's_crown.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20150215153402" width="250" height="118" class="image thumb alignLeft withCaption" data-image-name="Garth&#39;s crown.png" data-image-key="Garth's_crown.png"  type="image" /> 
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":1,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->House Gardener is one of only three Great Houses which ever went extinct, the other two being <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Durrandon","link":"House Durrandon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Durrandon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113737218656cca956cdd54" href="/wiki/House_Durrandon" title="House Durrandon">House Durrandon</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stormlands","link":"the Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113737218656cca956cdd54" href="/wiki/The_Stormlands" title="The Stormlands">the Stormlands</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Hoare","link":"House Hoare","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Hoare]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113737218656cca956cdd54" href="/wiki/House_Hoare" title="House Hoare">House Hoare</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113737218656cca956cdd54" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a>. The Durrandon and Hoare bloodlines were extinguished in the male line, but they did survive in the female line. The daughter of the last Durrandon king was married by the founder of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113737218656cca956cdd54" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a>, so there is some continuity with its replacement, and apparently no surviving Durrandon relatives could challenge the resulting Baratheon line. In the Iron Islands, House Volmark is said to descend from House Hoare in the female line, but due to the odd elective kingship practices of the ironborn, they do not have a particularly better claim to rule than <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Greyjoy","link":"House Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113737218656cca956cdd54" href="/wiki/House_Greyjoy" title="House Greyjoy">House Greyjoy</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Gardener, however, had intermarried with many of the other noble Houses of the Reach, making them younger branches of the main Gardener line. These include the Florents and Tyrells, as well as the Rowans and Oakhearts. The Tyrells can only claim descent from the Gardeners through the female line, however, the Florents are directly descended from the Gardeners through a male line (a younger Gardener brother married a Florent at one point). Because the Tyrells willingly surrendered Highgarden, however, the Targaryens skipped over the Florents to appoint the Tyrells as rulers of the Reach. This makes the situation in the Reach quite unique among the Seven Kingdoms, because the deposed former Great House isn't exactly "extinct" (despite being formally disestablished) and actually possesses a superior claim to rule based on male-line descent than the current ruling House.
</p>
