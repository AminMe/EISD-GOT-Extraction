<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129972618856cca984cebd9" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Baelor Blacktyde's father was killed in a battle of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129972618856cca984cebd9" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>. Baelor was taken as a hostage for eight years in the city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oldtown","link":"Oldtown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oldtown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129972618856cca984cebd9" href="/wiki/Oldtown" title="Oldtown">Oldtown</a>. Upon his return to Blacktyde, Baelor had converted to the mainland's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Faith of the Seven","link":"Faith of the Seven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Faith of the Seven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129972618856cca984cebd9" href="/wiki/Faith_of_the_Seven" title="Faith of the Seven">Faith of the Seven</a>. Nevertheless, several Blacktydes gather around <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon Greyjoy","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129972618856cca984cebd9" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon Greyjoy</a> when he crowns himself King of the Iron Islands again.
</p>
