<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108359290956cca95e75d26" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Payne is noted as a valued vassal of the Lannisters. Ser Ilyn Payne was a loyal and stalwart warrior in the service of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108359290956cca95e75d26" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> and commander of his personal guard. His tongue was torn out with hot pincers at the order of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mad King","link":"Mad King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mad King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108359290956cca95e75d26" href="/wiki/Mad_King" title="Mad King" class="mw-redirect">Mad King</a> after Ilyn was overheard suggesting that Tywin really ruled the realm (as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hand of the King","link":"Hand of the King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hand of the King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108359290956cca95e75d26" href="/wiki/Hand_of_the_King" title="Hand of the King">Hand of the King</a>) rather than the insane monarch. Tywin wasn't in a position to protest. After the rebellion overthrew the Mad King, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108359290956cca95e75d26" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> made Ilyn King's Justice (head executioner) as a favor to Tywin Lannister.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Podrick Payne is the only other member of the family to appear. He is a very distant cousin of Ser Ilyn's from a different branch of the family. Podrick Payne was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108359290956cca95e75d26" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>'s squire, but has since been given to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne of Tarth","link":"Brienne of Tarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne of Tarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108359290956cca95e75d26" href="/wiki/Brienne_of_Tarth" title="Brienne of Tarth">Brienne of Tarth</a>.
</p>
