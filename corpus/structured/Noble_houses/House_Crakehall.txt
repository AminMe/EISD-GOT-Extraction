</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Crakehall</b> is a vassal house that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-27818455056cca975e0a02" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Casterly Rock","link":"Casterly Rock","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Casterly Rock]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-27818455056cca975e0a02" href="/wiki/Casterly_Rock" title="Casterly Rock">Casterly Rock</a>. Their <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"lands","link":"Crakehall","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crakehall|lands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-27818455056cca975e0a02" href="/wiki/Crakehall" title="Crakehall">lands</a> are located at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Searoad","link":"Searoad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Searoad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-27818455056cca975e0a02" href="/wiki/Searoad" title="Searoad">Searoad</a>, at the south of the Westerlands, between <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the sea","link":"Sunset Sea","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sunset Sea|the sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-27818455056cca975e0a02" href="/wiki/Sunset_Sea" title="Sunset Sea">the sea</a> and a large forest. The current head of the family is Desmond Crakehall. The Crakehall sigil is a black and white brindled boar on brown.
</p>
