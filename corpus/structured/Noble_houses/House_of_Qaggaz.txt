<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The <b>​House of Qaggaz</b> is one of the noble <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"slaver","link":"Slavery","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slavery|slaver]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-137250330256cca9b0017d9" href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">slaver</a> families from the city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meereen","link":"Meereen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meereen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-137250330256cca9b0017d9" href="/wiki/Meereen" title="Meereen">Meereen</a>. They continue to secretly practice slavery in the area outside Meereen despite the fact that it is abolished.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i>A Song of ice and Fire</i> novels the House of Qaggaz is one of the most powerful families of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yunkai","link":"Yunkai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yunkai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-137250330256cca9b0017d9" href="/wiki/Yunkai" title="Yunkai">Yunkai</a>. The known members of the family are Yezzan zo Qaggaz and his four nephews, and his heirs, one of which is killed by either the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Second Sons","link":"Second Sons (mercenary company)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Second Sons (mercenary company)|Second Sons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-137250330256cca9b0017d9" href="/wiki/Second_Sons_(mercenary_company)" title="Second Sons (mercenary company)" class="mw-redirect">Second Sons</a> or the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormcrows","link":"Stormcrows","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormcrows]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-137250330256cca9b0017d9" href="/wiki/Stormcrows" title="Stormcrows">Stormcrows</a> when the Yunkish forces march on Meereen.
</p>
