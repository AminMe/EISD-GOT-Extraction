<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>House Strong</b> is an extinct noble house of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>. Their sigil was a tripartite pale blue, red, and green on white.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Their seat was the castle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>, which was granted to them after the extinction of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Harroway","link":"House Harroway","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Harroway]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/House_Harroway" title="House Harroway">House Harroway</a>. However, the Strongs also died out, like all the Houses to hold Harrenhal, and the castle was passed on to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lothston","link":"House Lothston","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lothston]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/House_Lothston" title="House Lothston">House Lothston</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Strong's backstory is the same. Some descendants of the House live in exile in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Cities","link":"Free Cities","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Cities]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Free_Cities" title="Free Cities">Free Cities</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Strong held Harrenhal from the time of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaehaerys I Targaryen","link":"Jaehaerys I Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaehaerys I Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen" title="Jaehaerys I Targaryen">Jaehaerys I Targaryen</a> to the time of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaenyra","link":"Rhaenyra Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaenyra Targaryen|Rhaenyra]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Rhaenyra_Targaryen" title="Rhaenyra Targaryen">Rhaenyra</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon II Targaryen","link":"Aegon II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Aegon_II_Targaryen" title="Aegon II Targaryen">Aegon II Targaryen</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">There have been a number of historical Strongs mentioned in the books. One was Ser Lucamore Strong known as "Lucamore the Lusty".  He was a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight" class="mw-redirect">knight</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> during the reign of Jaehaerys I who broke the Kingsguard vows of chastity by secretly marrying three women and fathering sixteen children.  His sworn brothers discovered this and informed the king, who then had Lucamore <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"gelded","link":"Eunuch","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eunuch|gelded]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Eunuch" title="Eunuch" class="mw-redirect">gelded</a> and sent to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> to join the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Another notable Strongs were Lord Larys Strong, Lord of Harrenhal, who served as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Master of Whisperers","link":"Master of Whisperers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Master of Whisperers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Master_of_Whisperers" title="Master of Whisperers">Master of Whisperers</a> under King Viserys I and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King Aegon II","link":"Aegon II Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon II Targaryen|King Aegon II]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56103053956cca97a2d84e" href="/wiki/Aegon_II_Targaryen" title="Aegon II Targaryen">King Aegon II</a>, and Ser Harwin Strong, lover of Rhaenyra Targaryen and rumored father of her first three children.   
</p>
