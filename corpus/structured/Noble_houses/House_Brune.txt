</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Brune</b> is a vassal house from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Crownlands","link":"the Crownlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Crownlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/The_Crownlands" title="The Crownlands">the Crownlands</a> that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon of King's Landing","link":"House Baratheon of King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon of King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing" title="House Baratheon of King's Landing">House Baratheon of King's Landing</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lothor Brune","link":"Lothor Brune","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lothor Brune]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Lothor_Brune" title="Lothor Brune">Lothor Brune</a> is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"freerider","link":"Mercenary#Freeriders","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mercenary#Freeriders|freerider]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Mercenary#Freeriders" title="Mercenary">freerider</a> in the service of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Petyr Baelish","link":"Petyr Baelish","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Petyr Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Petyr_Baelish" title="Petyr Baelish">Petyr Baelish</a>. He is set to fight in the tournament held on King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a>'s nameday. The tournament is poorly attended, and Lothor's scheduled opponent Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dontos Hollard","link":"Dontos Hollard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dontos Hollard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Dontos_Hollard" title="Dontos Hollard">Dontos Hollard</a> shows up slightly drunk. This annoys Joffrey, who orders the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> to kill Dontos before the fight can begin, but at the urging of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a> he relents and decides to keep Dontos alive as a court <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fool","link":"Fool","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fool|fool]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Fool" title="Fool">fool</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The North Remembers]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Brune is a poor but old family from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Crackclaw Point","link":"Crackclaw Point","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crackclaw Point]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Crackclaw_Point" title="Crackclaw Point">Crackclaw Point</a>, a backwater of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Crownlands","link":"the Crownlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Crownlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/The_Crownlands" title="The Crownlands">the Crownlands</a> whose lords descend primarily from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"First Men","link":"First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/First_Men" title="First Men">First Men</a>. House Brune of Dyre Den is the main branch of the family, though a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"cadet branch","link":"Cadet branch","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cadet branch|cadet branch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Cadet_branch" title="Cadet branch">cadet branch</a> known as House Brune of Broomhollow also exists at the time of the novels, also located in Crackclaw Point, though they are only landed knights, not noble lords. Their sigil is a brown bear claw on a white background, with a double brown line running around the edge.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lothor Brune is a distant minor relative of House Brune of Broomhollow - itself a poor cadet branch of landed knights stemming from an already poor main family of minor nobles. It is therefore unsurprising that Lothor grew up poor and fighting for a living, explaining why he is a freerider serving another lord at the beginning of the story. After his father's death, Lothor tried to find a place with House Brune of Broomhollow, but they said he was no close kin of theirs and turned him away.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Subsequently, however, Lothor's fortunes rise as a trusted and capable fighter in the retinue of Petyr "Littlefinger" Baelish. He serves Baelish in King's Landing, and when he took part in the Tourney of the Hand he even managed to beat <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jory Cassel","link":"Jory Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jory Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Jory_Cassel" title="Jory Cassel">Jory Cassel</a>, captain of Eddard Stark's guards (though the victory was awarded as a judgement by King Robert after a very close match). Lothor performed very well at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>, killing two knights of House Fossoway and capturing a third, and in reward Lothor received as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knighthood","link":"Knighthood","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Knighthood|knighthood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Knighthood" title="Knighthood">knighthood</a>. Ser Lothor accompanies Baelish back to the Vale after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Purple Wedding","link":"Purple Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Purple Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63491476356cca9a37be7a" href="/wiki/Purple_Wedding" title="Purple Wedding">Purple Wedding</a>: Lothor commanded the team of crossbowmen on Baelish's ship that kill Dontos Hollard at Baelish's order (coincidentally, the same man Lothor was supposed to face at Joffrey's tournament).
</p>
