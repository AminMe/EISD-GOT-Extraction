</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>House Clegane</b> is a vassal house that holds fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Casterly Rock","link":"Casterly Rock","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Casterly Rock]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Casterly_Rock" title="Casterly Rock">Casterly Rock</a>. Their lands are southeast of Casterly Rock and consist of a small patch of land and a modest house. They are landed <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knights","link":"knights","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knights]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Knights" title="Knights" class="mw-redirect">knights</a> rather than a full <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"noble house","link":"noble house","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[noble house]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Noble_house" title="Noble house">noble house</a>, but have strong ties to the Lannisters.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The current head of the family is Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a>, known as <b>"The Mountain That Rides".</b> Gregor has a younger brother, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sandor Clegane","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">Sandor Clegane</a>, known as <b>"The Hound".</b>
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Clegane sigil is three black dogs on a dark yellow background. The three dogs signify the three dogs that died fighting off the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"lioness","link":"lions","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[lions|lioness]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Lions" title="Lions">lioness</a> that attacked <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tytos Lannister","link":"Tytos Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tytos Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Tytos_Lannister" title="Tytos Lannister">Tytos Lannister</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[[Complete Guide to Westeros]] - House Clegane]]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"founder","link":"Clegane (founder)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Clegane (founder)|founder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Clegane_(founder)" title="Clegane (founder)">founder</a> of House Clegane was the grandfather of Gregor and Sandor, who served as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"kennelmaster","link":"kennelmaster","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[kennelmaster]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Kennelmaster" title="Kennelmaster">kennelmaster</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Casterly Rock","link":"Casterly Rock","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Casterly Rock]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Casterly_Rock" title="Casterly Rock">Casterly Rock</a>. While accompanying Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tytos Lannister","link":"Tytos Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tytos Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Tytos_Lannister" title="Tytos Lannister">Tytos Lannister</a> on an excursion, they were attacked by a lioness. He rescued his master, losing three dogs and a leg in the process, and was awarded lands and title for his actions.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/cleganes-keep\/ HBO viewer's guide, season 2 map, Clegane's Keep entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, the kennelmaster lost three hounds and a leg in the process of saving Lord Tytos. As a reward, Lord Tytos gave him lands and a towerhouse and took his son as his squire. Their sigil is three dogs on a yellow field; the three dogs represent the dogs that died saving Lord Tytos. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Thoros","link":"Thoros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thoros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Thoros" title="Thoros">Thoros</a> commented that in view of the atrocities performed by the Cleganes, an image of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"two","link":"Aegon Targaryen (Son of Rhaegar)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon Targaryen (Son of Rhaegar)|two]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Aegon_Targaryen_(Son_of_Rhaegar)" title="Aegon Targaryen (Son of Rhaegar)">two</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bloody infants","link":"Rhaenys Targaryen (daughter of Rhaegar)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaenys Targaryen (daughter of Rhaegar)|bloody infants]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Rhaenys_Targaryen_(daughter_of_Rhaegar)" title="Rhaenys Targaryen (daughter of Rhaegar)">bloody infants</a> would have been more suitable for their house sigil.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Clegane's grandson, Gregor, became notable for his extreme size, and the rumor that he burned his younger brother's face as a child for playing with a toy without asking him. During <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, Gregor Clegane proved his worth and ferocity in battle for Lord Tywin Lannister, who took note of him and made him one of his inner council of battle leaders, noting that he was the most feared man in the realm.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Gregor's father, his younger sister, and his first two wives all died under unusual circumstances, leaving him and Sandor as the last members of the house.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to the TV series official <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"pronunciation guide","link":"pronunciation guide","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[pronunciation guide]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111249826656cca94fee8d7" href="/wiki/Pronunciation_guide" title="Pronunciation guide">pronunciation guide</a> developed for the cast and crew, "Clegane" is pronounced "Cli-GAIN", as opposed to "CLEG-ain", etc.
</p>
