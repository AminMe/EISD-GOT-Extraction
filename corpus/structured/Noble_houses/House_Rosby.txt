<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36343578356cca99ebcf1e" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Rosby was one of the firsts to kneel to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon the Conqueror","link":"Aegon the Conqueror","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon the Conqueror]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36343578356cca99ebcf1e" href="/wiki/Aegon_the_Conqueror" title="Aegon the Conqueror" class="mw-redirect">Aegon the Conqueror</a>. Rosbys are not known for their robustness, tending to be sickly persons. Bethany Rosby is the deceased sixth wife of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36343578356cca99ebcf1e" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a> and mother to five Freys including <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Olyvar","link":"Olyvar Frey","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Olyvar Frey|Olyvar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36343578356cca99ebcf1e" href="/wiki/Olyvar_Frey" title="Olyvar Frey">Olyvar</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Roslin Frey","link":"Roslin Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Roslin Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36343578356cca99ebcf1e" href="/wiki/Roslin_Frey" title="Roslin Frey">Roslin Frey</a>. According to the semi-canonical <i>A Song of Ice and Fire Roleplaying Campaign Guide: A Game of Thrones Edition</i> from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Green Ronin Publishing","link":"wikipedia:Green Ronin Publishing","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wikipedia:Green Ronin Publishing|Green Ronin Publishing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-36343578356cca99ebcf1e" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Green_Ronin_Publishing" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Green Ronin Publishing">Green Ronin Publishing</a> Bethany Rosby is Lord Gyles Rosby's daughter. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Their blazon consists of three red chevronels on ermine. Their motto is not mentioned in the books
