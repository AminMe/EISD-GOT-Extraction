<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>House Hollard</b> is an extinct vassal house of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Crownlands","link":"Crownlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crownlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63285695356cca96942921" href="/wiki/Crownlands" title="Crownlands" class="mw-redirect">Crownlands</a>, holding fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon of King's Landing","link":"House Baratheon of King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon of King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63285695356cca96942921" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing" title="House Baratheon of King's Landing">House Baratheon of King's Landing</a>. The house was once powerful, but over the years it fell into disgrace.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63285695356cca96942921" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Hollard was an ancient house sworn to House Darklyn, having three daughters wedded to Darklyn kings during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Age of Heroes","link":"Age of Heroes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Age of Heroes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63285695356cca96942921" href="/wiki/Age_of_Heroes" title="Age of Heroes">Age of Heroes</a>. Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Defiance of Duskendale","link":"Defiance of Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Defiance of Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63285695356cca96942921" href="/wiki/Defiance_of_Duskendale" title="Defiance of Duskendale">Defiance of Duskendale</a>, the entire House was executed at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys II","link":"Mad King","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mad King|Aerys II]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63285695356cca96942921" href="/wiki/Mad_King" title="Mad King" class="mw-redirect">Aerys II</a>'s command, except for the infant Dontos Hollard, who was allowed to live at Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy's","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy|Barristan Selmy's]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-63285695356cca96942921" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy's</a> request.
