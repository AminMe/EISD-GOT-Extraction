In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Uller is one of House Martell's most powerful vassals, and reputed to be impulsive and unpredictable. There's a saying in Dorne that goes Half of the Ullers are half-mad, and the other half are worse. Their arms are a rayonne yellow over crimson while their words are unknown.
