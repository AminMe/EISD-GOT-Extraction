<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131021712456cca9667dd4a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Malaquo Maegyr is one of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Triarch","link":"Triarch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Triarch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131021712456cca9667dd4a" href="/wiki/Triarch" title="Triarch">Triarchs</a> of Volantis, the city's three rulers. Triarchs like Malaquo Maegyr are elected from amongst those noble families who can prove unbroken descent from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"old Valyria","link":"old Valyria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[old Valyria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131021712456cca9667dd4a" href="/wiki/Old_Valyria" title="Old Valyria">old Valyria</a>. Malaquo Maegyr is a "Tiger," from the "Old Bloods" political faction who favor more resource allocation into preparing for war. Fittingly, the sigil next to the onscreen credit for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oona Chaplin","link":"Oona Chaplin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oona Chaplin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131021712456cca9667dd4a" href="/wiki/Oona_Chaplin" title="Oona Chaplin">Oona Chaplin</a> in the opening titles, who portrays <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talisa Maegyr","link":"Talisa Maegyr","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talisa Maegyr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131021712456cca9667dd4a" href="/wiki/Talisa_Maegyr" title="Talisa Maegyr" class="mw-redirect">Talisa Maegyr</a>, is two tigers circling one another. Nevertheless, Volantene families do not employ sigils, which is a Westerosi custom.
</p>
