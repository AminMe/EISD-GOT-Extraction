<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Smallwood is a noble house sworn to House Vance of Wayfarer's Rest. Their sigil is six brown acorns, 3-2-1, on yellow and their motto is "From These Beginnings." Its known members are:
</p>
<ul data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Lord Theomar Smallwood, Lord of Acorn Hall.
<ul><li>Lady Ravella Swann, his wife.
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Carellen Smallwood, their daughter.
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">an unknown son who died at the age of seven.
</li></ul>
</li></ul>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Members with unspecified kinship to the main branch:
</p>
<ul data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><li>{Thoren Smallwood}, a ranger and senior officer of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. </li></ul>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">An historical member of the house, Myles Smallwood, was a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hand of the King","link":"Hand of the King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hand of the King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/Hand_of_the_King" title="Hand of the King">Hand of the King</a> ​who was renowned for his courage.  He was a disaster as the Hand and now a warning to those who become Hands.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a> House Smallwood follows their liege lord against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a>, while Lady Ravella sends Carellen to her great-aunt at a motherhouse in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oldtown","link":"Oldtown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oldtown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/Oldtown" title="Oldtown">Oldtown</a>. Acorn Hall, in the meantime, serves as safe haven for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brotherhood Without Banners","link":"Brotherhood Without Banners","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brotherhood Without Banners]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/Brotherhood_Without_Banners" title="Brotherhood Without Banners">Brotherhood Without Banners</a>. Smallwood men are present at the camps outside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Twins","link":"the Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">the Twins</a> prior to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>. Lord Theomar bends the knee to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142796581956cca9935c969" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a> following the massacre.
