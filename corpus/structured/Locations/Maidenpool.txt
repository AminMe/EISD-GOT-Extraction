<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Maidenpool is famed in song as the location where the foolish knight <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Florian","link":"Florian","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Florian]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Florian" title="Florian">Florian</a> met and fell in love with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jonquil","link":"Jonquil","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jonquil]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Jonquil" title="Jonquil">Jonquil</a>. He fell in love with her when he saw her bathing with her sisters.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/maidenpool\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Maidenpool entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> quizzes <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> on where she is from, she says Maidenpool. Tywin realizes she is lying when she cannot identify House Mooton's sigil. She then admits to being from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>, but says that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barrowton","link":"Barrowton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barrowton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Barrowton" title="Barrowton">Barrowton</a> is her home town. She identifies the arms of the ruling <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Dustin","link":"House Dustin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Dustin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/House_Dustin" title="House Dustin">House Dustin</a> accurately.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Maidenpool is a port town on the east coast of Westeros, on the Bay of Crabs. It is linked to King's Landing by two roads, one that runs along the coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Crackclaw Point","link":"Crackclaw Point","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crackclaw Point]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Crackclaw_Point" title="Crackclaw Point">Crackclaw Point</a> to the east and another that passes directly south through <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a> in the Crownlands. Fisherfolk northwest of Maidenpool fish the waters in leather coracles and collect clams. Maidenpool's main gate is called the Fool's Gate.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Maidenpool is sacked during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime","link":"Jaime","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Jaime" title="Jaime" class="mw-redirect">Jaime</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cleos Frey","link":"Alton Lannister#In the books","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Alton Lannister#In the books|Cleos Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Alton_Lannister#In_the_books" title="Alton Lannister">Cleos Frey</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne","link":"Brienne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Brienne" title="Brienne" class="mw-redirect">Brienne</a> pass through the town on their way to King's Landing, noticing that the town walls are deserted, the gates are smashed, and half the homes and shops are burned or plundered. After the Battle of Duskendale, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Randyll Tarly","link":"Randyll Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Randyll Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25756816256cca8dea8dc7" href="/wiki/Randyll_Tarly" title="Randyll Tarly">Randyll Tarly</a> marches on Maidenpool, takes the town and locks Lord William Mooton in a tower cell. Later he begins to rebuild the town while hunting the outlaws in the area.
</p>
