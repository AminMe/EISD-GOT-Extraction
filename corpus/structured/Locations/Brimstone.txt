<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113289205256cca90c40ba3" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Brimstone appears to have its source directly in the desert of southern Dorne, which seems to be unlikely. The source of the river may originate in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Mountains","link":"Red Mountains","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Mountains]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113289205256cca90c40ba3" href="/wiki/Red_Mountains" title="Red Mountains">Red Mountains</a> further north.  Despite being the only major river in central Dorne it is of little comfort to those who must pass through the harsh desert:  the waters of the river are sulphurous.  
</p>
