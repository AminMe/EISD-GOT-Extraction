<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61709210856cca8f037131" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Cape Kraken is named for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"Ironborn","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironborn|ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61709210856cca8f037131" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> (whose sigil is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"kraken","link":"Krakens","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Krakens|kraken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61709210856cca8f037131" href="/wiki/Krakens" title="Krakens">kraken</a>) who used to raid heavily along its coasts.
