<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lannisport is located just south of Casterly Rock and is a large and bustling seaport with a population of approximately 250,000, about half the size of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100217132656cca8872fb7a" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>.  It is the third-largest city in Westeros, after King's Landing and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oldtown","link":"Oldtown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oldtown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100217132656cca8872fb7a" href="/wiki/Oldtown" title="Oldtown">Oldtown</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100217132656cca8872fb7a" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> and her friends used to sneak into the city while young to see the sights.
</p>
