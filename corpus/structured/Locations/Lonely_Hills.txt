</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Knife","link":"White Knife","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Knife]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156187733256cca8dd292f9" href="/wiki/White_Knife" title="White Knife">White Knife</a> river flows south out of Long Lake to the west:  a tributary of the White Knife originates in the Lonely Hills, merging with the main river further south.
