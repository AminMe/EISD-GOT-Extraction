<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Gods Eye</b> is an immense lake, the largest inland body of water in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It is located in the heart of the continent, in the southeast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>. Small fishing villages dot its shores, but the most significant settlement is the ill-omened castle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>, located on the north shore.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Gods Eye feeds a river which is a tributary of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackwater Rush","link":"Blackwater Rush","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackwater Rush]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Blackwater_Rush" title="Blackwater Rush">Blackwater Rush</a>. The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Isle of Faces","link":"Isle of Faces","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Isle of Faces]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Isle_of_Faces" title="Isle of Faces">Isle of Faces</a> is located within Gods Eye.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> tells his son <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> that following the destruction of the western Lannister army-group in the Riverlands at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a>, the eastern Lannister army led by Tywin himself will fall back from the location of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Green Fork","link":"Battle of the Green Fork","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Green Fork]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Green_Fork" title="Battle of the Green Fork">Battle of the Green Fork</a> and regroup at Harrenhal, on the north shore of Gods Eye lake. Tywin specifically says that to cover the retreat of the main army, he is sending out Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> and his riders to set fire to the farms and fields of the Riverlands everywhere between the Red Fork of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Trident","link":"Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Trident" title="Trident" class="mw-redirect">Trident</a> river and Gods Eye lake.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Fire and Blood]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Isle of Faces is reputed to be located in the center of Gods Eye, the home of "greenseers" who keep the traditions and secrets of the long-vanished <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Children of the Forest","link":"Children of the Forest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Children of the Forest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Children_of_the_Forest" title="Children of the Forest">Children of the Forest</a>. The island is said to be home to many <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"weirwood","link":"weirwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[weirwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196088952956cca8900ca11" href="/wiki/Weirwood" title="Weirwood">weirwood</a> trees. The Isle has a fell reputation and most avoid it.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Gods Eye feeds a tributary of the Blackwater Rush, but does not appear to be navigable by large ships. Another river, the Rippledown Rill, also feeds the lake.
</p>
