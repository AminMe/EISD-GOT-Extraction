
The Golden Fields is a region in western Essos. This area is located between Dagger Lake and a tributary of the Rhoyne. In the books
In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the Golden Fields are located in the same area. The tributary of the Rhoyne that lies along their south-western edge is called the Lhorulu.
