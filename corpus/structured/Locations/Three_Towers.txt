<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58006224456cca92a5b16e" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Three Towers is the seat of House Costayne. It is located on the southern coast of Whispering Sound, where it empties into the Redwyne Straits. Along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackcrown","link":"Blackcrown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackcrown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58006224456cca92a5b16e" href="/wiki/Blackcrown" title="Blackcrown">Blackcrown</a>, it guards the entrance to the Sound and the approaches to Oldtown.
