<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201962875856cca92c887e2" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, Faircastle is the seat of House Farman and is located on Fair Isle, just off the coast of the Westerlands. In the past it has suffered raids from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201962875856cca92c887e2" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a>. During the reign of King Aerys I, the ironborn sacked the island and stole half of its wealth. Later, during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201962875856cca92c887e2" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201962875856cca92c887e2" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> led the Royal Fleet in engaging the ironborn off Fair Isle. Stannis lured the Iron Fleet into the strait between Fair Isle and the mainland and then hit it from both sides, virtually destroying the ironborn force. During the battle Aeron Greyjoy, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy|Balon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201962875856cca92c887e2" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon</a>'s younger brother, was forced overboard and was rescued by seamen on the <i>Fury</i>, Stannis's flagship. The Battle of Fair Isle was vital as it allowed King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201962875856cca92c887e2" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> and his army to land on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pyke","link":"Pyke","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pyke]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-201962875856cca92c887e2" href="/wiki/Pyke" title="Pyke">Pyke</a> and storm it, ending the war.
</p>
