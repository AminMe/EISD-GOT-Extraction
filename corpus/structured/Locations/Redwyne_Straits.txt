<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177964029656cca9167ce51" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Redwyne Straits are usually peaceful. They are a center of trade, with ships passing through the straits from the city of Oldtown and ports on the Arbor to all parts of Westeros and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Essos","link":"Essos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Essos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177964029656cca9167ce51" href="/wiki/Essos" title="Essos">Essos</a>, as well as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Summer Islands","link":"Summer Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Summer Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177964029656cca9167ce51" href="/wiki/Summer_Islands" title="Summer Islands">Summer Islands</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stepstones","link":"Stepstones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stepstones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177964029656cca9167ce51" href="/wiki/Stepstones" title="Stepstones">Stepstones</a>. In ancient times the straits were less safe, with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177964029656cca9167ce51" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> sometimes raiding as far south as their waters.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Towns such as Vinetown, Starfish Harbor and Ryamsport sit alongside it.
</p>
