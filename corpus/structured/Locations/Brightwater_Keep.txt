<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Florent of Brightwater Keep also lays claim to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Highgarden","link":"Highgarden","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Highgarden]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/Highgarden" title="Highgarden">Highgarden</a>, due to a closer blood relationship to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Gardener","link":"House Gardener","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Gardener]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/House_Gardener" title="House Gardener">House Gardener</a>, the Kings of the Reach before <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon the Conqueror","link":"Aegon the Conqueror","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon the Conqueror]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/Aegon_the_Conqueror" title="Aegon the Conqueror" class="mw-redirect">Aegon the Conqueror</a> extinguished their line on the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Field of Fire","link":"Field of Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Field of Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/Field_of_Fire" title="Field of Fire">Field of Fire</a>. As a result of this, the Florents are surly and reluctant bannermen to the Tyrells.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Following the Battle of the Blackwater, House Florent is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"attainted","link":"Attainting","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Attainting|attainted]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/Attainting" title="Attainting">attainted</a> by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a> and stripped of lands and titles. Brightwater Keep is granted to Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Garlan Tyrell","link":"Garlan Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Garlan Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/Garlan_Tyrell" title="Garlan Tyrell">Garlan Tyrell</a>, the second son of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mace Tyrell","link":"Mace Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mace Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-97592626956cca8c0666f8" href="/wiki/Mace_Tyrell" title="Mace Tyrell">Mace Tyrell</a>, who has been cut from the series so far.
</p>
