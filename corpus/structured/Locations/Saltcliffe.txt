<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><b>Saltcliffe</b> is one of the seven <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a>, an archipelago located on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironman's Bay","link":"Ironman's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironman's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Ironman's_Bay" title="Ironman's Bay">Ironman's Bay</a>, on the western side of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">As one of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a>'s sadistic torments of the captive <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a>, before Theon knew his real identity, he pretended to be a spy sent by his sister <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yara","link":"Yara Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yara Greyjoy|Yara]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Yara_Greyjoy" title="Yara Greyjoy">Yara</a> to help him escape. When Theon asked who he was, he claimed he was an ironborn from Saltcliffe.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[And Now His Watch is Ended]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> In the books </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Saltcliffe is the least noteworthy of any of the seven Iron Islands, without much of a distinguishing economy, culture, or history.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">At least two major noble Houses are located on the island: House Saltcliffe of Saltcliffe, and House Sunderly of Saltcliffe. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon Greyjoy","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon Greyjoy</a>'s mother was a Sunderly - grandmother of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yara","link":"Yara Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yara Greyjoy|Yara]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-9467633656cca91d2c56d" href="/wiki/Yara_Greyjoy" title="Yara Greyjoy">Yara</a>.
</p>
