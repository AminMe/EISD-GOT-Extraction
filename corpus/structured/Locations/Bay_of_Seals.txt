<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->The <b>Bay of Seals</b> is a large bay off the north-eastern coast of the continent of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It is part of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shivering Sea","link":"Shivering Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shivering Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Shivering_Sea" title="Shivering Sea">Shivering Sea</a>. The bay runs from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wildling","link":"wildling","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wildling]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Wildling" title="Wildling" class="mw-redirect">wildling</a> territories <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"beyond the Wall","link":"beyond the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[beyond the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Beyond_the_Wall" title="Beyond the Wall">beyond the Wall</a> to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Cliffs","link":"Grey Cliffs","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Cliffs]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Grey_Cliffs" title="Grey Cliffs" class="mw-redirect">Grey Cliffs</a> north-east of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Karhold","link":"Karhold","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Karhold]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Karhold" title="Karhold">Karhold</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eastwatch-by-the-Sea","link":"Eastwatch-by-the-Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eastwatch-by-the-Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Eastwatch-by-the-Sea" title="Eastwatch-by-the-Sea">Eastwatch-by-the-Sea</a>, the eastern-most castle on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>, is located on the edge of the bay. The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> controls the coast southwards for 150 miles (the eastern edge of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Gift","link":"the Gift","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Gift]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/The_Gift" title="The Gift">the Gift</a>). The coast south of that is controlled by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Umber","link":"House Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/House_Umber" title="House Umber">House Umber</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Karstark","link":"House Karstark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Karstark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/House_Karstark" title="House Karstark">House Karstark</a> controls much of the southern coast of the bay.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The islands of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Skane","link":"Skane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Skane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Skane" title="Skane">Skane</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Skagos","link":"Skagos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Skagos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/Skagos" title="Skagos">Skagos</a> (amongst others) are located in the bay.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/castle-black\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Castle Black entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114737029656cca8b6b8d49" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Bay of Seals is reported as being difficult to cross in autumn, as storms make it almost impassable. Ships risk being dashed against the rocky coastline of Skagos if they are not careful.
</p>
