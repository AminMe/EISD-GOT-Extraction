<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Stoney Sept was the site of one of the most important battles of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> hid in the town with the aid of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"smallfolk","link":"smallfolk","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[smallfolk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Smallfolk" title="Smallfolk">smallfolk</a>. When the royal army arrived to search the town from house to house the bells of the local <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sept","link":"sept","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[sept]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Sept" title="Sept">sept</a> were rang, giving the ensuing battle its name: the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Bells","link":"Battle of the Bells","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Bells]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Bells" title="Battle of the Bells">Battle of the Bells</a>. The arrival of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Arryn","link":"Jon Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Jon_Arryn" title="Jon Arryn">Jon Arryn</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a> secured the rebel victory.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[[Robert's Rebellion (Complete Guide to Westeros)]]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels Stoney Sept refers to a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sept","link":"sept","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[sept]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Sept" title="Sept">sept</a> and its surrounding town. The sept itself sits on a hill. Below it is a holdfast made of grey stone that seems undersized for the town that surrounds it. The heart of Stoney Sept is a market square with a fountain in the shape of a leaping trout. Stoney Sept isn't nearly big enough to be considered even a minor city, but it is a fairly large market town. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> is brought there after she encounters the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brotherhood Without Banners","link":"Brotherhood Without Banners","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brotherhood Without Banners]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25154455856cca8c799f90" href="/wiki/Brotherhood_Without_Banners" title="Brotherhood Without Banners">Brotherhood Without Banners</a>, and notes that it is the biggest town she's seen since she left King's Landing.
</p>
