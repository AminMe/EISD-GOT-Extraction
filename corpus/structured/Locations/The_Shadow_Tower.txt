<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-33955714556cca879c39aa" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Shadow Tower is under the command of Ser Denys Mallister, an experienced and conservative commander, and uncle to Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jason Mallister","link":"Jason Mallister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jason Mallister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-33955714556cca879c39aa" href="/wiki/Jason_Mallister" title="Jason Mallister">Jason Mallister</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seagard","link":"Seagard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seagard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-33955714556cca879c39aa" href="/wiki/Seagard" title="Seagard">Seagard</a>. It is not the western-most castle on the Wall, which used to be Westwatch-by-the-Bridge. However, Westwatch has fallen into ruin and is no longer garrisoned. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qhorin Halfhand","link":"Qhorin Halfhand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qhorin Halfhand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-33955714556cca879c39aa" href="/wiki/Qhorin_Halfhand" title="Qhorin Halfhand" class="mw-redirect">Qhorin Halfhand</a> was usually based at the Shadow Tower.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jeor Mormont","link":"Jeor Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jeor Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-33955714556cca879c39aa" href="/wiki/Jeor_Mormont" title="Jeor Mormont">Jeor Mormont</a>, at the start of the books there are about 200 soldiers in the Shadow Tower. Qhorin led 100 of them to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Ranging","link":"Great Ranging","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Ranging]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-33955714556cca879c39aa" href="/wiki/Great_Ranging" title="Great Ranging">Great Ranging</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Patrols and scouts from the Shadow Tower have to keep a close eye on the surrounding terrain. Though extremely difficult, it is possible to by-pass the Wall by descending through the Gorge and crossing the Bridge of Skulls, or by tracing a dangerous route through the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Frostfangs","link":"Frostfangs","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Frostfangs]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-33955714556cca879c39aa" href="/wiki/Frostfangs" title="Frostfangs">Frostfangs</a> to the west. The Night's Watch has to keep an eye on these routes for signs of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wildling","link":"wildling","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wildling]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-33955714556cca879c39aa" href="/wiki/Wildling" title="Wildling" class="mw-redirect">wildling</a> activity.
</p>
