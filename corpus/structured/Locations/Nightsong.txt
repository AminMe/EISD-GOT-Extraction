<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35323034756cca8c507835" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Nightsong is a major stronghold of the Dornish Marches. House Caron are known as the "Lords of the Marches", suggesting they may have some superior authority over the other march lords (such as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Dondarrion","link":"House Dondarrion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Dondarrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35323034756cca8c507835" href="/wiki/House_Dondarrion" title="House Dondarrion">House Dondarrion</a>). Houses Caron and Dondarrion allied together to fight the Vulture King, a bandit lord based in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Mountains","link":"Red Mountains","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Mountains]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35323034756cca8c507835" href="/wiki/Red_Mountains" title="Red Mountains">Red Mountains</a>, some ninety years before the events of the books. Ser Arlen of Pennytree and his squire, the future Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35323034756cca8c507835" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a>, accompanied this campaign.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, House Caron is effectively destroyed when its last surviving members are killed during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35323034756cca8c507835" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>, resulting in the castle of Nightsong passing to House Foote.
</p>
