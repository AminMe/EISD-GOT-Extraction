<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119540582056cca8d2863f3" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Bay of Ice is a large bay in the North that is bordered by the Frozen Shore to the north and Sea Dragon Point to the south. Bear Island sits within the bay. Wildling raiders from the Frozen Shore sometimes raid across the Bay of Ice, attacking both Bear Island and the lands around <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Deepwood Motte","link":"Deepwood Motte","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Deepwood Motte]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119540582056cca8d2863f3" href="/wiki/Deepwood_Motte" title="Deepwood Motte">Deepwood Motte</a>. The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119540582056cca8d2863f3" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> at the height of their power were able to send <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"longships","link":"Iron Fleet","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Fleet|longships]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119540582056cca8d2863f3" href="/wiki/Iron_Fleet" title="Iron Fleet">longships</a> into the Bay of Ice and controlled Bear Island for several centuries until losing it in a wrestling match to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King in the North","link":"King in the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King in the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119540582056cca8d2863f3" href="/wiki/King_in_the_North" title="King in the North">King in the North</a>, who gifted it to a loyal retainer (who became the founder of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Mormont","link":"House Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119540582056cca8d2863f3" href="/wiki/House_Mormont" title="House Mormont">House Mormont</a>).
</p>
