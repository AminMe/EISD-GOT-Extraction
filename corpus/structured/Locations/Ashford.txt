<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ashford is the seat of House Ashford. It consists of a castle, a surrounding town and a large meadow nearby which is a perfect location for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tournament","link":"tournament","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[tournament]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">tournaments</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ashford is the site of two important events in the history of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>. In 209 AL Ashford was the site of a tournament where the much-storied <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight" class="mw-redirect">knight</a> Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a> first began his rise to fame and where he first met his even more famous companion, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Egg","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen|Egg]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Egg</a>. Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Baelor 'Breakspear' Targaryen","link":"Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)|Baelor 'Breakspear' Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)" title="Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)">Baelor 'Breakspear' Targaryen</a>, the king's heir, was also accidentally killed during this tourney, changing the future history of the Seven Kingdoms.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In 281 AL, during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, Ashford was the site of a major battle and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>'s only notable defeat. The army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> was advancing towards the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormlands","link":"Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Stormlands" title="Stormlands" class="mw-redirect">Stormlands</a> with the intention of destroying Robert's army and taking <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>. Robert elected to fight a field battle rather than withstand siege and intercepted the Tyrell army near Ashford. Robert attacked the Tyrell vanguard (which Robert's army outnumbered at a local level, since the bulk of the Tyrell army had not yet arrived). However, Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Randyll Tarly","link":"Randyll Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Randyll Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Randyll_Tarly" title="Randyll Tarly">Randyll Tarly</a>, commanding the Tyrell van, withstood the attack and drove the Baratheon army from the field. Robert's army was defeated, but managed to withdraw in good order northwards towards the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>. The Tyrell army was commanded to march on to Storm's End (held by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a>) and besiege it rather than pursue Robert. This proved unwise, as Robert was able to combine his army with those of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Starks","link":"House Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark|Starks]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">Starks</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arryns","link":"House Arryn","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Arryn|Arryns]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/House_Arryn" title="House Arryn">Arryns</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tullys","link":"House Tully","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully|Tullys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-136764017956cca8db61018" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">Tullys</a>, enabling him to ultimately triumph in the war.
</p>
