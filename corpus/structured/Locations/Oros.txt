<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69942038956cca8ae835b9" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Oros is connected by a Valyrian road to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mantarys","link":"Mantarys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mantarys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69942038956cca8ae835b9" href="/wiki/Mantarys" title="Mantarys">Mantarys</a>. It was located close to the Fourteen Fires, the great volcanic mountain range stretching across the neck of the Valyrian Peninsula. Given its close proximity to the volcanoes, it was likely laid waste during the Doom.
</p>
