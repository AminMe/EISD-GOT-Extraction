In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Mistwood is the seat of House Mertyns. Their only claim to fame is that they once produced a famous member of the Kingsguard, Ser Michael Mertyns, the White Owl. 

Mistwood is located on Cape Wrath, in the Rainwood. It is located inland from the Sea of Dorne. See also References
