<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155722077256cca90313904" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Mistwood is the seat of House Mertyns. Their only claim to fame is that they once produced a famous member of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155722077256cca90313904" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a>, Ser Michael Mertyns, the White Owl.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Mistwood is located on Cape Wrath, in the Rainwood. It is located inland from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sea of Dorne","link":"Sea of Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sea of Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155722077256cca90313904" href="/wiki/Sea_of_Dorne" title="Sea of Dorne">Sea of Dorne</a>.
