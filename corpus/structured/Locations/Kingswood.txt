<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The <b>Kingswood</b> is a forest in central <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It begins in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Crownlands","link":"the Crownlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Crownlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/The_Crownlands" title="The Crownlands">the Crownlands</a> immediately south of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> and extends into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stormlands","link":"the Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/The_Stormlands" title="The Stormlands">the Stormlands</a>. The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsroad","link":"Kingsroad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsroad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Kingsroad" title="Kingsroad">Kingsroad</a> passes through the Kingswood as it runs from King's Landing to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Twenty years prior to the events of the series, an outlaw band known as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingswood Brotherhood","link":"Kingswood Brotherhood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingswood Brotherhood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Kingswood_Brotherhood" title="Kingswood Brotherhood">Kingswood Brotherhood</a> took up residence in the Kingswood, led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Simon Toyne","link":"Simon Toyne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Simon Toyne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Simon_Toyne" title="Simon Toyne">Simon Toyne</a>. They caused significant problems before the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mad King","link":"Mad King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mad King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Mad_King" title="Mad King" class="mw-redirect">Mad King</a> sent a small army to root them out. Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a> slew Simon Toyne with a counter-riposte, impressing the fifteen-year-old <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> (who killed his first man during the battle, beheading him).<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[[Lord Snow]]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Robert is hunting in the Kingswood, accompanied by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>, Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a> and his squire, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lancel Lannister","link":"Lancel Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lancel Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/Lancel_Lannister" title="Lancel Lannister" class="mw-redirect">Lancel Lannister</a>. He suffers a life-threatening injury while trying to slay a boar.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Golden Crown]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51316060056cca8e111704" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Kingswood is an extensive, large and deep forest with many small villages located within its eaves.
