<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Siege of Meereen","link":"Siege of Meereen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Meereen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213216297356cca8a4d4182" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Meereen" title="Siege of Meereen">Siege of Meereen</a>, as the local <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"slave","link":"Slavery","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slavery|slave]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213216297356cca8a4d4182" href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">slave</a> population rises in arms against the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Masters","link":"Great Masters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Masters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213216297356cca8a4d4182" href="/wiki/Great_Masters" title="Great Masters">Great Masters</a>, an enormous black banner with the three-headed red dragon of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213216297356cca8a4d4182" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a> is placed over the harpy atop the pyramid. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213216297356cca8a4d4182" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Great Pyramid had been built as an echo of the Great Pyramid of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ghis","link":"Old Ghis","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Old Ghis|Ghis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213216297356cca8a4d4182" href="/wiki/Old_Ghis" title="Old Ghis">Ghis</a>, whose colossal ruins had once been visited by noted explorer <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lomas Longstrider","link":"Lomas Longstrider","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lomas Longstrider]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213216297356cca8a4d4182" href="/wiki/Lomas_Longstrider" title="Lomas Longstrider">Lomas Longstrider</a>. Like its ancient predecessor, the Pyramid has thirty three levels, a number chosen intentionally, since it is considered sacred to the gods of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ghiscari religion","link":"Ghiscari religion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ghiscari religion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213216297356cca8a4d4182" href="/wiki/Ghiscari_religion" title="Ghiscari religion">Ghiscari religion</a>.
