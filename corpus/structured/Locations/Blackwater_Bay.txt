<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-174082191456cca893bdffd" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Blackwater Bay is a fairly large body of water, held between the arms of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Crackclaw Point","link":"Crackclaw Point","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crackclaw Point]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-174082191456cca893bdffd" href="/wiki/Crackclaw_Point" title="Crackclaw Point">Crackclaw Point</a> to the north and Massey's Hook to the south, with the island of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dragonstone","link":"Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-174082191456cca893bdffd" href="/wiki/Dragonstone" title="Dragonstone" class="mw-redirect">Dragonstone</a> guarding the entrance to the bay. Elements of the King's Fleet patrol the Bay and ensure it is kept free from piracy.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Aside from King's Landing and Dragonstone, the largest port on the bay is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-174082191456cca893bdffd" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a> (which serves as a secondary port for King's Landing when the main port is overwhelmed with traffic), while the largest island is Driftmark, the seat of House Velaryon.
</p>
