<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[Image:Lands of Always Winter.png|thumb|right|200px|A map showing the rough location of the Lands of Always Winter on the continent of Westeros.]]","title":"Lands_of_Always_Winter.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Lands of Always Winter","align":"right","caption":"A map showing the rough location of the Lands of Always Winter on the continent of Westeros.","width":200,"captionParsed":"A map showing the rough location of the Lands of Always Winter on the continent of Westeros."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-125474371556cca85dd3147" alt="" src="http://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/b/b3/Lands_of_Always_Winter.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20120513155430" width="200" height="516" class="image thumb alignRight withCaption" data-image-name="Lands of Always Winter.png" data-image-key="Lands_of_Always_Winter.png"  type="image" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125474371556cca85dd3147" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Lands of Always Winter are mostly unexplored. They lie beyond the northern-most wildling kingdom, the land of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Thenns","link":"Thenns","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thenns]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125474371556cca85dd3147" href="/wiki/Thenns" title="Thenns">Thenns</a> in the northern valleys of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Frostfangs","link":"Frostfangs","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Frostfangs]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125474371556cca85dd3147" href="/wiki/Frostfangs" title="Frostfangs">Frostfangs</a>. The wildlings believe that the White Walkers come from this region, but even the northernmost wildling tribes have little if any knowledge of the region - as few who have ventured so far north have ever lived to tell about it, if only due to the deathly cold temperatures.
</p>
