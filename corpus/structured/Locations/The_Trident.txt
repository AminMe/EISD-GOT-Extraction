<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><b>The Trident</b> is one of the largest and most well-known rivers on the continent of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. Technically it is three rivers which flow into one another just before they reach the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Narrow Sea","link":"Narrow Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Narrow Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a>, several hundred miles north of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="2">Forks of the Trident</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>The Red Fork</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The longest branch rises in the Westerlands and flows north to a confluence with its tributary, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tumblestone","link":"Tumblestone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tumblestone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Tumblestone" title="Tumblestone">Tumblestone</a>, which flows from the northwest. The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> castle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a> is located where the two rivers meet. The Red Fork then flows east through the center of the Riverlands to where it joins the main body of the Trident. The lands along the Red Fork of the Trident are located in the very heart of the entire continent of Westeros.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Blue Fork</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The shortest and least noteworthy of the three branches rises in the hills south-east of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seagard","link":"Seagard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seagard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Seagard" title="Seagard">Seagard</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironman's Bay","link":"Ironman's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironman's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Ironman's_Bay" title="Ironman's Bay">Ironman's Bay</a>. It flows south-easterly into the main body of the Trident.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Green Fork</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Green Fork","link":"Green Fork","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Green Fork]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Green_Fork" title="Green Fork" class="mw-redirect">Green Fork</a> starts in the bogs of the Neck, technically within the southern borders of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>. The river flows south, crossing the border into the Riverlands, and then past the two towers of the castle known as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Twins","link":"the Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">the Twins</a>, the seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Frey","link":"House Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/House_Frey" title="House Frey">House Frey</a>. It flows south-east for several hundred more miles before reaching the main body of the Trident. Just before that confluence is the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ruby Ford","link":"Ruby Ford","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ruby Ford]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Ruby_Ford" title="Ruby Ford">Ruby Ford</a>, where the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Trident","link":"Battle of the Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Trident" title="Battle of the Trident">Battle of the Trident</a> during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a> was fought and where <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> slew <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegar Targaryen","link":"Rhaegar Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegar Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/Rhaegar_Targaryen" title="Rhaegar Targaryen">Rhaegar Targaryen</a> in combat. The ford is named for the rubies that ornamented Rhaegar's breast plate that were said to have been scattered in the river when he was killed by Robert's war hammer.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/the-ruby-ford\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, The Ruby Ford entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77450478756cca87a9e0f3" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Trident is the same. The Tumblestone is a tributary of the Red Fork that flows from the north out of the Westerlands and joins the Red Fork coming up from the south at Riverrun. For much of its length the Red Fork is meandering and slow-moving, with frequent sandbars and islets. There are fords a few miles south of Riverrun and another ford, known as the Mummer's Ford, further east.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The books haven't made clear what the proper name of the river is for the brief part of its run east of where all three forks merge, but before it enters the ocean. It may be known simply as "the Trident" at this point, but as with several rivers in real life, one of the three branches may be considered the main river, i.e. how the Ohio River is a tributary of the Mississippi River, but the waters below where they join are considered to be "the Mississippi" and not a new river. Such nomenclature is often based on local factors, i.e. the riverbed of the lower Mississippi more closely matches the riverbed of the upper Mississippi than the river bed of the Ohio, etc. Its possible, due to vague references that the Battle of the Trident at Ruby Ford was fought "on the Green Fork of the Trident", that the Green Fork of the Trident is considered to be the one which flows all the way to the ocean.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The headlands of the Green Fork are allegedly close to the location of the mysterious Greywater Watch, the seat of House Reed.
</p>
