<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">While in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qarth","link":"Qarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Qarth" title="Qarth">Qarth</a>, Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jorah Mormont","link":"Jorah Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jorah Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Jorah_Mormont" title="Jorah Mormont">Jorah Mormont</a> informs <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> that he has procured transport on a ship bound for Astapor. However, Daenerys refuses to leave without having first recovered her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"dragons","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a> from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of the Undying","link":"House of the Undying","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of the Undying]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/House_of_the_Undying" title="House of the Undying">House of the Undying</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Prince of Winterfell]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Daenerys is convinced to travel to Astapor to procure the services of the eunuch slave-army known as the Unsullied. In Astapor she is nearly assassinated by the use of a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"manticore","link":"manticore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[manticore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Manticore" title="Manticore" class="mw-redirect">manticore</a>, but is saved by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a>, who pledges his service to her.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Valar Dohaeris]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Daenerys later visits the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walk of Punishment","link":"Walk of Punishment (Astapor)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walk of Punishment (Astapor)|Walk of Punishment]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Walk_of_Punishment_(Astapor)" title="Walk of Punishment (Astapor)">Walk of Punishment</a>, where slaves who have committed transgressions are tortured and put on display as a warning to others. Daenerys attempts to comfort a slave who has been condemned to death with water.  He instead begs her to be allowed to die.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Walk of Punishment]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After purchasing the entirety of Unsullied of Astapor, as well as the slave-boys still in training to become Unsullied, Daenerys double crosses the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Good Masters","link":"Good Masters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Good Masters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Good_Masters" title="Good Masters">Good Masters</a>. Under her command, the Unsullied put all slaver masters in the city to the sword, sparing only the children, and liberate the slaves, while <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogon","link":"Drogon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Drogon" title="Drogon">Drogon</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Viserion","link":"Viserion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Viserion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Viserion" title="Viserion">Viserion</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegal","link":"Rhaegal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Rhaegal" title="Rhaegal">Rhaegal</a> are also <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"unleashed","link":"Sack of Astapor","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of Astapor|unleashed]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Sack_of_Astapor" title="Sack of Astapor">unleashed</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[And Now His Watch is Ended]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While meeting with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Razdal mo Eraz","link":"Razdal mo Eraz","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Razdal mo Eraz]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Razdal_mo_Eraz" title="Razdal mo Eraz">Razdal mo Eraz</a> outside of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yunkai","link":"Yunkai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yunkai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Yunkai" title="Yunkai">Yunkai</a>, Daenerys demands the release of every slave in the city or she and her army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Unsullied","link":"Unsullied","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Unsullied]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Unsullied" title="Unsullied">Unsullied</a> will take it by force. Highly offended, Eraz spits back that Yunkai is not Astapor or Qarth, and that the Yunkai'i have "powerful friends" who would take great pleasure in bringing Daenerys down.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"'''The Bear and the Maiden Fair'''\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Astapor, like its sister-cities of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yunkai","link":"Yunkai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yunkai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Yunkai" title="Yunkai">Yunkai</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meereen","link":"Meereen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meereen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Meereen" title="Meereen">Meereen</a>, is culturally and historically <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ghiscar","link":"Ghiscar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ghiscar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Ghiscar" title="Ghiscar">Ghiscari</a> in origin, until the defeat of the old Ghiscari Empire by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyria","link":"Valyria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Valyria" title="Valyria" class="mw-redirect">Valyria</a> thousands of years ago. Following the Doom of Valyria, Astapor, Yunkai, and Meereen have reestablished their Ghiscari traditions, but have not joined the resurgent Ghiscari nation based at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"New Ghis","link":"New Ghis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[New Ghis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/New_Ghis" title="New Ghis">New Ghis</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Astapor is located at the mouth of the Worm River. It is ruled by the Good Masters, slave-owners and traders who have become immensely wealthy. They live on large, stepped pyramids similar to those in Meereen, but not as large. It is called "the Red City" for the red brick its buildings are made of. The city contains fighting pits and slave markets.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Slaves who displease their masters are tortured and displayed in the Plaza of Punishment, which is the first place a new slave brought into the city sees.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The trade of slaves is usually conducted in the Plaza of Pride, an open market where the slavers take their customers to view their purchases. In the center of the plaza stands a red brick fountain with a hammered bronze version of the Astapori harpy standing in its center.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Of the three slaver-cities, Astapor is bigger than Yunkai, but Meereen has a bigger population than Astapor and Yunkai put together. The population of Astapor is not as big as that of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qarth","link":"Qarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Qarth" title="Qarth">Qarth</a> or <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pentos","link":"Pentos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pentos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79703036656cca86640ec4" href="/wiki/Pentos" title="Pentos">Pentos</a>.
</p>
