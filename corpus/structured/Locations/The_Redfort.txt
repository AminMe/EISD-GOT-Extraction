<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41702538156cca8ee8d873" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Redfort is the seat of the eponymous House Redfort, loyal and powerful vassals of House Arryn. House Redfort, like <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Royce","link":"House Royce","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41702538156cca8ee8d873" href="/wiki/House_Royce" title="House Royce">House Royce</a>, is noted by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41702538156cca8ee8d873" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> as being descended from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"First Men","link":"First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41702538156cca8ee8d873" href="/wiki/First_Men" title="First Men">First Men</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Domeric Bolton","link":"Domeric Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Domeric Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41702538156cca8ee8d873" href="/wiki/Domeric_Bolton" title="Domeric Bolton">Domeric Bolton</a> was fostered at the Redfort for three years, and served as a squire to Lord Horton Redfort.
