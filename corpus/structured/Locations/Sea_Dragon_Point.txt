It extends northwards, separating the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bay of Ice","link":"Bay of Ice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bay of Ice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41798501656cca8e557f12" href="/wiki/Bay_of_Ice" title="Bay of Ice">Bay of Ice</a> to the east from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sunset Sea","link":"Sunset Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sunset Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41798501656cca8e557f12" href="/wiki/Sunset_Sea" title="Sunset Sea">Sunset Sea</a> in the west. It is located west of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wolfswood","link":"Wolfswood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wolfswood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41798501656cca8e557f12" href="/wiki/Wolfswood" title="Wolfswood">Wolfswood</a>. The nearest major castle is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Deepwood Motte","link":"Deepwood Motte","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Deepwood Motte]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41798501656cca8e557f12" href="/wiki/Deepwood_Motte" title="Deepwood Motte">Deepwood Motte</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/bear-island\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Bear Island entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41798501656cca8e557f12" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41798501656cca8e557f12" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41798501656cca8e557f12" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> moored on Sea Dragon Point, although they were <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"beaten back","link":"Raid on Sea Dragon Point","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raid on Sea Dragon Point|beaten back]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41798501656cca8e557f12" href="/wiki/Raid_on_Sea_Dragon_Point" title="Raid on Sea Dragon Point">beaten back</a>.
</p>
