
Sea Dragon Point is a large peninsula in the North. It extends northwards, separating the Bay of Ice to the east from the Sunset Sea in the west. It is located west of the Wolfswood. History
During the Greyjoy Rebellion, ironborn moored on Sea Dragon Point, although they were beaten back.

In the books
In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Sea Dragon Point is in the same location.
