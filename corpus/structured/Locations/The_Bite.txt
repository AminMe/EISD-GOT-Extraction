<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>The Bite</b> is a large bay located off the north-eastern coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It is an inlet of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shivering Sea","link":"Shivering Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shivering Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/Shivering_Sea" title="Shivering Sea">Shivering Sea</a>. It separates the southern coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a> from the northern coast of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>, with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Neck","link":"the Neck","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Neck]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/The_Neck" title="The Neck">the Neck</a> running along the western edge of the bay. The islands known as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Three Sisters","link":"the Three Sisters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Three Sisters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/The_Three_Sisters" title="The Three Sisters" class="mw-redirect">the Three Sisters</a> are located within the Bite.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Several northern settlements are on the coastland of the Bite. The city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Harbor","link":"White Harbor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Harbor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/White_Harbor" title="White Harbor">White Harbor</a> lies at the estuary of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Knife","link":"White Knife","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Knife]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/White_Knife" title="White Knife">White Knife</a> river on the northern edge of the Bite. The town <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsgate","link":"Ramsgate","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsgate]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/Ramsgate" title="Ramsgate">Ramsgate</a> lies at the estuary of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Broken Branch","link":"Broken Branch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Broken Branch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/Broken_Branch" title="Broken Branch">Broken Branch</a> river.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/moat-cailin\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Moat Cailin entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Fingers","link":"Fingers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fingers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/Fingers" title="Fingers" class="mw-redirect">Fingers</a> peninsulas project into the southern mouth of the bay. Beyond the bay lies the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shivering Sea","link":"Shivering Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shivering Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/Shivering_Sea" title="Shivering Sea">Shivering Sea</a>, which extends to the north of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Essos","link":"Essos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Essos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/Essos" title="Essos">Essos</a>, beyond the limits of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the known world","link":"the known world","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the known world]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" href="/wiki/The_known_world" title="The known world" class="mw-redirect">the known world</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/the-vale-of-arryn\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, The Vale of Arryn entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16604008956cca8e369e34" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
