<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130154720556cca89f94179" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Arbor is the biggest shipyard in southern Westeros. Its fleet is one of the largest in the realm, behind only the King's Fleet and the Iron Fleet of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130154720556cca89f94179" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a>. The Arbor has a warm and pleasant climate, due to its position surrounded by water off Westeros's southern coast. This climate is ideal for viticulture, and the Arbor is home to vast vineyards. Locations on the Arbor include Starfish Harbor and Vinetown.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Samwell Tarly","link":"Samwell Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Samwell Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130154720556cca89f94179" href="/wiki/Samwell_Tarly" title="Samwell Tarly">Samwell Tarly</a> is sent to the Arbor as a page and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"cupbearer","link":"cupbearer","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[cupbearer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130154720556cca89f94179" href="/wiki/Cupbearer" title="Cupbearer">cupbearer</a> where Horas and Hobber Redwyne despised him and found many different ways to shame and humiliate him. If he pleased Lord Paxter, Samwell would have been betrothed to Lord Paxter's daughter, but he is ultimately sent home to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Horn Hill","link":"Horn Hill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Horn Hill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130154720556cca89f94179" href="/wiki/Horn_Hill" title="Horn Hill">Horn Hill</a>.
