<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><b>Ruby Ford</b> is a major crossing of the River <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Trident","link":"The Trident","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Trident|Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34735561256cca8a891f02" href="/wiki/The_Trident" title="The Trident">Trident</a>, located just east of the point where the Green, Blue, and Red Forks of the Trident converge into a single river, and the waters are thus slow enough to cross.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Armies moving north or south of the Trident river system (which separates both <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34735561256cca8a891f02" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34735561256cca8a891f02" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a> from the rest of Westeros) are basically faced with two choices for their route. The first choice is to travel along the lighter headwaters of the three forks of the Trident, though this does necessitate crossing the Green Fork at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Twins","link":"The Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34735561256cca8a891f02" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">The Twins</a> castle ruled by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Frey","link":"House Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34735561256cca8a891f02" href="/wiki/House_Frey" title="House Frey">House Frey</a>. The other option is to march east and use the Ruby Ford. The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsroad","link":"Kingsroad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsroad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34735561256cca8a891f02" href="/wiki/Kingsroad" title="Kingsroad">Kingsroad</a> itself, extending between <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34735561256cca8a891f02" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34735561256cca8a891f02" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>, crosses the Trident at the Ruby Ford. East of the Ruby Ford the combined size of the single river, now fed by all three tributaries, makes it too wide and deep to cross. This makes the Ruby Ford of vital strategic importance.
