
Blazewater Bay is a region off the north-western coast of the continent of Westeros. It is part of the Sunset Sea. The bay is located between Cape Kraken and the south coast of the Rills. In the books
In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Blazewater Bay forms the mouth of the Saltspear, a long, narrow bay between the Barrowlands and the Neck, which in turn is fed by the Fever River.
