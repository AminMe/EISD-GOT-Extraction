 The Mummer's Ford is a town in the Riverlands, the region controlled by House Tully of Riverrun. 

The Battle at the Mummer's Ford was fought here in the opening days of the War of the Five Kings. Ser Gregor Clegane and his men battled the host sent by Eddard Stark under the leadership of Lord Beric Dondarrion. In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Mummer's Ford is so-named because it is located on a ford across the Red Fork of the Trident. See also (spoilers from the books) References
