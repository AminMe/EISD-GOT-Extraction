<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> In the books </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213518318856cca8f3db643" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Harlaw is the second largest island after <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Wyk","link":"Great Wyk","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Wyk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213518318856cca8f3db643" href="/wiki/Great_Wyk" title="Great Wyk">Great Wyk</a>, and is the richest and most populous island.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Harlaw","link":"House Harlaw","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Harlaw]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213518318856cca8f3db643" href="/wiki/House_Harlaw" title="House Harlaw">House Harlaw</a> are the overlords of much if not most of the island, and they are one of the most powerful vassals of the Greyjoys.  <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon Greyjoy","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213518318856cca8f3db643" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon Greyjoy</a>'s wife <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Alannys","link":"Alannys Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Alannys Greyjoy|Alannys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213518318856cca8f3db643" href="/wiki/Alannys_Greyjoy" title="Alannys Greyjoy">Alannys</a> was also born into House Harlaw.  The current head of House is Rodrik Harlaw, maternal uncle to Theon and Asha (called <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yara","link":"Yara Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yara Greyjoy|Yara]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-213518318856cca8f3db643" href="/wiki/Yara_Greyjoy" title="Yara Greyjoy">Yara</a> in the TV series).  Rodrik and House Harlaw are major supporters of Asha.
</p>
