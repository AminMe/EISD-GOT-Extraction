<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-122333589356cca8d122737" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Black Cliffs are a series of cliffs that are situated on the northwest coast of Slaver's Bay, south of the Painted Mountains. They lie between <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tolos","link":"Tolos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tolos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-122333589356cca8d122737" href="/wiki/Tolos" title="Tolos">Tolos</a> and the ruined city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bhorash","link":"Bhorash","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bhorash]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-122333589356cca8d122737" href="/wiki/Bhorash" title="Bhorash">Bhorash</a>, south of the Demon Road.
</p>
