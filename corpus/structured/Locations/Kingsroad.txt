<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The royal party travels north from King's Landing to Winterfell, spending more than a month on the road<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Winter is Coming (episode)|Winter is Coming]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />. They travel back the same way, stopping at the Crossroads Inn. At the same time, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Benjen Stark","link":"Benjen Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Benjen Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Benjen_Stark" title="Benjen Stark">Benjen Stark</a> travel north from Winterfell to Castle Black, where the road terminates<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsroad]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a> leads a group of recruits for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> north along the Kingsroad, including <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hot Pie","link":"Hot Pie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hot Pie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Hot_Pie" title="Hot Pie">Hot Pie</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lommy Greenhands","link":"Lommy Greenhands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lommy Greenhands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Lommy_Greenhands" title="Lommy Greenhands" class="mw-redirect">Lommy Greenhands</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a>. Also traveling with them is a disguised <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> (now traveling back north up the Kingsroad), whom Yoren intends to drop off at the safety of Winterfell on their way to the Wall. However, while they are still in the Riverlands south of the Trident River, they are accosted by several <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Goldcloaks","link":"City Watch","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[City Watch|Goldcloaks]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/City_Watch" title="City Watch">Goldcloaks</a> under the command of the Lannisters, who are looking for Gendry. Yoren manages to scare them off but they promise to return with more men.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Night Lands]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> The Goldcloaks follow through on their threat and return with House Lannister soldiers led by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Amory Lorch","link":"Amory Lorch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Amory Lorch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Amory_Lorch" title="Amory Lorch">Amory Lorch</a>. They attack during the night when the Night's Watch recruits have camped just off the Kingsroad. Yoren and Lommy are killed in the ensuing skirmish, while Arya, Gendry, and the rest are taken as prisoners to the nearby castle of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[What is Dead May Never Die]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a>'s army makes a swift, forced march from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a> down the west side of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gods Eye","link":"Gods Eye","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gods Eye]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Gods_Eye" title="Gods Eye">Gods Eye</a> lake and down the Blackwater River. In the northern <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingswood","link":"Kingswood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingswood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Kingswood" title="Kingswood">Kingswood</a>, his army unites with the army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> coming up the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Roseroad","link":"Roseroad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Roseroad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Roseroad" title="Roseroad">Roseroad</a>, and the two united armies continue north along the brief stretch of the Kingsroad between the point where it joins the Roseroad and King's Landing. When they arrive at the city, they decisively defeat the forces of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Blackwater (episode)|Blackwater]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Kingsroad extends for over two thousand miles from Storm's End to the Wall. Its quality varies from place to place, but it greatly speeds travel in the northern half of the continent. The road was built over two centuries ago during the reign of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaehaerys I Targaryen","link":"Jaehaerys I Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaehaerys I Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75684848956cca8824d4f9" href="/wiki/Jaehaerys_I_Targaryen" title="Jaehaerys I Targaryen">Jaehaerys I Targaryen</a>, using existing roads and pathways where possible.
</p>
