<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Littlefinger's brothel</b> is an upscale brothel located in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, expensively furnished and with high quality <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"prostitutes","link":"Prostitution","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Prostitution|prostitutes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Prostitution" title="Prostitution">prostitutes</a>, whose target clientele are wealthy merchants and members of the upper nobility.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">It is one of several brothels owned by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Petyr \"Littlefinger\" Baelish","link":"Petyr Baelish","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Petyr Baelish|Petyr \"Littlefinger\" Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Petyr_Baelish" title="Petyr Baelish">Petyr "Littlefinger" Baelish</a>, which he bought at some point during his career in the city as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Master of Coin","link":"Master of Coin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Master of Coin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Master_of_Coin" title="Master of Coin">Master of Coin</a>. The brothel therefore displays Littlefinger's personal mockingbird <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sigil","link":"Heraldry","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Heraldry|sigil]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Heraldry" title="Heraldry">sigil</a> at the front entrance. Baelish has been known to use the brothel and its employees to spy on the secrets of powerful and influential customers in their intimate moments.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Because it is only one of several business ventures that Baelish owns in the city itself, he often delegates the day to day running of the brothel to a trusted employee (usually the lead prostitute). When Littlefinger isn't in the city, and particularly after he returned to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Vale","link":"The Vale of Arryn","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Vale of Arryn|the Vale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/The_Vale_of_Arryn" title="The Vale of Arryn">the Vale</a>, this madam/majordomo/<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"procurer","link":"Wikipedia:Procuring","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wikipedia:Procuring|procurer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Procuring" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Procuring">procurer</a> steps up to actively run all aspects of the brothel (and sends a portion of the profits to Baelish as the legal owner).
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The brothel itself strives to be a "refined" establishment. It is richly furnished with fine tapestries, curtains, and artwork. Harpers serenade the common rooms with background music, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fine food and wine","link":"Food and Drink","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Food and Drink|fine food and wine]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Food_and_Drink" title="Food and Drink">fine food and wine</a> is also provided. Littlefinger has his brothel cater to every taste, staffing both male and female prostitutes, either of which will provide pleasure to men or women, if the customer is so inclined.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to Varys, it rumored that on rare occasion, Littlefinger has been known to discretely accommodate more unusual "inclinations" as a private favor to powerful clients who can pay a very high price (ranging from providing amputees, to providing little boys or little girls). Even if the rumors are true, this apparently didn't involve the main brothel itself, where there were too many prying eyes, but was a side business that Littlefinger would pursue on a case by case basis. For example, Littlefinger gave Ros to King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a> as a punishment, so Joffrey could thrill-kill her using his crossbow, but he sent her to Joffrey's private castle quarters instead of letting him kill her in the actual brothel.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> In the books </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the upscale brothel where Barra's mother works is actually owned and run by a Chataya, a tall and elegant black-skinned woman from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Summer Islands","link":"Summer Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Summer Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Summer_Islands" title="Summer Islands">Summer Islands</a>. Summer Islanders feel no shame about sexuality, but see it as a gift that the gods gave humanity, and even many upper-class nobles in the Summer Islands engage in ritual prostitution. Therefore Chataya's own daughter Alayaya voluntarily works as a prostitute in the brothel, who is noted as being very beautiful and good-natured. Among the clients of the brothel are <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oberyn Martell","link":"Oberyn Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oberyn Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Oberyn_Martell" title="Oberyn Martell">Oberyn Martell</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn of the Blackwater","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn of the Blackwater|Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Bronn_of_the_Blackwater" title="Bronn of the Blackwater" class="mw-redirect">Bronn</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Anguy","link":"Anguy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Anguy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Anguy" title="Anguy">Anguy</a>, and an unnamed <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Hand","link":"King's Hand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Hand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/King's_Hand" title="King's Hand" class="mw-redirect">King's Hand</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Tyrion visits Chataya's brothel often, presumably to have sex with Alayaya, but actually to cover his visits at the mance where he placed Shae before arranging for her to work at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a>. A secret passage leads from Alayaya's room to a stable, from where Tyrion rides in disguise to the mance. The trick works well, but it costs Alayaya dearly: she is abducted and beaten at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister|Cersei]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei</a>'s command, who mistakes her for Tyrion's real mistress, to make sure no harm is caused to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tommen","link":"Tommen Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tommen Baratheon|Tommen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Tommen_Baratheon" title="Tommen Baratheon">Tommen</a> (in the show she is replaced with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ros","link":"Ros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Ros" title="Ros">Ros</a>). After the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a> Alayaya is flogged at Tywin's command and sent back to the brothel. Tyrion feels guilty and sends Alayaya expensive jewels as a compensation.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Littlefinger is stated to own multiple brothels in King's Landing (along with his many other business ventures throughout the Seven Kingdoms), but he does not own Chataya's. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> arrives in King's Landing, Littlefinger does hide her in one of the other brothels which he does own - but it is a common, modest brothel. It does not appear again in the narrative afterwards.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Littlefinger does <i>visit</i> Chataya's, when he accompanies <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> there to investigate King Robert's bastards. While there he does mention that he owns several other brothels, and that he is <i>considering</i> making an offer to buy Chataya's. He gives the explanation he made in the TV series that "Brothels are a much sounder investment than ships, I've found. Whores seldom sink..." though in the book he goes on to add: "...and when they are boarded by pirates, why, the pirates pay good coin like everyone else."
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The TV series condensed the two together, omitting Chataya and Alayaya, and simply having Littlefinger himself own the upscale and very expensive brothel visited by King Robert. It then moved the one instance of Catelyn hiding in Littlefinger's common brothel, to hiding her in the same upper-class brothel that Robert's bastard daughter was in.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Several other scenes which happened at "Littlefinger's brothel" in the TV series actually happened at "Chataya's brothel" in the books - reinforcing the conception that the TV version <i>is</i> the same brothel (geographically), but ownership in the TV-version was simply changed to Littlefinger:
</p>
<ul data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><li>King Robert's bastard daughter Barra was born at Chataya's brothel, as Eddard Stark discovers. Her mother appeared in the book but wasn't given a name, but the TV series named her "Mhaegen". Barra was also later killed there; in the book her mother was killed in the book when she tried to defend her baby, but the TV series left Mhaegen's fate ambiguous.
</li><li>Bronn celebrated his new knighthood - which he gained after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a> - and increased pay by visiting Chataya's brothel, where he had sex with Alayaya and Marei simultaneously. The TV series similarly had Bronn celebrate his new knighthood by going to Littlefinger's brothel, but he was with the prostitute Mirelle.
</li><li>Oberyn Martell <i>mentions</i> to Tyrion when he arrives at King's Landing that he and Ellaria are interested in finding a good brothel (and this scene includes the detail that both of them are bisexual, and that Oberyn is looking for a woman to share with Ellaria). Tyrion recommends Chataya's as a good establishment: Oberyn and Ellaria apparently do visit it, but it happens "off screen". The TV version slightly modified this to actually show Oberyn and Ellaria at Littlefinger's brothel. In the book, Oberyn arrived with the rest of the Dornish embassy, and on seeing him Tyrion is shocked because he was expecting Oberyn's older and calmer brother Doran. The TV series built up the tension a little bit by having Oberyn ride into the city ahead of the rest of his followers, to explain why the viewpoint character Tyrion has to go <i>find</i> him in Littlefinger's brothel.
</li></ul>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Chataya's brothel is located on the Street of Silk, a major red light district in King's Landing. The road is located on the northwest side of Rhaenys's Hill - the completely opposite side of the hill from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Flea Bottom","link":"Flea Bottom","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Flea Bottom]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-144140455756cca86a5a050" href="/wiki/Flea_Bottom" title="Flea Bottom">Flea Bottom</a>, and fairly far away from the Red Keep, which is in the eastern corner of the city. In contrast, Littlefinger's modest brothel where he hid Catelyn was located on the exact opposite side of the city, on the southeast side bordering the Blackwater river, on the lower slopes of Aegon's High Hill where the Red Keep is located. Given that in all other scenes all other indications seem to present that Littlefinger's brothel in the TV version <i>is</i> Chataya's brothel from the books, it is reasonable to conclude that "Littlefinger's brothel" in the TV version is also located on the Street of Silk, northwest of Rhaenys's Hill.
</p>
