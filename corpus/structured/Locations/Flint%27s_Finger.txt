<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-106738577656cca8eb9dbd2" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Flint's Finger is the stronghold of one of the two cadet branches of Clan Flint, also known as the First Flints. The other branch is based at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Widow's Watch","link":"Widow's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Widow's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-106738577656cca8eb9dbd2" href="/wiki/Widow's_Watch" title="Widow's Watch">Widow's Watch</a> on the opposite coast of the North, and is arguably more powerful. Flint's Finger is noted for being built over towering cliffs, which the young <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon Greyjoy","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-106738577656cca8eb9dbd2" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon Greyjoy</a> once scaled during a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"raid","link":"Raiding","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raiding|raid]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-106738577656cca8eb9dbd2" href="/wiki/Raiding" title="Raiding">raid</a>.
</p>
