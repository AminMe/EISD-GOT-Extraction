</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Summerhall</b> is a ruined castle in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormlands","link":"Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Stormlands" title="Stormlands" class="mw-redirect">Stormlands</a>, near the borders of both <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a>. It was formerly the summer palace of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a> and the third and newest of the strongholds of that house, after <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dragonstone","link":"Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Dragonstone" title="Dragonstone" class="mw-redirect">Dragonstone</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"viewers guide\">[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/summerhall\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Summerhall entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Summerhall was destroyed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fire","link":"Tragedy at Summerhall","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tragedy at Summerhall|fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Tragedy_at_Summerhall" title="Tragedy at Summerhall">fire</a> approximately forty years before the events of the series begin. The fire killed both <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon V Targaryen","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Aegon V Targaryen</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his heir","link":"Duncan Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan Targaryen|his heir]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Duncan_Targaryen" title="Duncan Targaryen">his heir</a>, and his lifelong friend <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ser Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall|Ser Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Ser Duncan the Tall</a>. It is rumored to have been caused by an attempt to hatch <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragon","link":"dragon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon" class="mw-redirect">dragon</a> eggs.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"viewers guide\"\/>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> killed his first man during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Summerhall","link":"Battle of Summerhall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Summerhall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Summerhall" title="Battle of Summerhall">Battle of Summerhall</a>, a soldier of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tarly","link":"House Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/House_Tarly" title="House Tarly">House Tarly</a>, during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Lord Snow]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Summerhall has special significance for several members of House Targaryen. King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daeron II","link":"Daeron II Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daeron II Targaryen|Daeron II]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Daeron_II_Targaryen" title="Daeron II Targaryen">Daeron II</a> built it as a place of peace and relaxation away from court. One of his sons, Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maekar","link":"Maekar Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maekar Targaryen|Maekar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Maekar_Targaryen" title="Maekar Targaryen">Maekar</a>, adopted it as his main household. Along with King Aegon V, and his heir Prince Duncan the Small many members of the Targaryen household were killed during the fire. The deaths resulted in King Jaehaerys II taking the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a>. However, the fire was said to have traumatised King Jaehaerys, and he died young only two years later, leaving his son <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys II Targaryen","link":"Aerys II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Aerys_II_Targaryen" title="Aerys II Targaryen">Aerys II Targaryen</a> as King. Summerhall was also the place, where a midget woods witch, who had prophesied  that "a promised prince" would be born of Aerys and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaella Targaryen","link":"Rhaella Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaella Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Rhaella_Targaryen" title="Rhaella Targaryen">Rhaella Targaryen</a>. Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a> was likely killed in the fire as well, since Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gerold Hightower","link":"Gerold Hightower","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gerold Hightower]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Gerold_Hightower" title="Gerold Hightower">Gerold Hightower</a> was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> the following year when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a> joined.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Aerys II's own son, Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegar Targaryen","link":"Rhaegar Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegar Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91111384256cca899e4fa4" href="/wiki/Rhaegar_Targaryen" title="Rhaegar Targaryen">Rhaegar Targaryen</a>, was fascinated by the ruins of Summerhall. He was born on the day that the fire took place and visited it several times.
</p>
