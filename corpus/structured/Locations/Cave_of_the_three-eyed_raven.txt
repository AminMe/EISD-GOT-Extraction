<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->The <b>cave of the three-eyed raven</b> is a place located <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Beyond the Wall","link":"Beyond the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beyond the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Beyond_the_Wall" title="Beyond the Wall">Beyond the Wall</a>. It serves as the abode of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"greenseer","link":"greenseer","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[greenseer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Greenseer" title="Greenseer">greenseer</a> known as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"three-eyed raven","link":"three-eyed raven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[three-eyed raven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Three-eyed_raven" title="Three-eyed raven">three-eyed raven</a> and a group of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"children of the forest","link":"Children of the Forest","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Children of the Forest|children of the forest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Children_of_the_Forest" title="Children of the Forest">children of the forest</a> who protect it and him.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Above the cave lies an ancient and massive <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"weirwood","link":"weirwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[weirwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Weirwood" title="Weirwood">weirwood</a> tree with a face carved on its trunk. The weirwood's roots dig deep into the cave and have merged with the body of the three-eyed raven. The entrance to the cave is protected by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"magic","link":"magic","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[magic]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Magic" title="Magic">magic</a> of the Children of the Forest and prevents <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wights","link":"wights","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wights]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Wights" title="Wights">wights</a> from entering it, destroying the undead creatures if they attempt to enter.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:CaveOfTheThreeEyedRavenApproximateLocation.png|thumb|300px|''Loosely'' approximate location of the cave of the three-eyed raven - due north from Craster's Keep, and roughly east from the Fist of the First Men.]]","title":"CaveOfTheThreeEyedRavenApproximateLocation.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"CaveOfTheThreeEyedRavenApproximateLocation","caption":"''Loosely'' approximate location of the cave of the three-eyed raven - due north from Craster's Keep, and roughly east from the Fist of the First Men.","width":300,"captionParsed":"<i>Loosely<\/i> approximate location of the cave of the three-eyed raven - due north from Craster's Keep, and roughly east from the Fist of the First Men."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" alt="" src="http://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/f/f3/CaveOfTheThreeEyedRavenApproximateLocation.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/300?cb=20151205193012" width="300" height="162" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="CaveOfTheThreeEyedRavenApproximateLocation.png" data-image-key="CaveOfTheThreeEyedRavenApproximateLocation.png"  type="image" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the cave of the three-eyed crow appears in the fifth book, though its location is not specified. <i>The Lands of Ice and Fire</i> map collection places it within the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Haunted Forest","link":"Haunted Forest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Haunted Forest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Haunted_Forest" title="Haunted Forest">Haunted Forest</a>, east of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Fist of the First Men","link":"Fist of the First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fist of the First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Fist_of_the_First_Men" title="Fist of the First Men">Fist of the First Men</a> and somewhat near the headwaters of the Antler River. <i>Very</i> loosely, if one draws a line straight east from the Fist of the First Men, and another line straight north from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's Keep","link":"Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Craster's_Keep" title="Craster's Keep">Craster's Keep</a>, the cave is in the general area where the two lines intersect.  Logically, it isn't as drastically far north as the distant northern edge of the map or the mountain valley of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Thenns","link":"Thenns","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thenns]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-99684651256cca876ceed7" href="/wiki/Thenns" title="Thenns">Thenns</a> - the crippled Bran Stark and his companions could not have traveled such a vast distance on foot.  
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The cave is actually a great cavern under a wooded hill, deep within a system of caves. The three-eyed crow is seated on a weirwood throne in the great cavern, by an abyss, attended to by the remaining Children of the Forest.
</p>
