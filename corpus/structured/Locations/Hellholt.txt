<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-72807785256cca8cac7597" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels Hellholt is the seat of House Uller. It is located in the Dornish desert, and is difficult to reach overland. The castle is named after an unpleasant event in which unwelcome visitors to the castle were locked up and burned to death.
</p>
