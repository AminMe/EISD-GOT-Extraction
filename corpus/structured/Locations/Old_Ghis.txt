</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Old Ghis</b> is a ruined city, located in the region of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ghiscar","link":"Ghiscar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ghiscar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/Ghiscar" title="Ghiscar">Ghiscar</a> on the southern coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Essos","link":"Essos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Essos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/Essos" title="Essos">Essos</a>. It was once the ancient capital of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ghiscari Empire","link":"Ghiscari Empire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ghiscari Empire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/Ghiscari_Empire" title="Ghiscari Empire">Ghiscari Empire</a>, but was destroyed by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian Freehold","link":"Valyrian Freehold","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian Freehold]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/Valyrian_Freehold" title="Valyrian Freehold">Valyrian Freehold</a> with the use of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"dragons","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"The Complete Guide to Westeros: Valyria and the Dragons\" featurette<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The city is located south of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Slaver's Bay","link":"Slaver's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slaver's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/Slaver's_Bay" title="Slaver's Bay">Slaver's Bay</a>, on the eastern coastline of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gulf of Grief","link":"Gulf of Grief","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gulf of Grief]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/Gulf_of_Grief" title="Gulf of Grief">Gulf of Grief</a>. The city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"New Ghis","link":"New Ghis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[New Ghis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/New_Ghis" title="New Ghis">New Ghis</a>, its successor, is located on an island not far to the south.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/astapor\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Astapor entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Old Ghis led five great wars against Valyria before being destroyed once and for all. Frustrated by the city's constant uprisings, the Valyrians pulled down the city's walls, burned the buildings to ashes and cinders and poisoned the surrounding lands with salt, sulphur and skulls to prevent it from rising again.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The city and the Ghiscari Empire were founded by Grazdan the Great, a mighty leader. Its symbol, and that of the Empire it led, was that of a harpy clutching a thunderbolt.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Inspiration</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">​Old Ghis has many similiarities with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Carthage","link":"wikipedia:Carthage","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wikipedia:Carthage|Carthage]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carthage" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Carthage">Carthage</a>, the great rival of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rome","link":"wikipedia:Roman_Republic","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wikipedia:Roman_Republic|Rome]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-139416002256cca8a34a4c4" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Roman_Republic" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:Roman Republic">Rome</a> during its republican phase. The two empires engaged in three bloody wars. At the end of the last, the Romans destroyed the city and according to legend sowed the soil with salt to prevent the city rising again.
</p>
