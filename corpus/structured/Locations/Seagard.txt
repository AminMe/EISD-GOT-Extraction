<p data-rte-fromparser="true">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, Seagard was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"attacked","link":"Battle of Seagard","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Seagard|attacked]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Seagard" title="Battle of Seagard">attacked</a> by an <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> fleet led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Greyjoy","link":"Rodrik Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Greyjoy" title="Rodrik Greyjoy">Rodrik Greyjoy</a>, Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy|Balon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon</a>'s eldest son and heir. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Seagard is the largest and indeed only port of note on the west coast of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>, consisting of a castle, town and port. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Mallister","link":"House Mallister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Mallister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/House_Mallister" title="House Mallister">House Mallister</a> maintains a small fleet of half a dozen war <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"longships","link":"ships","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ships|longships]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Ships" title="Ships">longships</a> and two war galleys at Seagard.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Mallister is a loyal vassal of House Tully, and fought underneath their banner during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jason Mallister","link":"Jason Mallister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jason Mallister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Jason_Mallister" title="Jason Mallister">Jason Mallister</a> is noted as one of the most stalwart, reliable and honorable of the river lords. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a> declared for King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84496280956cca8adb963b" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>, Lord Jason also supported that move and threw his loyalty behind Robb.
</p>
