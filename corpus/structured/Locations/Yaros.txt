<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><b>Yaros</b> is a small island in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Slaver's Bay","link":"Slaver's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slaver's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204155800056cca93e370a6" href="/wiki/Slaver's_Bay" title="Slaver's Bay">Slaver's Bay</a>, located just off the coast southwest from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yunkai","link":"Yunkai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yunkai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204155800056cca93e370a6" href="/wiki/Yunkai" title="Yunkai">Yunkai</a>, though it is apparently the largest island in Slaver's Bay itself (which is bounded to the west by the large Isle of Cedars). Yaros lies directly between Yunkai and Astapor.
</p>
