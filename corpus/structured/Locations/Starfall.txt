<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209612415456cca88f20f1f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Starfall is the seat of House Dayne. It is located at the mouth of the River <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Torentine","link":"Torentine","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Torentine]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209612415456cca88f20f1f" href="/wiki/Torentine" title="Torentine">Torentine</a>, downriver from Blackmont and the High Hermitage, which is held by a lesser branch of the Daynes.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Starfall is so-called because of a meteor, referred to as a "fallen star", which fell out of the sky nearby, almost two thousand years ago. The Daynes forged a sword out of the meteorite ore, called "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dawn","link":"Dawn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dawn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209612415456cca88f20f1f" href="/wiki/Dawn" title="Dawn">Dawn</a>", which is as resilient and formidable as a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian steel","link":"Valyrian steel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian steel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209612415456cca88f20f1f" href="/wiki/Valyrian_steel" title="Valyrian steel">Valyrian steel</a> weapon in combat.
</p>
