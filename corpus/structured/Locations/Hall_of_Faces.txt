The Hall of Faces houses skinned faces of the dead, and is found in a great hall within the House of Black and White. The Faceless Men use the combination of magic spells and faces of the dead to create their different camouflages. Cleansed faces of corpses are skinned and hung to dry within the Hall of Faces. Structure 

The Hall of Faces lies within a large, secret chamber. It is created of stone, with large pillars that house the faces of the dead. History 

 Season 5 

Upon passing the game of faces, Jaqen H'ghar brings Arya to the Hall of Faces.
