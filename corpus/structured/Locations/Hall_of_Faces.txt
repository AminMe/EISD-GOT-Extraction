</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The <b>"Hall of Faces"</b> houses skinned faces of the dead, and is found in a great hall within the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of Black and White","link":"House of Black and White","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of Black and White]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34800807556cca8521f9e4" href="/wiki/House_of_Black_and_White" title="House of Black and White">House of Black and White</a>. Cleansed faces of corpses are skinned and hung to dry within the Hall of Faces.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Structure</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Hall of Faces lies within a large, secret chamber. It is created of stone, with large pillars that house the faces of the dead.
</p>
