<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176831721256cca91944979" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Valysar is a vassal town of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Volantis","link":"Volantis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Volantis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176831721256cca91944979" href="/wiki/Volantis" title="Volantis">Volantis</a>. As it is located on the western side of the river, it does not have any fear of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176831721256cca91944979" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a> or a need to pay them tribute, unlike its sister-city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Selhorys","link":"Selhorys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Selhorys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176831721256cca91944979" href="/wiki/Selhorys" title="Selhorys">Selhorys</a> to the north. Valysar is a middling-sized town by the standards of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Cities","link":"Free Cities","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Cities]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176831721256cca91944979" href="/wiki/Free_Cities" title="Free Cities">Free Cities</a>, but would be a small city by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176831721256cca91944979" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westerosi</a> standards.
</p>
