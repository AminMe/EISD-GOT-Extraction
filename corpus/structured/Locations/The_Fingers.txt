<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-173074595156cca8922f5d5" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Fingers are lonely, cold and windswept. The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Andals","link":"Andals","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Andals]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-173074595156cca8922f5d5" href="/wiki/Andals" title="Andals">Andals</a> landed on the Fingers during their invasion of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-173074595156cca8922f5d5" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a> and weathered stones bearing the symbol of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Faith of the Seven","link":"Faith of the Seven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Faith of the Seven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-173074595156cca8922f5d5" href="/wiki/Faith_of_the_Seven" title="Faith of the Seven">Faith of the Seven</a> can be found dotted along the shore in some areas.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Baelish owns a small, unassuming tower on the smallest of the Fingers without name or <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"maester","link":"maester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-173074595156cca8922f5d5" href="/wiki/Maester" title="Maester" class="mw-redirect">maester</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baelish (Histories & Lore)","link":"House Baelish (Histories & Lore)","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baelish (Histories & Lore)]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-173074595156cca8922f5d5" href="/wiki/House_Baelish_(Histories_&_Lore)" title="House Baelish (Histories &amp; Lore)">House Baelish (Histories &amp; Lore)</a>", Baelish says that the Fingers consist of four peninsulas, but the <i>World of Ice and Fire</i> sourcebook (2014) confirms that there are actually <i>five</i> fingers (the title "Lord of the Five Fingers" was used by the rulers of an Andal proto-kingdom). "Littlefinger" Baelish is from the smallest of these, the northernmost, nearest to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Three Sisters","link":"Three Sisters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Three Sisters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-173074595156cca8922f5d5" href="/wiki/Three_Sisters" title="Three Sisters">Three Sisters</a> islands. The mistake is easy to make: the peninsula is so "little" that it isn't clear just by looking at a map if it is actually considered a "peninsula" or just a variation of the shape of the coastline. It also was never stated before the <i>World</i> book exactly how many peninsulas make up "the Fingers".
</p>
