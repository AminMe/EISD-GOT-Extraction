<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The chamber is where <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> holds meetings with his court, including <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maester Cressen","link":"Maester Cressen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maester Cressen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Maester_Cressen" title="Maester Cressen" class="mw-redirect">Maester Cressen</a> and the Red Priestess <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Melisandre","link":"Melisandre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Melisandre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Melisandre" title="Melisandre">Melisandre</a>. After receiving <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a>'s message concerning the true heritage of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King Robert","link":"King Robert","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King Robert]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/King_Robert" title="King Robert" class="mw-redirect">King Robert</a>'s children, Stannis dictates a letter to his scribe in the Chamber of the Painted Table to be sent to lords throughout the land revealing this information and therefore establishing his claim to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a> as the rightful heir. Maester Cressen tries to poison Melisandre in the chamber to remove her influence on Stannis, only to die from the poison himself while Melisandre is unharmed.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The North Remembers]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Melisandre seduces Stannis on the table, which leads to the birth of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shadow","link":"Shadow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shadow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Shadow" title="Shadow">Shadow</a> she uses to assassinate Stannis' brother and rival <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Night Lands]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->After his defeat at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>, Stannis returns to Dragonstone and confronts Melisandre in the Chamber of the Painted Table about the validity of her predictions. He begins to strangle her in fury but relents when she reminds him of the spell they used to kill Renly. He experiences remorse of murdering his brother. Melisandre warns him that he will commit worse betrayals before their long war is over but insists that he must fight on. She shows him a vision in the flames that awes him and restores his faith in her.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Valar Morghulis]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h2>In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Chamber of the Painted Table is located at the top of the central keep of Dragonstone, called the Stone Drum. It has four tall windows facing each point of the compass. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon the Conqueror","link":"Aegon the Conqueror","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon the Conqueror]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Aegon_the_Conqueror" title="Aegon the Conqueror" class="mw-redirect">Aegon the Conqueror</a> planned his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"invasion","link":"War of Conquest","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of Conquest|invasion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/War_of_Conquest" title="War of Conquest">invasion</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-142532970956cca8a419562" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a> from the chamber. The Painted Table itself is more than fifty feet long: roughly twenty-five feet wide at its widest point and four feet at its thinnest. At the precise location of Dragonstone is a raised seat that allows the occupant to view the entire map. The series omits this raised seat, with Stannis sitting at the head of the table near Dorne and the Reach (the Wall can be glimpsed at the opposite end of the table from where Stannis sits).
</p>
