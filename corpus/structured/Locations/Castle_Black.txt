</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Castle Black is one of only three manned castles left on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-59104652856cca85162d2f" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eastwatch-by-the-Sea","link":"Eastwatch-by-the-Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eastwatch-by-the-Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-59104652856cca85162d2f" href="/wiki/Eastwatch-by-the-Sea" title="Eastwatch-by-the-Sea">Eastwatch-by-the-Sea</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Shadow Tower","link":"The Shadow Tower","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Shadow Tower]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-59104652856cca85162d2f" href="/wiki/The_Shadow_Tower" title="The Shadow Tower">The Shadow Tower</a>. There are nineteen castles along the Wall, but as their numbers fell over the centuries the Night's Watch had to abandon sixteen of them. Castle Black is located between the unmanned castles of Queensgate and Oakensheild.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Castle Black has a sept, but no <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Godswood","link":"Godswood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Godswood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-59104652856cca85162d2f" href="/wiki/Godswood" title="Godswood">Godswood</a>.
