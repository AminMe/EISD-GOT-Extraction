<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-73737657456cca90ae00bf" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels Wendish Town is sacked at the outset of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-73737657456cca90ae00bf" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister|Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-73737657456cca90ae00bf" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">Lannister</a> raiders slip across the border and attack the town. The inhabitants retreat to the holdfast, but the raiders burn it. As the townsfolk flee, they are shot down with arrows.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The town is noted as the home of the popular song <i>On a Misty Morn</i>, one of the songs in the repertoire of the bard <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Marillion","link":"Marillion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Marillion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-73737657456cca90ae00bf" href="/wiki/Marillion" title="Marillion">Marillion</a>.
</p>
