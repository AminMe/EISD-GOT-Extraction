<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Baelish Keep</b> is a castle in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>. It is the seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baelish","link":"House Baelish","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/House_Baelish" title="House Baelish">House Baelish</a> a recently established House that once held fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Arryn","link":"House Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/House_Arryn" title="House Arryn">House Arryn</a> of the Vale before the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>.   House Baelish was founded by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Petyr Baelish","link":"Petyr Baelish","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Petyr Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Petyr_Baelish" title="Petyr Baelish">Petyr Baelish</a>'s great-grandfather, a common sellsword from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a>, and it was his son (Petyr's grandfather) who was awarded the small keep the most minor of lordships, just a few stony acres in the northernmost and smallest of the peninsulas known as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Fingers","link":"The Fingers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Fingers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/The_Fingers" title="The Fingers">The Fingers</a>.   
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">House Baelish is now one of the major houses, being the ruling house of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Riverlands","link":"The Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">The Riverlands</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ruling protectors","link":"Lord Protector","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Protector|ruling protectors]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Lord_Protector" title="Lord Protector">ruling protectors</a> of the Vale. The castle is located north-east of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Eyrie","link":"the Eyrie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Eyrie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/The_Eyrie" title="The Eyrie">the Eyrie</a>, on the smallest of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Fingers","link":"The Fingers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Fingers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/The_Fingers" title="The Fingers">The Fingers</a>' peninsula on the coast of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Narrow Sea","link":"Narrow Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Narrow Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a>. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The current head of House Baelish and Lord of Baelish Keep is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Petyr Baelish","link":"Petyr Baelish","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Petyr Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Petyr_Baelish" title="Petyr Baelish">Petyr Baelish</a>, great-grandson of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"a Braavosi sellsword who founded House Baelish","link":"Baelish (founder)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelish (founder)|a Braavosi sellsword who founded House Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Baelish_(founder)" title="Baelish (founder)">a Braavosi sellsword who founded House Baelish</a>, and grandson of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"a hedge knight who was rewarded the castle","link":"Baelish (hedge knight)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelish (hedge knight)|a hedge knight who was rewarded the castle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Baelish_(hedge_knight)" title="Baelish (hedge knight)">a hedge knight who was rewarded the castle</a>.  There is no <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"maester","link":"Maesters","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maesters|maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Maesters" title="Maesters">maester</a> appointed to the castle, and was never given an official name.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baelish Histories & Lore","link":"House Baelish (Histories & Lore)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baelish (Histories & Lore)|House Baelish Histories & Lore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/House_Baelish_(Histories_&_Lore)" title="House Baelish (Histories &amp; Lore)">House Baelish Histories &amp; Lore</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Fingers","link":"The Fingers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Fingers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/The_Fingers" title="The Fingers">The Fingers</a> and the castle are described as "four rocky peninsulas plagued by wind and rain and barren to all but the hardiest of weeds, the ancestral home of House Baelish... a small tower on the gull-stained rocks of the Fingers". It was revealed that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Petyr Baelish","link":"Petyr Baelish","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Petyr Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Petyr_Baelish" title="Petyr Baelish">Petyr Baelish</a> did not enjoy his childhood there, and described <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his fathers","link":"Baelish (father of Petyr)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelish (father of Petyr)|his fathers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Baelish_(father_of_Petyr)" title="Baelish (father of Petyr)">his fathers</a> efforts to allow him to be fostered at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a> as "heroic".
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the novels</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84229097656cca89df2883" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Sansa actually visits Baelish Keep briefly, as it is the first place she and Littlefinger land when they arrive on the coast of the Vale, after escaping from King's Landing.  She then proceeds from there to the Eyrie, but the TV series simply skipped this.  
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The castle is an old flint tower, located on the smallest of the Fingers. A staircase winds around the inside wall, and each floor has only one room. The ground level houses servants and dogs in the kitchen, the middle floor is a hall, and the third floor contains the bedchamber. It has arrowslits for defense but no windows. A shield depicting the Baelish titan's head hangs above the hearth. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The vicinity includes a flock of sheep, a village of a dozen families. There is no safe anchorage nearby for large ships.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In conversation with Sansa Stark, Petyr Baelish denounces the castle and gives it the derogatory names "Sheepshit" and "Drearfort".
</p>
