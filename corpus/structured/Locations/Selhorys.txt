<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-23192340156cca8ce49a77" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels Selhorys is a settlement under the rule of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Volantis","link":"Volantis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Volantis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-23192340156cca8ce49a77" href="/wiki/Volantis" title="Volantis">Volantis</a>. It is located at the confluence of the Rhoyne and the Selhoru. It pays tribute to passing <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-23192340156cca8ce49a77" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a> <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"khalasar","link":"khalasar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[khalasar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-23192340156cca8ce49a77" href="/wiki/Khalasar" title="Khalasar">khalasars</a></i>, which can easily reach the city from the western-most edge of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki sea","link":"Dothraki sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-23192340156cca8ce49a77" href="/wiki/Dothraki_sea" title="Dothraki sea" class="mw-redirect">Dothraki sea</a>, which is located not far to the east of the settlement. By the standards of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Cities","link":"Free Cities","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Cities]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-23192340156cca8ce49a77" href="/wiki/Free_Cities" title="Free Cities">Free Cities</a>, Selhorys is a middling-sized town, although by the standards of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-23192340156cca8ce49a77" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a> it would be a small city.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The events that transcur in Volantis in the TV series take place in Selhorys, most notably Tyrion Lannister being kidnapped by Jorah Mormont, who then does take him to Volantis.
</p>
