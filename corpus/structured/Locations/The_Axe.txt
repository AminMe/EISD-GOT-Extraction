<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11758859556cca8bc4528f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Axe is a mountainous headland, an extension of the hills and mountains to the south. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Norvos","link":"Norvos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Norvos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11758859556cca8bc4528f" href="/wiki/Norvos" title="Norvos">Norvos</a> maintains mines in this area. Ores are taken by land down to the Darkwash River and thence to Norvos and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qohor","link":"Qohor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qohor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11758859556cca8bc4528f" href="/wiki/Qohor" title="Qohor">Qohor</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to legend, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Andals","link":"Andals","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Andals]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11758859556cca8bc4528f" href="/wiki/Andals" title="Andals">Andals</a> inhabited the Axe before they migrated further west to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Andalos","link":"Andalos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Andalos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11758859556cca8bc4528f" href="/wiki/Andalos" title="Andalos">Andalos</a>.
