It is ruled by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Reed","link":"House Reed","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Reed]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-133705057856cca8793be99" href="/wiki/House_Reed" title="House Reed">House Reed</a>, a vassal house holding fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-133705057856cca8793be99" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-133705057856cca8793be99" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. It is the southernmost stronghold in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-133705057856cca8793be99" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>, and as such its rulers have a longstanding enmity with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Frey","link":"House Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-133705057856cca8793be99" href="/wiki/House_Frey" title="House Frey">House Frey</a>, who rule the area of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-133705057856cca8793be99" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a> on the southern side of the Neck.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-133705057856cca8793be99" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Greywater Watch is the seat of House Reed, one of the most loyal vassals of the Starks. No raven can reach Greywater Watch because it is said to be constantly moving.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Greywater Watch has not been mentioned in the series yet except for a map that mistakenly refers to the settlement as 'Greytower Watch.'
</p>
