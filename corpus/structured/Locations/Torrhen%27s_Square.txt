<h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a> </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a> is dispatched by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his father","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy|his father]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">his father</a> to raid the fishing villages along the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stony Shore","link":"Stony Shore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stony Shore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Stony_Shore" title="Stony Shore">Stony Shore</a>. After consulting with his first mate, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dagmer","link":"Dagmer","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dagmer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Dagmer" title="Dagmer">Dagmer</a>, Theon decides to mount a daring <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"attack","link":"Raid on Torrhen's Square","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raid on Torrhen's Square|attack]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Raid_on_Torrhen's_Square" title="Raid on Torrhen's Square">attack</a> inland on Torrhen's Square. He muses that they will not be able to hold the castle, as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> will send troops to retake it rapidly. Sure enough, word of an attack on Torrhen's Square reaches Winterfell. Fearing it might be a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannister","link":"Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Lannister" title="Lannister" class="mw-redirect">Lannister</a> raid behind the lines, Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Cassel","link":"Rodrik Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Cassel" title="Rodrik Cassel">Rodrik Cassel</a> decides to muster 200 men and march to the relief of the castle. Maester <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Luwin","link":"Luwin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Luwin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Luwin" title="Luwin">Luwin</a> is dubious about sending so many troops given their shortage in manpower, but agrees with the decision.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=S2E05>\"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i>A Song of Ice and Fire</i> books, Torrhen's Square is the seat of House Tallhart and ruled by the Master of Torrhen's Square, Ser Helman Tallhart.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the novels, Torrhen's Square is slightly further away from Winterfell, possibly 150-200 miles, as compared to the 120 in the TV series. This may be further evidence of the TV version of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a> being slightly smaller than the book version, or an error in the distance given by the writers or by the characters.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Theon Greyjoy sends Dagmer to besiege Torrhen's Square as a diversion that will allow him to take <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. The ploy works, Ser Rodrik takes most of Winterfell's garrison and forces Dagmer and his troops to retreat back to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stony Shore","link":"Stony Shore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stony Shore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Stony_Shore" title="Stony Shore">Stony Shore</a>. Unfortunately, in the meantime Theon captures the undefended Winterfell. After Rodrik returns to Winterfell and besieges it, Dagmer and his men return to Torrhen's Square and easily take the keep. Currently Dagmer is still there.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sack of Winterfell","link":"sack of Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[sack of Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Sack_of_Winterfell" title="Sack of Winterfell">sack of Winterfell</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fall of Moat Cailin","link":"fall of Moat Cailin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[fall of Moat Cailin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Fall_of_Moat_Cailin" title="Fall of Moat Cailin">fall of Moat Cailin</a> and the liberation of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Deepwood Motte","link":"Deepwood Motte","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Deepwood Motte]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-61025660956cca8977e9a7" href="/wiki/Deepwood_Motte" title="Deepwood Motte">Deepwood Motte</a>, Torrhen's Square is the last and only settlement at the North that is still held by the ironborn.
</p>
