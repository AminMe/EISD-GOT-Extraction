<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51649029856cca91a0d8a3" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ar Noy was once a great city of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhoynar","link":"Rhoynar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhoynar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51649029856cca91a0d8a3" href="/wiki/Rhoynar" title="Rhoynar">Rhoynar</a> kingdom ruled by Nymeria. Like most of the other Rhoynar cities, it was destroyed by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"dragons","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51649029856cca91a0d8a3" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyria","link":"Valyria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51649029856cca91a0d8a3" href="/wiki/Valyria" title="Valyria" class="mw-redirect">Valyria</a> approximately one thousand years ago.
</p>
