<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Sow's Horn is a small settlement that serves as an seat to House Hogg, a family of landed knights. It consists of a small village dominated by a towerhouse with thick walls. House Hogg is sworn to House Hayford, which controls the surrounding lands. Due to its proximity to the Riverlands and the frontline between the factions of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>, Sow's Horn has seen its share of raiders and foragers. It was raided by foragers led by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Amory Lorch","link":"Amory Lorch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Amory Lorch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/Amory_Lorch" title="Amory Lorch">Amory Lorch</a>, sworn to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a>, despite its allegiance to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon of King's Landing","link":"House Baratheon of King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon of King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon_of_King's_Landing" title="House Baratheon of King's Landing">House Baratheon of King's Landing</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, Sow's Horn is located in the western Crownlands, near the border with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a> (it isn't quite clear if it is north or south of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gods Eye","link":"Gods Eye","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gods Eye]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/Gods_Eye" title="Gods Eye">Gods Eye</a> lake). The map that appeared on-screen in the TV series, however, inaccurately placed it north of King's Landing but south of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Antlers","link":"Antlers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Antlers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/Antlers" title="Antlers">Antlers</a>, along the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsroad","link":"Kingsroad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsroad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/Kingsroad" title="Kingsroad">Kingsroad</a>, which is in the core of the Crownlands (the same map also failed to mark <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-190649883056cca938a9911" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a>, the Crownlands' secondary port).
</p>
