<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->The <b>Blackwater Rush</b>, also called the <b>Blackwater</b> or <b>River Blackwater</b>, is a significant river of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It flows out of the hills and valleys of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westerlands","link":"Westerlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westerlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Westerlands" title="Westerlands" class="mw-redirect">Westerlands</a> and proceeds eastwards for several hundred miles before flowing into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackwater Bay","link":"Blackwater Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackwater Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Blackwater_Bay" title="Blackwater Bay">Blackwater Bay</a>. The city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> overlooks the mouth of the Blackwater. A tributary of the Blackwater feeds the lake of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gods Eye","link":"Gods Eye","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gods Eye]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Gods_Eye" title="Gods Eye">Gods Eye</a> to the north.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"Viewers Guide\">[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/kings-landing\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, King's Landing entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The town of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stoney Sept","link":"Stoney Sept","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stoney Sept]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Stoney_Sept" title="Stoney Sept">Stoney Sept</a> is located not far from its headwaters.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Blackwater forms the boundary between the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Crownlands","link":"Crownlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crownlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Crownlands" title="Crownlands" class="mw-redirect">Crownlands</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westerlands","link":"Westerlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westerlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Westerlands" title="Westerlands" class="mw-redirect">Westerlands</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Reach","link":"Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Reach" title="Reach" class="mw-redirect">Reach</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52086087056cca8bf3975e" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, the capital city of the Seven Kingdoms, is built on the mouth of the Blackwater where it enters Blackwater Bay.
</p>
