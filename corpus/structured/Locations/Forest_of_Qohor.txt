<h2>History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-105256514656cca8d66dd62" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The <i>khalasar</i> of Khal <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogo","link":"Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-105256514656cca8d66dd62" href="/wiki/Drogo" title="Drogo">Drogo</a> passes through the Forest of Qohor on its way from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pentos","link":"Pentos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pentos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-105256514656cca8d66dd62" href="/wiki/Pentos" title="Pentos">Pentos</a> to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vaes Dothrak","link":"Vaes Dothrak","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vaes Dothrak]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-105256514656cca8d66dd62" href="/wiki/Vaes_Dothrak" title="Vaes Dothrak">Vaes Dothrak</a>. Based on comments by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jorah Mormont","link":"Jorah Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jorah Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-105256514656cca8d66dd62" href="/wiki/Jorah_Mormont" title="Jorah Mormont">Jorah Mormont</a>, it is possible that the immensely tall forest of reeds that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-105256514656cca8d66dd62" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> orders the <i>khalasar</i> to halt in is part of the Forest of Qohor<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[[Lord Snow]]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-105256514656cca8d66dd62" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-105256514656cca8d66dd62" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Forest of Qohor is traversed by Drogo's <i>khalasar</i>, taking half a month to pass through its eaves. The trees are huge, with barks as big as city gates and possessing leaves that give off a golden hue in the sunlight. Lemurs, spotted tigers, and great elks are found within the forest.
</p>
