<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i>A Song of Ice and Fire</i> novels, Castamere is located near Tarbeck Hall, seat of House Tarbeck, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Crag","link":"The Crag","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Crag|the Crag]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47176060856cca8627c249" href="/wiki/The_Crag" title="The Crag">the Crag</a>, seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Westerling","link":"House Westerling","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Westerling]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47176060856cca8627c249" href="/wiki/House_Westerling" title="House Westerling">House Westerling</a>. It is rich in gold mines, which were captured by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greatjon Umber","link":"Greatjon Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greatjon Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47176060856cca8627c249" href="/wiki/Greatjon_Umber" title="Greatjon Umber" class="mw-redirect">Greatjon Umber</a> following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Oxcross","link":"Battle of Oxcross","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Oxcross]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47176060856cca8627c249" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Oxcross" title="Battle of Oxcross">Battle of Oxcross</a>. Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47176060856cca8627c249" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>, Castamere is awarded to House Spicer, the maternal family of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47176060856cca8627c249" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>'s Queen, Jeyne Westerling, for their part in the fall of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King in the North","link":"King in the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King in the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47176060856cca8627c249" href="/wiki/King_in_the_North" title="King in the North">King in the North</a>.
</p>
