<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158232230956cca90634619" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Shield Islands are named as follows:
</p>
<ul data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Greenshield, the easternmost of the islands, ruled by House Chester.
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Greyshield, the westernmost of the islands, ruled by House Grimm.
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Oakenshield, the northernmost of the islands, ruled by House Hewett.
</li><li data-rte-spaces-before="1">Southshield, the southernmost of the islands, ruled by House Serry.
</li></ul>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The islands are so-named as they used to serve as a shield against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158232230956cca90634619" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> attacks during the days of their independence. The islands were actually held by the ironborn for centuries until <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Gardener","link":"House Gardener","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Gardener]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158232230956cca90634619" href="/wiki/House_Gardener" title="House Gardener">House Gardener</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kings of the Reach","link":"Kings of the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kings of the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158232230956cca90634619" href="/wiki/Kings_of_the_Reach" title="Kings of the Reach" class="mw-redirect">Kings of the Reach</a>, liberated them almost two thousand years before the events of the series. The Gardeners fortified the islands so they could defend themselves from further attack.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The islands are equipped with beacon fires. At the first sign of longships, the fires are lit, resulting in a relay of warnings being sent across all four islands.
</p>
