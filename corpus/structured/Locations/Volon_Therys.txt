<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-7561995356cca90950b26" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Volon Therys is a vassal of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free City","link":"Free City","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free City]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-7561995356cca90950b26" href="/wiki/Free_City" title="Free City" class="mw-redirect">Free City</a> of Volantis. Volon Therys is the final stop for traders and travellers before Volantis itself, and Volantis maintains a watch on who is passing through the city, sometimes preventing them from travelling on to the main city. It is a noted waystop for mercenary companies unable to get permission to travel on to Volantis itself.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">By the standards of the Free Cities, Volon Therys is a middling-sized town, but would be counted as a small city by the standards of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-7561995356cca90950b26" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to <i>The World of Ice and Fire</i>, Volon Therys was founded by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian Freehold","link":"Valyrian Freehold","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian Freehold]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-7561995356cca90950b26" href="/wiki/Valyrian_Freehold" title="Valyrian Freehold">Valyrian Freehold</a>.
</p>
