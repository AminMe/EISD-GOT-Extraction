<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-62279068656cca8a1b2d42" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, New Ghis is located near the larger island of Ghaen. Much like <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Volantis","link":"Volantis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Volantis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-62279068656cca8a1b2d42" href="/wiki/Volantis" title="Volantis">Volantis</a> once tried to do with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian Freehold","link":"Valyrian Freehold","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian Freehold]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-62279068656cca8a1b2d42" href="/wiki/Valyrian_Freehold" title="Valyrian Freehold">Valyrian Freehold</a>, New Ghis seeks to rebuild the Ghiscari Empire under its leadership.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">New Ghis has a powerful army and fleet, and uses elephants in battle. Its legions draw inspiration from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Unsullied","link":"Unsullied","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Unsullied]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-62279068656cca8a1b2d42" href="/wiki/Unsullied" title="Unsullied">Unsullied</a> warrior-eunuchs, but while the Unsullied are slaves the troops of New Ghis are free men who serve for three-year terms.
</p>
