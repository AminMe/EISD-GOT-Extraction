 Horn Hill is the seat of House Tarly in the Reach. It is located southeast of Highgarden. Its current Lord is Randyll Tarly. In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Horn Hill lies a hundred leagues northeast of Oldtown among the thickly-wooded foothills of the Red Mountains of Dorne. There is a pond that lies below the castle. See also References
