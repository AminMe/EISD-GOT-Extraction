 Sharp Point is a castle in the Crownlands, the region controlled directly by the Iron Throne in King's Landing. The castle is located north of Stonedance, at the end of a peninsula where the southern portion of Blackwater Bay meets the Narrow Sea.\The Wolf and the Lion\\/ref} data-rte-instance130814-83178605956cca90de11db classplaceholder placeholder-ext srcdata:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw type=ext / In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels Sharp Point is located at the end of Massey's Hook and is the seat of House Bar Emmon, a noble house sworn to Dragonstone. The castle is a large watchtower upon which a great fire burns atop. References
