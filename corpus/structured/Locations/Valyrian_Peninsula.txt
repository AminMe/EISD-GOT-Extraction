<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Geography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Valyrian Peninsula was shattered in the Doom. Vast amounts of land fell into the sea, with the surviving remnants becoming islands separated by water. Volcanic activity continues even four centuries after the original eruptions, with the water often turning poisonous and giving off fumes which can kill.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The city of Valyria itself remains on the central island. Though ruined, it is still somewhat intact and a tempting target for looters and treasure-seekers. However, raiding the ruins is extremely hazardous and most who travel to Valyria never return.
</p>
