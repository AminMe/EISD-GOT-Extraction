<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Geography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Pentos is located at roughly the mid-latitude of the Free Cities, between <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a> in the north and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lys","link":"Lys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Lys" title="Lys">Lys</a> in the south. It is due east of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, directly across the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Narrow Sea","link":"Narrow Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Narrow Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a>. It is surrounded by fertile coastal plains known as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Flatlands","link":"Flatlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Flatlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Flatlands" title="Flatlands">Flatlands</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Economy</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Pentos's economy is based mostly on trade and commerce of products such as cheese. As a result, Pentoshi traders like <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Illyrio Mopatis","link":"Illyrio Mopatis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Illyrio Mopatis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Illyrio_Mopatis" title="Illyrio Mopatis">Illyrio Mopatis</a> are often referred to derogatively as "cheese-mongers". They have a brisk trade with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a> across the Narrow Sea. They use their wealth to pay tribute to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a> to avoid being sacked by the nomadic people.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/pentos\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Pentos entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Pentoshi servant.jpg|thumb|150px|Slavery is nominally illegal in Pentos, but the [[Pentoshi servant|Pentoshi &quot;servant&quot;]] at Illyrio's mansion shackled with a bronze collar is a slave in all but name.]]","title":"Pentoshi_servant.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Pentoshi servant","caption":"Slavery is nominally illegal in Pentos, but the [[Pentoshi servant|Pentoshi \"servant\"]] at Illyrio's mansion shackled with a bronze collar is a slave in all but name.","width":150,"captionParsed":"Slavery is nominally illegal in Pentos, but the <a _rte_dataidx=\"0057\" href=\"\/wiki\/Pentoshi_servant\" title=\"Pentoshi servant\">Pentoshi \"servant\"<\/a> at Illyrio's mansion shackled with a bronze collar is a slave in all but name."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/4/4f/Pentoshi_servant.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/150?cb=20120316100527" width="150" height="304" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Pentoshi servant.jpg" data-image-key="Pentoshi_servant.jpg"  type="image" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Pentos was the northernmost of the large <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyria","link":"Valyria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Valyria" title="Valyria" class="mw-redirect">Valyrian</a> colony-cities when the Doom destroyed the empire four centuries ago. Pentos and the other Free Cities became independent over the course of the many-sided war known as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Century of Blood","link":"Century of Blood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Century of Blood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Century_of_Blood" title="Century of Blood">Century of Blood</a>. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Pentos is a large seaport consisting of squat brick buildings and a number of large palaces and holdings, both within the walls and outside on the plains known as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Flatlands","link":"Flatlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Flatlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Flatlands" title="Flatlands">Flatlands</a>.  Pentos's location makes it especially vulnerable to raids from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a>. As such the Pentoshi merchants are prone to feasting and gifting the passing Dothraki warlords with lavish parties and gifts to appease them into not destroying their holdings.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Slavery","link":"Slavery","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slavery]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">Slavery</a> is technically illegal in Pentos due to a treaty imposed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a> a hundred years before the events in the novels. However, the wealthy Magisters flout this law and keep bronze-collared "servants" that are slaves in all but name. Some Magisters are even involved in the slave trade.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to the TV series official <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"pronunciation guide","link":"pronunciation guide","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[pronunciation guide]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-83927623456cca85cddf51" href="/wiki/Pronunciation_guide" title="Pronunciation guide">pronunciation guide</a> developed for the cast and crew, "Pentos" is pronounced "PEN-tos", as opposed to "Pen-TOES", etc.
</p>
