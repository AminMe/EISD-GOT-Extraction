<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Uplands is the seat of House Mullendore. It is located due east of Oldtown on a tributary of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Honeywine","link":"Honeywine","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Honeywine]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/Honeywine" title="Honeywine">Honeywine</a>, amongst the foothills of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Mountains","link":"Red Mountains","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Mountains]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/Red_Mountains" title="Red Mountains">Red Mountains</a> (which give it its name). House Mullendore is a vassal of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Hightower","link":"House Hightower","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Hightower]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/House_Hightower" title="House Hightower">House Hightower</a> of Oldtown rather than the Tyrells directly.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Mark Mullendore of Uplands was a noted <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight" class="mw-redirect">knight</a> in the service of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>, noted for his trained black-and-white monkey. He switched allegiance to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> after Renly's death. He lost an arm in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a> and was taken prisoner by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannisters","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister|Lannisters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">Lannisters</a>. He bent the knee and swore fealty back to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-185811939156cca93c1570c" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a>.
</p>
