<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189271752756cca8edca326" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels the Roseroad is in the same location. It greatly speeds travel and commerce between King's Landing and Highgarden. It was apparently named for the rose-sigil of House Tyrell, because it links their castle seat at Highgarden to the capital city.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189271752756cca8edca326" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> uses the Roseroad to make a forced march from Bitterbridge to King's Landing to arrive in time to help swing the tide at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189271752756cca8edca326" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>. Prior to the battle <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189271752756cca8edca326" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a>'s army left the Riverlands by following along the southern bank of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackwater","link":"Blackwater Rush","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackwater Rush|Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189271752756cca8edca326" href="/wiki/Blackwater_Rush" title="Blackwater Rush">Blackwater</a> River. Tywin's army joined with the Tyrell army under the eves of the northern Kingswood, near where the Roseroad merges with the Kingsroad on the southern bank of the Blackwater, before crossing to the capital city on the northern bank.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the TV series, the joint Lannister-Tyrell army attacks Stannis' army near the walls of Kings Landing itself. This is a condensation, as in the books, the majority of Stannis' forces marched by land to the southern bank of the mouth of the Blackwater, and his fleet was there to ferry soldiers to the north side of the bay (instead of carrying his entire army by sea from Storm's End). Because they were already on the southern bank of the river, the Lannister-Tyrell army coming up the Roseroad didn't have to cross the river to engage them right in front of the city walls.
</p>
