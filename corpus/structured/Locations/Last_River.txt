It is located in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-210907003656cca906f1052" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-210907003656cca906f1052" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels the Last River is in the same location. It isn't the last river at all before the Wall (which is still several hundred miles to the north), as smaller bodies of water can be found (such as the lake at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Queenscrown","link":"Queenscrown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Queenscrown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-210907003656cca906f1052" href="/wiki/Queenscrown" title="Queenscrown">Queenscrown</a>), but it is the last major river of any note.
</p>
