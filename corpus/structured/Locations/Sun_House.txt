<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171872047256cca928f38b7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels Sun House is the seat of House Cuy and is located on the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Summer Sea","link":"Summer Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Summer Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171872047256cca928f38b7" href="/wiki/Summer_Sea" title="Summer Sea">Summer Sea</a> near the mouth of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Torentine","link":"Torentine","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Torentine]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171872047256cca928f38b7" href="/wiki/Torentine" title="Torentine">Torentine</a>. It was called 'Sun House' on the maps of the earlier books, but in the more recent novels it has been changed to 'Sunflower Hall'.
</p>
