</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Pyke</b> is the stronghold and seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Greyjoy","link":"House Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/House_Greyjoy" title="House Greyjoy">House Greyjoy</a>, located on the island of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"same name","link":"Pyke (island)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pyke (island)|same name]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Pyke_(island)" title="Pyke (island)">same name</a>, which is one of the seven major <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a>. The castle is the regional capital of the Iron Islands as a whole. Pyke is an ancient stronghold and the cliff it was built on has been eroded by the sea leaving the towers standing on stone stacks. The towers are connected by swaying rope bridges. The rocky moss covered stone at its base is not suitable for ships landing so traffic to the island flows through the nearby harbor town <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lordsport","link":"Lordsport","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lordsport]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Lordsport" title="Lordsport">Lordsport</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/pyke\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Pyke entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Night Lands]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"Pyke" is also the name given to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bastards","link":"bastards","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bastards]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Bastards" title="Bastards" class="mw-redirect">bastards</a> born on the Iron Islands.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Pyke is an ancient, storm-worn fortress built atop several rocky towers jutting out of the sea. It is connected to the main island by a treacherous-footed rope bridge, and defended by tall walls.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Pyke was long considered impregnable, but during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a> immense siege-ships commanded by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> smashed the walls down with boulders, allowing ground troops to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"storm","link":"Siege of Pyke","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Pyke|storm]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Pyke" title="Siege of Pyke">storm</a> the castle. The onslaught was so severe that the first men through the breach, including <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Thoros","link":"Thoros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thoros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Thoros" title="Thoros">Thoros</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Myr","link":"Myr","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Myr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Myr" title="Myr">Myr</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jorah Mormont","link":"Jorah Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jorah Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/Jorah_Mormont" title="Jorah Mormont">Jorah Mormont</a>, suffered relatively few losses and were able to secure a bridgehead within the walls.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to <i>The World of Ice and Fire</i> (2014), officially, no one is sure if the island is named after the castle or the other way around. The Greyjoys claim that the island is named after their Pyke castle (after they came to dominate the rest of the island), while the smallfolk of the island say that it was always called "Pyke", and the castle is named after the island.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">It isn't clear how "Pyke" came to be used as the special surname for bastards from the Iron Islands, i.e. before the Targaryen Conquest 300 years ago, the ironborn were ruled by other Houses who weren't from the castle or island of Pyke, such as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Hoare","link":"House Hoare","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Hoare]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149577967556cca8719bc16" href="/wiki/House_Hoare" title="House Hoare">House Hoare</a> of Orkmont island. They may have used different bastard surnames before the Conquest.
</p>
