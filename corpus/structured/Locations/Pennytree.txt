</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Pennytree is a settlement in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>, the region controlled by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>. It is located northeast of Riverrun, in between <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stone Hedge","link":"Stone Hedge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stone Hedge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/Stone_Hedge" title="Stone Hedge">Stone Hedge</a>, seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bracken","link":"House Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/House_Bracken" title="House Bracken">House Bracken</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Raventree Hall","link":"Raventree Hall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raventree Hall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/Raventree_Hall" title="Raventree Hall">Raventree Hall</a>, seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Blackwood","link":"House Blackwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Blackwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/House_Blackwood" title="House Blackwood">House Blackwood</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the Books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Pennytree lies in the frontline of frequent border disputes between the Blackwoods and Brackens and possession of the town has changed a lot over the centuries. It is named for a huge old oak tree, to which hundreds of copper pennies have been nailed.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a>, as a young boy born in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Flea Bottom","link":"Flea Bottom","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Flea Bottom]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155355397056cca934c4fd0" href="/wiki/Flea_Bottom" title="Flea Bottom">Flea Bottom</a>, once squired for a hedge knight called Ser Arlan, hailing from Pennytree.
</p>
