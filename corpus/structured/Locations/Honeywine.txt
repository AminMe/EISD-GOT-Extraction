<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95665590856cca931b3ea2" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Honeywine is noted as being one of the agricultural centers of the Reach. The river divides the city of Oldtown in half, with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Citadel","link":"Citadel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Citadel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95665590856cca931b3ea2" href="/wiki/Citadel" title="Citadel" class="mw-redirect">Citadel</a> located along its banks. The river is pleasant, with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"maesters","link":"maesters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[maesters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95665590856cca931b3ea2" href="/wiki/Maesters" title="Maesters">maesters</a> and their acolytes known to frequent the inns and other establishments along its banks. Further upriver, the Honeywine flows past <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Honeyholt","link":"Honeyholt","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Honeyholt]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95665590856cca931b3ea2" href="/wiki/Honeyholt" title="Honeyholt">Honeyholt</a>, before it finds its source in hills just north of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brightwater Keep","link":"Brightwater Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brightwater Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95665590856cca931b3ea2" href="/wiki/Brightwater_Keep" title="Brightwater Keep">Brightwater Keep</a>, the seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Florent","link":"House Florent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Florent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95665590856cca931b3ea2" href="/wiki/House_Florent" title="House Florent">House Florent</a>.
</p>
