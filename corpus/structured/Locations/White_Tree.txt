<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100414807756cca890c4dbc" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, it is spelled "Whitetree". Whitetree is a small wildling village, consisting of only four rough communal cabins, a sheepfold, and a well. Its name comes from the giant <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"weirwood","link":"weirwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[weirwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100414807756cca890c4dbc" href="/wiki/Weirwood" title="Weirwood">weirwood</a> that is in its midst. It is found similarly abandoned in <i>A Clash of Kings</i>.
