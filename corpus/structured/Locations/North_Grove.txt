<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">A few years prior to Gared's quest to find the North Grove, one of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ice-river clans","link":"ice-river clans","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ice-river clans]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202365435656cca873b7588" href="/wiki/Ice-river_clans" title="Ice-river clans">ice-river clans</a> set out to find the it. They had heard stories that the cold couldn't touch it, and that it is safe even in the dark of winter. The clan set out, full of hope, but none of them ever returned. It is implied that this clan became the undead wildling army that protects the North Grove.
</p>
