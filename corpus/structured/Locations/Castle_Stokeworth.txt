<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Castle Stokeworth</b> is a castle in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Crownlands","link":"The Crownlands","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Crownlands|the Crownlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/The_Crownlands" title="The Crownlands">the Crownlands</a>. It is the seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stokeworth","link":"House Stokeworth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stokeworth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/House_Stokeworth" title="House Stokeworth">House Stokeworth</a>, a vassal House sworn directly to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a>. It is often referred to simply as "<b>Stokeworth</b>". It is located northeast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, on the road between <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rosby","link":"Rosby","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rosby]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Rosby" title="Rosby">Rosby</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> bribes Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a> not to aid <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion</a> by arranging for him to marry <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lollys Stokeworth","link":"Lollys Stokeworth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lollys Stokeworth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Lollys_Stokeworth" title="Lollys Stokeworth">Lollys Stokeworth</a>. Tyrion points out that Lollys has an older sister and therefore Castle Stokeworth will pass to her, not to Lollys. Bronn is aware of Lollys's older sister, but <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Falyse","link":"Falyse Stokeworth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Falyse Stokeworth|Falyse]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Falyse_Stokeworth" title="Falyse Stokeworth">Falyse</a> is childless, past forty years old, and barren. Bronn also bluntly implies that he intends to arrange for an "accident" to befall Falyse, so that after her death Lollys will inherit Castle Stokeworth.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Mockingbird]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lollys Stokeworth","link":"Lollys Stokeworth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lollys Stokeworth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Lollys_Stokeworth" title="Lollys Stokeworth">Lollys Stokeworth</a> and her newly betrothed Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a> of the Blackwater walk on the grounds outside the castle, as Lollys talks excitedly about plans for their upcoming wedding. Bronn muses that he never thought he'd end up settling down in a fine a place as this. Confused, Lollys corrects him that after her mother (<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tanda Stokeworth","link":"Tanda Stokeworth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tanda Stokeworth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Tanda_Stokeworth" title="Tanda Stokeworth">Tanda Stokeworth</a>) dies, the castle will pass to her sister, because she is older than Lollys. She also says that her sister hates her, calls her names, and even still she pulls Lollys's hair when she thinks their mother isn't looking. Bronn assures Lollys that her sister is mean, and mean people always end up getting what's coming to them - clearly still holding to his plan to surreptitiously kill off Falyse after he marries Lollys.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">They then see that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> has arrived at the castle. He privately informs Bronn that he needs him to accompany a secret mission he is taking to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a>. To encourage Bronn to go, the Lannisters have now promised Lollys to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wyllis Bracken","link":"Wyllis Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wyllis Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Wyllis_Bracken" title="Wyllis Bracken">Wyllis Bracken</a>: Jaime vaguely promises that they will find Bronn a better bride with an even bigger castle, though Bronn is not amused.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The House of Black and White]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Stokeworth rules over the eponymous town of Stokeworth. It is located northeast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, on the road between <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rosby","link":"Rosby","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rosby]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Rosby" title="Rosby">Rosby</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1410929756cca8ab6146d" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a>.
</p>
