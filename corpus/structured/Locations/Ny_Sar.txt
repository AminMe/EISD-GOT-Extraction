<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179178069556cca917added" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ny Sar was ruled by Princess <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Nymeria","link":"Nymeria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nymeria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179178069556cca917added" href="/wiki/Nymeria" title="Nymeria">Nymeria</a> before the defeat of the allied Rhoynar city-states by the Valyrians.  After their armies were defeated, Nymeria led an exodus of her people from all the lands of the Rhoynar, fleeing into the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Summer Sea","link":"Summer Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Summer Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179178069556cca917added" href="/wiki/Summer_Sea" title="Summer Sea">Summer Sea</a> and thence to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179178069556cca917added" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>, where they landed in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179178069556cca917added" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a> and allied with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Martell","link":"House Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-179178069556cca917added" href="/wiki/House_Martell" title="House Martell">House Martell</a>, helping them become the dominant power in the region.
