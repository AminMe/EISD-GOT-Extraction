</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Shipbreaker Bay</b> is a body of water located off the south-eastern coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It is an inlet of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Narrow Sea","link":"Narrow Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Narrow Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a>, running along the eastern coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stormlands","link":"the Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/The_Stormlands" title="The Stormlands">the Stormlands</a>, separating the mainland from the island of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tarth","link":"Tarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Tarth" title="Tarth">Tarth</a>. The bay is noted for its severe, frequent storms. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a>'s ancestral castle, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>, is located on the bay.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season1\/#!\/locations\/kings-landing\/ HBO viewers guide, season 1 map, King's Landing entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels Shipbreaker Bay is named for the hundreds, if not thousands, of ships that have been wrecked in storms along its shores. Most notably, the parents of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon|Robert]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a> were killed in a shipwreck close to Storm's End twenty years before the events of the series.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">As well as Storm's End, the castles of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Griffin's Roost","link":"Griffin's Roost","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Griffin's Roost]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Griffin's_Roost" title="Griffin's Roost">Griffin's Roost</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rain House","link":"Rain House","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rain House]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Rain_House" title="Rain House">Rain House</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Evenfall Hall","link":"Evenfall Hall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Evenfall Hall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18040040056cca8c451770" href="/wiki/Evenfall_Hall" title="Evenfall Hall">Evenfall Hall</a> are located along the coast of the bay.
</p>
