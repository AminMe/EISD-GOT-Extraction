<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a> is being tortured by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a>, Ramsay forces Theon to play a guessing game of their location. Theon guesses Last Hearth, but is ultimately incorrect and after another wrong guess, Ramsay flays Theon's little finger.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Climb]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">​<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a> decides that he must go north of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hodor","link":"Hodor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hodor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Hodor" title="Hodor">Hodor</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meera","link":"Meera Reed","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meera Reed|Meera]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Meera_Reed" title="Meera Reed">Meera</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jojen Reed","link":"Jojen Reed","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jojen Reed]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Jojen_Reed" title="Jojen Reed">Jojen Reed</a> and realizes that his younger brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickon Stark","link":"Rickon Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickon Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Rickon_Stark" title="Rickon Stark">Rickon Stark</a> cannot go with him. To keep Rickon safe, he instructs him to leave with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Osha","link":"Osha","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Osha]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Osha" title="Osha">Osha</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shaggydog","link":"Shaggydog","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shaggydog]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" href="/wiki/Shaggydog" title="Shaggydog">Shaggydog</a> and attempt to reach the relative safety of Last Hearth.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Rains of Castamere (episode)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18329352456cca867b94b7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
