<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->The <b>Dothraki Sea</b> is a region on the continent of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Essos","link":"Essos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Essos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Essos" title="Essos">Essos</a>, thousands of miles to the east of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It is located in the continental interior, east of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Cities","link":"Free Cities","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Cities]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Free_Cities" title="Free Cities">Free Cities</a>, and is a vast landscape of steppes and plains covered in low green grass which makes it look like a sea from afar. It is named for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a> people who inhabit it, horse-mounted warriors who migrate across the plains in large hordes called <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"khalasar","link":"khalasar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[khalasar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Khalasar" title="Khalasar">khalasars</a></i> (each of which may contain thousands of riders) to plunder neighboring lands. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vaes Dothrak","link":"Vaes Dothrak","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vaes Dothrak]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Vaes_Dothrak" title="Vaes Dothrak">Vaes Dothrak</a>, the only city of the Dothraki and central hub of their society, lies on the northeastern edge of the Dothraki Sea.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Dothraki Sea lies north of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lhazar","link":"Lhazar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lhazar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Lhazar" title="Lhazar">Lhazar</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Slaver's Bay","link":"Slaver's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slaver's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Slaver's_Bay" title="Slaver's Bay">Slaver's Bay</a>, and the forbidding <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Waste","link":"Red Waste","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Waste]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Red_Waste" title="Red Waste">Red Waste</a>, which even the Dothraki fear to cross. The southern border is loosely marked by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Skahazadhan","link":"Skahazadhan","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Skahazadhan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Skahazadhan" title="Skahazadhan">Skahazadhan</a> river. The Red Waste protects the city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qarth","link":"Qarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Qarth" title="Qarth">Qarth</a> from Dothraki attack. The Dothraki sea stretches almost all the way to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jade Sea","link":"Jade Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jade Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Jade_Sea" title="Jade Sea" class="mw-redirect">Jade Sea</a>, but stops short due to intervening mountains and forests. However, the Dothraki still occasionally raid the rich cities of the western Jade Sea for treasure and slaves. The Dothraki Sea borders the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Forest of Qohor","link":"Forest of Qohor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Forest of Qohor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/Forest_of_Qohor" title="Forest of Qohor">Forest of Qohor</a> to the west, separating it from the Free Cities. However the forest does not present a significant obstacle to their hordes, which frequently threaten the Free Cities with raids or to exact tribute..<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/dothraki-sea\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Dothraki Sea entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77192659656cca87032e45" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels the Dothraki Sea is east of the Free Cities, north of Lhazar and Slaver's Bay and northwest of the Red Waste and the Jade Sea. The Dothraki raid surrounding lands from the "sea" of grass plains, which is unimaginably vast.
</p>
