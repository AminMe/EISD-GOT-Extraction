<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Ironman's Bay</b> is a large area of water off the west coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. The bay is formed by the Flint Cliffs and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cape Kraken","link":"Cape Kraken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cape Kraken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" href="/wiki/Cape_Kraken" title="Cape Kraken">Cape Kraken</a> in the north, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cape of Eagles","link":"Cape of Eagles","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cape of Eagles]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" href="/wiki/Cape_of_Eagles" title="Cape of Eagles">Cape of Eagles</a> to the east and the north coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Westerlands","link":"the Westerlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Westerlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" href="/wiki/The_Westerlands" title="The Westerlands">the Westerlands</a> to the south. The coastlands of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a> and the Westerlands all border the bay.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Most famously, the bay contains the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a>, and its waters are controlled by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Fleet","link":"Iron Fleet","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Fleet]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" href="/wiki/Iron_Fleet" title="Iron Fleet">Iron Fleet</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season1\/#!\/locations\/pyke\/ HBO viewers guide, season 1 map, Pyke entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/locations\/pyke\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 map, Pyke entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204290729456cca907ba98b" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
