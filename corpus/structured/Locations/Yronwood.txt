<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50613125256cca8d1e9ad7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Yronwood is the seat of House Yronwood. It is located near the southern end of the Boneway. Prince Quentyn Martell, the oldest son, but not heir under Dornish law, of Prince Doran Martell, was fostered at Yronwood for many years.
</p>
