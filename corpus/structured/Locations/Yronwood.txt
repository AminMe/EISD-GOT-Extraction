In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Yronwood is the seat of House Yronwood. It is located near the southern end of the Boneway. Prince Quentyn Martell, the oldest son, but not heir under Dornish law, of Prince Doran Martell, was fostered at Yronwood for many years. See also References
