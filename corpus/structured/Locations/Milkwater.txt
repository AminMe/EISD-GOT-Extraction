<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The <b>Milkwater</b> is a river located <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"beyond the Wall","link":"beyond the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[beyond the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Beyond_the_Wall" title="Beyond the Wall">beyond the Wall</a>. It is created from several tributaries that originate in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Frostfangs","link":"Frostfangs","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Frostfangs]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Frostfangs" title="Frostfangs">Frostfangs</a>, and runs south along the mountain range until it pours its waters into the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bay of Ice","link":"Bay of Ice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bay of Ice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Bay_of_Ice" title="Bay of Ice">Bay of Ice</a>.  It carves a deep gorge through the mountains as it empties into the sea at the western end of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>, which is guarded by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Shadow Tower","link":"the Shadow Tower","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Shadow Tower]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/The_Shadow_Tower" title="The Shadow Tower">the Shadow Tower</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Season 2 Bluray: War of the Five Kings feature<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Milkwater has four main branches, none of which has been revealed to have a formal name - they are all considered part of "the Milkwater".  One major branch runs all the way north along the mountain range, almost to the valley of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Thenn","link":"Thenn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thenn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Thenn" title="Thenn" class="mw-redirect">Thenn</a>.  The next branch runs northwest from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Fist of the First Men","link":"Fist of the First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fist of the First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Fist_of_the_First_Men" title="Fist of the First Men">Fist of the First Men</a>, north of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Skirling Pass","link":"Skirling Pass","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Skirling Pass]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Skirling_Pass" title="Skirling Pass">Skirling Pass</a>.  A third branch runs south of the Fist and the Skirling Pass.  The fourth and southernmost branch actually doesn't originate in the Frostfangs, but in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Haunted Forest","link":"Haunted Forest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Haunted Forest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Haunted_Forest" title="Haunted Forest">Haunted Forest</a> to the east.  Indeed, it extends as far east as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a> (though at a much more northern latitude), and runs past <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's Keep","link":"Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171385924956cca8baa7780" href="/wiki/Craster's_Keep" title="Craster's Keep">Craster's Keep</a>.  This final branch joins the river somewhat north of the Shadow Tower.  
</p>
