<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, Long Table is spelled "Longtable". It is the seat of House Merryweather. House Merryweather are notable for providing one of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hands of the King","link":"Hand of the King","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hand of the King|Hands of the King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Hand_of_the_King" title="Hand of the King">Hands of the King</a> under <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys II Targaryen","link":"Aerys II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Aerys_II_Targaryen" title="Aerys II Targaryen">Aerys II Targaryen</a>, the Mad King, after <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a>'s resignation. Lord Owen Merryweather was the Hand until the early stages of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, which he did not take seriously. For his laxity, Aerys stripped him of his lands and sent him into exile, replacing him with Lord Jon Connington of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Griffin's Roost","link":"Griffin's Roost","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Griffin's Roost]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Griffin's_Roost" title="Griffin's Roost">Griffin's Roost</a>. After taking the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> restored some of the lands to Owen's heirs, including Longtable. The current lord of the house, Orton, is married to the Lady Taena of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free City","link":"Free City","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free City]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Free_City" title="Free City" class="mw-redirect">Free City</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Myr","link":"Myr","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Myr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67899011456cca915b9151" href="/wiki/Myr" title="Myr">Myr</a>.
</p>
