<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79718248056cca88cab6e7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Frozen Shore is inhabited by clans of warriors who ride chariots made out of walrus bone and pulled by dogs. The clans of the Frozen Shore warred incessantly with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ice-river clans","link":"Ice-river clans","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ice-river clans]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79718248056cca88cab6e7" href="/wiki/Ice-river_clans" title="Ice-river clans">Ice-river clans</a> to their north, until the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King-beyond-the-Wall","link":"King-beyond-the-Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King-beyond-the-Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79718248056cca88cab6e7" href="/wiki/King-beyond-the-Wall" title="King-beyond-the-Wall" class="mw-redirect">King-beyond-the-Wall</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mance Rayder","link":"Mance Rayder","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mance Rayder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79718248056cca88cab6e7" href="/wiki/Mance_Rayder" title="Mance Rayder">Mance Rayder</a>, negotiated a truce. Frozen Shore warriors now fight in his army.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The men of the Frozen Shore aren't too isolated from the other wildling tribes, despite living on the west side of the Frostfangs, because they can move along the coast to bypass the mountains.  Still, the lands they live in are harsh, and they survive mostly on resources from the sea.  
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the past, raiders from the Frozen Shore have raided <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bear Island","link":"Bear Island","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bear Island]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79718248056cca88cab6e7" href="/wiki/Bear_Island" title="Bear Island">Bear Island</a> to the south.
</p>
