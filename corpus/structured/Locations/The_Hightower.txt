</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>The Hightower</b> is a massive lighthouse located in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oldtown","link":"Oldtown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oldtown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85257787956cca8b926995" href="/wiki/Oldtown" title="Oldtown">Oldtown</a>, the oldest city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85257787956cca8b926995" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. It serves as the seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Hightower","link":"House Hightower","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Hightower]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85257787956cca8b926995" href="/wiki/House_Hightower" title="House Hightower">House Hightower</a>, proud and wealthy bannermen of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85257787956cca8b926995" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> of Highgarden.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i>A Song of Ice and Fire</i> novels, the Hightower is described as being 800 feet tall - a hundred feet taller than <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85257787956cca8b926995" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> itself (though still not approaching the sheer scale of the Wall, which stretches from one side of the continent to the other). It is the tallest structure in all of Westeros.
</p>
