<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Runestone</b> is a castle in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>. House Royce are now one of the major vassal houses holding fealty to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Arryn","link":"House Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/House_Arryn" title="House Arryn">House Arryn</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Eyrie","link":"the Eyrie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Eyrie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/The_Eyrie" title="The Eyrie">the Eyrie</a>.  The castle is located east of the Eyrie, on the coast of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Narrow Sea","link":"Narrow Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Narrow Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a>. It is north of the city port of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gulltown","link":"Gulltown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gulltown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/Gulltown" title="Gulltown">Gulltown</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season-5\/episode-1\/map\/location\/43\/runestone HBO viewers guide, season 5: episode 1 map, Runestone entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"maester","link":"Maesters","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maesters|maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/Maesters" title="Maesters">maester</a> appointed to the castle is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Helliweg","link":"Helliweg","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Helliweg]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/Helliweg" title="Helliweg">Helliweg</a> and its <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"master-at-arms","link":"master-at-arms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[master-at-arms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/Master-at-arms" title="Master-at-arms" class="mw-redirect">master-at-arms</a> is Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Samwell Stone","link":"Samwell Stone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Samwell Stone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/Samwell_Stone" title="Samwell Stone">Samwell Stone</a>.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the novels</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Royce proudly descends from the First Men, one of the very few of their Houses to survive the Andal invasion in the Vale. The head of House Royce wears ceremonial bronze armor covered in the rune symbols of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Old Tongue","link":"Old Tongue","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Old Tongue]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124596240456cca87d03218" href="/wiki/Old_Tongue" title="Old Tongue">Old Tongue</a> of the First Men, which are also featured on House Royce's sigil.  It seems probable that "Rune-stone" was named after runes left behind by the First Men in the area.
</p>
