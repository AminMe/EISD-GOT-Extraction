<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><b>Blacktyde</b> is one of the seven main <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a>, an archipelago located on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironman's Bay","link":"Ironman's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironman's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Ironman's_Bay" title="Ironman's Bay">Ironman's Bay</a>, on the western side of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> In the books </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Blacktyde does not have a very distinguished history. However, at the beginning of the narrative its current lord is Baelor Blacktyde, who spent eight years as a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ward","link":"ward","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ward]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Ward" title="Ward">ward</a> on the mainland after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, much as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a> was sent as a hostage to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. Unlike Theon, Baelor Blacktyde spent his years as a ward in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oldtown","link":"Oldtown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oldtown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Oldtown" title="Oldtown">Oldtown</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a>, one of the major centers of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Andal","link":"Andal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Andal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Andal" title="Andal" class="mw-redirect">Andal</a> culture. When Baelor returned (when he came of age, seven years ago), he was an ardent convert to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Faith of the Seven","link":"Faith of the Seven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Faith of the Seven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Faith_of_the_Seven" title="Faith of the Seven">Faith of the Seven</a>. Due to Baelor Blacktyde's household, Blacktyde is the only one of the Iron Islands with any minority to speak of who follow the Faith of the Seven instead of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drowned God","link":"Drowned God","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drowned God]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47341875556cca8ddd7064" href="/wiki/Drowned_God" title="Drowned God">Drowned God</a>.
</p>
