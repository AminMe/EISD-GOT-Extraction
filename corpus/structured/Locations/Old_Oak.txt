 Old Oak is a castle in the Reach. It is the seat of House Oakheart, a vassal house holding fealty to House Tyrell of Highgarden. The castle is located in the north-west of the Reach. In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Old Oak is the home of Ser Arys Oakheart, a noted member of the Kingsguard who accompanies Myrcella Baratheon to Dorne.
