<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85062746156cca912926ae" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Searoad is in the same location. Curiously, the first four novels in the series label is the "Searoad", as does the official map for HBO's TV series adaptation. However, the map accompanying the fifth novel, <i>A Dance With Dragons</i>, inexplicably labels it the "Ocean Road". It's not clear if this is an alternate name, or a mistake in publication.
</p>
