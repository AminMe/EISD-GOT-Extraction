She is the oldest and only daughter of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Glenmore","link":"Glenmore (Lord)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Glenmore (Lord)|Lord Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Glenmore_(Lord)" title="Glenmore (Lord)">Lord Glenmore</a> and is betrothed to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Forrester","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik Forrester</a> at the beginning of the series.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Elaena is the betrothed of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Forrester","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik Forrester</a>, and the two have known each other since they were young. They played hide-and-seek in the grove, where Elaena would always hide behind the same tree so Rodrik would find her.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>" </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:TLL_Fireside_Chat.png|thumb|left|Elaena and Rodrik reunite.]]","title":"TLL_Fireside_Chat.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"TLL Fireside Chat","align":"left","caption":"Elaena and Rodrik reunite.","captionParsed":"Elaena and Rodrik reunite."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" alt="" src="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/e/e4/TLL_Fireside_Chat.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20151221025835" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb alignLeft withCaption" data-image-name="TLL Fireside Chat.png" data-image-key="TLL_Fireside_Chat.png"  type="image" />Elaena Glenmore arrives at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a> after hearing Rodrik survived the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a> to discuss their betrothal. While happy to see him, she soon reveals that her family is having second thoughts about the betrothal. Based on the player's choice of discourse, she either decides to go ahead with the betrothal, or call it off as it's too dangerous.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">If <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mira","link":"Mira Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mira Forrester|Mira]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Mira_Forrester" title="Mira Forrester">Mira</a> had forged a note to Elaena from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Margaery Tyrell","link":"Margaery Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Margaery Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Margaery_Tyrell" title="Margaery Tyrell">Margaery Tyrell</a>, Elaena will feel like she's being coerced into marrying Rodrik, but can be convinced by allowing her to make her own choice. Alternatively, if Mira did not forge the letter, Rodrik can offer half of the Forresters' ironwood to satisfy the Glenmores.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:TLL_Elaena_Rodrik_and_Ludd.png|thumb|Ludd Whitehill interrupts Elaena and Rodrik.]]","title":"TLL_Elaena_Rodrik_and_Ludd.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"TLL Elaena Rodrik and Ludd","caption":"Ludd Whitehill interrupts Elaena and Rodrik.","captionParsed":"Ludd Whitehill interrupts Elaena and Rodrik."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" alt="" src="http://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/e/e4/TLL_Elaena_Rodrik_and_Ludd.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20151221025946" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="TLL Elaena Rodrik and Ludd.png" data-image-key="TLL_Elaena_Rodrik_and_Ludd.png"  type="image" />After their discussion, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Ludd Whitehill","link":"Ludd Whitehill","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ludd Whitehill|Lord Ludd Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Ludd_Whitehill" title="Ludd Whitehill">Lord Ludd Whitehill</a> rudely enters. He asks if Elaena is still marrying Rodrik, arguing that his son, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gryff","link":"Gryff Whitehill","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gryff Whitehill|Gryff]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Gryff_Whitehill" title="Gryff Whitehill">Gryff</a>, still needs a wife. Depending on whether or not the betrothal was won, Elaena will either proudly affirm that she is marrying Rodrik, or say that she has not yet reached a decision. After Ludd demands Rodrik kiss his ring, Elaena will either (depending on player choice) express relief that Rodrik didn't show weakness, or disappointment for him showing weakness.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sons of Winter","link":"Sons of Winter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sons of Winter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Sons_of_Winter" title="Sons of Winter">Sons of Winter</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Elaena appears in the Ironwood Grove while Royland and Rodrik are training to fight despite his injured leg. She appears with her brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arthur","link":"Arthur Glenmore","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arthur Glenmore|Arthur]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Arthur_Glenmore" title="Arthur Glenmore">Arthur</a> and the Glenmore Elite Guard. She tells Rodrik that the betrothal between them (if it was secured) will not carry on due to Ludd Whitehill paying Elaena's father a visit and convincing him to have Elaena Glenmore marry <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gryff Whitehill","link":"Gryff Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gryff Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Gryff_Whitehill" title="Gryff Whitehill">Gryff Whitehill</a>. Her brother Arthur is also shocked by Lord Glenmore accepting the offer and says that the Glenmore Elite Guard is Rodrik's if Gryff is taken down. Rodrik can promise Elaena to kill Gryff or not, either way -- you aren't able to.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:SOW_Elaena_Confronting_Gryff.png|thumb|left|Elaena angrily confronts Gryff Whitehill.]]","title":"SOW_Elaena_Confronting_Gryff.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"SOW Elaena Confronting Gryff","align":"left","caption":"Elaena angrily confronts Gryff Whitehill.","captionParsed":"Elaena angrily confronts Gryff Whitehill."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/8/88/SOW_Elaena_Confronting_Gryff.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20151221030047" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb alignLeft withCaption" data-image-name="SOW Elaena Confronting Gryff.png" data-image-key="SOW_Elaena_Confronting_Gryff.png"  type="image" />Elaena asks everyone else to give them some privacy. Rodrik can choose to kiss her or say farewell. Later on, Elaena, Rodrik, Arthur and the Elite Guard go inside the Great Hall, and Rodrik tells them to come out when he strikes his cane. Gryff questions why Elaena is here and she claims she'd rather marry a dead horse. She stands there and watches as Rodrik beats Gryff or does nothing, saying Gryff looks like he 'wants more' if Rodrik hesitates. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady Forrester","link":"Elissa Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elissa Forrester|Lady Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Elissa_Forrester" title="Elissa Forrester">Lady Forrester</a> stops Rodrik, she accepts and says it's okay not to kill Gryff right now. It is assumed she stayed at Ironrath when Rodrik, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Royland","link":"Royland Degore","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Royland Degore|Royland]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Royland_Degore" title="Royland Degore">Royland</a>/<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan","link":"Duncan Tuttle","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan Tuttle|Duncan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Duncan_Tuttle" title="Duncan Tuttle">Duncan</a>, Elissa and possibly the Glenmore Elite Guard head to Highpoint.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Nest of Vipers","link":"A Nest of Vipers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Nest of Vipers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/A_Nest_of_Vipers" title="A Nest of Vipers">A Nest of Vipers</a>" </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:ANOV_Rodrik_Comforting_Elaena.png|thumb|Rodrik comforts Elaena.]]","title":"ANOV_Rodrik_Comforting_Elaena.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"ANOV Rodrik Comforting Elaena","caption":"Rodrik comforts Elaena.","captionParsed":"Rodrik comforts Elaena."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/3/3f/ANOV_Rodrik_Comforting_Elaena.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20151221030154" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="ANOV Rodrik Comforting Elaena.png" data-image-key="ANOV_Rodrik_Comforting_Elaena.png"  type="image" />Elaena appears after her brother Arthur is killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Snow" title="Ramsay Snow" class="mw-redirect">Ramsay Snow</a>. She comes to the ironwood grove looking for Arthur, and becomes distraught upon seeing his body. Blaming herself for getting him involved, Elaena explains to Rodrik how, when he was first learning to use a bow, he shook so much that his father gave him the nickname "Quiver." She accuses Rodrik of causing his death, but quickly apologizes and continues to sob. Rodrik can either offer to keep her company or give her some time alone, saying that she can stay at Ironrath for as long as she wants. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">If Rodrik offered to keep Elaena company, she is later seen in his bed. They imply that they've been sleeping together for several weeks, and talk about what the future holds for them. If Rodrik tells her to return to her family, Elaena says that she can never go back, as she worries that her father will blame her for Arthur's death. Talia then interrupts their conversation with important information for Rodrik. Embarassed, Elaena covers herself with a sheet and exits the room.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Ice Dragon","link":"The Ice Dragon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Ice Dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/The_Ice_Dragon" title="The Ice Dragon">The Ice Dragon</a>" </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:TID_Elaena_and_Talia_Mourn_Rodrik.png|thumb|left|Elaena and Talia mourn the loss of Rodrik.]]","title":"TID_Elaena_and_Talia_Mourn_Rodrik.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"TID Elaena and Talia Mourn Rodrik","align":"left","caption":"Elaena and Talia mourn the loss of Rodrik.","captionParsed":"Elaena and Talia mourn the loss of Rodrik."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" alt="" src="http://vignette4.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/3/3d/TID_Elaena_and_Talia_Mourn_Rodrik.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20151221030328" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb alignLeft withCaption" data-image-name="TID Elaena and Talia Mourn Rodrik.png" data-image-key="TID_Elaena_and_Talia_Mourn_Rodrik.png"  type="image" />If Rodrik is killed in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ambush at the Harbor","link":"Ambush at the Harbor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ambush at the Harbor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Ambush_at_the_Harbor" title="Ambush at the Harbor">Ambush at the Harbor</a>, Elaena is present when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Asher","link":"Asher Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Asher Forrester|Asher]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Asher_Forrester" title="Asher Forrester">Asher</a> arrives at Ironrath. Upon learning of Rodrik's death, she and Talia hold each other and cry for him. When Asher later begins discussing what to do about the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Whitehills","link":"House Whitehill","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Whitehill|Whitehills]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/House_Whitehill" title="House Whitehill">Whitehills</a>, Elaena angrily states that they've lost the war with Rodrik's death. Asher's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sentinel","link":"Sentinel","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sentinel|sentinel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Sentinel" title="Sentinel">sentinel</a> backs up her claim, saying that under Ramsay's terms, his death means that the Whitehills have won.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Elaena later joins the rest of the Forrester household in meeting Ludd Whitehill and his army at the gates of Ironrath. After demanding Asher's surrender, Ludd returns Rodrik's body as a token of his goodwill. Elaena gives the corpse of her lover a brief, mournful look before walking away. She is not seen for the rest of the episode, leaving it unknown as to whether she escaped the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Ironrath","link":"Battle of Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Ironrath" title="Battle of Ironrath">Battle of Ironrath</a> or was captured by the Whitehills.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">If Rodrik survived the ambush, Elaena happily greets him when he returns to Ironrath. She stands by as he convinces the pit fighters to stay loyal to him and rallies the smallfolk to prepare for battle.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:TID_Elaena_Greets_Rodrik.png|thumb|Elaena happily greets Rodrik.]]","title":"TID_Elaena_Greets_Rodrik.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"TID Elaena Greets Rodrik","caption":"Elaena happily greets Rodrik.","captionParsed":"Elaena happily greets Rodrik."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" alt="" src="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/8/80/TID_Elaena_Greets_Rodrik.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20151221030534" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="TID Elaena Greets Rodrik.png" data-image-key="TID_Elaena_Greets_Rodrik.png"  type="image" />As the Whitehill army lays siege to Ironrath, Elaena initially stays hidden in the cellars with Talia. At some point, Maester <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ortengryn","link":"Ortengryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ortengryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Ortengryn" title="Ortengryn">Ortengryn</a> advises her to flee the castle to House Glenmore's cousins in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53788784556cca61e3991d" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>. Elaena confronts Rodrik and asks him to allow her to stay at his side. Rodrik can demand that she leave for her own safety, or allow her to stay. She then wishes Rodrik good luck as he goes to infiltrate the Whitehill camp.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Despite the Forresters' best efforts, Ironrath eventually falls to the Whitehills. If Elaena was allowed to stay, she is captured by Whitehill soldiers and dragged out of the castle. A heavily wounded Rodrik sees this and attempts to help her, but is unable to muster the strength to move, leaving her fate unknown.
</p>
