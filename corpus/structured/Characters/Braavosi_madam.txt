<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">This Braavosi Madam is the proprietress, or at least a senior procuress, of a brothel in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123093156156cca711624b0" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123093156156cca711624b0" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Braavosi Madam is present when Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meryn Trant","link":"Meryn Trant","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meryn Trant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123093156156cca711624b0" href="/wiki/Meryn_Trant" title="Meryn Trant">Meryn Trant</a> visits the brothel she works in, seeking for a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"prostitute","link":"Prostitution","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Prostitution|prostitute]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123093156156cca711624b0" href="/wiki/Prostitution" title="Prostitution">prostitute</a>. She presents three prostitutes to him, but he claims that they are all too old. Realizing with some distaste what Trant is seeking, she presents him with a very young serving girl. Trant informs the madam that he expects a "fresh" one tomorrow night, again to her distaste. Throughout the exchange, the Madam is vexed by the presence of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lanna","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark|Lanna]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123093156156cca711624b0" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Lanna</a>, a young oyster-seller, so deep in the brothel, and warns her not to show up there again.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Dance of Dragons]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123093156156cca711624b0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
