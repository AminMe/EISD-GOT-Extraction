<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35790963856cca7a804518" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The soldier is relaxing with three other Frey men by a campfire, boasting about the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35790963856cca7a804518" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Another soldier","link":"Frey soldier 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Frey soldier 1|Another soldier]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35790963856cca7a804518" href="/wiki/Frey_soldier_1" title="Frey soldier 1">Another soldier</a> in particular mocks the death of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35790963856cca7a804518" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> and the desecration of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35790963856cca7a804518" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>'s corpse, including attaching the head of his direwolf, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Wind","link":"Grey Wind","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Wind]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35790963856cca7a804518" href="/wiki/Grey_Wind" title="Grey Wind">Grey Wind</a>, to it. This soldier reminds the other that he was the one who did it, to which the other soldier responds in the affirmative. This soldier is then the first to notice <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35790963856cca7a804518" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> approaching from behind. After she asks to keep warm, the soldier orders her to "fuck off", until she offers a coin to the other soldier. However, she quickly stabs the other soldier, prompting this soldier and the two others to take up arms. However, they're quickly slain by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sandor Clegane","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-35790963856cca7a804518" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">Sandor Clegane</a> before they have a chance to hurt Arya.
</p>
