<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130077963256cca74c9696b" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Gwayne Corbray's actions are recorded in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Book","link":"White Book","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Book]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130077963256cca74c9696b" href="/wiki/White_Book" title="White Book" class="mw-redirect">White Book</a>. During the Battle of the Redgrass Field, he fought in the vanguard of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arryn","link":"Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130077963256cca74c9696b" href="/wiki/Arryn" title="Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Arryn</a> and dueled Daemon Blackfyre bearing <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady Forlorn","link":"Lady Forlorn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lady Forlorn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130077963256cca74c9696b" href="/wiki/Lady_Forlorn" title="Lady Forlorn">Lady Forlorn</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian steel","link":"Valyrian steel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian steel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130077963256cca74c9696b" href="/wiki/Valyrian_steel" title="Valyrian steel">Valyrian steel</a> blade of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Corbray","link":"House Corbray","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Corbray]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130077963256cca74c9696b" href="/wiki/House_Corbray" title="House Corbray">House Corbray</a>, while Daemon bore <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackfyre","link":"Blackfyre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackfyre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130077963256cca74c9696b" href="/wiki/Blackfyre" title="Blackfyre">Blackfyre</a>. Daemon defeated Ser Gwayne after an hour, but in a demonstration of chivalry, had his supporter, a knight known as Redtusk, take Ser Gwayne to the Blackfyre rear to see to his wounds.
</p>
