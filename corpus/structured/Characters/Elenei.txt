<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">According to legend, Elenei was daughter of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sea god","link":"God of the Sea","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[God of the Sea|sea god]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" href="/wiki/God_of_the_Sea" title="God of the Sea">sea god</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wind goddess","link":"Goddess of the Wind","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Goddess of the Wind|wind goddess]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" href="/wiki/Goddess_of_the_Wind" title="Goddess of the Wind">wind goddess</a>. She fell in love with a mortal man named <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Durran","link":"Durran","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Durran]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" href="/wiki/Durran" title="Durran">Durran</a>, who took her as his wife. By giving up her maidenhead to him, Elenei became mortal, while Durran earned the hatred of Elenei's parents.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Durran_stormsend.jpg|thumb|Elenei became the mortal Queen of Durran, the first Storm King]]","title":"Durran_stormsend.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Durran stormsend","caption":"Elenei became the mortal Queen of Durran, the first Storm King","captionParsed":"Elenei became the mortal Queen of Durran, the first Storm King"}}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/9/98/Durran_stormsend.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120327163448" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Durran stormsend.jpg" data-image-key="Durran_stormsend.jpg"  type="image" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In rage, the gods sent a storm to destroy Durran's keep over <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shipbreaker Bay","link":"Shipbreaker Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shipbreaker Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" href="/wiki/Shipbreaker_Bay" title="Shipbreaker Bay">Shipbreaker Bay</a>. Declaring war against them, Durran built a new castle, larger and more formidable than the previous one, though this one too was destroyed by the divine storms.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Four more keeps Durran built and lost to the fury of the gods. Finally, the seventh castle, which Durran built with the aid of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran the Builder","link":"Brandon Stark (the Builder)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brandon Stark (the Builder)|Bran the Builder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" href="/wiki/Brandon_Stark_(the_Builder)" title="Brandon Stark (the Builder)">Bran the Builder</a>, withstood the rage of the Elenei's parents and became known as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>, while Durran earned the nickname of "Durran's Godsgrief."<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Age of Heroes (Complete Guide to Westeros)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Stormlands (Histories & Lore)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145146974856cca731cf6ad" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
