Vickon Greyjoy is an unseen character in Game of Thrones. He is deceased when the events of the series begin. 

Lord Vickon was the head of House Greyjoy and the Lord Reaper of Pyke during the reign of King Harren the Black of House Hoare. After Harren and his line were burned alive by Aegon the Conquerorand hisdragon inside his castle of Harrenhalduring theWar of Conquest, the ironborn were expelled from the Riverlands back to the Iron Islands. In exchange for their allegiance, Aegon allowed theironborn to choose their new leader.
