</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>The Tickler</b> is a recurring character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"second season","link":"Season 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2|second season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">second season</a>. He is played by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Anthony Morris","link":"Anthony Morris","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Anthony Morris]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Anthony_Morris" title="Anthony Morris">Anthony Morris</a> and debuts in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Garden of Bones","link":"Garden of Bones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Garden of Bones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Garden_of_Bones" title="Garden of Bones">Garden of Bones</a>." He is a soldier serving under Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> who specializes in interrogation and torture techniques.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">After <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hot Pie","link":"Hot Pie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hot Pie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Hot_Pie" title="Hot Pie">Hot Pie</a> are imprisoned in a village near <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a> taken over by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannister","link":"Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Lannister" title="Lannister" class="mw-redirect">Lannister</a> army, they hear the Tickler interrogating a prisoner. The following day, another villager is selected by Gregor Clegane. The Tickler has the prisoner strapped and a bucket with a rat inside put over his chest. He interrogates the prisoner about hidden gold, jewels and the whereabouts of "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Brotherhood","link":"The Brotherhood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Brotherhood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/The_Brotherhood" title="The Brotherhood" class="mw-redirect">The Brotherhood</a>" and which villagers are helping them. When the prisoner denies knowledge, the Tickler has <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Weasel","link":"Weasel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Weasel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Weasel" title="Weasel">Weasel</a> heat the bucket with fire to make the rat claw its way through the prisoner's chest. After a short time, the prisoner denounces a butcher named <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gaines","link":"Gaines","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gaines]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Gaines?action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Gaines (page does not exist)">Gaines</a> and his son, but the Tickler continues with the torture, killing the prisoner.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The next day, it is Gendry who is chosen and the Tickler goes through the same process again, but Gendry is saved when Tywin Lannister arrives and decides it's stupid to kill able-bodied workers.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:The Tickler dead.png|thumb|The Tickler dead of a broken neck in Ghost of Harrenhal.]]","title":"The_Tickler_dead.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"The Tickler dead","caption":"The Tickler dead of a broken neck in Ghost of Harrenhal.","captionParsed":"The Tickler dead of a broken neck in Ghost of Harrenhal."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" alt="" src="http://vignette4.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/a/a1/The_Tickler_dead.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120527193918" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="The Tickler dead.png" data-image-key="The_Tickler_dead.png"  type="image" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Tickler is an associate of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> and serves in his band of man-at-arms alongside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Polliver","link":"Polliver","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Polliver]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Polliver" title="Polliver">Polliver</a>, Chiswyck, Raff "the Sweetling", Dunsen and others. His face is so ordinary and his garb so plain that Arya might have thought him one of the villagers before she had seen him at his work. His real name is not revealed.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The torturing takes place during stops on the forced march to Harrenhal, not at Harrenhal itself. Sometimes Chiswyck assists the Tickler with the torturing, sometimes others of the Mountain's men. Every day the Tickler picks people at random from the captives (but not Gendry). He always asks them the same questions:
</p>
<ul data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><li>Was there gold hidden in the village? Silver, gems?
</li><li>Was there more food?
</li><li>Where was Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Beric Dondarrion","link":"Beric Dondarrion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beric Dondarrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Beric_Dondarrion" title="Beric Dondarrion">Beric Dondarrion</a>?
</li><li>Which of the village folk had aided him?
</li><li>When he rode off, where did he go?
</li><li>How many men were with them? How many knights, how many bowmen, how many men-at-arms?
</li><li>How were they armed? How many were horsed? How many were wounded?
</li><li>What other enemy had they seen? How many? When? What banners did they fly? Where did they go?
</li></ul>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">None of the tortured captives survived the Tickler's interrogation. The strongest lasted past evenfall. Their bodies were hung beyond the fires for the wolves. The answers they gave were contradictory: that Beric had ten starvelings with him, or else a hundred mounted knights; that he had ridden west, or north, or south; that he had crossed the lake in a boat; that he was strong as an aurochs, or weak from the bloody flux, etc. - but the Tickler did not seem to care. The Tickler's great cruelty was that he <i>knew</i> that nearly all of the people he tortured were being honest when they said they had no more gold, and that they knew nothing about the Brotherhood, but he tortured them to death anyway simply because he could.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Tywin Lannister does not come to Harrenhal while the Tickler is there (he is one of Gregor Clegane's men and Tywin Lannister doesn't come until later). Unlike his television counterpart, he is not killed by Jaqen H'ghar. The Tickler leaves Harrenhal and his story continues in the books.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Arya and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Hound","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane|the Hound]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">the Hound</a> encounter the Tickler, accompanied by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Polliver","link":"Polliver","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Polliver]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Polliver" title="Polliver">Polliver</a> and a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sarsfield","link":"House Sarsfield","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Sarsfield|Sarsfield]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/House_Sarsfield" title="House Sarsfield">Sarsfield</a> squire, at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-87953364356cca62a2f7d9" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a>. During the ensuing brawl Arya kills the Tickler by stabbing him repeatedly in the back. With every stab, Arya sarcastically screams the questions the Tickler used to ask his victims.
</p>
