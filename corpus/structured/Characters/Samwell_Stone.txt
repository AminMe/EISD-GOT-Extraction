<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"<i>A Song of Ice and Fire<\/i>","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire|''A Song of Ice and Fire'']]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182425872356cca7485141a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire"><i>A Song of Ice and Fire</i></a> novels Ser Samwell Stone, dubbed "Strong Sam Stone", is a renowned knight and the master-at-arms of Runestone. He supports his liege lord Yohn Royce when the latter gathers a group of nobles, dubbed the "Lords Declarant", to denounce Littlefinger and oppose his rule over the Vale.
