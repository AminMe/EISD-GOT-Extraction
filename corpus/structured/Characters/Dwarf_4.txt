<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">A <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dwarf","link":"Dwarfism","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dwarfism|dwarf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/Dwarfism" title="Dwarfism">dwarf</a>, he makes a living by performing for other peoples' amusement.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Along with a group of other dwarfs, he takes part in a mock "reenactment" of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King Joffrey's wedding","link":"Purple Wedding","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Purple Wedding|King Joffrey's wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/Purple_Wedding" title="Purple Wedding">King Joffrey's wedding</a>. He dresses as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> and rides a horse while wearing a wolf's head helmet - cruelly mocking how Robb's corpse was desecrated at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>, by having his head cut off and his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"direwolf","link":"direwolf","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[direwolf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/Direwolf" title="Direwolf" class="mw-redirect">direwolf</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Wind","link":"Grey Wind","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Wind]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/Grey_Wind" title="Grey Wind">Grey Wind</a>'s head sewn on in its place. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a> is visibly upset by this cruel mockery of her deceased brother.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Lion and the Rose]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206046810456cca7279945e" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
