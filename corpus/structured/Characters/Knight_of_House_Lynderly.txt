<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Knight is present when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> demands a trial by combat for the charges of murdering <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Arryn","link":"Jon Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" href="/wiki/Jon_Arryn" title="Jon Arryn">Jon Arryn</a> and conspiring to murder <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a>. He's the first to offer himself as the champion for Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lysa Arryn","link":"Lysa Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lysa Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" href="/wiki/Lysa_Arryn" title="Lysa Arryn">Lysa Arryn</a>, followed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eon Hunter","link":"Eon Hunter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eon Hunter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" href="/wiki/Eon_Hunter" title="Eon Hunter">Eon Hunter</a> and other men. However, Lady Lysa chooses Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vardis Egen","link":"Vardis Egen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vardis Egen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" href="/wiki/Vardis_Egen" title="Vardis Egen">Vardis Egen</a> as her champion.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Golden Crown]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166632349556cca7092f4de" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, a member of House Lynderly of Snakewood is the first to offer himself as Lysa Tully's champion. Tyrion however doesn't recognize the Lynderly's identity. Later books identify two other members of House Lynderly: Lord Jon Lynderly and his son, Terrance, a young squire.
</p>
