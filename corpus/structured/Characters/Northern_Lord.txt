 A Northern Lord is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Pat Mooney and only appears in The North Remembers. Biography 

 Season 2 

He visits Winterfell to complain about the state of his holdfast and to request help rebuilding it in the coming winter. He annoys Prince Bran Stark by showing a lack of respect for King Robb Stark.
