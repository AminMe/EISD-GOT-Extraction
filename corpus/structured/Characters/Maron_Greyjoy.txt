</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Maron Greyjoy</b> is an unseen character in <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Game of Thrones","link":"Game of Thrones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Game of Thrones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25076746056cca6b0de7b4" href="/wiki/Game_of_Thrones" title="Game of Thrones" class="mw-redirect">Game of Thrones</a></i>. He is deceased when the events of the series begin and is not expected to appear in the series. Maron is the son of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon Greyjoy","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25076746056cca6b0de7b4" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon Greyjoy</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Reaper of Pyke","link":"Lord Reaper of Pyke","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Reaper of Pyke]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25076746056cca6b0de7b4" href="/wiki/Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke" title="Lord Reaper of Pyke">Lord Reaper of Pyke</a>. He was killed fighting for his father in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25076746056cca6b0de7b4" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25076746056cca6b0de7b4" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Maron was killed defending the South tower on Pyke during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25076746056cca6b0de7b4" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>. He was crushed to death when the tower collapsed.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In <i>"A Clash of Kings"</i> and <i>"A Dance with Dragons"</i> it is revealed that Maron and Rodrik used to bully Theon. Even after so many years, Theon still remembers the beating and cruel japes he suffered from them.
