A Bolton soldier is an unnamed minor character in the third season. He is played by guest star Gary Lightbody and only appears in Walk of Punishment. Biography 

 Background 

This soldier is fighting for House Bolton in the War of the Five Kings. Season 3 

He is a member of the force led by Locke and is present when they capture Jaime Lannister and Brienne of Tarth.
