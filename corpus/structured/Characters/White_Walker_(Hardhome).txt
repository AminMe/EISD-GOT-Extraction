<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Biography </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176112759556cca641f40b0" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">During the attack on Hardhome, while Jon and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Loboda","link":"Loboda","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Loboda]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176112759556cca641f40b0" href="/wiki/Loboda" title="Loboda">Loboda</a> search for a bag of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragon glass","link":"dragon glass","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragon glass]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176112759556cca641f40b0" href="/wiki/Dragon_glass" title="Dragon glass" class="mw-redirect">dragon glass</a> weapons inside a burning <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wildling","link":"wildling","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wildling]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176112759556cca641f40b0" href="/wiki/Wildling" title="Wildling" class="mw-redirect">wildling</a> hut, the White Walker gives chase, extinguishing the flames with his mere presence. Loboda tries to fight him to buy Jon time to retrieve the dragon glass but upon contact with the Walker's icy spear his axe shatters. Loboda is slain by the White Walker. Jon, though plainly outmatched, takes on the Walker until with both combatants are astonished to find that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Longclaw","link":"Longclaw","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Longclaw]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176112759556cca641f40b0" href="/wiki/Longclaw" title="Longclaw">Longclaw</a> doesn't shatter but successfully counters the strike from the Walker's spear. Jon lands a blow on the White Walker, causing it to instantly shatter into a cloud of icy dust and fragments.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"S05E08\">\"[[Hardhome (episode)]]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176112759556cca641f40b0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">His death is observed by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's King","link":"Night's King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176112759556cca641f40b0" href="/wiki/Night's_King" title="Night's King">Night's King</a>, who continues to watch Jon with interest, having finally seen something approaching actual resistance to the White Walkers' march.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"S05E08\" \/>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-176112759556cca641f40b0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
