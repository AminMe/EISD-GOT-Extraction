<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">A messenger reports news from Ser Addam's outriders to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a>. Ser Addam's scouts have noticed <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>'s forces have crossed <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Neck","link":"the Neck","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Neck]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/The_Neck" title="The Neck">the Neck</a> and are moving south along the east bank of the Green Fork of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Trident","link":"the Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/The_Trident" title="The Trident">the Trident</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Pointy End]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> Tywin's forces later engage and defeat the Stark force in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Green Fork","link":"Battle of the Green Fork","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Green Fork]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Green_Fork" title="Battle of the Green Fork">Battle of the Green Fork</a>. The Stark army is much smaller than the scouts reported because they separated at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Twins","link":"the Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">the Twins</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Baelor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Addam is present for a strategy meeting with Tywin following the defeat in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a> by the main portion of the Stark army. Also present are <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kevan Lannister","link":"Kevan Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kevan Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Kevan_Lannister" title="Kevan Lannister">Kevan Lannister</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Leo Lefford","link":"Leo Lefford","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Leo Lefford]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Leo_Lefford" title="Leo Lefford">Leo Lefford</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harys Swyft","link":"Harys Swyft","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harys Swyft]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Harys_Swyft" title="Harys Swyft">Harys Swyft</a> and another <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bannerman","link":"Lannister bannerman 2 (The Ghost of Harrenhal)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lannister bannerman 2 (The Ghost of Harrenhal)|bannerman]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Lannister_bannerman_2_(The_Ghost_of_Harrenhal)" title="Lannister bannerman 2 (The Ghost of Harrenhal)">bannerman</a>, as well as Tywin's servant and several Lannister officers. He asks about <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>, and Kevan confirms that both Baratheon brothers have declared against the Lannisters. Later he dismisses suing for a truce so as to not appear weak, and proposes that they march against the Starks instead. Tywin dismisses his bannermen and then announces plans to relocate to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Fire and Blood]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Addam Marbrand is the most aggressive of the Lannister bannermen at the meeting and decries the idea of a truce. However, Marbrand is the same age as Ser Jaime Lannister and has long red hair and wears a smoke grey cape.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During his youth, Addam was a page at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Casterly Rock","link":"Casterly Rock","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Casterly Rock]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Casterly_Rock" title="Casterly Rock">Casterly Rock</a> and a childhood friend of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Addam is one of Tywin's bannermen present at Harrenhal and departs the ruined castle a day before his liege lord. He survives the Battle of the Fords and joins Tywin in the march south when the latter receives word of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a>'s imminent attack. After the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>, Addam is made Commander of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"City Watch","link":"City Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[City Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/City_Watch" title="City Watch">City Watch</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After Jaime returns to King's Landing, he practices with Addam for a while, but fearing that Addam may get drunk and reveal what poor fighter Jaime is now, he moves to practice with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ilyn Payne","link":"Ilyn Payne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ilyn Payne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17426863456cca6f4f3e4f" href="/wiki/Ilyn_Payne" title="Ilyn Payne">Ilyn Payne</a>.
</p>
