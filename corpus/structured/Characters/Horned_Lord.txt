<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80436601456cca75180733" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Horned King, a.k.a. the Horned Lord, was an ancient King-Beyond-the-Wall who raided south of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80436601456cca75180733" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>. A quote is attributed to him: "Sorcery is a sword without a hilt. There is no safe way to grasp it."
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Horned Lord lived about one or two thousand years ago.
