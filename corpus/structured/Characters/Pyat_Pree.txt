<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Pyat Pree is one of the alleged warlocks of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qarth","link":"Qarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/Qarth" title="Qarth">Qarth</a>, a wise man who claims mystical powers. Together with the rest of his order, he dwells in the mysterious <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of the Undying","link":"House of the Undying","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of the Undying]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/House_of_the_Undying" title="House of the Undying">House of the Undying</a>, located within the city of Qarth. Even though the power of and respect for the warlocks has considerably waned over years and many dismiss them as little more than charlatans performing parlor tricks, tradition and custom has allowed Pyat Pree a place among <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Thirteen","link":"the Thirteen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Thirteen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/The_Thirteen" title="The Thirteen">the Thirteen</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Pyat Pree is present when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Thirteen","link":"the Thirteen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Thirteen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/The_Thirteen" title="The Thirteen">the Thirteen</a> greet <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> at the gates of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qarth","link":"Qarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/Qarth" title="Qarth">Qarth</a>, and she and her people are allowed into the city.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

Using magic, he chains Daenerys, telling her she will stay with him for an eternity to fuel his magic; however, as he does this, he inadvertently provokes the dragons. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Pyat Pree is a pale man with blue lips, like most of his order. He, along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Xaro Xhoan Daxos","link":"Xaro Xhoan Daxos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Xaro Xhoan Daxos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos" title="Xaro Xhoan Daxos">Xaro Xhoan Daxos</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Quaithe","link":"Quaithe","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Quaithe]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/Quaithe" title="Quaithe">Quaithe</a>, approach <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> in the ruined city of Vaes Tolorro (not included in the TV series) and invite her to Qarth (instead of greeting Daenerys at Qarth in the TV show). Later on he invites her to the House of the Undying, and instructs her how to move inside without getting lost.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books Pree shows no ability to conjure doubles; he doesn't steal Daenerys' dragons, nor try to imprison her, neither is he killed. He is not a member of the Thirteen.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After Daenerys steps out of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of the Undying","link":"House of the Undying","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of the Undying]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212423337456cca5f7000bd" href="/wiki/House_of_the_Undying" title="House of the Undying">House of the Undying</a>, Pyat Pree is angry at her for burning the palace and injuring his masters, the Undying. He draws a knife and charges at her, but is stopped by Drogon, Jhogo, and Rakharo.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Daenerys soon learns that Pyat Pree is gathering the surviving warlocks together to work ill on her. Thus she has no choice but to leave Qarth urgently. Currently Pyat Pree is still after her.
</p>
