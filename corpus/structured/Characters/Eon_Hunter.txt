Eon Hunter is a minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Barrington Cullen and only appears in A Golden Crown. Eon Hunter is the Lord of Longbow Hall and the head of House Hunter, a vassal family to House Arryn of the Eyrie. Biography 

 Season 1 

Lord Hunter is present when Tyrion Lannister demands a trial by combat for the charges of murdering Jon Arryn and conspiring to murder Bran Stark. After a young knight offers to champion for Lady Lysa Arryn, Lord Hunter asks for the chance to avenge the death of Lord Arryn as well.  In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Eon Hunter is the head of House Hunter of Longbow Hall and has been so for nearly sixty years. Half-crippled by gout, Lord Eon is older than Jon Arryn and has three quarrelsome sons: Gilwood, Eustace and Harlan. He's one of the many suitors of Lady Lysa.
