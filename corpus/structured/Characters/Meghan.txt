Meghan is an unseen character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. History 

 Background 

Meghan was a friend of Rodrik Forrester and Namond. The three of them would play together on Red Rob's farm when they were children. The Sword in the Darkness 

Rodrik travels to Red Rob's abandoned farm to meet in secret with Gwyn Whitehill.
