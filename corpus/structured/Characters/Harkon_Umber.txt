<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Harkon Umber is an early member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Umber","link":"House Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-28987561156cca7ecb2497" href="/wiki/House_Umber" title="House Umber">House Umber</a>. He was the first born son of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hothor Umber","link":"Hothor Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hothor Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-28987561156cca7ecb2497" href="/wiki/Hothor_Umber" title="Hothor Umber">Hothor Umber</a> and his wife Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Amarylis Umber","link":"Amarylis Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Amarylis Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-28987561156cca7ecb2497" href="/wiki/Amarylis_Umber" title="Amarylis Umber">Amarylis Umber</a>. He was tragically killed in a bear hunt at the age of fourteen, leaving no heirs behind
</p>
