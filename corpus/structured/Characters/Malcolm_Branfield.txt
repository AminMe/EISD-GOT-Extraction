<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="2">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">As a young man, Malcolm was the black sheep of the Branfield family. He liked to roam the countryside in the style of a hedge knight, refusing his father’s offers of marriage and lands. Malcolm <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fought","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion|fought]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192214136456cca6b899bbf" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">fought</a> bravely alongside his brothers when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Branfield","link":"House Branfield","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Branfield]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192214136456cca6b899bbf" href="/wiki/House_Branfield" title="House Branfield">House Branfield</a> fell; yet he and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his sister","link":"Elissa Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elissa Forrester|his sister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192214136456cca6b899bbf" href="/wiki/Elissa_Forrester" title="Elissa Forrester">his sister</a> were the only two survivors. Malcolm now lives with Lady Elissa’s new family at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192214136456cca6b899bbf" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>, though he still tends to disappear for weeks at a stretch. Malcolm has fathered no children, thus he is the last of the Branfield line.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Malcolm Branfield - Game Codex Entry<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192214136456cca6b899bbf" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
