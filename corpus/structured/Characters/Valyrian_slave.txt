<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is one of the slaves held in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meereen","link":"Meereen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meereen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155012653456cca84204e4e" href="/wiki/Meereen" title="Meereen">Meereen</a> during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155012653456cca84204e4e" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a>'s conquest of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Slaver's Bay","link":"Slaver's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slaver's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155012653456cca84204e4e" href="/wiki/Slaver's_Bay" title="Slaver's Bay">Slaver's Bay</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155012653456cca84204e4e" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is one of the slaves that are persuaded by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Worm","link":"Grey Worm","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Worm]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155012653456cca84204e4e" href="/wiki/Grey_Worm" title="Grey Worm">Grey Worm</a> to revolt against the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Masters","link":"Great Masters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Masters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155012653456cca84204e4e" href="/wiki/Great_Masters" title="Great Masters">Great Masters</a> of Meereen, despite being hesitant at first and claiming the revolts always end with the Masters winning and punishing the slaves. He also points out that the slaves have no training and no weapons.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Oathkeeper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-155012653456cca84204e4e" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
