<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">This soldier is loyal to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon of Dragonstone","link":"House Baratheon of Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon of Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone" title="House Baratheon of Dragonstone">House Baratheon of Dragonstone</a>, serving <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a>'s army in his campaign to retake the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"North","link":"The North","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The North|North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">North</a> from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bolton","link":"House Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" href="/wiki/House_Bolton" title="House Bolton">House Bolton</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Following the burning of Stannis' daughter <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shireen","link":"Shireen Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shireen Baratheon|Shireen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" href="/wiki/Shireen_Baratheon" title="Shireen Baratheon">Shireen</a>, this soldier discovers that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Selyse Baratheon","link":"Selyse Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Selyse Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" href="/wiki/Selyse_Baratheon" title="Selyse Baratheon">Selyse Baratheon</a> hanged herself in anguish over her death. He reports this to Stannis, and leads him to his wife's corpse. Once there, Stannis orders this soldier to cut Selyse down. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">This soldier later participates in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Winterfell","link":"Battle of Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Winterfell" title="Battle of Winterfell">Battle of Winterfell</a> and is presumably killed by the Bolton army.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Mother's Mercy]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-3124442856cca7d12a71a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
