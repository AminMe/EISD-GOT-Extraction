ABaratheon soldier is an unnamed minor character in the fifth season. He appears only in Mother's Mercy. Biography 

 Background 

This soldier is loyal to House Baratheon of Dragonstone, serving Stannis Baratheon's army in his campaign to retake the North from House Bolton. Season 5 

Following the burning of Stannis' daughter Shireen, this soldier discovers that Selyse Baratheon hanged herself in anguish over her death. He reports this to Stannis, and leads him to his wife's corpse. Once there, Stannis orders this soldier to cut Selyse down.
