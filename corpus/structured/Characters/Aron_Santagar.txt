<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128465182256cca6f3aff0c" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Aron Santagar is the Master-at-Arms of the Red Keep. He's described by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Cassel","link":"Rodrik Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128465182256cca6f3aff0c" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Cassel" title="Rodrik Cassel">Rodrik Cassel</a> as a vain but honest man. In the first book, Ser Aron is secretly approached by Ser Rodrik to see if he might recognize the dagger used by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"assassin","link":"Catspaw assassin","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catspaw assassin|assassin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128465182256cca6f3aff0c" href="/wiki/Catspaw_assassin" title="Catspaw assassin">assassin</a> sent to murder <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128465182256cca6f3aff0c" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a>. He also participates in the Tourney of the Hand.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Aron perishes in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riot of King's Landing","link":"Riot of King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riot of King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128465182256cca6f3aff0c" href="/wiki/Riot_of_King's_Landing" title="Riot of King's Landing">Riot of King's Landing</a>. In the episode several courtiers are shown to be attacked by the rioting mob; it is unclear if any of these were meant to be Ser Aron.
</p>
