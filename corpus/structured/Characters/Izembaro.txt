<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Izembaro is a larger-than-life actor who specializes in portraying drunken aristocrats. He is a huge presence on stage and rather nasty to his company when off-stage.
</p>
