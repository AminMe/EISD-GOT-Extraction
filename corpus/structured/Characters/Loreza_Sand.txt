 Background 

Loreza Sand is the last of the eight bastard daughters of Prince Oberyn Martell, known as the Sand Snakes. Her mother is Oberyn's paramour, Ellaria Sand. Season 4 

Oberyn mentions his eight bastard daughters to Queen Cersei Lannister.\First of His Name\\/ref} data-rte-instance130814-127242085856cca67e62cd5 classplaceholder placeholder-ext src=data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw typeext / Family Tree
