<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Rennick is a soldier on sentry duty at the camp of the Lannister forces commanded by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stafford Lannister","link":"Stafford Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stafford Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Stafford_Lannister" title="Stafford Lannister">Stafford Lannister</a> on their way to reinforce Tywin Lannister's army in the Riverlands.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is on duty with a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannister guardsman","link":"Lannister guardsman (Garden of Bones)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lannister guardsman (Garden of Bones)|Lannister guardsman]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Lannister_guardsman_(Garden_of_Bones)" title="Lannister guardsman (Garden of Bones)">Lannister guardsman</a> at the perimeter of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stafford Lannister","link":"Stafford Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stafford Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Stafford_Lannister" title="Stafford Lannister">Stafford Lannister</a>'s camp prior to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Oxcross","link":"Battle of Oxcross","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Oxcross]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Oxcross" title="Battle of Oxcross">Battle of Oxcross</a>. They debate on who is the greatest swordsman in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>. He argues against Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> who is big rather than skilled, and Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Loras Tyrell","link":"Loras Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Loras Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Loras_Tyrell" title="Loras Tyrell">Loras Tyrell</a> who is just too pretty, though he does give credit to "our man Jaime, if he ever gets out".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">They both hear that the horses are spooked, but they aren't too concerned. Then he hears something and walks out into the darkness. The other guardsman follows hesitantly. When Rennick starts to draw his sword the man thinks they are being attacked, but Rennick merely passes gas and starts laughing at him.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Rennick 2.jpg|thumb|Rennick looks up as a guardsman screams.]]","title":"Rennick_2.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Rennick 2","caption":"Rennick looks up as a guardsman screams.","captionParsed":"Rennick looks up as a guardsman screams."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" alt="" src="http://vignette4.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/7/7b/Rennick_2.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120321124446" width="180" height="99" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Rennick 2.jpg" data-image-key="Rennick_2.jpg"  type="image" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">As Rennick stops to urinate, the guardsman sees that the horses are getting more upset not less, but Rennick is not paying attention until the man screams his name. Rennick looks up just in time to see the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"direwolf","link":"Direwolves","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Direwolves|direwolf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Direwolves" title="Direwolves">direwolf</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Wind","link":"Grey Wind","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Wind]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" href="/wiki/Grey_Wind" title="Grey Wind">Grey Wind</a> leaping out of the darkness at him. In the subsequent attack by the Stark army the camp is taken completely by surprise and the Lannister army is annihilated.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The next morning the camera pans across the battlefield, showing Rennick's body lying on the ground. He has lost part of his face.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67358454856cca6d100236" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
