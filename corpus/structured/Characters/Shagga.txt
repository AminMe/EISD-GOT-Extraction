<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Shagga, son of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dolf","link":"Dolf","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dolf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Dolf" title="Dolf">Dolf</a>, is the leader of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stone Crows","link":"Stone Crows","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stone Crows]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Stone_Crows" title="Stone Crows">Stone Crows</a>, one of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"hill tribes","link":"hill tribes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[hill tribes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Hill_tribes" title="Hill tribes">hill tribes</a> who live in the foothills of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mountains of the Moon","link":"Mountains of the Moon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mountains of the Moon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Mountains_of_the_Moon" title="Mountains of the Moon">Mountains of the Moon</a> on the western border of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>. He is a notable warrior who holds fealty to no <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"lord","link":"Lordship","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lordship|lord]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Lordship" title="Lordship">lord</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Shagga leads the party of tribesmen which finds <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a> traveling out of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Vale","link":"the Vale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Vale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/The_Vale" title="The Vale" class="mw-redirect">the Vale</a> into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>. Shagga intends to rob the travellers and take Tyrion captive as a fool. Tyrion offers an alliance between the hill tribes and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a> on the basis that they share mutual enemies, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Arryn","link":"House Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/House_Arryn" title="House Arryn">House Arryn</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a>, promising gold and weapons. Shagga agrees to the alliance and escorts Tyrion to the Lannister army camped in the Riverlands. The force is led by Tyrion's father, Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a>. They are joined by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Timett","link":"Timett","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Timett]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Timett" title="Timett">Timett</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Burned Men","link":"Burned Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Burned Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Burned_Men" title="Burned Men">Burned Men</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Chella","link":"Chella","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Chella]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Chella" title="Chella">Chella</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Black Ears","link":"Black Ears","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Black Ears]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Black_Ears" title="Black Ears">Black Ears</a> along the way. There are also clansmen from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Moon Brothers","link":"Moon Brothers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Moon Brothers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Moon_Brothers" title="Moon Brothers">Moon Brothers</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Painted Dogs","link":"Painted Dogs","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Painted Dogs]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Painted_Dogs" title="Painted Dogs">Painted Dogs</a> in their group. Tywin accepts the alliance and Shagga's insistence that they fight alongside Tyrion on the battlefield.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Pointy End]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Tyrion reports to his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"father","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister|father]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">father</a> and his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"uncle","link":"Kevan Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kevan Lannister|uncle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Kevan_Lannister" title="Kevan Lannister">uncle</a> that while at camp, a Moon Brother stabbed a Stone Crow over a sausage, at which the Moon Brother was set upon by three Stone Crows who opened his throat. Bronn managed to restrain Shagga from cutting off the dead man's genitals - "which was fortunate" - but <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ulf","link":"Ulf","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ulf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Ulf" title="Ulf">Ulf</a> of the Moon Brothers demanded blood money from the Stone Crows, which Shagga and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gunthor","link":"Gunthor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gunthor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Gunthor" title="Gunthor">Gunthor</a> refused to pay. Tywin criticized Tyrion for the lack of discipline in these new allies he had brought to their side.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Baelor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Shagga demands two axes from the Lannister stores to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kevan Lannister","link":"Kevan Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kevan Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Kevan_Lannister" title="Kevan Lannister">Kevan Lannister</a>'s chagrin. He and his men actually fight well at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Green Fork","link":"Battle of the Green Fork","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Green Fork]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Green_Fork" title="Battle of the Green Fork">Battle of the Green Fork</a> and win a convincing victory over their foes. Tyrion fails to join them after he is accidentally knocked unconscious by his own men in the rush to the fight. Shagga and his troops join in the post-battle looting of the fallen bodies.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Baelor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Shagga, son of Dolf, is a member of the Stone Crows, one of the mountain clans. He is a strong, large man who prefers to wield two weapons in combat. His favored taunt is to threaten to remove someone's manhood and feed it to "the goats" -even when there are no goats in the vicinity. He is not the most intelligent man, but is extremely dangerous in battle.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Tyrion and Bronn are on their way leaving the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale","link":"Vale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Vale" title="Vale" class="mw-redirect">Vale</a> for the Lannister camp, Bronn captures a goat for food, and then they are met by the Stone Crows clan including <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gunthor son of Gurn","link":"Gunthor","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gunthor|Gunthor son of Gurn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Gunthor" title="Gunthor">Gunthor son of Gurn</a> and Shagga, who claim the goat to be theirs - as Shagga claims, "our mountain, our goat." And then Tyrion persuades them into joining him as depicted in the TV series.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>, Tyrion recognized that the hill tribes, while ferocious, are inexperienced at siege-warfare, so he sent them south across the river to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingswood","link":"Kingswood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingswood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164150240556cca6695c3bc" href="/wiki/Kingswood" title="Kingswood">Kingswood</a> to harass the scouts of Stannis Baratheon's army as they approached the city. They performed quite well, destroying Stannis's scouts and screening forces to such an extent that he had no warning of the approach of Tywin Lannister’s massive reinforcements approaching the city.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After the battle Tywin pays off the hill tribes and most return home to the Mountains of the Moon in the Vale. Shagga and the Stone Crows, however, decide to stay in the Kingswood, feeling that there are better opportunities for them there.
</p>
