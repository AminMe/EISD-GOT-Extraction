</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Donnel Hill</b>​ is a minor character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fourth","link":"Season 4","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4|fourth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">fourth</a> season. He is a member of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>.
</p>
<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Donnel Hill is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bastard","link":"Bastardy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bastardy|bastard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Bastardy" title="Bastardy">bastard</a> from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Westerlands","link":"the Westerlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Westerlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/The_Westerlands" title="The Westerlands">the Westerlands</a>. It is unknown which <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"noble house","link":"noble house","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[noble house]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Noble_house" title="Noble house">noble house</a> he hailed from.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">​<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Castle Black","link":"Battle of Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Castle_Black" title="Battle of Castle Black">Battle of Castle Black</a>, Hill takes part in defending <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> against Mance Rayder's forces. When Castle Black's inner gate is threatened by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"giant","link":"Giants","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Giants|giant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Giants" title="Giants">giant</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg","link":"Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg" title="Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg">Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg</a>, he accompanies <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grenn","link":"Grenn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grenn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Grenn" title="Grenn">Grenn</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cooper","link":"Cooper","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cooper]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Cooper" title="Cooper">Cooper</a>, and other black brothers to defend it. Although he initially wavers and considers fleeing upon seeing the Giant, he is convinced by Grenn to stay and fulfill his oath to the Night's Watch. They successfully kill the giant, but all of the black brothers are slain in the process. Their bodies are discovered by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Samwell Tarly","link":"Samwell Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Samwell Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Samwell_Tarly" title="Samwell Tarly">Samwell Tarly</a> after the battle.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Watchers on the Wall]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> After the arrival of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> and his forces, though not seen Donnel is among the numerous bodies of fallen black brothers burned in Castle Black's courtyard.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Children]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2>​In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels there is a steward of the Night's Watch named Donnel Hill, nicknamed "Sweet Donnel Hill". He claims to be a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bastard","link":"bastard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bastard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Bastard" title="Bastard" class="mw-redirect">bastard</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ranging","link":"Great Ranging","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Ranging|ranging]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Great_Ranging" title="Great Ranging">ranging</a> force camps at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Fist of the First Men","link":"Fist of the First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fist of the First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Fist_of_the_First_Men" title="Fist of the First Men">Fist of the First Men</a>, Donnel is among the brothers who plot to murder <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jeor Mormont","link":"Jeor Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jeor Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Jeor_Mormont" title="Jeor Mormont">Jeor Mormont</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sam","link":"Sam","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sam]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Sam" title="Sam">Sam</a> and other prominent brothers to end the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Ranging","link":"Great Ranging","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Ranging]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Great_Ranging" title="Great Ranging">Great Ranging</a> and desert. The sudden snowfall and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"attack of the Others","link":"Battle of the Fist of the First Men","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Fist of the First Men|attack of the Others]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Fist_of_the_First_Men" title="Battle of the Fist of the First Men">attack of the Others</a> foils the plan. However, during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","link":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mutiny at Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep" title="Mutiny at Craster's Keep">Mutiny at Craster's Keep</a> Donnel sides with those who are loyal to Mormont. He and the other Loyalists make it to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a> long before <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon","link":"Jon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Jon" title="Jon">Jon</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sam","link":"Sam","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sam]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Sam" title="Sam">Sam</a>. He survives the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Castle Black","link":"Battle of Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Castle_Black" title="Battle of Castle Black">Battle of Castle Black</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Donnel is one of the four brothers who, at Jon's command, shoot arrows at Mance Rayder (actually, it is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Bones","link":"Lord of Bones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Bones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-78172301356cca69d3b5f5" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Bones" title="Lord of Bones">Lord of Bones</a> in disguise) while he is executed by burning, in order to grant him a quick and painless death.
</p>
