</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Willem Lannister</b> is a character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"third season","link":"Season 3","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3|third season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">third season</a>. Along with his brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Martyn","link":"Martyn Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Martyn Lannister|Martyn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Martyn_Lannister" title="Martyn Lannister">Martyn</a>, he is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"squire","link":"squire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[squire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Squire" title="Squire">squire</a> in the Lannister army. Both are younger sons of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kevan Lannister","link":"Kevan Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kevan Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Kevan_Lannister" title="Kevan Lannister">Kevan Lannister</a>, and younger brothers of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lancel Lannister","link":"Lancel Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lancel Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Lancel_Lannister" title="Lancel Lannister" class="mw-redirect">Lancel Lannister</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Willem, along with his brother Martyn, is captured by the forces of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Edmure Tully","link":"Edmure Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Edmure Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Edmure_Tully" title="Edmure Tully">Edmure Tully</a> following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Stone Mill","link":"Battle of Stone Mill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Stone Mill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Stone_Mill" title="Battle of Stone Mill">Battle of Stone Mill</a>. The two are kept as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"hostage","link":"hostage","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[hostage]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Hostage" title="Hostage">hostages</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>, the seat of the Tullys.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> visits Riverrun to attend the funerals of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a>, Willem and Martyn are visited in their cell by Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talisa Stark","link":"Talisa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talisa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Talisa_Stark" title="Talisa Stark">Talisa Stark</a>, who tends to Martyn's wounded hand. Willem looks in silence while Martyn asks if the tales told among the Lannisters about Robb turning into a wolf at night or devouring his enemies are true.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"Walk of Punishment\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Martyn and Willem are murdered inside their cells by Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickard Karstark","link":"Rickard Karstark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickard Karstark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Rickard_Karstark" title="Rickard Karstark">Rickard Karstark</a> in retribution for his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"son","link":"Torrhen Karstark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Torrhen Karstark|son]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Torrhen_Karstark" title="Torrhen Karstark">son</a> being killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>, and in defiance of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>'s orders. Rickard is consequently executed for treason by Robb, causing the Karstarks to desert Robb's cause.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Kissed by Fire]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Willem and Martyn are the twin sons of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kevan Lannister","link":"Kevan Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kevan Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Kevan_Lannister" title="Kevan Lannister">Kevan Lannister</a>, and younger brothers of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lancel Lannister","link":"Lancel Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lancel Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Lancel_Lannister" title="Lancel Lannister" class="mw-redirect">Lancel Lannister</a>. He and his cousin Tion Frey - son of Genna Lannister and Emmon Frey - are captured at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198504023756cca62df29d0" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a>. Later, Willem and Tion are killed by Rickard Karstark.
</p>
