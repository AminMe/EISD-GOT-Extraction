<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207933817656cca5f4a2fab" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Grenn is a tall, well-built young man. He is nicknamed the "Aurochs" because of his relatively large size and slow speed. He befriends Jon Snow, Pypar, and Samwell Tarly and is trained alongside them by Ser Alliser Thorne. They have another friend, Halder, who is also noted as being large and takes part in their practice sessions. For the TV show, the character of Halder has been eliminated and some of his characteristics and story functions combined with Grenn's.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Grenn and about dozen more remain loyal to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jeor Mormont","link":"Jeor Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jeor Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207933817656cca5f4a2fab" href="/wiki/Jeor_Mormont" title="Jeor Mormont">Jeor Mormont</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","link":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mutiny at Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207933817656cca5f4a2fab" href="/wiki/Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep" title="Mutiny at Craster's Keep">Mutiny at Craster's Keep</a>. Being outnumbered 1:2 by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Betrayers","link":"Betrayers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Betrayers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207933817656cca5f4a2fab" href="/wiki/Betrayers" title="Betrayers" class="mw-redirect">Betrayers</a>, they have to retreat, reluctantly leaving <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sam","link":"Sam","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sam]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207933817656cca5f4a2fab" href="/wiki/Sam" title="Sam">Sam</a> behind. They make it to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207933817656cca5f4a2fab" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a> long before Jon and Sam.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Grenn doesn't perish during the Battle of Castle Black. Instead, it is Donal Noye, the castle's one-armed blacksmith and four others, who make a stand against Mag the Mighty and die slaying the giant.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Jon leaves the Wall to survey the situation at Castle Black, Grenn is left in charge as opposed to Dolorous Edd. Grenn survives the battle.
</p>
