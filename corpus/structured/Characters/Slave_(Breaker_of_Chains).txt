<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18576023756cca7c61e46b" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is one of the many slaves that gather over the walls of Meereen when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18576023756cca7c61e46b" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> and her army appear before the city gates to lay <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"siege","link":"Siege of Meereen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Meereen|siege]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18576023756cca7c61e46b" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Meereen" title="Siege of Meereen">siege</a> to the city. After the death of Meereenese champion <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oznak zo Pahl","link":"Oznak zo Pahl","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oznak zo Pahl]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18576023756cca7c61e46b" href="/wiki/Oznak_zo_Pahl" title="Oznak zo Pahl">Oznak zo Pahl</a> and Daenerys' words to the slaves present, he watches as a slave picks up one of the hundreds of broken slave collars thrown into the city in catapulted barrels. He then picks up the collar that belonged to a former slave and glances at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his master","link":"Great Master 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Master 2|his master]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18576023756cca7c61e46b" href="/wiki/Great_Master_2" title="Great Master 2" class="mw-redirect">his master</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Breaker of Chains]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18576023756cca7c61e46b" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
