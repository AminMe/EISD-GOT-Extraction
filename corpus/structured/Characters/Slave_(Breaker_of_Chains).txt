A slave is an unnamed minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Joshua Sher and appears in Breaker of Chains. Biography 

 Season 4 

He is one of the many slaves that gather over the walls of Meereen when Daenerys Targaryen and her army appear before the city gates to lay siege to the city. After the death of Meereenese champion Oznak zo Pahl and Daenerys' words to the slaves present, he watches as a slave picks up one of the hundreds of broken slave collars thrown into the city in catapulted barrels.
