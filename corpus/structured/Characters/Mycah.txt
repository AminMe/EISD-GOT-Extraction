<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Mycah is a butcher's son who has befriended <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He travels the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsroad","link":"Kingsroad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsroad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Kingsroad" title="Kingsroad">Kingsroad</a> with his father as part of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>'s retinue.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When he is encountered by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a> practicing sword-fighting with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a>, Joffrey confronts him for 'pretending to be a knight'. Despite his protests that the weapon he was using was only a stick, Joffrey forces him to pick it up. Joffrey sadistically begins pressing his sword against the boy's cheek, which prompts Arya to attack him. Mycah manages to escape but later is killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Hound","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane|the Hound]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">the Hound</a> under the orders of the prince much to Ned Stark's horror.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsroad]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brotherhood Without Banners","link":"Brotherhood Without Banners","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brotherhood Without Banners]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Brotherhood_Without_Banners" title="Brotherhood Without Banners">Brotherhood Without Banners</a> sentences the Hound to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"trial by combat","link":"trial by combat","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[trial by combat]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Trial_by_combat" title="Trial by combat">trial by combat</a> for Mycah's murder after Arya accuses him.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[And Now His Watch is Ended]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Mycah is thirteen years old. He falls in with King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>'s party as it returns to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> and befriends <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After the Hound defeats <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Beric Dondarrion","link":"Beric Dondarrion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beric Dondarrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Beric_Dondarrion" title="Beric Dondarrion">Beric Dondarrion</a>, he teases Arya by telling her (without the slightest remorse or regret) "I rode him [Mycah] down and cut him in half, and laughed".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jeyne Poole","link":"Jeyne Poole","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jeyne Poole]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-170712020156cca63799290" href="/wiki/Jeyne_Poole" title="Jeyne Poole">Jeyne Poole</a>, Mycah has been cut up in so many pieces that his body was brought back to his father in a bag. At first the poor man had thought it was a slaughtered pig.
</p>
