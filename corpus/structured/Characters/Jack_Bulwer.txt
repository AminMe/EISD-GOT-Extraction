<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kegs","link":"Kegs","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kegs]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Kegs" title="Kegs">Kegs</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mully","link":"Mully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Mully" title="Mully">Mully</a>, he travels to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"brothel","link":"Prostitution","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Prostitution|brothel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Prostitution" title="Prostitution">brothel</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mole's Town","link":"Mole's Town","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mole's Town]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Mole's_Town" title="Mole's Town">Mole's Town</a>. They sit drinking and listening to a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"whore","link":"Mole's Town whore","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mole's Town whore|whore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Mole's_Town_whore" title="Mole's Town whore">whore</a> belching out the melodies to popular songs. Jack correctly guesses that she is belching "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Bear and the Maiden Fair","link":"The Bear and the Maiden Fair (song)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair (song)|The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair_(song)" title="The Bear and the Maiden Fair (song)">The Bear and the Maiden Fair</a>". She compliments him on his hearing, but quips that it is a shame that he has a hangnail for a penis. They are later slaughtered when a group of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wildlings","link":"Free Folk","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Folk|wildlings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Free_Folk" title="Free Folk">wildlings</a> led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tormund","link":"Tormund","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tormund]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Tormund" title="Tormund">Tormund</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Styr","link":"Styr","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Styr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Styr" title="Styr">Styr</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"attack","link":"Sack of Mole's Town","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of Mole's Town|attack]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Sack_of_Mole's_Town" title="Sack of Mole's Town">attack</a> the village. News of their deaths soon reaches <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Mountain and the Viper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2>In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130113220356cca79ed9d5d" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Black Jack Bulwer is not killed in a wildling attack on Mole's Town. He survives the Battle of Castle Black.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In "A Dance with Dragons", Jon sends Jack Bulwer and eight more rangers to find Tormund. He and two of the others (Garth Greyfeather and Hairy Hal) are killed by the Wildling raider known as the Weeper, and their eyeless heads are brought to Castle Black.
</p>
