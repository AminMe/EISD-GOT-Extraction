</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Jason Mallister</b> is the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Seagard","link":"Lord of Seagard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Seagard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Seagard" title="Lord of Seagard">Lord of Seagard</a> and the head of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Mallister","link":"House Mallister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Mallister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/House_Mallister" title="House Mallister">House Mallister</a>. He is a loyal bannerman of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, Lord Jason led the a victorious defense of his ancestral seat of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seagard","link":"Seagard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seagard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Seagard" title="Seagard">Seagard</a> against an <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"attack","link":"Battle of Seagard","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Seagard|attack]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Seagard" title="Battle of Seagard">attack</a> by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> forces led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Greyjoy","link":"Rodrik Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Greyjoy" title="Rodrik Greyjoy">Rodrik Greyjoy</a> and killed him.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Greyjoy Rebellion (Histories & Lore)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2>In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Lord Jason Mallister is a loyal bannerman of House Tully. He has at least one son, Patrek Mallister. As a young knight, he was defeated by Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegar Targaryen","link":"Rhaegar Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegar Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Rhaegar_Targaryen" title="Rhaegar Targaryen">Rhaegar Targaryen</a> in a tourney at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>. During the early stage of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, he attended the wedding of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Tully","link":"Catelyn Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Tully" title="Catelyn Tully" class="mw-redirect">Catelyn Tully</a>, giving them lavish gifts. He participated in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Trident","link":"Battle of the Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Trident" title="Battle of the Trident">Battle of the Trident</a>, slaying three of Prince Rhaegar's bannermen, thus avenging the death of his kinsman Jeffory Mallister, who had been killed under orders of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mad King","link":"Aerys II Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II Targaryen|Mad King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Aerys_II_Targaryen" title="Aerys II Targaryen">Mad King</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Jason takes part in the Tourney of the Hand and later, during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>, takes part in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a> and the liberation of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a> at the Battle of the Camps. He returns to Seagard after <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> is declared the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King in the North","link":"King in the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King in the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/King_in_the_North" title="King in the North">King in the North</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Jason is one of the seven nobles who are chosen to launch the funeral boat of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a>. Jason meets King Robb on his march north to the Twins. Robb orders him to send two ships into the Neck, one carrying <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Galbart Glover","link":"Galbart Glover","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Galbart Glover]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Galbart_Glover" title="Galbart Glover">Galbart Glover</a> and the other <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maege Mormont","link":"Maege Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maege Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Maege_Mormont" title="Maege Mormont">Maege Mormont</a>, to find <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greywater Watch","link":"Greywater Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greywater Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178798556956cca6be0cceb" href="/wiki/Greywater_Watch" title="Greywater Watch">Greywater Watch</a>.
</p>
