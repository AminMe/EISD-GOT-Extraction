<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Margaery's handmaiden rides with her mistress in a palanquin as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon|King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">King</a>'s party returns to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a> from services at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Sept of Baelor","link":"Great Sept of Baelor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Sept of Baelor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/Great_Sept_of_Baelor" title="Great Sept of Baelor">Great Sept of Baelor</a>. Somewhere in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Flea Bottom","link":"Flea Bottom","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Flea Bottom]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/Flea_Bottom" title="Flea Bottom">Flea Bottom</a>, Lady Margaery stops the procession and disembarks. The handmaiden cautiously follows. Margaery begins to cross a muddy path into an orphanage when her handmaiden warns she will ruin her dress, a concern Margaery brushes off. As Lady Margaery speaks with children in an orphanage, the handmaiden hands out toys, suggesting she was aware of at least some part of Margaery's plan.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Valar Dohaeris]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Margaery Tyrell arrives in King's Landing to wed King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a> accompanied by three handmaidens, Elinor Tyrell, Megga Tyrell, and Alla Tyrell. The girls are all daughters of different cadet branches of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a>. Elinor Tyrell is the only one of the three to have reached puberty. Other companions of Margaery include Meredyth Crane and Alyce Graceford.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Both of the handmaidens seen in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valar Dohaeris","link":"Valar Dohaeris","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valar Dohaeris]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/Valar_Dohaeris" title="Valar Dohaeris">Valar Dohaeris</a>", as well as the two attending Lady Olenna in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"And Now His Watch is Ended","link":"And Now His Watch is Ended","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[And Now His Watch is Ended]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4085585556cca76295164" href="/wiki/And_Now_His_Watch_is_Ended" title="And Now His Watch is Ended">And Now His Watch is Ended</a>" are obviously adults, so that particular detail appears to have been ignored. Their relationship to Margaery might be the same, however, since those two refer to Olenna as "nana", which presumably means she is their grandmother.
</p>
