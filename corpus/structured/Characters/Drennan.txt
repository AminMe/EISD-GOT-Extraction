</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Drennan</b> is a recurring character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"second season","link":"Season 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2|second season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">second season</a>. Drennan is an <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> raider and part of the crew of the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sea Bitch","link":"Sea Bitch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sea Bitch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Sea_Bitch" title="Sea Bitch">Sea Bitch</a></i>. He was killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Osha","link":"Osha","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Osha]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Osha" title="Osha">Osha</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Drennan is a crewman of the <i>Sea Bitch</i>, the ship Theon's father Balon Greyjoy gave him to raid the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stony Shore","link":"Stony Shore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stony Shore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Stony_Shore" title="Stony Shore">Stony Shore</a>. Theon used an attack on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Torrhen's Square","link":"Torrhen's Square","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Torrhen's Square]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Torrhen's_Square" title="Torrhen's Square">Torrhen's Square</a> as a diversion to drain forces from Winterfell. Drennan then takes part in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"seizing","link":"Fall of Winterfell","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fall of Winterfell|seizing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Fall_of_Winterfell" title="Fall of Winterfell">seizing</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a>. When the people of the castle are brought together in the courtyard for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a> to officially surrender to Theon, Drennan is behind the kennelmaster <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Farlen","link":"Farlen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Farlen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Farlen" title="Farlen">Farlen</a>. Farlen yells insults at Theon and Drennan hits him over the head with a club.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Old Gods and the New]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Osha kills.jpg|thumb|left|Osha kills Drennan.]]","title":"Osha_kills.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Osha kills","align":"left","caption":"Osha kills Drennan.","captionParsed":"Osha kills Drennan."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/6/6d/Osha_kills.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120525145919" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb alignLeft withCaption" data-image-name="Osha kills.jpg" data-image-key="Osha_kills.jpg"  type="image" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Drennan is one of Theon's men. When Winterfell was taken, he and another man named Squint rape <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Palla","link":"Palla","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Palla]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-194640678256cca6ec56acb" href="/wiki/Palla" title="Palla">Palla</a>, the daughter of Farlen. Theon orders them whipped to show the people of Winterfell that he will treat them fairly as their new lord. To ease the escape of Bran, Rickon, Hodor, Summer and Shaggydog, Osha comes to him with a plate of food. He is found slumped over a table, with his pants around his ankles and his throat cut with his own knife.
</p>
