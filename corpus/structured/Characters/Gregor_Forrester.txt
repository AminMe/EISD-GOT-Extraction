<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="2">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lord Forrester is the Lord of Ironrath and a bannerman of House Stark. As a youth, he famously arm-wrestled <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Galbart Glover","link":"Galbart Glover","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Galbart Glover]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Galbart_Glover" title="Galbart Glover">Galbart Glover</a> for the privilege of becoming the man's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"squire","link":"squire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[squire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Squire" title="Squire">squire</a>; Glover later joked that he'd gladly lose the match all over again, for Gregor was a talented warrior. Gregor married <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Elissa Branfield","link":"Elissa Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elissa Forrester|Elissa Branfield]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Elissa_Forrester" title="Elissa Forrester">Elissa Branfield</a>, a daughter of Southron <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen|Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">Targaryen</a> loyalists, before <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> seized the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a> Gregor ascended to the lordship after his father died at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Trident","link":"Battle of the Trident","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Trident|the Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Trident" title="Battle of the Trident">the Trident</a>, and has led House Forrester for sixteen years. <img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Gregor Forrester - </p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He is the father of six healthy children bored by Elissa Forrester: <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester|Rodrik]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Asher","link":"Asher Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Asher Forrester|Asher]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Asher_Forrester" title="Asher Forrester">Asher</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mira","link":"Mira Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mira Forrester|Mira]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Mira_Forrester" title="Mira Forrester">Mira</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ethan","link":"Ethan Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ethan Forrester|Ethan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Ethan_Forrester" title="Ethan Forrester">Ethan</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talia","link":"Talia Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talia Forrester|Talia]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Talia_Forrester" title="Talia Forrester">Talia</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ryon","link":"Ryon Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ryon Forrester|Ryon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Ryon_Forrester" title="Ryon Forrester">Ryon</a>.

The two grew close, sharing food, drink, and women, and when Gregor became lord he named Duncan his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"castellan","link":"castellan","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[castellan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Castellan" title="Castellan">castellan</a>. <img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>https:\/\/www.telltalegames.com\/gameofthrones\/forester<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />. After seeing his exploits in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, Lord Forrester asked Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Royland Degore","link":"Royland Degore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Royland Degore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Royland_Degore" title="Royland Degore">Royland Degore</a> to serve as Ironrath's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"master-at-arms","link":"master-at-arms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[master-at-arms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Master-at-arms" title="Master-at-arms" class="mw-redirect">master-at-arms</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Royland Degore - Game Codex Entry<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">At the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tourney","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|Tourney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">Tourney</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannisport","link":"Lannisport","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lannisport]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Lannisport" title="Lannisport">Lannisport</a>, Gregor met <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>, and the two struck up a brief conversation, during which Tyrion found him very honorable, and even then he didn't trust <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Roose Bolton","link":"Roose Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Roose Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Roose_Bolton" title="Roose Bolton">Roose Bolton</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Iron From Ice]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">His son Asher had always been a rebellious youth, finding ways to raise his father's ire. But his life took a dark turn when, at age seventeen, he fell in love with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gwyn Whitehill","link":"Gwyn Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gwyn Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Gwyn_Whitehill" title="Gwyn Whitehill">Gwyn Whitehill</a>, the eldest daughter of his father's bitter rival. He returns to camp to find Thermund dead, and Lord Forrester and Norren fighting the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Freys","link":"House Frey","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Frey|Freys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/House_Frey" title="House Frey">Freys</a>. Gregor asks what happened to Rodrik, and is saddened at his supposed demise. Three Frey men approach, and Lord Forrester saves Gared's life, but is then hit by a crossbow bolt. He curses <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a>, and Norren advises him to flee with Gared. He complies, and Norren runs back into the fray.

He orders his squire to return to Ironrath with the Forrester greatsword, and to deliver the message that "The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"North Grove","link":"North Grove","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[North Grove]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-95027205656cca61bb0089" href="/wiki/North_Grove" title="North Grove">North Grove</a> must never be lost." to Gared's uncle Duncan.
