<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The White Walker travels to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Haunted Forest","link":"Haunted Forest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Haunted Forest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/Haunted_Forest" title="Haunted Forest">Haunted Forest</a> near <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's Keep","link":"Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/Craster's_Keep" title="Craster's Keep">Craster's Keep</a> and seizes <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's last son","link":"Craster's last son","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's last son]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/Craster's_last_son" title="Craster's last son">Craster's last son</a>, who had been left out in the snow by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rast","link":"Rast","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rast]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/Rast" title="Rast">Rast</a>. He takes the child deep into the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lands of Always Winter","link":"Lands of Always Winter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lands of Always Winter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/Lands_of_Always_Winter" title="Lands of Always Winter">Lands of Always Winter</a>, where he places him on an icy altar. His <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"master","link":"White Walkers' master","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walkers' master|master]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/White_Walkers'_master" title="White Walkers' master" class="mw-redirect">master</a> then takes the baby and turns him into a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Walker","link":"White Walkers","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walkers|White Walker]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/White_Walkers" title="White Walkers">White Walker</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Oathkeeper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135734634756cca6b4ca001" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Craster's wives are convinced that the baby boys given by Craster to the Others are, in turn, transformed into new Others. However, no such event has ever been witnessed in the books so far.
</p>
