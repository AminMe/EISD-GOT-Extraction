<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-187457235856cca732561d3" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Palla is also the daughter of Farlen the kennelmaster. She is gang raped by the ironborn including <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drennan","link":"Drennan","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drennan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-187457235856cca732561d3" href="/wiki/Drennan" title="Drennan">Drennan</a> when Winterfell is captured and Theon has the men whipped to show people of Wintefell he means to be just, although they still blame him for the rape, which Theon considers unfair. Later, after Bran and Rickon escape he threatens Palla's safety if her father will not lead the search to find them with the Winterfell dogs. When the northern collaborator "Reek" - actually a disguised <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-187457235856cca732561d3" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Snow" title="Ramsay Snow" class="mw-redirect">Ramsay Snow</a> - offers to get Dreadfort men to aid Theon against a siege of Northmen led by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Cassel","link":"Rodrik Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-187457235856cca732561d3" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Cassel" title="Rodrik Cassel">Rodrik Cassel</a> and other northern nobles, he demands Palla as his reward.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sack of Winterfell","link":"Sack of Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-187457235856cca732561d3" href="/wiki/Sack_of_Winterfell" title="Sack of Winterfell">Sack of Winterfell</a>, Palla is brought to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dreadfort","link":"Dreadfort","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dreadfort]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-187457235856cca732561d3" href="/wiki/Dreadfort" title="Dreadfort" class="mw-redirect">Dreadfort</a>. Her fate is unknown.
</p>
