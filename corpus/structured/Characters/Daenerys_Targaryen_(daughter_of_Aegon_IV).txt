<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Daenerys Targaryen</b> is an unseen character in <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Game of Thrones","link":"Game of Thrones (TV series)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Game of Thrones (TV series)|Game of Thrones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Game_of_Thrones_(TV_series)" title="Game of Thrones (TV series)">Game of Thrones</a></i>. She is not expected to appear in the series, being long dead by the time it begins.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Daenerys was a Princess of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen|Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">Targaryen</a> dynasty and the namesake of the later "Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a>, First of Her Name, the Stormborn, Mother of Dragons." She lived about one century before Daenerys Stormborn. Specifically, she was the younger sister of King Daeron II, who was the grandfather of Daenerys Stormborn's own grandfather, King Aegon V. Put another way, she was the paternal aunt of Daenerys's great-grandfather, King Maekar.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Daenerys was the daughter of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon IV Targaryen","link":"Aegon IV Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon IV Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Aegon_IV_Targaryen" title="Aegon IV Targaryen">Aegon IV Targaryen</a> and Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Naerys Targaryen","link":"Naerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Naerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Naerys_Targaryen" title="Naerys Targaryen">Naerys Targaryen</a>, born on the year 182 after Aegon's Landing. She was the sister of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daeron II Targaryen","link":"Daeron II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daeron II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Daeron_II_Targaryen" title="Daeron II Targaryen">Daeron II Targaryen</a>. She also had a half-brother, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daemon Blackfyre","link":"Daemon Blackfyre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daemon Blackfyre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Daemon_Blackfyre" title="Daemon Blackfyre">Daemon Blackfyre</a>, born of the affair between her father and Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daena Targaryen","link":"Daena Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daena Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Daena_Targaryen" title="Daena Targaryen">Daena Targaryen</a>, the sister-wife of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Baelor I Targaryen","link":"Baelor I Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelor I Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Baelor_I_Targaryen" title="Baelor I Targaryen" class="mw-redirect">Baelor I Targaryen</a>. She married the Prince of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maron","link":"Maron Martell","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maron Martell|Maron]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Maron_Martell" title="Maron Martell">Maron</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Martell","link":"House Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/House_Martell" title="House Martell">House Martell</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Through her brother King Daeron II, Daenerys was the aunt of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maekar","link":"Maekar Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maekar Targaryen|Maekar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Maekar_Targaryen" title="Maekar Targaryen">Maekar</a>, and thus the great-aunt of both King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon V the Unlikely","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen|Aegon V the Unlikely]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Aegon V the Unlikely</a> and Aegon V's brother, Maester <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aemon","link":"Aemon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aemon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Aemon" title="Aemon">Aemon</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Princess Daenerys was born well after her older brother, Daeron II, to the point that Daeron had already married and had a son, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Baelor Breakspear","link":"Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)|Baelor Breakspear]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)" title="Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)">Baelor Breakspear</a>. Her betrothal to the Prince of Dorne, Maron Martell, was brokered by her brother Daeron as part of the treaty bringing Dorne under the authority of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a>.
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->Daenerys supposedly loved her bastard half-brother, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daemon Blackfyre","link":"Daemon Blackfyre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daemon Blackfyre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Daemon_Blackfyre" title="Daemon Blackfyre">Daemon Blackfyre</a>. It was said the main reason Daemon rose in rebellion against Daeron was that Daenerys was denied to him. A year after the rebellion concluded, in 197 AL, the marriage between Daenerys and Maron was carried out and Dorne formally joined to the rest of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After Daemon's death, Prince Maron built his new bride the Water Gardens, a place of beauty made of colored marble with pools and trees. Her children played in the pools along with the children of other highborn lords. One day when the sun was hot, she took pity on the children of the servants and guards and allowed them to play in the pools with the highborn children, starting a tradition that is still kept in Dorne to this day.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While her brother's descendant Daenerys Stormborn is the second member of the Targaryen family to be named "Daenerys", Daenerys Stormborn is <i>not</i> called "Daenerys II", because "Daenerys, daughter of Aegon IV" never ruled as a queen. In fact, there has never been a ruling Targaryen queen before. When Daenerys Stormborn proclaims herself the rightful heir of the Targaryen dynasty (after her brother dies), she is officially styled as "Daenerys of House Targaryen, First of Her Name". This has been confirmed to be the same in the TV series continuity, as Xaro refers to her as "Daenerys, First of Her Name" in Season 2's "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Ghost of Harrenhal","link":"The Ghost of Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-107764266856cca6636ba9f" href="/wiki/The_Ghost_of_Harrenhal" title="The Ghost of Harrenhal">The Ghost of Harrenhal</a>", and this is also how she is formally introduced throughout Seasons 3 and 4.
</p>
