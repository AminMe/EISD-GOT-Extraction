Cooper​is a minor character in thefourth season. He is a member of the Night's Watch. Biography 

 ​Season 4 

During theBattle of Castle Black, Cooper takes part in defending the Wall against Mance Rayder 's forces. He helps load the barrels onto the ramps. WhenCastle Black's inner gate is threatened by a giant, he accompanies Grenn, Hill, and other black brothers to defend it. They successfully kill the giant, but all of the black brothers are slain in the process.
