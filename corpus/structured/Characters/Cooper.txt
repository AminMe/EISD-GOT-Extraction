<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>​<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Castle Black","link":"Battle of Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Castle_Black" title="Battle of Castle Black">Battle of Castle Black</a>, Cooper takes part in defending the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wall","link":"Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Wall" title="Wall" class="mw-redirect">Wall</a> against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mance Rayder","link":"Mance Rayder","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mance Rayder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Mance_Rayder" title="Mance Rayder">Mance Rayder</a> 's forces. He helps load the barrels onto the ramps. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black's","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black|Castle Black's]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black's</a> inner gate is threatened by a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"giant","link":"Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg|giant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Mag_Mar_Tun_Doh_Weg" title="Mag Mar Tun Doh Weg">giant</a>, he accompanies <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grenn","link":"Grenn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grenn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Grenn" title="Grenn">Grenn</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hill","link":"Hill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Hill" title="Hill" class="mw-redirect">Hill</a>, and other black brothers to defend it. They successfully kill the giant, but all of the black brothers are slain in the process. Their bodies are discovered by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Samwell Tarly","link":"Samwell Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Samwell Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Samwell_Tarly" title="Samwell Tarly">Samwell Tarly</a> after the battle.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Watchers on the Wall]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> After the arrival of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> and his forces, though not seen Cooper is among the numerous bodies of fallen black brothers burned in Castle Black's courtyard.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Children]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149380651356cca6ce8dd37" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
