A Great Master is an unnamed minor character in the fourth season. He is a slave-trader from the city of Meereen, on the coast of Slaver's Bay. He is played by guest star Derek Horsham and appears in Breaker of Chains. Biography 

 Season 4 

He is one of the many Great Masters that gather over the walls of Meereen when Daenerys Targaryen and her army appear before the city gates to lay siege to the city.
