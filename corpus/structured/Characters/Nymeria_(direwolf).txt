<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Nymeria_bites_Joffrey.png|thumb|left|Nymeria defends Arya]]","title":"Nymeria_bites_Joffrey.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Nymeria bites Joffrey","align":"left","caption":"Nymeria defends Arya","captionParsed":"Nymeria defends Arya"}}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" alt="" src="http://vignette4.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/d/d3/Nymeria_bites_Joffrey.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20150404115158" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb alignLeft withCaption" data-image-name="Nymeria bites Joffrey.png" data-image-key="Nymeria_bites_Joffrey.png"  type="image" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->Nymeria was adopted as a pup along with the rest of her siblings when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> and his entourage came upon the pups and their deceased mother. At the request of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a>, the pups were spared a quick death and each Stark child adopted one as their own. Arya names her direwolf after a fearsome warrior-queen.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Winter is Coming]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Screen_Shot_2013-06-16_at_12.46.37_AM.png|thumb|Arya says goodbye to Nymeria in &quot;The Kingsroad.&quot;]]","title":"Screen_Shot_2013-06-16_at_12.46.37_AM.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Screen Shot 2013-06-16 at 12.46.37 AM","caption":"Arya says goodbye to Nymeria in \"The Kingsroad.\"","captionParsed":"Arya says goodbye to Nymeria in \"The Kingsroad.\""}}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/0/0f/Screen_Shot_2013-06-16_at_12.46.37_AM.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20150404115442" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Screen Shot 2013-06-16 at 12.46.37 AM.png" data-image-key="Screen_Shot_2013-06-16_at_12.46.37_AM.png"  type="image" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Arya trains her to help her pack for the journey south to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, and accompanies her as far as the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Crossroads Inn","link":"Crossroads Inn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Crossroads Inn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Crossroads_Inn" title="Crossroads Inn" class="mw-redirect">Crossroads Inn</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Nymeria is faithfully loyal to Arya and defends her against Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a> by biting his arm when he attempts to strike Arya with a sword. Arya, fearing for Nymeria's well-being, forces her to leave. After the incident, Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> orders the death of Nymeria. However, as she is nowhere to be found, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark|Sansa]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa</a>'s direwolf <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady","link":"Lady","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lady]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Lady" title="Lady">Lady</a> is killed in her place.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsroad]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Nymeria is currently running loose somewhere in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>, her exact whereabouts unknown.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Nymeria is an exuberant and playful wolf, but is also fiercely defensive of Arya Stark. Arya names her for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Nymeria","link":"Nymeria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nymeria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Nymeria" title="Nymeria">Nymeria</a>, the warrior-queen of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhoynar","link":"Rhoynar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhoynar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Rhoynar" title="Rhoynar">Rhoynar</a> who led her people to safety in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a>, a thousand years before the events of the series.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to the TV series official <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"pronunciation guide","link":"pronunciation guide","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[pronunciation guide]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178607996256cca5e4289dc" href="/wiki/Pronunciation_guide" title="Pronunciation guide">pronunciation guide</a> developed for the cast and crew, "Nymeria" is pronounced "Nigh-MEER-ee-uh", as opposed to "Nih-MER-ee-uh".
</p>
