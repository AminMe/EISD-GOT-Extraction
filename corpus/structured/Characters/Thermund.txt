<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Background </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Like <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Norren","link":"Norren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Norren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/Norren" title="Norren">Norren</a>, Thermund serves in the Northern army, and fought at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a>. Thermund appears to be friends with Norren or at the very least known him before the events of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a> as he joked around with him about Norren claiming to had a large part in capture of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron From Ice","link":"Iron From Ice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron From Ice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/Iron_From_Ice" title="Iron From Ice">Iron From Ice</a>" </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Thermund is seen outside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Twins","link":"The Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">The Twins</a> with the other <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Forrester","link":"House Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/House_Forrester" title="House Forrester">House Forrester</a> warriors drinking alcohol and having fun during the Wedding. Thermund begins to questions Norren's story about him having a hand in capturing Jaime Lannister and playfully makes fun of him. He joins the other Forrester men, Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Forrester","link":"Gregor Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/Gregor_Forrester" title="Gregor Forrester">Gregor Forrester</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gared Tuttle","link":"Gared Tuttle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gared Tuttle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/Gared_Tuttle" title="Gared Tuttle">Gared Tuttle</a> in toasting to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22850135756cca70635a3a" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>.
