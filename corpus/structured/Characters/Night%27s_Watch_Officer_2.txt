<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">During Season 1 it was believed McKeever was playing <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Othell&nbsp;Yarwyck","link":"Othell\u00a0Yarwyck","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Othell\u00a0Yarwyck|Othell&nbsp;Yarwyck]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Othell_Yarwyck" title="Othell Yarwyck">Othell<span data-rte-entity="nbsp">&nbsp;</span>Yarwyck</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brian Fortune","link":"Brian Fortune","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brian Fortune]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Brian_Fortune" title="Brian Fortune">Brian Fortune</a> was playing <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bowen Marsh","link":"Bowen Marsh","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bowen Marsh]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Bowen_Marsh" title="Bowen Marsh">Bowen Marsh</a>, based on comments by Fortune and McKeever's character delivering a line said by Yarwyck in the books. Neither of them were absolutely confirmed to be playing these characters, however, as their names were never stated in on-screen dialogue or in the credits. McKeever reappeared in Seasons <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2|2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">2</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3|3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">3</a> in a background role in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Ranging","link":"Great Ranging","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Ranging]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Great_Ranging" title="Great Ranging">Great Ranging</a>, in which Yarwyck didn't partake. In <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a>, Fortune's character was directly identified in on-screen dialogue as Yarwyck, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Michael Condron","link":"Michael Condron","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Michael Condron]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Michael_Condron" title="Michael Condron">Michael Condron</a> was subsequently cast as Marsh for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a>. This establishes that Fortune's character should always be considered to have been Yarwyck, while McKeever was simply playing an unnamed Night's Watch officer. While this might technically be a retcon, Season 1 never absolutely confirmed which characters these actors were playing, so it introduced no internal continuity errors.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, about a dozen loyal Night's Watch members managed to escape from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","link":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mutiny at Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep" title="Mutiny at Craster's Keep">Mutiny at Craster's Keep</a> and return to Castle Black. The TV series, however, explicitly showed Jon Snow, Samwell Tarly, Edd, and Grenn are the only black brothers who went on the expedition north of the Wall who managed to return alive. The books also never explicitly showed what happened to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mutineers","link":"Betrayers","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Betrayers|Mutineers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164444431356cca7c548860" href="/wiki/Betrayers" title="Betrayers" class="mw-redirect">Mutineers</a> after the mutiny, but the TV series did, and no other loyalists were shown to have survived.
