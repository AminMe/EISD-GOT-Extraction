<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Cressen is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"maester","link":"Maesters","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maesters|maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Maesters" title="Maesters">maester</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Citadel","link":"the Citadel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Citadel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/The_Citadel" title="The Citadel">the Citadel</a>, a man of learning assigned to a castle to advise its inhabitants in matters of science and medicine. Cressen has served Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dragonstone","link":"Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Dragonstone" title="Dragonstone" class="mw-redirect">Dragonstone</a> for many years.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/guide\/houses\/sbaratheon\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 guide to houses, House Baratheon of Dragonstone entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Cressen is worried about the influence that the red priestess <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Melisandre","link":"Melisandre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Melisandre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Melisandre" title="Melisandre">Melisandre</a> has over Stannis, and is horrified when she gets him to renounce the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Faith of the Seven","link":"Faith of the Seven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Faith of the Seven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Faith_of_the_Seven" title="Faith of the Seven">Faith of the Seven</a>. When she has the carved wooden statues of the Seven burned on the beach, he speaks out against doing this. He says that all of them were <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"named","link":"Nameday","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nameday|named]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Nameday" title="Nameday">named</a> in the light of the Seven, and they risk the wrath of the Gods. Cressen tries to talk to Davos about going against her, but Davos refuses, saying that right now is not an opportune time to act. The maester decides that if Stannis can't be talked into putting Melisandre aside she must be killed, before she leads him into a war he cannot win.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Painted Table Dragon motif 2x01.jpg|thumb|Cressen at the Painted Table]]","title":"Painted_Table_Dragon_motif_2x01.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Painted Table Dragon motif 2x01","caption":"Cressen at the Painted Table","captionParsed":"Cressen at the Painted Table"}}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/9/99/Painted_Table_Dragon_motif_2x01.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120821191515" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Painted Table Dragon motif 2x01.jpg" data-image-key="Painted_Table_Dragon_motif_2x01.jpg"  type="image" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Cressen is a long-standing servant of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a>. He was maester at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a> for many years, and advised Stannis during the year-long <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"siege","link":"Siege of Storm's End","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Storm's End|siege]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Storm's_End" title="Siege of Storm's End">siege</a> of the castle by the armies of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. After the death of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Steffon Baratheon","link":"Steffon Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Steffon Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Steffon_Baratheon" title="Steffon Baratheon">Steffon Baratheon</a> in a shipwreck, Cressen had adopted a fatherly attitude to the three Baratheon boys, particularly to Stannis, who lacked the charm of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon|Robert]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon|Renly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly</a>. After Stannis was made Lord of Dragonstone, Cressen followed him to his new home, something uncommon among maesters, who are assigned to keeps and castles and not to lords, and has continued to advise him there ever since. Approaching eighty years of age, Cressen has started to slow down, so Stannis asked the Citadel to send him an assistant, Pylos, to take up his burdens and eventually replace him, a prospect Cressen is not happy about.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While normally stoic and unsentimental, after his death Stannis later says that Cressen was like a second father to him after his own father Steffon died. Stannis did ask for Pylos to replace Cressen, though not because he simply wanted to dispose of the man, but because he was actually concerned about his well-being. As Stannis curtly explained to Cressen, his duties as a maester would require him to climb the steep stairs of the castle to the rookery every day, but with his failing health and bad hip, it was only a matter of time before he fell down the stairs and died - and Stannis would not have Cressen needlessly kill himself in his service.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Melisandre never openly mocks Cressen at any point in the novels. It is Stannis's wife Selyse and their daughter's motley-tattooed fool Patchface who ridicule him for being old, and while Melisandre knew about the poisoned cup, she actually seems to take pity on the maester, and even tells him not to throw his life away by going through with the plan. His death also occurs slightly differently. First Melisandre drinks and appears completely-unaffected, but Cressen then drinks and dies from the poison. This also occured before the burning of the idols and not after.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shireen Baratheon","link":"Shireen Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shireen Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Shireen_Baratheon" title="Shireen Baratheon">Shireen Baratheon</a> mentions in Season 5's "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The House of Black and White","link":"The House of Black and White","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The House of Black and White]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/The_House_of_Black_and_White" title="The House of Black and White">The House of Black and White</a>" that Cressen taught her to read. In the novels, Cressen was Shireen's teacher and had a kindly attitude towards her, pitying her frail health and how <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyscale","link":"Greyscale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyscale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Greyscale" title="Greyscale">Greyscale</a> had marred her face. Cressen and Shireen actually did have a scene together in the book: when Cressen received the special white <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"messenger-raven","link":"Ravens","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ravens|messenger-raven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140814783356cca63b5e309" href="/wiki/Ravens" title="Ravens">messenger-raven</a> from the Citadel announcing that autumn had begun, Shireen asked to see it and he obliged: he could never deny a request from her, feeling that life had already denied her so much. The scene between Cressen and Shireen didn't take place in the TV version, however, because Cressen is introduced and then dies in the Season 2 premiere - and the TV producers weren't even sure during Season 2 if Shireen would ever appear in the TV series, though they later decided to introduce her in Season 3.
</p>
