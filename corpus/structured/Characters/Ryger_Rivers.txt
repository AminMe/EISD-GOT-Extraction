<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ryger is present when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150806296956cca6ef60bbf" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> visits Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150806296956cca6ef60bbf" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a> to negotiate passage for her son's army across the trident. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The character's name is not given in "Baelor", but his dialogue matches that of the character Ryger Rivers in the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150806296956cca6ef60bbf" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels. Catelyn's chapter in the first book in which she enters the Twins to request that Lord Walder grant Robb's army passage over the river was the only point that Ryger Rivers has appeared in the narrative so far.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The dialogue in this scene matched the books fairly closely, describing Ryger as the bastard son of Walder and a woman who milked goats. He is described as being younger than Stevron Frey, who is 65 years old.
</p>
