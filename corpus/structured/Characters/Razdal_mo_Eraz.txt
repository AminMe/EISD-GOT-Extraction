He is portrayed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"George Georgiou","link":"George Georgiou","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[George Georgiou]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/George_Georgiou" title="George Georgiou">George Georgiou</a>.

<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Biography </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Razdal mo Eraz is a wealthy Yunkish slaver. He is fluent in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Common Tongue","link":"Common Tongue","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Common Tongue]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Common_Tongue" title="Common Tongue">Common Tongue</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Unlike <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kraznys mo Nakloz","link":"Kraznys mo Nakloz","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kraznys mo Nakloz]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Kraznys_mo_Nakloz" title="Kraznys mo Nakloz">Kraznys mo Nakloz</a> of Astapor, Razdal is not a braggart, but shrewd and politically savvy. A reserved, calculating man, he is capable of being polite when it is politically expedient. While he is still capable of making threats, they are subdued, part of calculated negotiation tactics instead of emotional outbursts.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Like many of the Wise Masters, he prides himself on Yunkai's descent from the old <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ghiscari Empire","link":"Ghiscari Empire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ghiscari Empire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Ghiscari_Empire" title="Ghiscari Empire">Ghiscari Empire</a>, which was already old and mighty when the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian Freehold","link":"Valyrian Freehold","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian Freehold]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Valyrian_Freehold" title="Valyrian Freehold">Valyrian Freehold</a> was young.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Razdal mo Eraz is sent by the Wise Masters to negotiate with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> when she sets camp outside of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yunkai","link":"Yunkai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yunkai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Yunkai" title="Yunkai">Yunkai</a> and sends word demanding the surrender of the city. He arrives atop a palanquin carried by several slaves. After being announced, he comes to close to Daenerys but is intimidated away by her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"dragons","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a>. Razdal offers Daenerys gifts of gold and ships for her to travel back to Westeros, so that Yunkai will be left in peace. Daenerys offers Razdal his own life if he goes back to the Wise Masters and ensures that all slaves are released and given payment for their years of service or else she will lay waste to Yunkai.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Drogon_protective_S3E7.jpg|thumb|Drogon menaces Razdal.]]","title":"Drogon_protective_S3E7.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Drogon protective S3E7","caption":"Drogon menaces Razdal.","captionParsed":"Drogon menaces Razdal."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/c/cd/Drogon_protective_S3E7.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20130515200258" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Drogon protective S3E7.jpg" data-image-key="Drogon_protective_S3E7.jpg"  type="image" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Dumbfounded at her boldness, Razdal stands defiant and retorts that Yunkai has <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"powerful allies","link":"Second Sons","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Second Sons|powerful allies]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Second_Sons" title="Second Sons">powerful allies</a> and that after the coming battle the Yunkai'i will enslave the survivors, even Daenerys herself, prompting <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogon","link":"Drogon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Drogon" title="Drogon">Drogon</a> to furiously hiss at him. He reminds Daenerys that he was offered safety but she retorts that her dragons react poorly to their "Mother" being threatened. Razdal orders his slaves to take back the gold, but the enraged Drogon stands in their way. Daenerys then sends him away and keeps the gold, as Razdal returns to his palanquin, muttering curses.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair (episode)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the envoy sent by the Wise Masters to parlay with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys</a> is called Grazdan mo Eraz. He arrives on a white camel with soldiers. He is described as lean and hard.  He also has hair pointed like a unicorn horn (aristocratic Ghiscari in the novels tease out their hair into exotic shapes, but this was omitted from the TV series). 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He does not offer her any ships or a massive amount of gold bars, only a chest of gold coins. He claimed they would enslave the survivors and Daenerys herself prior to offering the gold.  He claims that if they fight and defeat Daenerys, they will sell her as a bedslave to one of the Free Cities, as men would pay well to have sex with the last Targaryen. At Daenerys's command, Drogon spits fire at Grazdan, setting his tokar on fire. He yells in protest that Daenerys swore he should have safe conduct. Daenerys taunts him for whining over a tokar and sends him away, but doesn't keep his gold. The series has renamed him, as was done with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Astapor","link":"Astapor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Astapor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Astapor" title="Astapor">Astapori</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Good Master","link":"Good Masters","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Good Masters|Good Master]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Good_Masters" title="Good Masters">Good Master</a> Grazdan mo Ullhor, who was renamed as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greizhen mo Ullhor","link":"Greizhen mo Ullhor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greizhen mo Ullhor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Greizhen_mo_Ullhor" title="Greizhen mo Ullhor">Greizhen mo Ullhor</a>. "Grazdan" is a very common name in Slaver's Bay, due to the fact that the founder of the old <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ghiscari Empire","link":"Ghiscari Empire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ghiscari Empire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-148723860656cca6545a302" href="/wiki/Ghiscari_Empire" title="Ghiscari Empire">Ghiscari Empire</a> over five thousand years ago was Grazdan the Great.
</p>
