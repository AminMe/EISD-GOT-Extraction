<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Garth Gardener</b>, also known as <b>Garth Greenhand</b>, is an unseen character in <i>Game of Thrones</i>. He is deceased when the series begins and has only appeared in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Complete Guide to Westeros","link":"Complete Guide to Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Complete Guide to Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/Complete_Guide_to_Westeros" title="Complete Guide to Westeros" class="mw-redirect">Complete Guide to Westeros</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Histories & Lore","link":"Histories & Lore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Histories & Lore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/Histories_&_Lore" title="Histories &amp; Lore">Histories &amp; Lore</a> featurettes.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Garth Gardener was a legendary King of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"First Men","link":"First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/First_Men" title="First Men">First Men</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Age of Heroes","link":"Age of Heroes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Age of Heroes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/Age_of_Heroes" title="Age of Heroes">Age of Heroes</a>. He was the founder of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Gardener","link":"House Gardener","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Gardener]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/House_Gardener" title="House Gardener">House Gardener</a> and the first <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King of the Reach","link":"King of the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King of the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/King_of_the_Reach" title="King of the Reach">King of the Reach</a>. According to legend, Garth Greenhand wore a crown of vines and flowers and was said to make the land bloom.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">King Garth's line ruled <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a> until the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen|Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">Targaryen</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"conquest","link":"War of Conquest","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of Conquest|conquest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/War_of_Conquest" title="War of Conquest">conquest</a>, becoming extinct with the death of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mern IX","link":"Mern Gardener","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mern Gardener|Mern IX]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/Mern_Gardener" title="Mern Gardener" class="mw-redirect">Mern IX</a>, the last King of the Reach. Nevertheless, several of the noble houses of the Reach, such as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Florent","link":"House Florent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Florent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/House_Florent" title="House Florent">House Florent</a>, claim their descent to Garth Greenhand.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Age of Heroes (Complete Guide to Westeros)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[House Tyrell (Histories & Lore)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, half of the great noble Houses from the Reach claim descent from Garth Greenhand and House Gardener, of which House Tyrell and House Florent are the most prominent. Other powerful noble Houses in the Reach descended from Garth are House Rowan and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Oakheart","link":"House Oakheart","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Oakheart]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/House_Oakheart" title="House Oakheart">House Oakheart</a>. Because House Tyrell never ruled as kings, and because they are only one of several Houses that claim descent from Garth (and not even as close a descent as the Florents possess), they have always felt somewhat uneasy in their claim to dominion over the Reach.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">A large number of Garth's successors were named after him. There were ultimately at least twelve kings of the Reach named Garth, including the original, as there is mention of a coin minted during the reign of "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Garth XII","link":"Garth XII Gardener","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Garth XII Gardener|Garth XII]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109664207556cca71ca1cfb" href="/wiki/Garth_XII_Gardener?action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Garth XII Gardener (page does not exist)">Garth XII</a>".
</p>
