<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Alton is a cousin of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>. He fights for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a> against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a> and is captured by the Stark army.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, there is no character by this name. The character Cleos Frey has the role of being a captive of House Stark and delivering terms to House Lannister. Cleos Frey is the son of Ser Emmon Frey, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a>'s second son, and Genna Lannister, the only sister of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a>. His name appears to have been changed so that TV viewers would not be confused that he is on the Lannisters' side, despite House Frey officially declaring for Robb Stark.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While Alton does serve in the same role as Cleos Frey in the books, it is later made clear that he is <i>not</i> the son of Genna Lannister as he mentions his mother is Cynda Lannister, whom Jaime has difficulty remembering. Thus "Alton Lannister" isn't as important a familial relation as Cleos Frey is; he is not Lord Tywin's nephew and Jaime's first cousin the way Cleos is in the books.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Besides being the courier in negotiating peace terms, Cleos Frey also has in common with Alton Lannister that he takes part in the two escape attempts of Jaime Lannister. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> sends Cleos back with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark|Eddard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard</a>'s bones and the Lannister terms for peace to Robb Stark, there are four men mixed in with the other escorting soldiers that are there to break Jaime out. One does impressions and imitates <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Edmure Tully","link":"Edmure Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Edmure Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Edmure_Tully" title="Edmure Tully">Edmure Tully</a> ordering the gate raised, but the real Edmure was returning from a brothel in town and the escape is foiled. Cleos swore he knew nothing about the escape plan, but Edmure did not believe him and ordered to lock him in the cell where Jaime was previously kept, claiming that Cleos is "half Lannister, half Frey, and all liar". When Cleos was brought before <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn</a>, he fearfully swore he had no idea about Tyrion's plan. Catelyn Stark did not care much if he was telling the truth or not, and demanded to know what peace terms the Lannisters offered them. After Cleos told her the terms he brought, Catelyn asked about her daughters. He answered evasively, but after Catelyn threatened to hang him - he told her the truth that he only saw <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark|Sansa]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne","link":"Brienne of Tarth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne of Tarth|Brienne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198567806956cca59f42890" href="/wiki/Brienne_of_Tarth" title="Brienne of Tarth">Brienne</a> and Jaime left, it was Catelyn Stark who ordered the gate raised as she was sending Cleos Frey back to King's Landing with new terms, with Jaime Lannister hidden in the bottom of the boat. Cleos Frey left with Brienne and Jaime, headed for King's Landing.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">On the way to King's Landing, they were attacked by archer outlaws. Cleos and his horse were shot several times. He fell from the saddle, but his foot was caught in the stirrup, and his horse dragged him away, his head bouncing against the ground. As a result, Cleos' skull was smashed. Jaime did not express any sorrow for his cousin's death, but instead meant to strip him of his clothes and weapon. He used Cleos' sword to attack Brienne (not Brienne's sword).
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Jaime has a very negative opinion on Cleos, being a coward and flatterer. He mused that "Ser Cleos looked like a weasel, fought like a goose, and had the courage of an especially brave ewe".
</p>
