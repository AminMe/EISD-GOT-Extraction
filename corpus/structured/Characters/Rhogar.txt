Rhogar is a background character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is a member of the Lost Legion. Biography 

 The Sword in the Darkness 

As Asher Forrester, Beskha, and Malcolm Branfield are chased through the canyon, they enter a cave, with the Lost Legion right behind them. One of the members orders them to alert Rhogar that to gather the rest of the Legion as they follow the trio into the cave.
