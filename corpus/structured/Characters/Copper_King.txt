<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is a member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Thirteen","link":"the Thirteen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Thirteen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/The_Thirteen" title="The Thirteen">the Thirteen</a>, the rulers of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qarth","link":"Qarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/Qarth" title="Qarth">Qarth</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He receives <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> outside the gates of the city with the rest of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Thirteen","link":"the Thirteen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Thirteen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/The_Thirteen" title="The Thirteen">the Thirteen</a>. They refuse to admit her without seeing her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"dragons","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a>. She is defiant and is eventually admitted under the protection of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Xaro Xhoan Daxos","link":"Xaro Xhoan Daxos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Xaro Xhoan Daxos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos" title="Xaro Xhoan Daxos">Xaro Xhoan Daxos</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Daenerys visits him to obtain support for her campaign to take her father's throne. He offers Daenerys one ship on the condition that she have sex with him. He is refused.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Old Gods and the New]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He's present at a session of the Thirteen in which Daenerys accuses the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Spice King","link":"Spice King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Spice King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/Spice_King" title="Spice King">Spice King</a> of stealing her dragons. He's murdered by one of the doubles of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pyat Pree","link":"Pyat Pree","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pyat Pree]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/Pyat_Pree" title="Pyat Pree">Pyat Pree</a> along with the rest of the Qartheen merchant lords after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"warlock","link":"warlock","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[warlock]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" href="/wiki/Warlock" title="Warlock" class="mw-redirect">warlock</a> reveals it was him who stole the dragons and declares Xaro Xhoan Daxos the King of Qarth.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Man Without Honor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154448513756cca713bbb59" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
