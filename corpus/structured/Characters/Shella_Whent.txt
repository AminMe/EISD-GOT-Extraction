<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lady Shella Whent is an old woman. She's a loyal vassal of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16147616056cca75891539" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a>, the Lord of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16147616056cca75891539" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>. Her husband's younger brother was , Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oswell Whent","link":"Oswell Whent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oswell Whent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16147616056cca75891539" href="/wiki/Oswell_Whent" title="Oswell Whent">Oswell Whent</a>, a member of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys II Targaryen","link":"Aerys II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16147616056cca75891539" href="/wiki/Aerys_II_Targaryen" title="Aerys II Targaryen">Aerys II Targaryen</a>'s <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16147616056cca75891539" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/www.westeros.org\/Citadel\/SSM\/Entry\/1124 The Citadel: So Spake Martin]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16147616056cca75891539" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16147616056cca75891539" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Shella Whent was the Lady of Harrenhal until she surrendered the castle to forces belonging to House Lannister during the War of the Five Kings because she lacked the means to defend it. She is considered a friend of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16147616056cca75891539" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. On the fourth novel Petyr Baelish states that she died, but it is not confirmed.
</p>
