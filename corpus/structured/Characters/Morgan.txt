Morgan is an minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Chris Reily and appears in Two Swords. Biography 

 Season 4 

He visits Littlefinger's whore house with his friend. While there singing the Rains of Castemere, they are confronted by the foreigner, Oberyn Martell, who threatens Morgan and then stabs a dagger through his hand when he attempted to draw his sword at the table.
