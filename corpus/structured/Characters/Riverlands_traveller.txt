 A Riverlands traveller is a minor character in the third season. He is portrayed by Joe Purcell and appears in Dark Wings, Dark Words. Biography 

 Background 

He is a peasant who travels through the Riverlands to trade and sell. Season 3 

He saw Jaime Lannister and Brienne of Tarth travel in the Riverlands, and tipped them off to Locke.  In the books 

There is no mention of a traveller in the books. The noise Brienne and Jaime made while fighting was what led to their capture by a dozen of the Brave Companions, a sellsword company led by Vargo Hoat, the book counterpart of Locke. References
