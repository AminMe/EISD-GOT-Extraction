Moelleis a recurring character in the fifth season. She is portrayed by Maggie Hayesand debuts in The Gift. Biography 

 Background 

Moelle is a septa of the Faith of the Seven stationed at the Great Sept of Baelor in King's Landing. Season 5 

Moelle is present along with Septas Unella and Scolera during the arrest of Cersei Lannister.
