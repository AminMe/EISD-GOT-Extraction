<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Rorge is a criminal, forcibly recruited into the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a> during his visit to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. He is accompanying the rest of Yoren's new recruits northwards to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Fans have speculated that Rorge was one of three figures seen in the back of Yoren's wagon in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"final episode","link":"Fire and Blood","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fire and Blood|final episode]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Fire_and_Blood" title="Fire and Blood">final episode</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first season","link":"Season 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1|first season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">first season</a>. However, Andy Beckwith was not cast in the role until filming was underway on Season 2. Whether one of those characters was meant to be Rorge and the role has been effectively recast is not clear.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Rorge is described as thick, short, and very hairy. He was a petty thug in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> who at some time had lost his nose. To hide his missing nose, he wears a helm with a nose guard whenever he can. According to semi-canon source, he ran a dog and bear-fighting pit in the city and had his protege Biter fight in the pit for money. The pit was shut down and both were arrested and cast into the cells of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a> shortly before Yoren found them and forcibly recruited them into the Watch.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge is also a prolific raper, and his constant threats to shove a stick up the ass of anyone he dislikes are not merely rhetoric. Rorge committed rape so many times that he was eventually caught by the City Watch and castrated, but undeterred, Rorge continued to rape or sodomize people with sticks and other objects. His real pleasure wasn't in the actual sex act, but in sadistically inflicting pain and death on others.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> encountered Rorge in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>, his reaction was the same as in the TV series. Arya reminded him that she saved him, and the ingrate thug promised to rape her once more for that. Arya's mentioning of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a>'s name seemed to scare him, though.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge and Biter helped Arya and Jaqen to free the Northern prisoners. Afterwards they join the Brave Companions, the notorious sellsword company led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vargo Hoat","link":"Locke","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Locke|Vargo Hoat]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Locke" title="Locke">Vargo Hoat</a>. Rorge was among those who caught <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne","link":"Brienne of Tarth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne of Tarth|Brienne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Brienne_of_Tarth" title="Brienne of Tarth">Brienne</a>. Rorge, with Shagwell and Zollo, attempted to gang-rape Brienne although Hoat forbade them, but were foiled by Jaime.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While Vargo Hoat becomes delirious due to his festering head injury, many of the Brave Companions leave Harrenhal, among them Rorge and Biter. They and five more form a gang that sacks <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Saltpans","link":"Saltpans","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Saltpans]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Saltpans" title="Saltpans">Saltpans</a>. They never encounter Arya and the Hound, but Rorge finds the Hound's helmet and wears it, pretending to be him. The disguise causes spreading of false rumors about atrocities committed by the Hound. Jaime is rather puzzled to hear those rumors, because such atrocities are more typical to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Mountain","link":"The Mountain","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Mountain]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/The_Mountain" title="The Mountain" class="mw-redirect">The Mountain</a> rather to the Hound.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge and Biter have nothing to do with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dying man","link":"dying man","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dying man]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Dying_man" title="Dying man">dying man</a> that Arya and the Hound encounter. He was injured by a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bolton","link":"Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Bolton" title="Bolton" class="mw-redirect">Bolton</a> soldier.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge does not tell Arya and the Hound about Joffrey's death. They hear about it from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Polliver","link":"Polliver","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Polliver]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Polliver" title="Polliver">Polliver</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge's gang encounters <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne","link":"Brienne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Brienne" title="Brienne" class="mw-redirect">Brienne</a> near the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77451397456cca63968dcc" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a>.
