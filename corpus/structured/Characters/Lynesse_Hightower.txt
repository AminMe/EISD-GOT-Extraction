<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65427454356cca604830f0" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Lynesse is described as extremely beautiful with blonde hair and pale skin. Jorah Mormont says she looked a bit like Daenerys Targaryen. Lynesse married Jorah after he won a tournament to celebrate the victory of King Robert against the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65427454356cca604830f0" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, in which Jorah wore her favor. Lynesse was used to the riches and luxury of Oldtown and found Bear Island primitive and rude. Jorah's attempts to please his wife led to him spending all of his money. He disgraced himself through behavior unbefitting a lord, by arresting poachers and selling them to slavers. Slavery is illegal in the Seven Kingdoms and has been for millennia. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65427454356cca604830f0" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> came to arrest Jorah, he and Lynesse fled across the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Narrow Sea","link":"Narrow Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Narrow Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65427454356cca604830f0" href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a> to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free City","link":"Free City","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free City]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65427454356cca604830f0" href="/wiki/Free_City" title="Free City" class="mw-redirect">Free City</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lys","link":"Lys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65427454356cca604830f0" href="/wiki/Lys" title="Lys">Lys</a>, leaving his aunt <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maege","link":"Maege Mormont","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maege Mormont|Maege]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65427454356cca604830f0" href="/wiki/Maege_Mormont" title="Maege Mormont">Maege</a> to become the ruling Lady of Bear Island. Jorah attempted to make his living as a sellsword, but was unable to provide an income acceptable to Lynesse. She left him and became a concubine to the merchant-lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tregar Ormollen","link":"Tregar Ormollen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tregar Ormollen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65427454356cca604830f0" href="/wiki/Tregar_Ormollen" title="Tregar Ormollen">Tregar Ormollen</a>.
</p>
