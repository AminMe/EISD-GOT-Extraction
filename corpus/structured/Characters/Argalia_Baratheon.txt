<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Argalia Baratheon</b>, née <b>Durrandon</b>, is an unseen character in <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Game of Thrones","link":"Game of Thrones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Game of Thrones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Game_of_Thrones" title="Game of Thrones" class="mw-redirect">Game of Thrones</a></i>. She is not expected to appear in the series, being long dead by the time it starts. Argalia is the daughter of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Argilac the Arrogant","link":"Argilac","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Argilac|Argilac the Arrogant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Argilac" title="Argilac" class="mw-redirect">Argilac the Arrogant</a>, the last of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm Kings","link":"Storm Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Storm_Kings" title="Storm Kings" class="mw-redirect">Storm Kings</a>, and wife of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Orys Baratheon","link":"Orys Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Orys Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Orys_Baratheon" title="Orys Baratheon">Orys Baratheon</a>, the founder of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Argalia Baratheon was the wife of Orys Baratheon, the first Lord Paramount of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormlands","link":"Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Stormlands" title="Stormlands" class="mw-redirect">Stormlands</a> and is the distant ancestor of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> and his brothers <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon|Renly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Orys, a commander in the army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon the Conqueror","link":"Aegon I Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon I Targaryen|Aegon the Conqueror]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Aegon_I_Targaryen" title="Aegon I Targaryen">Aegon the Conqueror</a> and his bastard half-brother, had been sent by Aegon to seize <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>, the ancestral seat of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm Kings","link":"Storm Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Storm_Kings" title="Storm Kings" class="mw-redirect">Storm Kings</a> and capital of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormlands","link":"Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Stormlands" title="Stormlands" class="mw-redirect">Stormlands</a>. Orys defeated King Argilac the Arrogant and took not only Storm's End but also his sigil - the black stag and his words, "Ours is the Fury" - as well as Argalia, to cement his rule over the Stormlands. He became the first Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[House Baratheon (Complete Guide to Westeros)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">A sample chapter posted on April 17, 2014 to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"George R.R. Martin","link":"George R.R. Martin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[George R.R. Martin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/George_R.R._Martin" title="George R.R. Martin">George R.R. Martin</a>'s website from the unpublished <i>The World of Ice and Fire</i>, a companion book to the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novel series, reveals that Argilac's daughter was named <b>Argella Durrandon</b>. Argilac offered Argella's hand in marriage to Aegon the Conqueror himself in return for a military alliance against King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harren the Black","link":"Harren Hoare","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harren Hoare|Harren the Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Harren_Hoare" title="Harren Hoare">Harren the Black</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a>, whom Argilac feared was planning an attack on the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormlands","link":"Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Stormlands" title="Stormlands" class="mw-redirect">Stormlands</a>. Aegon refused, instead offering the hand of Orys. Argilac spurned the offer of a bastard and severed the hands of Aegon's envoy in protest.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Orys defeated Argilac at the Battle of the Last Storm, Argella declared herself the Storm Queen and vowed to fight on. Her own vassals refused to obey, instead delivering her to Orys naked and in chains. Orys treated her with courtesy and they later married. While Orys is reported to have treated her chivalrously, Argella's feelings towards Orys remain unknown.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The name of "Argalia" is the creation of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bryan Cogman","link":"Bryan Cogman","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bryan Cogman]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-66128004456cca75707a94" href="/wiki/Bryan_Cogman" title="Bryan Cogman">Bryan Cogman</a>, who wrote the entries in the <i>Lineages and Histories</i> book.
</p>
