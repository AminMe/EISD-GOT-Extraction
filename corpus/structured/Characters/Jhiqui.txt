<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Jhiqui is a Dothraki woman in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogo","link":"Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/Drogo" title="Drogo">Drogo</a>'s khalasar.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Jhiqui, Irri, and Doreah tend to Daenerys in her tent. Jhiqui doesn't say much, and comes across as silent and obedient. However she does side with Irri in saying that the moon is a goddess, and not a giant <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragon","link":"dragon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon" class="mw-redirect">dragon</a> egg as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Doreah","link":"Doreah","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Doreah]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/Doreah" title="Doreah">Doreah</a> claims.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsroad]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Jhiqui has only appeared in a single episode. It is not clear if she remained loyal to Daenerys following the death of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogo","link":"Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/Drogo" title="Drogo">Drogo</a>, though if she did it is unlikely she survived long as Daenery's entire remaining <i>khalasar</i> is shown on camera in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Garden of Bones","link":"Garden of Bones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Garden of Bones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/Garden_of_Bones" title="Garden of Bones">Garden of Bones</a>" and she is not present. Nevertheless, a Dothraki handmaiden is shown in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Bear and the Maiden Fair","link":"The Bear and the Maiden Fair","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair" title="The Bear and the Maiden Fair">The Bear and the Maiden Fair</a>", though it is unknown is she's meant to represent Jhiqui.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Jhiqui is a member of a <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"khalasar","link":"khalasar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[khalasar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/Khalasar" title="Khalasar">khalasar</a></i> defeated in battle by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Khal Drogo","link":"Khal Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Khal Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-4792676356cca67ac43ab" href="/wiki/Khal_Drogo" title="Khal Drogo" class="mw-redirect">Khal Drogo</a>'s. She was enslaved and later given to Daenerys to teach her the Dothraki language.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, Jhiqui is frequently seen with Irri, both in service to Daenerys. In the TV series, however, she appears only briefly in one scene. Thus, it is unclear of the TV version of Jhiqui remains in Daenerys' khalassar or was among those who abandoned her after Drogo's death. The TV series condensed most of her role with that of Irri.
</p>
