<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Donnel is a son of Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Anya Waynwood","link":"Anya Waynwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Anya Waynwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Anya_Waynwood" title="Anya Waynwood">Anya Waynwood</a>, the current head of House Waynwood.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Donnel is at the Gate when Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Petyr Baelish","link":"Petyr Baelish","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Petyr Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Petyr_Baelish" title="Petyr Baelish">Petyr Baelish</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a> arrive. Littlefinger introduces Sansa as his niece, Alayne. Donnel believes him and lets them pass.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[First of His Name]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sandor Clegane","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">Sandor Clegane</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya&nbsp;Stark","link":"Arya\u00a0Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya\u00a0Stark|Arya&nbsp;Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya<span data-rte-entity="nbsp">&nbsp;</span>Stark</a> reaches the Bloody Gate and Donnel orders them to stop, Sandor tells him who they are expecting ransom for reuniting Arya with her aunt <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lysa Arryn","link":"Lysa Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lysa Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Lysa_Arryn" title="Lysa Arryn">Lysa Arryn</a>. After Donnel explains that Lysa died three days ago, Sandor is left in shock while Arya bursts out in laughter, because of the irony.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Mountain and the Viper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Donnel Waynwood is described as being a stocky man of twenty, with a wide nose and thick brown hair. Donnel is the second of Lady Anya's three sons: Morton, Donnel, and Wallace. He serves at the Bloody Gate and leads the sortie that saves <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> and her retinue from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mountain clans","link":"Mountain clans","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mountain clans]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Mountain_clans" title="Mountain clans" class="mw-redirect">Mountain clans</a> at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mountains of the Moon","link":"Mountains of the Moon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mountains of the Moon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Mountains_of_the_Moon" title="Mountains of the Moon">Mountains of the Moon</a>. After Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brynden Tully","link":"Brynden Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brynden Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-156093493656cca6e16a2a7" href="/wiki/Brynden_Tully" title="Brynden Tully">Brynden Tully</a> departs with his niece Catelyn, Donnel later replaces him as the new Knight of the Gate.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Later at the Bloody Gate, Lady Anya introduces her grandson Ser Roland Waynwood, a muscular man about 25 years old, as well as her youngest son Wallace, who is identified as Roland's uncle. It isn't clear if Roland is Morton's son or Donnel's son.
</p>
