<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Prince Aemon was called "the Dragonknight" on account of his heritage. He was raised to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> at age 17 and eventually became <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a>. According to tradition, he was once saved from a snake pit by his cousin <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Baelor I Targaryen","link":"Baelor Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelor Targaryen|Baelor I Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Baelor_Targaryen" title="Baelor Targaryen">Baelor I Targaryen</a>, whom the snakes refused to bite. He fought and barely defeated Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cregan Stark","link":"Cregan Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cregan Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Cregan_Stark" title="Cregan Stark">Cregan Stark</a>. He also wielded <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dark Sister","link":"Dark Sister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dark Sister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Dark_Sister" title="Dark Sister">Dark Sister</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian steel","link":"Valyrian steel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian steel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Valyrian_steel" title="Valyrian steel">Valyrian steel</a> sword of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Visenya Targaryen","link":"Visenya Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Visenya Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Visenya_Targaryen" title="Visenya Targaryen">Visenya Targaryen</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Oathkeeper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"<i>A Song of Ice and Fire<\/i>","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire|''A Song of Ice and Fire'']]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire"><i>A Song of Ice and Fire</i></a> novels, Aemon was knighted young and joined the Kingsguard at the age of seventeen. He served under five kings: His uncle <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon III Targaryen","link":"Aegon III Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon III Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Aegon_III_Targaryen" title="Aegon III Targaryen">Aegon III Targaryen</a>, his cousins <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daeron the Young Dragon","link":"Daeron I Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daeron I Targaryen|Daeron the Young Dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Daeron_I_Targaryen" title="Daeron I Targaryen">Daeron the Young Dragon</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Baelor the Blessed","link":"Baelor Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelor Targaryen|Baelor the Blessed]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Baelor_Targaryen" title="Baelor Targaryen">Baelor the Blessed</a>, his father <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Viserys II","link":"Viserys II Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Viserys II Targaryen|Viserys II]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Viserys_II_Targaryen" title="Viserys II Targaryen">Viserys II</a>, and his brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon the Unworthy","link":"Aegon IV Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon IV Targaryen|Aegon the Unworthy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Aegon_IV_Targaryen" title="Aegon IV Targaryen">Aegon the Unworthy</a>. Aemon has been referred to as the noblest and greatest knight that ever lived and his skills with a sword were legendary throughout Westeros.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Aemon took part in the conquest of Dorne, led by his cousin and king, Daeron I. Aemon won great fame by defeating a Dornish champion, and was present several years later after a rebellion in Dorne undid Daeron's conquest, and in which Daeron himself was killed. Aemon was taken captive by the Dornish, and put in a cage, placed above a pit filled with vipers. He was eventually saved by his cousin and new king, Baelor I.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Songs speak of his doomed love for his brother's queen, his own sister, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Naerys","link":"Naerys Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Naerys Targaryen|Naerys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Naerys_Targaryen" title="Naerys Targaryen">Naerys</a>. He supposedly cried when Naerys married their brother. After his brother Aegon became king, rumors started to spread that Aemon was the actual father of Aegon's son and heir, Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daeron","link":"Daeron II Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daeron II Targaryen|Daeron]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Daeron_II_Targaryen" title="Daeron II Targaryen">Daeron</a>. He defended his sister when she was accused of treason by the knight Ser Morgil.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Aemon once fought <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cregan Stark","link":"Cregan Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cregan Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Cregan_Stark" title="Cregan Stark">Cregan Stark</a>. Prince Aemon claimed he had never faced a finer swordsman.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Aemon once won a tourney, disguised as a mystery knight, after his brother had forbidden him from participating because he wanted to name his mistress at the time the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Queen of Love and Beauty","link":"Queen of Love and Beauty","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Queen of Love and Beauty]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Queen_of_Love_and_Beauty" title="Queen of Love and Beauty">Queen of Love and Beauty</a>. Aemon took part secretly under the disguise of <i>The Knight of Tears,</i> and was thus able to crown his sister Naerys the Queen of Love and Beauty, in place of the king's mistress.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Despite the difficult relationship between Aemon and his brother, Aemon died honorably defending his brother and king from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"brothers","link":"House Toyne","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Toyne|brothers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/House_Toyne" title="House Toyne">brothers</a> of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Terrence Toyne","link":"Terrence Toyne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Terrence Toyne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Terrence_Toyne" title="Terrence Toyne">Terrence Toyne</a>, who wanted to assassinate Aegon IV in revenge for him executing Terrence for sleeping with his mistress.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Maester <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aemon","link":"Aemon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aemon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-182687417756cca6385be2c" href="/wiki/Aemon" title="Aemon">Aemon</a> explicitly states that his grandfather Daeron II named him after Aemon the Dragonknight, who was Daeron II's uncle - though Maester Aemon notes there are those who said that the Dragonknight was actually Daeron II's own father, depending on which tale you believe.
</p>
