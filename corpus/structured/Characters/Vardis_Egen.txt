</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser <b>Vardis Egen </b>is a recurring character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first season","link":"Season 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1|first season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">first season</a>. He is played by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brendan McCormack","link":"Brendan McCormack","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brendan McCormack]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Brendan_McCormack" title="Brendan McCormack">Brendan McCormack</a> and debuts in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Wolf and the Lion","link":"The Wolf and the Lion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/The_Wolf_and_the_Lion" title="The Wolf and the Lion">The Wolf and the Lion</a>". Ser Vardis Egen is the captain of the guards at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Eyrie","link":"the Eyrie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Eyrie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/The_Eyrie" title="The Eyrie">the Eyrie</a>. In the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"trial by combat","link":"trial by combat","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[trial by combat]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Trial_by_combat" title="Trial by combat">trial by combat</a>, he is the champion for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lysa Arryn","link":"Lysa Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lysa Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Lysa_Arryn" title="Lysa Arryn">Lysa Arryn</a> and is killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a>, who fights for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion","link":"Tyrion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Tyrion" title="Tyrion" class="mw-redirect">Tyrion</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Vardis Egen was captain of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Arryn","link":"Jon Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Jon_Arryn" title="Jon Arryn">Jon Arryn</a>'s personal guard in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> and then became captain of the guards in the Eyrie for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robin Arryn","link":"Robin Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robin Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Robin_Arryn" title="Robin Arryn">Robin Arryn</a> and his mother, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lysa Arryn","link":"Lysa Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lysa Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Lysa_Arryn" title="Lysa Arryn">Lysa Arryn</a>. He is a knight of some reputation, but is no longer a young man.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Vardis leads the group of knights who meet <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> and her prisoner Tyrion Lannister before she enters the Eyrie.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> Lysa orders Tyrion imprisoned in the sky cells. After having spent quite some time in a sky cell, Tyrion finally claims that he wants to confess his crimes and then demands a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"trial by combat","link":"First Trial of Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[First Trial of Tyrion Lannister|trial by combat]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/First_Trial_of_Tyrion_Lannister" title="First Trial of Tyrion Lannister" class="mw-redirect">trial by combat</a>. Though many men, including Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eon Hunter","link":"Eon Hunter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eon Hunter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Eon_Hunter" title="Eon Hunter">Eon Hunter</a> and a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Knight of House Lynderly","link":"Knight of House Lynderly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Knight of House Lynderly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Knight_of_House_Lynderly" title="Knight of House Lynderly">Knight of House Lynderly</a>, offer to fight as Lysa's champion, she asks Ser Vardis to represent <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Arryn","link":"House Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/House_Arryn" title="House Arryn">House Arryn</a>. At first he says that fighting Tyrion would be unfair, but Vardis accepts when Tyrion asks that he be represented by a champion. The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sellsword","link":"sellsword","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[sellsword]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Sellsword" title="Sellsword" class="mw-redirect">sellsword</a> Bronn offers to be Tyrion's champion. Vardis fights dressed in full metal armor and carrying a large and heavy wooden shield. His speed and dexterity were easily outmatched by Bronn, who wore only a leather armored shirt. During the course of the fight, Bronn throws things into Ser Vardis' path and even trips him, while quickly dodging and weaving to avoid Vardis' attacks. Ultimately Bronn tires out the more heavily armored knight, severely injuring him with a quick slash and disarming him.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Bronn defeats Vardis.jpg|thumb|Vardis falls from the Moon Door.]]","title":"Bronn_defeats_Vardis.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Bronn defeats Vardis","caption":"Vardis falls from the Moon Door.","captionParsed":"Vardis falls from the Moon Door."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/1/18/Bronn_defeats_Vardis.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20110615180140" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Bronn defeats Vardis.jpg" data-image-key="Bronn_defeats_Vardis.jpg"  type="image" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->Bronn waits for Lysa Arryn to call for mercy, but she does not respond, so he stabs Vardis in the neck and pushes him through the "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Moon Door","link":"Moon Door","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Moon Door]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Moon_Door" title="Moon Door" class="mw-redirect">Moon Door</a>" in the center of the throne room, sending him down a thousand-foot drop. Lysa snaps angrily to Bronn that he didn't fight with honor. The sellsword agrees, before pointing to the Moon Door and noting that Ser Vardis did.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Golden Crown]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Vardis Egen is an extremely loyal servant and retainer of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Arryn","link":"House Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/House_Arryn" title="House Arryn">House Arryn</a>. He had been the captain of the guard when Jon Arryn was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hand of the King","link":"Hand of the King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hand of the King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206029134556cca6497d9d8" href="/wiki/Hand_of_the_King" title="Hand of the King">Hand of the King</a>. He is described as heavy, square, plain-faced, silver-haired, and having no humor. In the battle with Bronn, Vardis is not only quite a bit older and slower than Bronn, but Lysa has given him Jon Arryn's great sword to defend the honor of the House, which is a heavier and unfamiliar blade.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the book, Bronn kills Vardis without pushing him out of the Moon Door (which is set in the wall, not in the floor).
</p>
