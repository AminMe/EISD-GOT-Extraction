<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Will is a ranger of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80714800756cca62ed7e8a" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. He was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"not of noble birth","link":"Smallfolk","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Smallfolk|not of noble birth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80714800756cca62ed7e8a" href="/wiki/Smallfolk" title="Smallfolk">not of noble birth</a>, but was posthumously described by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Benjen Stark","link":"Benjen Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Benjen Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80714800756cca62ed7e8a" href="/wiki/Benjen_Stark" title="Benjen Stark">Benjen Stark</a> as a reliable soldier, a "true ranger".
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80714800756cca62ed7e8a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Will is famed for his skills as a tracker and a hunter, a result of his previous career as a poacher. After being caught by Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jason Mallister","link":"Jason Mallister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jason Mallister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80714800756cca62ed7e8a" href="/wiki/Jason_Mallister" title="Jason Mallister">Jason Mallister</a>'s woodsmen, he chose to take the black over losing a hand. He has been on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80714800756cca62ed7e8a" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> for four years when the events of the books begin.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the TV series, some of Will and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gared","link":"Gared","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gared]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-80714800756cca62ed7e8a" href="/wiki/Gared" title="Gared">Gared</a>'s story roles have been switched around from the books.
</p>
