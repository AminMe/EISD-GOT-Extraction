<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-94639774656cca70a35627" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Vance, along with Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yohn Royce","link":"Yohn Royce","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yohn Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-94639774656cca70a35627" href="/wiki/Yohn_Royce" title="Yohn Royce">Yohn Royce</a> and Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Anya Waynwood","link":"Anya Waynwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Anya Waynwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-94639774656cca70a35627" href="/wiki/Anya_Waynwood" title="Anya Waynwood">Anya Waynwood</a>, sits on a tribunal to investigate the death of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lysa Arryn","link":"Lysa Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lysa Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-94639774656cca70a35627" href="/wiki/Lysa_Arryn" title="Lysa Arryn">Lysa Arryn</a>. Like the other judges, Ser Vance assumes foul play on Littlefinger's part. During the tribunal, Ser Vance is made aware that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Petyr Baelish","link":"Petyr Baelish","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Petyr Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-94639774656cca70a35627" href="/wiki/Petyr_Baelish" title="Petyr Baelish">Petyr Baelish</a>'s niece, "Alayne", is actually <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-94639774656cca70a35627" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a>, but he agrees to keep her secret and accepts her testimony that Lady Arryn committed suicide.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Mountain and the Viper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-94639774656cca70a35627" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-94639774656cca70a35627" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, there is no character named Ser Vance Corbray.
