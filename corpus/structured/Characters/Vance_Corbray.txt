 Vance Corbray is a minor character in the fourth season, played by Richard Doubleday. SerVance Corbrayis a knight of House Corbray, a noble house sworn to House Arryn of the Eyrie. Biography 

 Season 4 

Ser Vance, along with Lord Yohn Royce and Lady Anya Waynwood, sits on a tribunal to investigate the death of Lysa Arryn. Like the other judges, Ser Vance assumes foul play on Littlefinger's part. In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there is no character named Ser Vance Corbray. There are two known Corbray knights, brothers to Lord Lyonel Corbray, Ser Lyn and Ser Lucas. Ser Lyn is a noted warrior, and is also known to be a homosexual. References
