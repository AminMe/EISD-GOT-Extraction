<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55714196256cca6de47f03" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Joss Stilwood squires for Ser Gregor Clegane, the Mountain, in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tourney of the Hand","link":"Tourney of the Hand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tourney of the Hand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55714196256cca6de47f03" href="/wiki/Tourney_of_the_Hand" title="Tourney of the Hand" class="mw-redirect">Tourney of the Hand</a>. After Ser Gregor is defeated in a joust by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Loras Tyrell","link":"Loras Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Loras Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55714196256cca6de47f03" href="/wiki/Loras_Tyrell" title="Loras Tyrell">Loras Tyrell</a>, who was riding a mare in heat to upset Ser Gregor's stallion, he orders Joss to bring him his greatsword. Ser Gregor then beheads his horse in anger.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55714196256cca6de47f03" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
