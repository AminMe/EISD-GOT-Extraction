<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Jory Cassel is the captain of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark|Lord Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Lord Eddard Stark</a>'s household guards at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. He is a loyal and trusted servant of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a> and a good swordsman in his own right. He is the nephew of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ser Rodrik Cassel","link":"Rodrik Cassel","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Cassel|Ser Rodrik Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Cassel" title="Rodrik Cassel">Ser Rodrik Cassel</a>. He has a noticeable scar, gained at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"siege of Pyke","link":"siege of Pyke","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[siege of Pyke]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Pyke" title="Siege of Pyke">siege of Pyke</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Jory is the son of Martyn Cassel, one of Eddard's companions who was killed in a duel with three members of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> at the Tower of Joy while they were searching for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lyanna","link":"Lyanna Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lyanna Stark|Lyanna]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Lyanna_Stark" title="Lyanna Stark">Lyanna</a> during the closing days of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. Jory is a skilled warrior and a stalwart servant of the house.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Jory's role in the series is somewhat expanded from the books. Dialogue from several different Stark guardsmen is given to Jory (most notably his promise to Arya that he will keep Eddard safe, which in the book was given by a guardsman named Desmond) and the scenes where he talks to Ser Hugh and later Jaime Lannister are inventions of the TV series. In the book he is not killed personally by Jaime, but by the Lannister guardsmen that ultimately overwhelmed the small Stark contingent.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to the TV series official <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"pronunciation guide","link":"pronunciation guide","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[pronunciation guide]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167079829956cca60002232" href="/wiki/Pronunciation_guide" title="Pronunciation guide">pronunciation guide</a> developed for the cast and crew, "Cassel" is pronounced "Cuss-EL", not "Kas-el" (that is, "Cassel" doesn't rhyme with "castle").
</p>
