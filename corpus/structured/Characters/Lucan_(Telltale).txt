<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lucan first appears when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mira Forrester","link":"Mira Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mira Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Mira_Forrester" title="Mira Forrester">Mira Forrester</a> attempts to sneak into the gardens at midnight to meet with an unknown person who left a note in her room. Lucan spots her and demands that she returns to her chambers. However, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Damien","link":"Damien","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Damien]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Damien" title="Damien">Damien</a> then arrives and assures Lucan that Mira is with him, and Lucan begrudgingly allows her to stay.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lucan is later seen searching for Damien after he attacks Mira and is either killed or left with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tom","link":"Tom (Telltale)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tom (Telltale)|Tom]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Tom_(Telltale)" title="Tom (Telltale)">Tom</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Sword in the Darkness","link":"The Sword in the Darkness","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Sword in the Darkness]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/The_Sword_in_the_Darkness" title="The Sword in the Darkness">The Sword in the Darkness</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">At the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"royal wedding","link":"Purple Wedding","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Purple Wedding|royal wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Purple_Wedding" title="Purple Wedding">royal wedding</a>, Lucan is on guard duty when he again encounters Mira with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sera Flowers","link":"Sera Flowers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sera Flowers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Sera_Flowers" title="Sera Flowers">Sera Flowers</a>. He demands to know what she was doing with Damien in the garden and tries to detain her, but he is prevented by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Morgryn","link":"Rickard Morgryn","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickard Morgryn|Lord Morgryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Rickard_Morgryn" title="Rickard Morgryn">Lord Morgryn</a>, who intentionally bumps into him and spills wine over his clothes. Morgryn then requests Mira's help to Lucan's initial refusal, until Morgryn reminds him of their different social statuses. Lucan then reluctantly leaves Mira and returns to his watch.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Nest of Vipers","link":"A Nest of Vipers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Nest of Vipers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/A_Nest_of_Vipers" title="A Nest of Vipers">A Nest of Vipers</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lucan seeks out Mira Forrester on behalf of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> and brings the handmaiden to the queen. Cersei reveals that Lucan spotted Mira at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tommen's","link":"Tommen Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tommen Baratheon|Tommen's]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Tommen_Baratheon" title="Tommen Baratheon">Tommen's</a> coronation festival.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lucan later escorts Mira to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion's","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion's]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion's</a> cell, giving her wine to help loosen his tongue. When Tyrion figures out that Mira was sent by Cersei, Lucan storms in, berating her. Shortly thereafter, he grabs Mira and forcefully removes her from the cell.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Ice Dragon","link":"The Ice Dragon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Ice Dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121724630756cca76362a37" href="/wiki/The_Ice_Dragon" title="The Ice Dragon">The Ice Dragon</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Betrayed by Morgryn, Mira is handed over to Lucan, he locked her up in a cell and wait for execution.
</p>
