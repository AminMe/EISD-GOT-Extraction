<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Tanselle was defended from Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerion Targaryen","link":"Aerion Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerion Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Aerion_Targaryen" title="Aerion Targaryen">Aerion Targaryen</a> by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a>, who was still a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"hedge knight","link":"hedge knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[hedge knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Hedge_knight" title="Hedge knight">hedge knight</a> at the time. Duncan broke several of Aerion's teeth in the process.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsguard (Histories & Lore)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Tanselle appears in <i>The Hedge Knight</i>, the first of the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tales of Dunk and Egg","link":"Tales of Dunk and Egg","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tales of Dunk and Egg]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Tales_of_Dunk_and_Egg" title="Tales of Dunk and Egg">Tales of Dunk and Egg</a></i> novellas, in which she is the love interest of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Tanselle was tall and slim, with small breasts. Her hair was black and her skin tone resembled the color of olives. Due to her height, she was nicknamed "Tanselle Too-Tall".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Tanselle was a puppeteer whom Dunk gained an instant attraction to upon meeting her at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tourney","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|tourney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">tourney</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ashford","link":"Ashford","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ashford]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Ashford" title="Ashford">Ashford</a>, at which he also befriended Egg, the boy who would grow up to become King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon V Targaryen","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Aegon V Targaryen</a>. Dunk asked her to paint his shield after seeing her puppet show. While performing a skit in which the hero kills a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragon","link":"Dragons","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dragons|dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragon</a>, she was attacked and insulted and had one of her fingers broken by Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerion Targaryen","link":"Aerion Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerion Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Aerion_Targaryen" title="Aerion Targaryen">Aerion Targaryen</a>, who took the dragon's death as a slight against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a> and accused her of treason. Dunk came to her rescue, but was taken prisoner for striking one of the royal princes. Tanselle and her family left for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a> as quickly as possible afterwards, but she left behind Dunk's painted shield. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">At some point between the ending of <i>The Hedge Knight</i> and the start of <i>The Sworn Sword</i>, Dunk and Egg reached <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Prince's Pass","link":"Prince's Pass","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Prince's Pass]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Prince's_Pass" title="Prince's Pass">Prince's Pass</a> and later <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vaith","link":"Vaith","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vaith]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-54140710356cca83f0e300" href="/wiki/Vaith" title="Vaith">Vaith</a> in Dorne. They did not find her, and Dunk gave up hope that they would. Her fate after this is unknown.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Tanselle would hence appear in any potential TV adaptation of <i>The Hedge Knight</i>.
</p>
