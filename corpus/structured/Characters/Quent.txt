<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Quent is a House Stark guardsman. He accompanies <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran","link":"Bran","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/Bran" title="Bran" class="mw-redirect">Bran</a> and Robb to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wolfswood","link":"Wolfswood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wolfswood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/Wolfswood" title="Wolfswood">Wolfswood</a> during Bran's first ride since being crippled. After the outbreak of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>, Quent marches south with the rest of the Northern force, and fights under the command of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hallis Mollen","link":"Hallis Mollen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hallis Mollen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/Hallis_Mollen" title="Hallis Mollen">Hallis Mollen</a>, the Winterfell captain of the guards after <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jory Cassel","link":"Jory Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jory Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/Jory_Cassel" title="Jory Cassel">Jory Cassel</a>'s departure. Quent is one of the guardsmen ordered to accompany <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ned Stark","link":"Ned Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ned Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/Ned_Stark" title="Ned Stark" class="mw-redirect">Ned Stark</a>'s remains to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> after they are returned to Robb Stark as a gesture of goodwill by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56061081756cca747799c1" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>.
</p>
