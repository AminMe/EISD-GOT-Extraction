<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118536922756cca6bf6e02e" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Mikken is the blacksmith at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118536922756cca6bf6e02e" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. It was not <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Farlen","link":"Farlen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Farlen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118536922756cca6bf6e02e" href="/wiki/Farlen" title="Farlen">Farlen</a> the kennelmaster but he that talked defiantly to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118536922756cca6bf6e02e" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a>. One of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"Stygg","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stygg|ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118536922756cca6bf6e02e" href="/wiki/Stygg" title="Stygg">ironborn</a> beat and killed Mikken for his defiance.
</p>
