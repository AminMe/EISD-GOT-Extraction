Mikken is a recurring background character in the first season. He is played uncredited by weapons master Boyd Rankin and debuts in the series premiere. Mikken is the blacksmith at Winterfell. At Jon's request, he forged the sword Needle for Arya. Biography 

 Season 1 

Mikken is seen in the background when Bran is practicing archery while his brothers watch (particularly in the close-up shot ofRickon'slaugh). Later Mikkenassembles with the Winterfell household to receive King Robert Baratheon.
