Lord Branfield was the head of House Branfield, and father to Elissa and Malcolm Branfield. He is mentioned in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. History 

 Background 

Lord Branfield was a Targaryen loyalist and fought for the crown during Robert's Rebellion. During the rebellion, he, along with most of his children, were slain. His daughter, Elissa, who married Gregor Forrester, and Malcolm managed to survive. Iron From Ice
