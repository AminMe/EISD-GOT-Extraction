<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lord Branfield was a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen|Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203515156456cca7e42a8fe" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">Targaryen</a> loyalist and fought for the crown during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203515156456cca7e42a8fe" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. During the rebellion, he, along with most of his children, were slain. His daughter, Elissa, who married <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Forrester","link":"Gregor Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203515156456cca7e42a8fe" href="/wiki/Gregor_Forrester" title="Gregor Forrester">Gregor Forrester</a>, and Malcolm managed to survive.
</p>
