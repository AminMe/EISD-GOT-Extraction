<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->A <b>Mole's Town whore</b> is an unnamed minor character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fourth season","link":"Season 4","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4|fourth season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">fourth season</a>. She is played by guest star <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lois Winstone","link":"Lois Winstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lois Winstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Lois_Winstone" title="Lois Winstone">Lois Winstone</a>.
</p>
<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">She is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"whore","link":"Prostitution","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Prostitution|whore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Prostitution" title="Prostitution">whore</a> working in the brothel in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mole's Town","link":"Mole's Town","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mole's Town]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Mole's_Town" title="Mole's Town">Mole's Town</a>.
</p>
<h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Samwell Tarly","link":"Samwell Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Samwell Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Samwell_Tarly" title="Samwell Tarly">Samwell Tarly</a> brings <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gilly","link":"Gilly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gilly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Gilly" title="Gilly">Gilly</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sam","link":"Sam","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sam]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Sam" title="Sam">Sam</a> to stay at the brothel, the whore approaches Gilly and begins asking questions. When Gilly tells her that she comes from north of there, the whore realizes that she is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wildling","link":"wildling","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wildling]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Wildling" title="Wildling" class="mw-redirect">wildling</a> and becomes antagonistic towards her.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Breaker of Chains]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Some weeks later, the whore tries to impress <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kegs","link":"Kegs","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kegs]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Kegs" title="Kegs">Kegs</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Black Jack Bulwer","link":"Jack Bulwer","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jack Bulwer|Black Jack Bulwer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Jack_Bulwer" title="Jack Bulwer">Black Jack Bulwer</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mully","link":"Mully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Mully" title="Mully">Mully</a> by belching out the melodies to popular songs such as "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Bear and the Maiden Fair","link":"The Bear and the Maiden Fair (song)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Bear and the Maiden Fair (song)|The Bear and the Maiden Fair]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/The_Bear_and_the_Maiden_Fair_(song)" title="The Bear and the Maiden Fair (song)">The Bear and the Maiden Fair</a>". She takes a break to complain to Gilly about her baby's crying; Gilly, however, is more concerned with the sound of a persistent owl she hears outside. During the subsequent raid on Mole's Town, the whore tries to fight, but dies when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ygritte","link":"Ygritte","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ygritte]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Ygritte" title="Ygritte">Ygritte</a> kills her with a spear thrust to the stomach.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Mountain and the Viper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Gilly is never taken to Mole's Town to seek refuge, as Gilly and Sam arrive to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a> much later into the storyline after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"battle","link":"Battle of Castle Black","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Castle Black|battle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67556398156cca6ca791e2" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Castle_Black" title="Battle of Castle Black">battle</a> is over, without passing through the village, which is abandoned at that point.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The books mention three whores of Mole's Town: Zei, Lady Meliana, and Sky Blue Su. They are among the villagers who come to seek refuge at Castle Black. None of them interacts with Gilly. Since very little information is given about them, it is unknown if the whore in the TV show is based on any of them.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rumors that the Mole's Town whore is named "Momo" are erroneous.
</p>
