<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Axel Baratheon is an early member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84858971556cca7ca8164d" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a>, a descendent of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Orys Baratheon","link":"Orys Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Orys Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84858971556cca7ca8164d" href="/wiki/Orys_Baratheon" title="Orys Baratheon">Orys Baratheon</a> and a distant ancestor of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84858971556cca7ca8164d" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> and his brothers <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84858971556cca7ca8164d" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon|Renly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-84858971556cca7ca8164d" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly</a>.
</p>
