<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Merry is one of the many granddaughters of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of the Crossing","link":"Lord of the Crossing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of the Crossing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/Lord_of_the_Crossing" title="Lord of the Crossing">Lord of the Crossing</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Along with several of Lord Frey's other daughters and granddaughters, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> had the option of marrying her. When he arrives at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Twins","link":"the Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">the Twins</a> for his uncle <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Edmure","link":"Edmure Tully","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Edmure Tully|Edmure]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/Edmure_Tully" title="Edmure Tully">Edmure</a>'s <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding|wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">wedding</a>, Robb apologizes for his slight against them. When introducing Merry, Lord Walder calls her Wertha, Walra, and Waldina before she meekly corrects him, though he quickly dismisses her.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Rains of Castamere (episode)|The Rains of Castamere]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> However, during the wedding feast she sits at the main table alongside Walder Frey's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wife","link":"Joyeuse Erenford","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joyeuse Erenford|wife]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/Joyeuse_Erenford" title="Joyeuse Erenford">wife</a>, Lord Walder himself, and the newlywed couple - the only one of Lord Walder's children (other than the bride Roslin) to sit as his table during the wedding.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Rains of Castamere (episode)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, there is a granddaughter of Lord Walder named Merianne Frey, who is also known as Merry, who is eleven years old (not to be confused with Marianne Vance, daughter of Walder's daughter Maegelle, whose first name is spelled with an "a" not an "e"). She is the daughter of Ser Whalen Frey and Sylwa Paege. Whalen is the third child of Lord Walder's fourth wife, Alyssa Blackwood. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lame Lothar Frey","link":"Lothar Frey","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lothar Frey|Lame Lothar Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53445500556cca75db8f8b" href="/wiki/Lothar_Frey" title="Lothar Frey">Lame Lothar Frey</a> is Walder's eldest child with Alyssa, making him Whalen's older brother and Merianne's uncle.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Merry was one of the Frey women presented to Robb Stark when he came to the Twins for Lord Edmure Tully's wedding. When she corrects her grandfather on her name, he refers to her as "Noisy", rather than dismissing her. 
</p>
