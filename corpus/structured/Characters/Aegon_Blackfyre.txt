 Background 

Aegon Blackfyre was the firstborn son of Daemon Blackfyre, a legitimized bastard son of King Aegon IV Targaryen and his cousin Daena Targaryen, by Serena Blackfyre. He had two brothers: Aemon, his twin, and Daemon II Blackfyre. Season 1
