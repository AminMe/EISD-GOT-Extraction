<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Bannen is a Ranger of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>, originally from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Harbor","link":"White Harbor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Harbor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/White_Harbor" title="White Harbor">White Harbor</a>, the only city in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>. He takes part in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Ranging","link":"Great Ranging","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Ranging]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/Great_Ranging" title="Great Ranging">Great Ranging</a> led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jeor Mormont","link":"Jeor Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jeor Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/Jeor_Mormont" title="Jeor Mormont">Jeor Mormont</a> and survives the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Fist of the First Men","link":"Battle of the Fist of the First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Fist of the First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Fist_of_the_First_Men" title="Battle of the Fist of the First Men">Battle of the Fist of the First Men</a>, though he's injured.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Bannen is one of survivors of the battle at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Fist of the First Men","link":"Fist of the First Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fist of the First Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-10421558756cca6a414148" href="/wiki/Fist_of_the_First_Men" title="Fist of the First Men">Fist of the First Men</a> who make it to Craster's Keep. He and eleven others are critically wounded. Sam and Giant try to treat him and the other injured men, but the medicine bags have been lost during the battle, thus all they can do is keep the wounded dry and warm and feed them. The ranger Kedge Whiteye has taken Bannen's mangled foot, but it is too little and too late. Bannen keeps saying "I'm cold. I'm so cold". Sam tries to feed him onion broth, but he cannot swallow. Craster nastily comments "That one's dead. Be kinder to stick a knife in his chest than that spoon down his throat, you ask me".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After lying in agony at least nine days, Bannen dies of his injuries, much as he did in the TV series. In the book it is Dirk who blames Craster in Bannen's death.
</p>
