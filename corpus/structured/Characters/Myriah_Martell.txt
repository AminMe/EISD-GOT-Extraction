 Background 

Myriah Martell was a Dornish Princess of the House Martell of Sunspear. She married King Daeron II Targaryen and became the mother of Baelor, Aerys, Rhaegel, and Maekar; and one daughter, Aelinor. Season 1

 In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Queen Myriah married Daeron II as part of the marriage pact that brought Dorne under the rule of the Iron Throne. Though originall named Myriah in the novels, her name has been retconned as Mariah Martell in The World of Ice and Fire. See also (spoilers from the books) References
