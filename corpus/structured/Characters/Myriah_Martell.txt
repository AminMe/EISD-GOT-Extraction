<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Myriah Martell was a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dornish","link":"Dorne","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne|Dornish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dornish</a> Princess of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Martell","link":"House Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/House_Martell" title="House Martell">House Martell</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sunspear","link":"Sunspear","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sunspear]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/Sunspear" title="Sunspear">Sunspear</a>. She married King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daeron II Targaryen","link":"Daeron II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daeron II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/Daeron_II_Targaryen" title="Daeron II Targaryen">Daeron II Targaryen</a> and became the mother of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Baelor","link":"Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)|Baelor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)" title="Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)">Baelor</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys","link":"Aerys I Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys I Targaryen|Aerys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/Aerys_I_Targaryen" title="Aerys I Targaryen">Aerys</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegel","link":"Rhaegel Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegel Targaryen|Rhaegel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/Rhaegel_Targaryen" title="Rhaegel Targaryen">Rhaegel</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maekar","link":"Maekar Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maekar Targaryen|Maekar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/Maekar_Targaryen" title="Maekar Targaryen">Maekar</a>; and one daughter, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aelinor","link":"Aelinor Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aelinor Targaryen|Aelinor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-149465353256cca714915bc" href="/wiki/Aelinor_Targaryen" title="Aelinor Targaryen">Aelinor</a>.
</p>
