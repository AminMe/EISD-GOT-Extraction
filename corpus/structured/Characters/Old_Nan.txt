<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Old Nan is an elderly woman living in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. She is a retired servant of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a> known for her tale-telling abilities. She has entertained the children of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark|Eddard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn</a> with stories throughout their childhoods. Her great grandson <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hodor","link":"Hodor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hodor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Hodor" title="Hodor">Hodor</a> is a stable hand at the castle.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">After <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> leaves for King's Landing, Old Nan sits by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a>’s bedside to watch him. She suggests the story of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a> and Bran replies that he hates her stories —he prefers the scary ones. She retorts that he is a <i>"sweet summer child"</i> who knows nothing about fear, and tells him that fear is for the winter and for the Long Night, a winter season thousand of years ago that lasted a generation, in which those who didn't freeze to death had to face the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Walkers","link":"White Walkers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walkers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/White_Walkers" title="White Walkers">White Walkers</a>, who ventured south for the first time and swept through Westeros.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Lord Snow]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a> interrupts another story and says that he would go mad if he was left alone with Old Nan.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Actress Margaret John, who played Old Nan, passed away only two months before Season 1 began to air, though all of her scenes had already been completed. The character does appear during the second book, however, in the TV series canon the showrunners decided to retire the character out of respect, so Old Nan is assumed to have quietly passed away between Season 1 and Season 2.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Old Nan is the oldest person living at Winterfell. She came to Winterfell to act as wetnurse for a Brandon Stark, which one is unsure, only that his mother had died birthing him. This Brandon Stark is believed to be either the brother of Lord Rickard Stark or a brother of his father. She's retired but tells stories and legends to the Stark children. She lost two sons in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a> and her grandson during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>, while her daughters moved away and died. Her only surviving family is her great-grandson, the gentle, slow-witted <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hodor","link":"Hodor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hodor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Hodor" title="Hodor">Hodor</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, it is Old Nan and not <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Osha","link":"Osha","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Osha]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Osha" title="Osha">Osha</a> who interprets the arrival of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Comet","link":"Red Comet","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Comet]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Red_Comet" title="Red Comet">Red Comet</a> to mean the return of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragon","link":"dragon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon" class="mw-redirect">dragons</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"Nan" might be short for "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Nymeria","link":"Nymeria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nymeria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-108284612556cca626ae27a" href="/wiki/Nymeria" title="Nymeria">Nymeria</a>".
