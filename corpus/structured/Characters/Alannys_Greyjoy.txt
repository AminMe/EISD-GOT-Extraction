<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Alannys Greyjoy was born into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Harlaw","link":"House Harlaw","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Harlaw]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/House_Harlaw" title="House Harlaw">House Harlaw</a><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/#!\/guide\/houses\/greyjoy\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 guide to houses, House Greyjoy entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> and married <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon Greyjoy","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon Greyjoy</a>, head of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Greyjoy","link":"House Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/House_Greyjoy" title="House Greyjoy">House Greyjoy</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Reaper of Pyke","link":"Lord Reaper of Pyke","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Reaper of Pyke]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke" title="Lord Reaper of Pyke">Lord Reaper of Pyke</a>. They had four children: <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik","link":"Rodrik Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Greyjoy|Rodrik]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Greyjoy" title="Rodrik Greyjoy">Rodrik</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maron","link":"Maron Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maron Greyjoy|Maron]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Maron_Greyjoy" title="Maron Greyjoy">Maron</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yara","link":"Yara Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yara Greyjoy|Yara]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Yara_Greyjoy" title="Yara Greyjoy">Yara</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy|Theon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon</a>. Only her daughter Yara and her youngest son Theon are still alive.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Her two eldest sons were killed in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, her marriage to Lord Balon Greyjoy and the children they had remains the same except Yara is named Asha. Because the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"island of Harlaw","link":"Harlaw","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harlaw|island of Harlaw]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Harlaw" title="Harlaw">island of Harlaw</a> is the wealthiest of the Iron Islands, House Harlaw is one of the few ironborn families that approach the Greyjoys in strength and influence. She has an older sister Gwynesse, who was widowed by the Greyjoy Rebellion, and a younger brother Lord Rodrik Harlaw known as Rodrik the Reader. Her brother refers to her by the pet name "Lanny."
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Alannys was devastated by Theon being taken hostage and the deaths of her two eldest sons in the Greyjoy Rebellion, and began wandering the halls at night shrieking out their names. She had five ironborn boys brought to be fostered at Pyke after Ned Stark brought Theon to Winterfell including Tristifer Botley, a childhood love of Asha's. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When her psychosis worsened, she left Pyke to retire to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ten Towers","link":"Ten Towers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ten Towers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-186463336256cca6643dc1e" href="/wiki/Ten_Towers" title="Ten Towers">Ten Towers</a>, her family's seat on the island of Harlaw. Thus she is not present at Pyke when Theon returns to see his father. Asha briefly visits her mother before leaving to conquer Deepwood Motte in her part in Balon's invasion of the North. During the visit Alannys constantly asks about Theon who she thinks of as perpetually ten years old, his age when he was taken hostage. Asha feels she no longer knows her mother though she is kind to her and answers her questions patiently. When Asha returns to the Iron Islands, she cannot bring herself to tell her mother Theon is dead (as Asha incorrectly presumes). Asha meets Tristifer Botley at Ten Towers who informs her that he still only loves Asha. He has asked and received Alannys's consent to marry Asha though Asha rejects the proposal.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Alannys has long white hair. She "never had the sort of beauty the singers cherished, but her daughter had loved her fierce strong face and the laughter in her eyes", although Asha now finds Alannys's eyes dim and cloudy.
</p>
