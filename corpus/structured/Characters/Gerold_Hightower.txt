<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Gerold Hightower was known as "the White Bull" for his immense strength. He was Lord Commander of the Kingsguard during the brief reign of King Jaehaerys II and then under King Aerys II.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Gerold Hightower, similarly to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arthur Dayne","link":"Arthur Dayne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arthur Dayne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Arthur_Dayne" title="Arthur Dayne">Arthur Dayne</a>, was blindly loyal to the Mad King, no matter how cruel and bloodthirsty Aerys was. He is present when Aerys <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"executes","link":"Execution of Rickard and Brandon Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Execution of Rickard and Brandon Stark|executes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Execution_of_Rickard_and_Brandon_Stark" title="Execution of Rickard and Brandon Stark">executes</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickard Stark","link":"Rickard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Rickard_Stark" title="Rickard Stark">Rickard Stark</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his son","link":"Brandon Stark (Son of Rickard)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brandon Stark (Son of Rickard)|his son]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Brandon_Stark_(Son_of_Rickard)" title="Brandon Stark (Son of Rickard)">his son</a>. He notices that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> disapproves of the unjust atrocity and tells him "You swore a vow to guard the king, not to judge him".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During Robert's Rebellion, Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegar","link":"Rhaegar Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegar Targaryen|Rhaegar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Rhaegar_Targaryen" title="Rhaegar Targaryen">Rhaegar</a> left Ser Gerold and his fellow Kingsguard Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oswell Whent","link":"Oswell Whent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oswell Whent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Oswell_Whent" title="Oswell Whent">Oswell Whent</a> and Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arthur Dayne","link":"Arthur Dayne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arthur Dayne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Arthur_Dayne" title="Arthur Dayne">Arthur Dayne</a> to guard <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lyanna Stark","link":"Lyanna Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lyanna Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Lyanna_Stark" title="Lyanna Stark">Lyanna Stark</a> at the Tower of Joy near the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Mountains","link":"Red Mountains","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Mountains]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Red_Mountains" title="Red Mountains">Red Mountains</a> of Dorne. After the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Trident","link":"Battle of the Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Trident" title="Battle of the Trident">Battle of the Trident</a>, Eddard Stark and six companions arrived to rescue Lyanna and were confronted by the Kingsguard. Despite it being apparent that the Targaryens had lost the war, Ser Gerold refused to surrender, insisting that "the Kingsguard does not flee." In the fight that followed, all three Kingsguard were slain, along with five of Stark's friends. Only Stark himself and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Howland Reed","link":"Howland Reed","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Howland Reed]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-6204388256cca684d075f" href="/wiki/Howland_Reed" title="Howland Reed">Howland Reed</a> survived. It is unclear exactly who killed Ser Gerold. Lord Stark later had the tower demolished and used the stone to make eight cairns, in honor of the eight who died there.
</p>
