<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171706773956cca6a64fc42" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Willem Darry was the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"master-at-arms","link":"master-at-arms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[master-at-arms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171706773956cca6a64fc42" href="/wiki/Master-at-arms" title="Master-at-arms" class="mw-redirect">master-at-arms</a> at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171706773956cca6a64fc42" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a> and the brother of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jonothor Darry","link":"Jonothor Darry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jonothor Darry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171706773956cca6a64fc42" href="/wiki/Jonothor_Darry" title="Jonothor Darry">Jonothor Darry</a> of the Kingsguard. He was a grey bear of a man who was half-blind. He had a gruff, kind voice and big wrinkled hands, soft as old leather.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After the Mad King's death in the Sack of King's Landing and Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaella's","link":"Rhaella Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaella Targaryen|Rhaella's]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171706773956cca6a64fc42" href="/wiki/Rhaella_Targaryen" title="Rhaella Targaryen">Rhaella's</a> death during labor, the garrison of Dragonstone considered selling Viserys and the infant Daenerys, to the new king. However, before they could come to a decision, Ser Willem and four loyal men smuggled Viserys and Daenerys from the island and fled to safety in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171706773956cca6a64fc42" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a>. However, Ser Willem was old and some years later he became sick and died, leaving Viserys and Daenerys to fend for themselves.
</p>
