<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-32379702156cca71ec150c" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Aelinor is not mentioned precisely as Aerys I's sister-wife, though it was assumed by fandom she was a Targaryen due to the custom of dynastic incest. The prop of the <i>Lineages</i> book that Eddard reads in Season 1 of the TV series (written by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bryan Cogman","link":"Bryan Cogman","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bryan Cogman]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-32379702156cca71ec150c" href="/wiki/Bryan_Cogman" title="Bryan Cogman">Bryan Cogman</a>) also made this assumpetion.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In <i>The World of Ice and Fire</i> sourcebook, however, it is revealed that she was actually a member of House Penrose, a noble house from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stormlands","link":"the Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-32379702156cca71ec150c" href="/wiki/The_Stormlands" title="The Stormlands">the Stormlands</a>, as well as a cousin to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-32379702156cca71ec150c" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a>. This information was only released in 2014, after Season 1. So far there has been no official update to her status in the TV continuity.
</p>
