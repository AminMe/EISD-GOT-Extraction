<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Kurleket is a loyal <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Man-at-Arms","link":"Man-at-arms","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Man-at-arms|Man-at-Arms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Man-at-arms" title="Man-at-arms">Man-at-Arms</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bracken","link":"House Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/House_Bracken" title="House Bracken">House Bracken</a>, a vassal house of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a>. He is traveling the Kingsroad following the Tourney of the Hand.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Kurleket is drinking with companions at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a> when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> arrives on his way south. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> is also in the inn and Tyrion recognizes her. She turns to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knights","link":"knights","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knights]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Knights" title="Knights" class="mw-redirect">knights</a> from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a> in the inn to help her to take Tyrion into custody, accusing him of attempting to murder her son <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a>. She asserts her origin as a daughter of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> and then tells the man of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bracken","link":"House Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/House_Bracken" title="House Bracken">House Bracken</a> that she recognizes the red stallion of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bracken","link":"House Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/House_Bracken" title="House Bracken">House Bracken</a> and says that her father Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a> considers Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jonos Bracken","link":"Jonos Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jonos Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/Jonos_Bracken" title="Jonos Bracken">Jonos Bracken</a> one of his most loyal bannermen. Catelyn announces that she will return Tyrion to Winterfell to await the King's justice. The Knight of House Bracken draws his sword and threatens Tyrion.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Tyrion in the inn.png|thumb|Kurleket holds his sword on Tyrion Lannister at the Crossroads Inn.]]","title":"Tyrion_in_the_inn.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Tyrion in the inn","caption":"Kurleket holds his sword on Tyrion Lannister at the Crossroads Inn.","captionParsed":"Kurleket holds his sword on Tyrion Lannister at the Crossroads Inn."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" alt="" src="http://vignette4.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/b/b9/Tyrion_in_the_inn.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20110403130357" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Tyrion in the inn.png" data-image-key="Tyrion_in_the_inn.png"  type="image" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, three men-at-arms from House Bracken accompany Catelyn into the Vale. Kurleket is the leader of the trio and he is the one who speaks to Catelyn in the Inn at the Crossroads. He assists in the capture of Tyrion Lannister and is among the party that travels into the Mountains of the Moon. He was slain during a battle with the Mountain clans.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Kurleket's name is an inside joke by author <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"George R.R. Martin","link":"George R.R. Martin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[George R.R. Martin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="/wiki/George_R.R._Martin" title="George R.R. Martin">George R.R. Martin</a>. All three men from House Bracken whom Catelyn recruits at the Crossroads Inn are named "Mohor", "Lharys", and "Kurleket" - as in Moe, Larry, and Curly, the infamous <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Three Stooges","link":"Wikipedia:The Three Stooges","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wikipedia:The Three Stooges|Three Stooges]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Three_Stooges" class="extiw" title="wikipedia:The Three Stooges">Three Stooges</a>. Martin has himself confirmed the reference.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/www.westeros.org\/Citadel\/SSM\/Category\/C91\/ So Spake Martin]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192891578356cca75a8f2e2" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
