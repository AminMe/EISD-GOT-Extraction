<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is an assassin who poses as a musician. Along with a group of others, he is hired by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lothar Frey","link":"Lothar Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lothar Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55272200056cca7e29897a" href="/wiki/Lothar_Frey" title="Lothar Frey">Lothar Frey</a> to participate in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55272200056cca7e29897a" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>.
</p>

<h2>In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55272200056cca7e29897a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the musicians at the Red Wedding are noted to be terrible, however this is due to the fact that they are actually sellswords and crossbowmen in disguise.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Based on Lothar's comment to Talisa in the TV series, the musicians are a group of assassins who developed musical talent to avoid suspicion, hence it cost the Freys a lot of gold to hire them.
