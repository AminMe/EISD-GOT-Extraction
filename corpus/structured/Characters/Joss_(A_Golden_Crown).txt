<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Joss is a peasant from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>.
</p>
<h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He attends court in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> escorted by some bannermen of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> to report a series of brutal raids being carried out by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>. He wears the sigil of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> on his sleeve. He brings a sack of dead fish that had been left by Clegane at the scene of his atrocities. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> responds by stripping Clegane of his titles and ordering his execution.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Golden Crown]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Song of Ice and Fire","link":"Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="Song of Ice and Fire" class="mw-redirect">Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the handful of surviving smallfolk from the town of Sherrer are brought before the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125421306556cca740c0449" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a> by a trio of knights from the Riverlands (Karyl Vance, Marq Piper, and Raymun Darry). The commoner who speaks directly to Ned Stark, and indicates that Gregor Clegane was responsible for the attack, is a brewer from Sherrer named Joss.  The TV series credits only listed him as "Riverlands refugee", but it is clear which book character he was portraying.
</p>
