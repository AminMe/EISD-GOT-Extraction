A Lordsport dockhand is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Jer O'Leary and only appears in The Night Lands. Biography 

 Season 2 

He is working when Theon Greyjoy arrives in Lordsport. He is unmoved by Theon introducing himself as the heir to the Iron Islands and last living son of Balon Greyjoy. Theon pays him to find a horse to take him to Pyke.
