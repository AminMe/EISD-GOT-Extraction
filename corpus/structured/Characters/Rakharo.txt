He is killed by a rival <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"khalasar","link":"khalasar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[khalasar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Khalasar" title="Khalasar">khalasar</a> while scouting the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Waste","link":"Red Waste","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Waste]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Red_Waste" title="Red Waste">Red Waste</a>.

<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Rakharo is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a> warrior who is skilled in combat. He is young, but extremely capable in battle. He was trained by his father, who was a bloodrider in the retinue of a famous <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"khal","link":"khal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[khal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Khal" title="Khal">khal</a></i>, Khal <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bharbo","link":"Bharbo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bharbo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Bharbo" title="Bharbo">Bharbo</a>. He is descended from a long line of bloodriders. He protected Daenerys from her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"brother","link":"Viserys Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Viserys Targaryen|brother]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Viserys_Targaryen" title="Viserys Targaryen">brother</a>'s vicious temper and was one of the first to declare her "blood of my blood." <img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"Rakharo viewers guide\">[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/guide\/houses\/targaryen\/rakharo\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 guide to houses, House Targaryen - Rakharo entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Notes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In discussing the ending of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"David Benioff","link":"David Benioff","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[David Benioff]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/David_Benioff" title="David Benioff">David Benioff</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dan Weiss","link":"Dan Weiss","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dan Weiss]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Dan_Weiss" title="Dan Weiss" class="mw-redirect">Dan Weiss</a> revealed that they did not want to kill off Rakharo, but that the actor was going to leave the show for other projects. Rather than have his character simply disappear, they decided to give him a proper send-off.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels Jhogo is sixteen years old at the start of the series and favors a combination of the whip and <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"arakh","link":"arakh","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[arakh]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Arakh" title="Arakh">arakh</a></i> in battle. He is a warrior in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Khal Drogo","link":"Khal Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Khal Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Khal_Drogo" title="Khal Drogo" class="mw-redirect">Khal Drogo</a>'s <i>khalasar</i> and is assigned to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> as bodyguard by her new husband.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The character of Rakharo is similar, but is older and more seasoned than Jhogo. He has a long, curving mustache and wields the <i>arakh</i> alone in combat. Based on his repeated use of the whip, the Rakharo of the TV series appears to be predominantly based on the Jhogo character from the books.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Following Khal Drogo's death, Rakharo and the other two bloodriders Jhogo and Aggo are among the few who remain with Daenerys. Before entering the funeral pyre, Daenerys gives Rakharo the great arakh that was her bride gift and names him her ko. Rakharo accepts the arakh with reservations, saying "You are khaleesi. I shall ride at your side to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vaes Dothrak","link":"Vaes Dothrak","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vaes Dothrak]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Vaes_Dothrak" title="Vaes Dothrak">Vaes Dothrak</a> beneath the Mother of Mountains, and keep you safe from harm until you take your place with the crones of the dosh khaleen. No more can I promise." However, once Daenerys comes out of the pyre alive and with the born dragons, Rakharo is the third of the bloodriders to declare "Blood of my blood", and since then he is totally loyal to Daenerys.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rakharo survives his scouting mission in the Red Waste. In the book it is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Doreah","link":"Doreah","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Doreah]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Doreah" title="Doreah">Doreah</a> who dies on the journey, contracting a wasting disease. So far, Rakharo, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aggo","link":"Aggo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aggo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Aggo" title="Aggo">Aggo</a> and Jhogo are alive and well.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Daenerys's handmaidens <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Irri","link":"Irri","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Irri]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Irri" title="Irri">Irri</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jhiqui","link":"Jhiqui","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jhiqui]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-100958081156cca60360db6" href="/wiki/Jhiqui" title="Jhiqui">Jhiqui</a> have a crush on Rakharo. Daenerys hear them argue over who he would prefer, but orders them to stop fighting over the blood of her blood.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Following Daenerys's departure from Meereen, Rakharo and Aggo (in the show - Jorah and Daario) and the rest of Daenerys’s khalasar have been dispatched across the river to search for their lost queen at the Dothraki sea.
</p>
