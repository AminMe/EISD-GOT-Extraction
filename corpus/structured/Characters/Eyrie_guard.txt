 An Eyrie Guard is an unnamed minor character in the fifth season. He is played by guest star Paul Kennedy and only appears in The House of Black and White. Biography 

 Background 

He is a guard in service to House Arryn who serves them at the Eyrie. Season 5 

He accompanies Lord Petyr Baelish and Sansa Stark from Runestone. During their stay at an inn, he stands guard as Brienne of Tarth approaches them and tries to convince Sansa to allow her to be her protector.  In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there are several knights, hedge knights, and men-at-arms in Littlefinger's service once he becomes Lord Protector of the Vale. Among them are Ser Lothor Brune, Oswell Kettleblack and Ser Shadrich of the Shady Glen the Mad Mouse, though it is unclear if this man is supposed to be any of them. References
