<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lorren is a famous <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34162680356cca69070b86" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> raider, noted for his experience and ferocity in battle. He is loyal to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Greyjoy","link":"House Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34162680356cca69070b86" href="/wiki/House_Greyjoy" title="House Greyjoy">House Greyjoy</a>, the rulers of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Islands","link":"Iron Islands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Islands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34162680356cca69070b86" href="/wiki/Iron_Islands" title="Iron Islands">Iron Islands</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34162680356cca69070b86" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Black Lorren is regarded as one of the most dangerous and formidable ironborn raiders, second only to Andrik the Unsmiling. He's described as a squat, thick man with a long black beard.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Theon goes to search for the Stark boys, he gives Lorren command over Winterfell and its residents as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"castellan","link":"castellan","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[castellan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34162680356cca69070b86" href="/wiki/Castellan" title="Castellan">castellan</a>. While Ser Rodrik is besieging Winterfell, it is Lorren who urges Theon to fight to the bitter end, but Theon tries instead to negotiate with the besiegers. Although Lorren does not actually defy Theon, he grows to despise him for his behavior, as it is considered weakness and cowardice in the eyes of ironborn.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Ramsay and his men turn on Theon and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sack","link":"Sack of Winterfell","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of Winterfell|sack]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34162680356cca69070b86" href="/wiki/Sack_of_Winterfell" title="Sack of Winterfell">sack</a> Winterfell and kill the ironborn, Lorren fights bravely. It takes four men to kill him.
</p>
