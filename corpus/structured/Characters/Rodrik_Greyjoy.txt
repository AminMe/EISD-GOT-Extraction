</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Rodrik Greyjoy</b> is an unseen character in <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Game of Thrones","link":"Game of Thrones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Game of Thrones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167291573756cca66dd5f2a" href="/wiki/Game_of_Thrones" title="Game of Thrones" class="mw-redirect">Game of Thrones</a></i>. He is deceased when the events of the series begin and is not expected to appear in the series. Rodrik Greyjoy was the son and heir of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balon Greyjoy","link":"Balon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167291573756cca66dd5f2a" href="/wiki/Balon_Greyjoy" title="Balon Greyjoy">Balon Greyjoy</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Reaper of Pyke","link":"Lord Reaper of Pyke","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Reaper of Pyke]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167291573756cca66dd5f2a" href="/wiki/Lord_Reaper_of_Pyke" title="Lord Reaper of Pyke">Lord Reaper of Pyke</a>. Rodrik was killed fighting for his father in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greyjoy Rebellion","link":"Greyjoy Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greyjoy Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167291573756cca66dd5f2a" href="/wiki/Greyjoy_Rebellion" title="Greyjoy Rebellion">Greyjoy Rebellion</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167291573756cca66dd5f2a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Rodrik led a raid on the coastal castle <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seagard","link":"Seagard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seagard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167291573756cca66dd5f2a" href="/wiki/Seagard" title="Seagard">Seagard</a> during the Greyjoy Rebellion. He was killed by Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jason Mallister","link":"Jason Mallister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jason Mallister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167291573756cca66dd5f2a" href="/wiki/Jason_Mallister" title="Jason Mallister">Jason Mallister</a> beneath the walls of Seagard.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In <i>"A Clash of Kings"</i> and <i>"A Dance with Dragons"</i> it is revealed that Rodrik and Maron used to bully Theon. Even after so many years, Theon still remembers the beating and cruel japes he suffered from them. He does not resent Lord Jason for killing Rodrik.
</p>
