<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He works as a food server for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. He wears the same color and fabric as the rest of House Tyrell's servants, suggesting he was brought from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Highgarden","link":"Highgarden","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Highgarden]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/Highgarden" title="Highgarden">Highgarden</a> and not simply employed by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a>.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He waits on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Olenna Tyrell","link":"Olenna Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Olenna Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/Olenna_Tyrell" title="Olenna Tyrell">Olenna Tyrell</a> and her retinue when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Margaery Tyrell","link":"Margaery Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Margaery Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/Margaery_Tyrell" title="Margaery Tyrell">Margaery Tyrell</a> arrives to introduce <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a> to her grandmother so they can learn about King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a>'s true character. A few minutes later, she berates him for insisting that he follow protocol and only serve cheese after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"lemon cakes","link":"Lemon cake","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lemon cake|lemon cakes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" href="/wiki/Lemon_cake" title="Lemon cake">lemon cakes</a>, and orders him to bring her the cheese dish immediately.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Dark Wings, Dark Words]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-85472686356cca769858a4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> Her harsh treatment of him may have been to ensure he was not present during Sansa's confession.
