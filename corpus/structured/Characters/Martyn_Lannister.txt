<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Martyn, along with his brother Willem, is captured by the forces of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Edmure Tully","link":"Edmure Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Edmure Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Edmure_Tully" title="Edmure Tully">Edmure Tully</a> following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Stone Mill","link":"Battle of Stone Mill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Stone Mill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Stone_Mill" title="Battle of Stone Mill">Battle of Stone Mill</a>. He is taken <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"hostage","link":"hostage","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[hostage]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Hostage" title="Hostage">hostage</a> and placed in a cell at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>, the seat of the Tullys.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> visits Riverrun to attend the funerals of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a>, Martyn and Willem are visited in their cell by Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talisa Stark","link":"Talisa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talisa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Talisa_Stark" title="Talisa Stark">Talisa Stark</a>, who tends to Martyn's wounded hand while Willem looks in silence. Martyn asks if its true that Robb can turn into a wolf at night and devours the flesh of his enemies.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Walk of Punishment]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Martyn and Willem are murdered inside their cells by Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickard Karstark","link":"Rickard Karstark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickard Karstark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Rickard_Karstark" title="Rickard Karstark">Rickard Karstark</a> as vengeance for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>'s killing of his son, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Torrhen Karstark","link":"Torrhen Karstark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Torrhen Karstark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Torrhen_Karstark" title="Torrhen Karstark">Torrhen Karstark</a>. Their bodies are presented to Robb, and as Rickard defends his actions as them being kin to Jaime, Robb furiously condemns this as saying they were innocent boys.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Kissed by Fire]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Behind the scenes </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Martyn is played by Dean-Charles Chapman, who would go on to be cast as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tommen Baratheon","link":"Tommen Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tommen Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Tommen_Baratheon" title="Tommen Baratheon">Tommen Baratheon</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fourth season","link":"season 4","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[season 4|fourth season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">fourth season</a> of the television series.
</p>
<h2>In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Martyn and Willem are the twin sons of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kevan Lannister","link":"Kevan Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kevan Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Kevan_Lannister" title="Kevan Lannister">Kevan Lannister</a>, and younger brothers of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lancel Lannister","link":"Lancel Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lancel Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Lancel_Lannister" title="Lancel Lannister" class="mw-redirect">Lancel Lannister</a>. Martyn is captured at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Oxcross","link":"Battle of Oxcross","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Oxcross]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-102369101256cca60878710" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Oxcross" title="Battle of Oxcross">Battle of Oxcross</a>. It is not Martyn but his cousin Tion Frey who is killed with Willem by Rickard Karstark. After Willem and Tion's death, Robb ordered to triple the guard on Martyn for his protection, and later exchanged him for a northman prisoner (Robett Glover). Martyn is not mentioned in the books afterwards.
</p>
