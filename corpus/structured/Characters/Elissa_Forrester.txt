<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Elissa Forrester was born into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Branfield","link":"House Branfield","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Branfield]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/House_Branfield" title="House Branfield">House Branfield</a>, a minor Southron house that was destroyed when the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen|Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">Targaryen</a> dynasty fell. She had been married to Gregor for many years when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a> signaled her family's demise; yet she nonetheless felt conflicted between her father's loyalties and her husband's. In time, Elissa came to feel that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>, and she embraced her role as the Forrester matriarch. She silently sworn that she'll never see her family destroyed again.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Elissa Forrester - Game Codex Entry<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">They had several children together: <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester|Rodrik]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Asher","link":"Asher Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Asher Forrester|Asher]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Asher_Forrester" title="Asher Forrester">Asher</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mira","link":"Mira Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mira Forrester|Mira]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Mira_Forrester" title="Mira Forrester">Mira</a>. During her fourth pregnancy, she dreamed she would birth a daughter with a voice like summer rain. But as destiny would have it, she gave birth to a boy. The infant, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ethan","link":"Ethan Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ethan Forrester|Ethan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Ethan_Forrester" title="Ethan Forrester">Ethan</a>, wailed for hours - until Lady Elissa entered a second labor, this time birthing a girl. She named the girl <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talia","link":"Talia Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talia Forrester|Talia]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Talia_Forrester" title="Talia Forrester">Talia</a>, after her own mother, and from then on Ethan never cried again. She gave birth to one more son a few years later, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ryon","link":"Ryon Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ryon Forrester|Ryon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Ryon_Forrester" title="Ryon Forrester">Ryon</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Ethan Forrester - Game Codex Entry<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Talia Forrester - Game Codex Entry<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Her son, Asher, later went into self-exile in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Essos","link":"Essos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Essos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Essos" title="Essos">Essos</a> to stop a war being started over his love for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gwyn Whitehill","link":"Gwyn Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gwyn Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Gwyn_Whitehill" title="Gwyn Whitehill">Gwyn Whitehill</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Asher Forrester - Game Codex Entry<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> Lady Elissa wanted her eldest daughter - Mira - to learn the ways of a southern court, so arranged for her to serve as a handmaiden for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Margaery Tyrell","link":"Margaery Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Margaery Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Margaery_Tyrell" title="Margaery Tyrell">Margaery Tyrell</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>Mira Forrester - Game Codex Entry<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron From Ice","link":"Iron From Ice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron From Ice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Iron_From_Ice" title="Iron From Ice">Iron From Ice</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gared Tuttle","link":"Gared Tuttle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gared Tuttle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Gared_Tuttle" title="Gared Tuttle">Gared Tuttle</a>, Gregor's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"squire","link":"squire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[squire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Squire" title="Squire">squire</a> returns from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Twins","link":"The Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">The Twins</a>, bearing news that Gregor and Rodrik have been slain in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>. He returns Gregor's Greatsword. Her son, Ethan is made the new Lord of Ironrath and Elissa attends his council meetings, giving him advice and helping to keep <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ser Royland Degore","link":"Royland Degore","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Royland Degore|Ser Royland Degore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Royland_Degore" title="Royland Degore">Ser Royland Degore</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan Tuttle","link":"Duncan Tuttle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan Tuttle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Duncan_Tuttle" title="Duncan Tuttle">Duncan Tuttle</a> from bickering.

Lord Whitehill is received by Ethan in the Great Hall and quickly begins antagonizing the young boy. Lady Elissa arrives and reminds him to show her son some respect as they are in his home.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">She writes to her daughter, Mira, in King's Landing asking her to try and convince Lady Margaery to use her influence in court to convince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King Joffrey","link":"King Joffrey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King Joffrey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/King_Joffrey" title="King Joffrey" class="mw-redirect">King Joffrey</a> to let House Forrester keep their Ironwood forests, overruling whatever House Bolton would decide, as Lord Whitehill planned to go to them, as House Forrester's new liege lords, so they could relinquish House Forrester's rights to the Ironwood forests and hand them over to House Whitehill.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind her son's back she arranges for her brother, Malcolm, to sail to Essos to find Asher and bring him back. She claims Asher has no interest in becoming lord, but could lead men in battle should a war begin between the Forresters and the Whitehills. Ethan can either agree with this plan or refuse it.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a> arrives with Lord Whitehill to discuss distributing control over the Ironwood forests, Elissa is present alongside Talia and Ryon. Ramsay takes glee in first taunting Ryon and then seems keen to take Talia on "a hunt". Due to Ethan's response to this, Ramsay stabs him in the throat. Elissa runs to her son's side and he bleeds out as she cradles his body. She calls out for her son as Lord Whitehill drags Ryon away as his captive.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Iron From Ice]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While Rodrik is passed out, Elissa tells Talia to write a song for Ethan and Gregor, to sing at their upcoming funeral. </p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Once Rodrik begins to recover and is able to walk again, she sees him in the Great Hall, where she informs him that she has invited his betrothed - <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady Elaena Glenmore","link":"Elaena Glenmore","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elaena Glenmore|Lady Elaena Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-44750995756cca6299e674" href="/wiki/Elaena_Glenmore" title="Elaena Glenmore">Lady Elaena Glenmore</a> - to visit him. He demands Rodrik shows him some respect by kneeling and kissing his ring, only agreeing to release Ryon temporarily to attend the funeral of Ethan and Gregor if he does. Desperate to see her youngest son, Elissa advises Rodrik to swallow his pride and kiss Whitehill's ring. Whitehill then overrules, Rodrik by taking all the Ironwood forests, defying House Bolton in the process as well.
