Derwa Freyis one of the manydaughters of Walder Frey. She only appears in the Season 3 episode The Rains of Castamere. Biography 

 Background 

Derwa is one of the many daughters of Walder Frey, the Lord of the Crossing. Season 3 

Along with several of Lord Frey's other daughters and granddaughters, Robb Stark had the option of marrying her.  In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Walder Frey has no daughter named Derwa - nor does he have any daughters or granddaughters named Derwa. For that matter, no character with the first name Derwa has ever appeared in the books. References
