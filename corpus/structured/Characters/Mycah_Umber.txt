<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Mycah Umber is an early member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Umber","link":"House Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-210835501456cca796dc0ec" href="/wiki/House_Umber" title="House Umber">House Umber</a>. He was the second born son of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hothor Umber","link":"Hothor Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hothor Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-210835501456cca796dc0ec" href="/wiki/Hothor_Umber" title="Hothor Umber">Hothor Umber</a> and his wife Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Amarylis Umber","link":"Amarylis Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Amarylis Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-210835501456cca796dc0ec" href="/wiki/Amarylis_Umber" title="Amarylis Umber">Amarylis Umber</a>. He swore himself to the brotherhood of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-210835501456cca796dc0ec" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> at the age of twenty but died a violent death fifteen years later at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-210835501456cca796dc0ec" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a>.
</p>
