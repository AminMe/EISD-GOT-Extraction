<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Simon Toyne was the leader of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingswood Brotherhood","link":"Kingswood Brotherhood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingswood Brotherhood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/Kingswood_Brotherhood" title="Kingswood Brotherhood">Kingswood Brotherhood</a>, a band of outlaws and bandits opposed to the rule of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a>. They inhabited the Kingswood, a large area of woodland south-east of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. Roughly twenty years before the events of the series, King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys II Targaryen","link":"Aerys II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/Aerys_II_Targaryen" title="Aerys II Targaryen">Aerys II Targaryen</a> ordered the Brotherhood destroyed, a mission led by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a>. Selmy himself killed Toyne with a counter-riposte, a move witnessed by the then-<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"squire","link":"squire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[squire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/Squire" title="Squire">squire</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Lord Snow]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Simon was a descendant of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Toyne","link":"House Toyne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Toyne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/House_Toyne" title="House Toyne">House Toyne</a>. House Toyne was formerly a house of honor, with one member serving in the Kingsguard of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon IV Targaryen","link":"Aegon IV Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon IV Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/Aegon_IV_Targaryen" title="Aegon IV Targaryen">Aegon IV Targaryen</a>. When Aegon IV found this knight abed with one of his mistresses, he had him executed in a horrible fashion. The knight's brothers declared war against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-75139661356cca6c6b0256" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a>, slaying the infamous Dragonknight, Ser Aemon Targaryen. Simon was their descendant and the last of his line.
</p>
