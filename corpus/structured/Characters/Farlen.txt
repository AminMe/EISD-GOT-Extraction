Farlen is the kennelmaster at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>.
</p>

He has a daughter, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Palla","link":"Palla","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Palla]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/Palla" title="Palla">Palla</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"E16\">\"[[The Old Gods and the New]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sack of Winterfell","link":"Sack of Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/Sack_of_Winterfell" title="Sack of Winterfell">Sack of Winterfell</a>, Farlen's fate is unknown.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, Farlen is also the loyal kennelmaster of Winterfell. It was not him but <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mikken","link":"Mikken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mikken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/Mikken" title="Mikken">Mikken</a> the blacksmith who talked defiantly to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a>, and was killed for that. Farlen was falsely accused by Theon of killing three of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ironborn","link":"ironborn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ironborn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/Ironborn" title="Ironborn">ironborn</a> (who were actually killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Snow" title="Ramsay Snow" class="mw-redirect">Ramsay Snow</a> at Theon's command), and was beheaded by Theon. In the TV series, he was replaced by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Cassel","link":"Rodrik Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-177214011556cca6d61da12" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Cassel" title="Rodrik Cassel">Rodrik Cassel</a> at the execution scene.
</p>
