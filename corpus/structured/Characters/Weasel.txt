<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Weasel is a servant of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Weasel aids <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Tickler","link":"the Tickler","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Tickler]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/The_Tickler" title="The Tickler">the Tickler</a> in the interrogation and torture of prisoners. While the Tickler asks questions, Weasel straps the victim, and then places a wooden bucket with a rat inside over the victim's bare chest. At the Tickler's command, Weasel begins to heat the bucket with a torch to make the rat claw its way out through the victim's chest.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Weese is the understeward of the Wailing Tower in Harrenhal and a servant of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Whent","link":"House Whent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Whent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/House_Whent" title="House Whent">House Whent</a> that later submits to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a>. He is a cruel and abusive man, resorting to violence at the slightest provocation, even just for staring at him. He treats <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya","link":"Arya","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Arya" title="Arya" class="mw-redirect">Arya</a> and other servants very brutally. He, not <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Amory Lorch","link":"Amory Lorch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Amory Lorch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Amory_Lorch" title="Amory Lorch">Amory Lorch</a>, is the second man whom Arya tells <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a> to kill, because he was mean to her.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"Weasel" is a different character, a little girl about two years old whose real name is unknown. She and a woman whose right hand was cut off, perhaps her mother, were found by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a> on the march north from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. The one-armed woman died shortly afterwards, leaving the girl in the custody of the caravan. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Mountain","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane|the Mountain]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">the Mountain</a> and his men caught Arya, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hot Pie","link":"Hot Pie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hot Pie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Hot_Pie" title="Hot Pie">Hot Pie</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lommy Greenhands","link":"Lommy Greenhands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lommy Greenhands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-130304540256cca6f4576c0" href="/wiki/Lommy_Greenhands" title="Lommy Greenhands" class="mw-redirect">Lommy Greenhands</a>, Weasel managed to escape, and has not been seen since then. Arya takes her name as an alias while at the castle. For the TV series, the young girl Weasel has been removed and the name given to Weese.
</p>
