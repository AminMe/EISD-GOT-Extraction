<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Not much is known about Bloodsong other than he is a arrogant and skillful pit fighter. He took great pleasure from killing other pit fighters.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Nest of Vipers","link":"A Nest of Vipers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Nest of Vipers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-208541136056cca68a8ef19" href="/wiki/A_Nest_of_Vipers" title="A Nest of Vipers">A Nest of Vipers</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Asher Forrester","link":"Asher Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Asher Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-208541136056cca68a8ef19" href="/wiki/Asher_Forrester" title="Asher Forrester">Asher Forrester</a> came to the fighting pit looking for fighters to aid his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"family","link":"House Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Forrester|family]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-208541136056cca68a8ef19" href="/wiki/House_Forrester" title="House Forrester">family</a>, the leader of a group a now freed pit fighter, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Amaya","link":"Amaya","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Amaya]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-208541136056cca68a8ef19" href="/wiki/Amaya" title="Amaya">Amaya</a>, asks Asher to fight Bloodsong to prove his worth. When Asher eventually wins, he is given the choice to either finish Bloodsong off or spare him. Killing him impresses the pit fighters, who cheer Asher's name.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">If spared, Bloodsong accompanies Asher to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-208541136056cca68a8ef19" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>, saying that he will seek a more honorable death there. When the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Whitehill","link":"House Whitehill","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Whitehill|Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-208541136056cca68a8ef19" href="/wiki/House_Whitehill" title="House Whitehill">Whitehill</a> ambush is sprung in the port town, Bloodsong is trapped outside of the gate along with most of the fighters. He escapes with either Asher or <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester|Rodrik]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-208541136056cca68a8ef19" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik</a> while the other Forrester brother stays behind.
</p>
<h3>"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Ice Dragon","link":"The Ice Dragon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Ice Dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-208541136056cca68a8ef19" href="/wiki/The_Ice_Dragon" title="The Ice Dragon">The Ice Dragon</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Bloodsong first appears with Rodrik or Asher he is either angry about how hungry he is or he is seen ambushing and killing Whitehill soldiers.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He later is present during the battle of ironrath he kills numerous soldiers. It's unknown what happens to him as he is not seen being killed along with the rest of the pit fighters and the remaining forrester soldiers, the lord, Talia and either the sentinel, traitor or Gwyn are the only known people who have escaped.
</p>
