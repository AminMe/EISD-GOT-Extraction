<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ternesio Terys is the captain of the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Titan's Daughter","link":"Titan's Daughter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Titan's Daughter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Titan's_Daughter" title="Titan's Daughter">Titan's Daughter</a></i>, a trading galleon from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a>.
</p>
<h3>​<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> approaches Terys at a small port in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Saltpans","link":"Saltpans","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Saltpans]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Saltpans" title="Saltpans">Saltpans</a> and asks him if he'll give her passage to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>. She is willing to pay or scrub the floors of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ship","link":"Ships","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ships|ship]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Ships" title="Ships">ship</a> in return. He tries to dissuade her from visiting <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>, opining that it is a land of "ice, war and pirates". Besides, he says his ship is actually bound for his home, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a>. Arya remembers the iron coin and the instructions that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a> had given her following her escape from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>, and shows the coin to him, whispering "<i>Valar Morghulis</i>". Terys, astounded, responds with "<i>Valar Dohaeris</i>" and promises her a cabin.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Children]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">After a long sea voyage, Ternesio and Arya Stark arrive at the city of Braavos. She is awestrack by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Titan","link":"Titan of Braavos","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Titan of Braavos|Titan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Titan_of_Braavos" title="Titan of Braavos">Titan</a>, so he tells her that, in the old times, whenever Braavos stood in danger, the Titan would wake and protect the city. Arya replies it's just a statue, and just then the Titan lets out a loud blast announcing their arrival, startling her, so the captain laughs. Terys personally brings Arya to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of Black and White","link":"House of Black and White","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of Black and White]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/House_of_Black_and_White" title="House of Black and White">House of Black and White</a>, where he claims she may find <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the man she seeks","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar|the man she seeks]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">the man she seeks</a>. Arya thanks Terys for bringing her this far and he responds that any man of Braavos would have done the same. They say their goodbyes, and he rows away.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The House of Black and White]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69928200156cca69664a75" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ternesio Terys is also the captain of the <i>Titan's Daughter</i>. He keeps his grey hair cut short and neat and has a square windburnt face. He is the father of Denyo and Yorko Terys.
</p>
