<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Qotho is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bloodrider","link":"bloodrider","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bloodrider]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Bloodrider" title="Bloodrider">bloodrider</a> in the service of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Khal Drogo","link":"Khal Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Khal Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Khal_Drogo" title="Khal Drogo" class="mw-redirect">Khal Drogo</a>. He is one of Drogo's closest allies and a stalwart warrior who favors the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arakh","link":"Arakh","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arakh]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Arakh" title="Arakh">Arakh</a>.
</p>

He objects to the Lhazareen godswife <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mirri Maz Duur","link":"Mirri Maz Duur","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mirri Maz Duur]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Mirri_Maz_Duur" title="Mirri Maz Duur">Mirri Maz Duur</a> treating a small wound Drogo takes, calling her <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maegi","link":"Maegi","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maegi]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Maegi" title="Maegi">Maegi</a></i>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Pointy End]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Drogo's wound festers and he becomes seriously ill. He falls from his horse in front of the Khalasar, a sign of weakness. While <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cohollo","link":"Cohollo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cohollo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Cohollo" title="Cohollo">Cohollo</a> comes down from his horse and kneels to Drogo's side, Qotho and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Haggo","link":"Haggo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Haggo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Haggo" title="Haggo">Haggo</a> remain mounted. Daenerys orders the khalasar to take camp, but Qotho objects, though he ultimately relents. Later Daenerys calls for Duur, who says that only <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"blood magic","link":"blood magic","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[blood magic]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Blood_magic" title="Blood magic">blood magic</a> can save him. Duur claims that she needs to kill Drogo's horse. Qotho objects to the ritual and a fight ensues; Qotho pushes Dany to the ground causing her premature labor. Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jorah Mormont","link":"Jorah Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jorah Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/Jorah_Mormont" title="Jorah Mormont">Jorah Mormont</a> intervenes and engages Qotho in a vicious duel. Though Qotho takes the upper hand with his younger age and superior speed, his <i>arakh</i> is unable to penetrate Jorah's armor, giving Jorah enough time to finish him.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Baelor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109001904656cca63dde955" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Qotho is a skilled warrior, but he is also considered to be cruel and malicious, the least-likable of Drogo's three bloodriders.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, Drogo's three bloodriders are Qotho, Cohollo, and Haggo. All three attempt to intervene when Mirri Maz Duur conducts her ritual to heal Drogo, and all three are killed in the resulting fight. Qotho was personally killed by Jorah, just as in the TV series. Unidentified extras fill in some of the actions carried out by Cohollo and Haggo but do not take part in the fight with Ser Jorah, so all three have been functionally condensed into Qotho.
</p>
