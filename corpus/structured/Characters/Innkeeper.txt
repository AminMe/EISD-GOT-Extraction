<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sandor Clegane","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">Sandor Clegane</a> arrive at his inn, the innkeeper is begging <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Polliver","link":"Polliver","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Polliver]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Polliver" title="Polliver">Polliver</a> to stop his men from raping <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his daughter","link":"Innkeeper's daughter","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Innkeeper's daughter|his daughter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Innkeeper's_daughter" title="Innkeeper's daughter">his daughter</a>. Polliver tells him to shut up and pour them more ale, and they may not take her with them when they are done with her. The innkeeper reluctantly pours them more ale. He and his daughter narrowly escape the battle between the Hound, Polliver and his men.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Two Swords]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Sandor warns the innkeeper that Polliver and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Tickler","link":"the Tickler","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Tickler]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/The_Tickler" title="The Tickler">the Tickler</a> - who is still alive at this point, though Arya kills him in the ensuing fight - will kill him instead of paying for their drinks. This causes the innkeeper and the other people to leave the room.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne","link":"Brienne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Brienne" title="Brienne" class="mw-redirect">Brienne</a> encounters three of the Brave Companions (Timeon, Pyg and Shagwell), they tell her that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rorge","link":"Rorge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rorge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Rorge" title="Rorge">Rorge</a> killed the innkeeper after he told them about the bloody brawl in the inn.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">This innkeeper's story is more similar to a story Arya hears Chiswyck, one of the Mountain's men, telling at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>. He recalls that after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tourney of the Hand","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|Tourney of the Hand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">Tourney of the Hand</a> Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> and his men gang raped Layna, the daughter of the keeper of an inn on the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Goldroad","link":"Goldroad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Goldroad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Goldroad" title="Goldroad">Goldroad</a>. Chiswyck estimates her to have been eighteen at the time of the rape, but Rafford corrects him by stating that she was only thirteen. The men find this hilarious, which angers Arya and influences her to name Chiswyck as the first to be killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140767371856cca78343e48" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a>.
</p>
