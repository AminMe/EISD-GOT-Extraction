 Background 

Serena Blackfyre was wife of Daemon Blackfyre, legitimized bastard son of King Aegon IV Targaryen and his cousin, Daena Targaryen. She was the mother of the twins Aegon and Aemon Blackfyre, and Daemon II Blackfyre. Season 1

 In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there's no mention of a Serena Blackfyre or the name of Daemon Blackfyre's wife. However, The World of Ice and Fire reveals Daemon was married to Rohanne of Tyrosh, a match that was arranged by his father, Aegon IV. See also References
