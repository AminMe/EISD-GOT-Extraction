<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Clea was a slave and at some point she was made a bedslave at a brothel in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Long Bridge","link":"Long Bridge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Long Bridge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57873431756cca6c3bb06c" href="/wiki/Long_Bridge" title="Long Bridge">Long Bridge</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Volantis","link":"Volantis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Volantis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57873431756cca6c3bb06c" href="/wiki/Volantis" title="Volantis">Volantis</a>. Like all bedslaves in Volantis, she was branded with a slave tattoo shaped like a tear on her cheek. She speaks the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Common Tongue","link":"Common Tongue","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Common Tongue]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57873431756cca6c3bb06c" href="/wiki/Common_Tongue" title="Common Tongue">Common Tongue</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57873431756cca6c3bb06c" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, there is no character by the name of Clea. Tyrion does encounter a Westerosi-looking prostitute in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Selhorys","link":"Selhorys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Selhorys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57873431756cca6c3bb06c" href="/wiki/Selhorys" title="Selhorys">Selhorys</a>, a town under Volantene rule. Contrary to the scene depicted in the TV series, however, Tyrion does not befriend her but does have sex with her, noticing that she just lies there, unmoving. Too drunk to care, Tyrion retches and has sex with her again, then leaving ashamed with himself.
</p>
