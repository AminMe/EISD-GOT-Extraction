<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is the Lord of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greywater Watch","link":"Greywater Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greywater Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Greywater_Watch" title="Greywater Watch">Greywater Watch</a> and the father of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meera","link":"Meera Reed","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meera Reed|Meera]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Meera_Reed" title="Meera Reed">Meera</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jojen Reed","link":"Jojen Reed","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jojen Reed]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Jojen_Reed" title="Jojen Reed">Jojen Reed</a>. The head of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Reed","link":"House Reed","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Reed]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/House_Reed" title="House Reed">House Reed</a>, he is a loyal vassal of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a>, ruling over the bog-filled lands of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Neck","link":"the Neck","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Neck]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/The_Neck" title="The Neck">the Neck</a>.Â According to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a> in a conversation with Jojen Reed, Howland saved <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ned Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark|Ned Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Ned Stark</a>'s life during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Howland Reed is a member and the ruler of the crannogmen, the inhabitants of the Neck. Disparagingly called "frog-eaters" because their lands are in the swamps of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Neck","link":"the Neck","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Neck]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/The_Neck" title="The Neck">the Neck</a>, the Reeds and the crannogmen keep to themselves and rarely travel beyond their borders. However, Howland Reed was an exception to this, becoming a noted friend and ally of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a>, fighting at his side during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. He remains one of House Stark's staunchest supporters but, so far, hasn't played a role in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">One point of note mentioned in the books is the fact that Howland Reed was with Ned when he finally rescued <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lyanna Stark","link":"Lyanna Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lyanna Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-48300441056cca5faa0413" href="/wiki/Lyanna_Stark" title="Lyanna Stark">Lyanna Stark</a>. Howland was one of only two survivors of that mission; the other was Ned himself.
</p>
