<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Tobho Mott is a master armorer and blacksmith living in the city of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. Despite being a foreigner from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free City","link":"Free Cities","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Cities|Free City]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Free_Cities" title="Free Cities">Free City</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qohor","link":"Qohor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qohor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Qohor" title="Qohor">Qohor</a>, he is well-regarded for the quality of his work and has received the patronage of many knights and even some lords. Mott is assisted by his apprentice smith, a young man named <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> learns that his predecessor <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Arryn","link":"Jon Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Jon_Arryn" title="Jon Arryn">Jon Arryn</a> visited Mott prior to his death. Stark goes to question Mott and learns that Arryn displayed a particular interest in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> Stark later realizes that Gendry is the bastard son of King Robert Baratheon. Robert is killed and Gendry sets off to join the Night's Watch.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Fire and Blood]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Mott is forcibly questioned and tortured by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gold Cloaks","link":"City Watch","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[City Watch|Gold Cloaks]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/City_Watch" title="City Watch">Gold Cloaks</a> about the whereabouts of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>. He tells <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Janos Slynt","link":"Janos Slynt","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Janos Slynt]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Janos_Slynt" title="Janos Slynt">Janos Slynt</a> that Gendry joined the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> and that he can be recognized by the bull-shaped helm he sports. <img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The North Remembers]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Tobho Mott is a native of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free City","link":"Free City","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free City]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Free_City" title="Free City" class="mw-redirect">Free City</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qohor","link":"Qohor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qohor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Qohor" title="Qohor">Qohor</a> who learned to smith there, including the working of the fabled <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian steel","link":"Valyrian steel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian steel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Valyrian_steel" title="Valyrian steel">Valyrian steel</a>. He moved to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a> when younger and has successfully built up his own business thanks to his formidable skills. His armory is a large building at the top of the Street of Steel, impressive in size and outfittings.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Eddard Stark asked Mott who paid him Gendry's apprentice fee, Mott answered evasively, but eventually admitted it was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"a mysterious lord","link":"Varys","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Varys|a mysterious lord]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Varys" title="Varys">a mysterious lord</a> who did not identify himself that paid him both the apprentice fee and to keep his mouth shut about the youth.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, Mott is not interrogated about Gendry's whereabouts. It is not explained how the Gold Cloaks knew that Gendry was with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a>'s caravan.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Mott, not a smith from Volantis, is the one who melts <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ice","link":"Ice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Ice" title="Ice">Ice</a> to forge two new swords at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-189804306056cca692dee89" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a>'s order.
</p>
