A Lannister guardsman is an unnamed minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Conor Delaney and only appears in The Kingsroad. Biography 

 Season 1 

He leads the Lannister guardsmen who accompany King Robert's party to Winterfell and back. On the return journey Prince Joffrey is bitten by the direwolf Nymeria. He leads a search for the wolf and reports his inability to find her to the King.
