<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He leads the Lannister guardsmen who accompany King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon|Robert]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert</a>'s party to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> and back. On the return journey Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon|Joffrey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey</a> is bitten by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"direwolf","link":"Direwolves","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Direwolves|direwolf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Direwolves" title="Direwolves">direwolf</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Nymeria","link":"Nymeria","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nymeria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Nymeria" title="Nymeria">Nymeria</a>. He leads a search for the wolf and reports his inability to find her to the King. When Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister|Cersei]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei</a> demands that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark|Sansa]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa</a>'s direwolf <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady","link":"Lady","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lady]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Lady" title="Lady">Lady</a> be killed in her place he reports that Lady is chained up outside the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsroad]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-51882096156cca7432597a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
