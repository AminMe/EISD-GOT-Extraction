</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Biter is a mute criminal from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> with filed teeth. Along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rorge","link":"Rorge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rorge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Rorge" title="Rorge">Rorge</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a>, he was fished out of the dungeons by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a> and forcibly recruited into the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. He is accompanying the rest of Yoren's new recruits northwards to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Biter, alongside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rorge","link":"Rorge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rorge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Rorge" title="Rorge">Rorge</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a> are kept inside a cage for their trip to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>. When Jaqen asks "Arry" (actually <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a>) for water, Rorge demands beer and insults Arya, who says that he should have asked nicely and hits Rorge's hand with a stick. He recoils and then tries to grab Arya through the cage. Biter moves up behind Rorge and hisses as Rorge threatens Arya again.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Night Lands]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The group is resting in a barn when they are roused to arms by Yoren and told they are being attacked. As they rush outside one man trips and drops his torch, setting a fire near the prisoner wagon. They are met by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Amory Lorch","link":"Amory Lorch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Amory Lorch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Amory_Lorch" title="Amory Lorch">Amory Lorch</a> and a force of men loyal to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a>. Lorch points out the Gold Cloaks with him and says that they have come for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>, a royal bastard. Lorch orders Yoren and the recruits to drop their weapons. Yoren remains defiant and Lorch orders him shot.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"E13\">\"[[What is Dead May Never Die]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Seeing their leader killed Gendry and many of the other recruits join the fight. Arya is distracted by Jaqen calling for help as the flames lick the bars of the cage. She hands him an axe before rushing to join the fray. They use it to escape the wagon. Gendry is overwhelmed and knocked down. As Arya runs round a corner she is knocked over and taken prisoner along with the other survivors.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"E13\" \/>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge, Biter and Jaqen are inducted into the Lannister army. They serve at Harrenhal, where their former fellow recruits are also taken as captives. Arya approaches Rorge and Biter looking for Jaqen. Rorge repeats his earlier threats and shows no acknowledgement of her saving his life.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Prince of Winterfell]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a> </i>novels, Biter is a mindless, mute thug from King's Landing, an associate of Rorge. According to semi-canon source, Rorge ran several fighting pits where dogs and bears would fight one another and also people. Biter fought in these pits and developed a reputation for savage cruelty. He has no tongue and only speaks through hisses. His teeth have been filed to points.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge and Biter helped <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a> to free the Northern prisoners at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>. Afterwards they join the Brave Companions, the notorious sellsword company led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vargo Hoat","link":"Locke","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Locke|Vargo Hoat]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Locke" title="Locke">Vargo Hoat</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biter is known for his bestial savagery. He tears flesh out of his victims' bodies with his sharpened teeth. According to Rorge, Biter once chewed the breasts of a septa sometime during their pillaging.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While Vargo Hoat becomes delirious due to his festering head injury, many of the Brave Companions leave Harrenhal, among them Rorge and Biter. They and five more form a gang that sacks <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Saltpans","link":"Saltpans","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Saltpans]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Saltpans" title="Saltpans">Saltpans</a>. They never encounter Arya and the Hound.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge and Biter have nothing to do with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dying man","link":"dying man","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dying man]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Dying_man" title="Dying man">dying man</a> that Arya and the Hound find. He was injured by a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bolton","link":"Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Bolton" title="Bolton" class="mw-redirect">Bolton</a> soldier.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rorge's gang encounters <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne","link":"Brienne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Brienne" title="Brienne" class="mw-redirect">Brienne</a> near the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a>. Brienne fights Rorge and kills him, but Biter tackles her down, overcomes her and savagely chews her face. Eventually <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196906520156cca6525bced" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a> intervenes and kills Biter by driving a spear through the back of his neck, but not before Brienne's face is horribly disfigured.
</p>
