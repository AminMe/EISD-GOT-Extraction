<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is drinking with companions at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a> when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> arrives on his way south. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> is also in the inn and Tyrion recognizes her. She turns to the knights from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a> in the inn to help her to take Tyrion into custody, accusing him of attempting to murder her son <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a>. She asserts her origin as a daughter of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> and then tells the Knight of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Frey","link":"House Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/House_Frey" title="House Frey">House Frey</a> that she knows his sigil and asks after Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a>. He reports that Lord Frey is about to celebrate his ninetieth name day and has invited Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>. Catelyn announces that she will return Tyrion to Winterfell to await the King's justice. The Knight of House Frey draws his sword and threatens Tyrion, along with the other soldiers loyal to the Tullys.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> Unlike the other men loyal to House Tully from the Inn he does not escort Catelyn further.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-124653993256cca76d38683" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
