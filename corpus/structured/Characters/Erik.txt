<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron From Ice","link":"Iron From Ice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron From Ice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178319985456cca67382269" href="/wiki/Iron_From_Ice" title="Iron From Ice">Iron From Ice</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Erik was caught stealing weapons by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Royland Degore","link":"Royland Degore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Royland Degore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178319985456cca67382269" href="/wiki/Royland_Degore" title="Royland Degore">Royland Degore</a> and brought to Ethan to be judged, Ethan has three options: he can remove three fingers the same way <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his father","link":"Gregor Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Forrester|his father]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178319985456cca67382269" href="/wiki/Gregor_Forrester" title="Gregor Forrester">his father</a> punished thieves, sent to join the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-178319985456cca67382269" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> or he can be freed and given food. If it's the last choice Erik is grateful but Ethan's advisers worry that it will lead to more like him. If one of the former two is chosen Erik is horrified but Ethan's advisers are satisfied.
</p>
