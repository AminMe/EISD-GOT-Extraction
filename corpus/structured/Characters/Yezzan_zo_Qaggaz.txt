<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Yezzan zo Qaggaz is a member of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of Qaggaz","link":"House of Qaggaz","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of Qaggaz]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/House_of_Qaggaz" title="House of Qaggaz">House of Qaggaz</a> and a slaver who purchases and trains <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"pit fighters","link":"Fighting pit","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fighting pit|pit fighters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Fighting_pit" title="Fighting pit">pit fighters</a>. He owns one of the lesser fighting pits.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Besides the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian","link":"High Valyrian","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[High Valyrian|Valyrian]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/High_Valyrian" title="High Valyrian">Valyrian</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dialect","link":"Low Valyrian","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Low Valyrian|dialect]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Low_Valyrian" title="Low Valyrian">dialect</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meereen","link":"Meereen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meereen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Meereen" title="Meereen">Meereen</a>, Yezzan also speaks the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Common Tongue","link":"Common Tongue","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Common Tongue]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Common_Tongue" title="Common Tongue">Common Tongue</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Yezzan is present at an impromptu slave auction of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Malko","link":"Malko","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Malko]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Malko" title="Malko">Malko</a>'s. He purchases <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jorah Mormont","link":"Jorah Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jorah Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Jorah_Mormont" title="Jorah Mormont">Jorah Mormont</a> for use in the recently reopened fighting pits. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> demands to be bought as well, Yezzan is uninterested in Tyrion's claims that he and Jorah are a team. He changes his mind when Tyrion savagely beats the slaver holding his chain, and pays for him, agreeing with Malko's assertion that Tyrion is "funny". When Tyrion suggests that he and Jorah be paid for their labor to avoid the Queen's ire for practicing slavery, Yezzan hits Tyrion and tosses him a single coin. Later, at one of the "lower" pits, Yezzan addresses his fighters and announces that those who survive the day will fight at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Pit of Daznak","link":"Daznak's Pit","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daznak's Pit|Great Pit of Daznak]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Daznak's_Pit" title="Daznak's Pit">Great Pit of Daznak</a>. Yezzan is shocked to see that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hizdahr zo Loraq","link":"Hizdahr zo Loraq","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hizdahr zo Loraq]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Hizdahr_zo_Loraq" title="Hizdahr zo Loraq">Hizdahr zo Loraq</a> themselves are present and hastily has his gladiators dedicate their kills to her. Yezzan is later knocked down by Jorah, who emerged from the dungeon early in a desperate attempt to reach the Queen.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Gift (episode)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Later, he is still at the pit observing two of his warriors when Jorah returns from Meereen. Wondering why he didn't just leave, Jorah then asks Yezzan to allow him to fight in the pit in front of the queen, and if he wins Yezzan will be allowed to sell Jorah.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[[Hardhome (episode)]]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Yezzan zo Qaggaz is not a Meereenese Great Master but a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yunkish","link":"Yunkai","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yunkai|Yunkish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Yunkai" title="Yunkai">Yunkish</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wise Master","link":"Wise Masters","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wise Masters|Wise Master]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Wise_Masters" title="Wise Masters">Wise Master</a>. He has yellow eyes and is morbidly obese to the point that he can no longer stand, hence his derogatory nickname "the yellow whale". He contracted a jungle disease while on a slaving raid in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sothoryos","link":"Sothoryos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sothoryos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Sothoryos" title="Sothoryos">Sothoryos</a> which he has been suffering from for years: he cannot hold his water so he always smells of urine, which even perfume cannot hide. He wears yellow silk <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tokar","link":"tokar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[tokar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96187167256cca658f332f" href="/wiki/Tokar" title="Tokar">tokars</a> with gold fringe. He is obsessed with the grotesque and often purchases slaves with physical deformities to add to his "collection". While overweight he is still shrewd and intelligent, a trait some of the other Wise Masters don't share.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Despite his personal grotesqueness, it is remarked that Yezzan isn't that bad compared to some of the other slavers, and isn't particularly cruel. So long as his slaves obey Yezzan treats them relatively well, and makes sure that they are well fed and looked after (though this is in the same sense that a man will make sure that the horses he owns are well fed and are not beaten, because he doesn't want to needlessly damage his own valuable property). Unlike him, however, his slave overseer, Nurse, tends to be cruel and harsh towards his master's slaves.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Yezzan is also one of the few Wise Masters who urges that Yunkai should try to make peace with Daenerys, fearing that further conflict with her Unsullied army and increasingly large dragons is more trouble than it is worth.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Yezzan is not involved in pit fights. There is a Meereenese crone called Zahrina that purchases cheap slaves to fight in the arenas, "meat" to be slaughtered by the more formidable fighters.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Yezzan purchases Tyrion and his other companion for entertainment. Tyrion notices that Zahrina is about to purchase Jorah, and he persuades Nurse to buy Jorah too, claiming that he is part of their show. Tyrion complies to his new master and is treated correctly. Jorah, however, does not cooperate, and as a result is treated very brutally by Nurse: he is locked in a cage and beaten severely every evening.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Yurkhaz zo Yunzak, the commander of Yunkai's army, persuades Yezzan to have Tyrion and his dwarf companion entertain the crowd at the Great Pit of Daznak - and afterwards the lions will be turned loose on them (Tyrion is not informed of the last part). Yezzan agrees, but Tyrion spots a hint of anger in his eyes. Apparently Yezzan did not like the idea of allowing his slaves to be killed and eaten by beasts, but had to obey Yurkhaz. Luckily for Tyrion and his companion, Daenerys spares their lives and forbids that the lions slaughter them.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Shortly after Daenerys departs from Meereen, Yezzan becomes sick. Tyrion seizes the opportunity to escape with Jorah Mormont and their other companion. They join the Second Sons who betrayed Daenerys and switched sides earlier, and convince them to rejoin Daenerys in exchange for the wealth of Casterly Rock.
</p>
