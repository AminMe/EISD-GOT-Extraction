<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"<i>A Song of Ice and Fire<\/i>","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire|''A Song of Ice and Fire'']]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire"><i>A Song of Ice and Fire</i></a> novels Jonothor Darry's history is the same. Just like most of the other members of the Kingsguard at the time, he too was fiercely loyal to his oaths, fighting for the crown during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He was present at the aftermath of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Bells","link":"Battle of the Bells","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Bells]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Bells" title="Battle of the Bells">Battle of the Bells</a> to rally the remaining royal forces and later on lead the army of the royalists to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Trident","link":"Battle of the Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Trident" title="Battle of the Trident">Battle of the Trident</a>, alongside Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegar Targaryen","link":"Rhaegar Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegar Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Rhaegar_Targaryen" title="Rhaegar Targaryen">Rhaegar Targaryen</a>, Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lewyn Martell","link":"Lewyn Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lewyn Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Lewyn_Martell" title="Lewyn Martell">Lewyn Martell</a> and Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a>. He died at the Trident fighting until the end for his cause.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Although <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> remembers him as a earnest and honorable man, he did nothing against the atrocities committed by the Mad King, not even intervening when the King raped Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaella","link":"Rhaella Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaella Targaryen|Rhaella]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Rhaella_Targaryen" title="Rhaella Targaryen">Rhaella</a>. He stated that they were sworn to protect the royal family, except from the King.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He was the brother of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Willem Darry","link":"Willem Darry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Willem Darry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Willem_Darry" title="Willem Darry">Willem Darry</a>, another Targaryen loyalist, who would later on smuggle the surviving <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen","link":"Viserys Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Viserys Targaryen|Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Viserys_Targaryen" title="Viserys Targaryen">Targaryen</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"children","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen|children]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">children</a> away to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Cities","link":"Free Cities","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Cities]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-119132172256cca74b19bfc" href="/wiki/Free_Cities" title="Free Cities">Free Cities</a>.
</p>
