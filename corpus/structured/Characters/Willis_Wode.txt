<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Willis Wode is sworn to the service of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Whent","link":"House Whent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Whent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/House_Whent" title="House Whent">House Whent</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>, a vassal house to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a>. He is traveling the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsroad","link":"Kingsroad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsroad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/Kingsroad" title="Kingsroad">Kingsroad</a> following the Tourney of the Hand. He carries a shield emblazoned with the black bat of Harrenhal.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Willis is drinking with another man at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a> when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> arrives on his way south. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> is also in the inn and Tyrion recognizes her. She turns to the men of her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"father","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully|father]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">father</a>'s bannermen to help her to take Tyrion into custody, accusing him of attempting to murder her son, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a>. Catelyn announces that she will return Tyrion to Winterfell to await the King's justice. Ser Willis draws his sword and threatens Tyrion.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-52766028056cca75e84102" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Willis Wode is a hedge knight sworn to the service of Lady Shella Whent. He is a stiff necked and stolid man. He survives the journey to the Eyrie in the books.
