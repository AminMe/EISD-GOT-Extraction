</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Timett</b> is a recurring character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first","link":"Season 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1|first]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">first</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"second","link":"Season 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2|second]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">second</a> seasons. He is played by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tobias Winter","link":"Tobias Winter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tobias Winter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Tobias_Winter" title="Tobias Winter">Tobias Winter</a> and debuts in the "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Pointy End","link":"The Pointy End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Pointy End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/The_Pointy_End" title="The Pointy End">The Pointy End</a>." Timett is the leader of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Burned Men","link":"Burned Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Burned Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Burned_Men" title="Burned Men">Burned Men</a> and a renowned fearless warrior.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Timett, son of Timett, is the leader of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Burned Men","link":"Burned Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Burned Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Burned_Men" title="Burned Men">Burned Men</a>, one of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"hill tribes","link":"hill tribes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[hill tribes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Hill_tribes" title="Hill tribes">hill tribes</a> living in the foothills of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mountains of the Moon","link":"Mountains of the Moon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mountains of the Moon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Mountains_of_the_Moon" title="Mountains of the Moon">Mountains of the Moon</a> on the western edge of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>. He is a large and fierce young man with only one eye. Despite his youth, his savagery and success in battle have seen him rise high amongst the hill tribes.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> is captured by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shagga","link":"Shagga","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shagga]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Shagga" title="Shagga">Shagga</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stone Crows","link":"Stone Crows","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stone Crows]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Stone_Crows" title="Stone Crows">Stone Crows</a> while traversing the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>. Tyrion offers an alliance to the Hill Tribes; fight the enemies of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a> in exchange for weapons, gold and autonomy from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Eyrie","link":"The Eyrie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Eyrie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/The_Eyrie" title="The Eyrie">The Eyrie</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Golden Crown]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> Timett agrees to the alliance and escorts Tyrion to the Lannister encampment in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>. They are joined by other clans including <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Chella","link":"Chella","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Chella]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Chella" title="Chella">Chella</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Black Ears","link":"Black Ears","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Black Ears]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Black_Ears" title="Black Ears">Black Ears</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Moon Brothers","link":"Moon Brothers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Moon Brothers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Moon_Brothers" title="Moon Brothers">Moon Brothers</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Painted Dogs","link":"Painted Dogs","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Painted Dogs]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Painted_Dogs" title="Painted Dogs">Painted Dogs</a>. Timett is presented to Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> by Tyrion. Tywin accepts the alliance and Shagga's insistence that they fight alongside Tyrion on the battlefield.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Pointy End]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> The Burned Men take part in the Lannister victory in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Green Fork","link":"Battle of the Green Fork","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Green Fork]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Green_Fork" title="Battle of the Green Fork">Battle of the Green Fork</a>. They then engage in post-battle looting.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Baelor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Timett and his men escort Tyrion to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> where he becomes Acting <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hand of the King","link":"Hand of the King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hand of the King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Hand_of_the_King" title="Hand of the King">Hand of the King</a> on behalf of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The North Remembers]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After Tyrion discovers that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grand Maester","link":"Grand Maester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grand Maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Grand_Maester" title="Grand Maester">Grand Maester</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pycelle","link":"Pycelle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pycelle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Pycelle" title="Pycelle">Pycelle</a> is spying on him for Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei","link":"Cersei","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/Cersei" title="Cersei" class="mw-redirect">Cersei</a>, Timett and Bronn accompany him to confront Pycelle. Tyrion orders Timett to cut off Pycelle's manhood and feed it to the goats. Timett points out that there are no goats nearby and Tyrion is exasperated. Tyrion settles for shortening his beard and confining him to the Black Cells.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[What is Dead May Never Die]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-1349244656cca659c94fd" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the Burned Men are noted as the most unpredictable and savage of the mountain clans, and Timett is the most feared of them. Normally Burned Men are expected to burn off a finger or nipple as a show of might during their rite of manhood, but Timett burned out an eye as a sign of bravery and might, which earned him great respect within his tribe and the title of Red Hand.
</p>
