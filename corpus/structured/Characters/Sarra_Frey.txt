Sarra Freyis one of the many granddaughters of Walder Frey. She only appears in the Season 3 episode The Rains of Castamere. Biography 

 Background 

Sarra is one of the many granddaughters of Walder Frey, the Lord of the Crossing. Season 3 

Along with several of Lord Frey's other daughters and granddaughters, Robb Stark had the option of marrying her. When he arrives at The Twins for his uncle Edmure'swedding, Robb apologizesfor his slight against them.
