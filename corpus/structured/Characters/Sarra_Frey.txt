<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Sarra is one of the many granddaughters of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-163828424856cca73df1d92" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of the Crossing","link":"Lord of the Crossing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of the Crossing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-163828424856cca73df1d92" href="/wiki/Lord_of_the_Crossing" title="Lord of the Crossing">Lord of the Crossing</a>.

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Along with several of Lord Frey's other daughters and granddaughters, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-163828424856cca73df1d92" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> had the option of marrying her. When he arrives at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Twins","link":"The Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-163828424856cca73df1d92" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">The Twins</a> for his uncle <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Edmure","link":"Edmure Tully","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Edmure Tully|Edmure]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-163828424856cca73df1d92" href="/wiki/Edmure_Tully" title="Edmure Tully">Edmure</a>'s <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding|wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-163828424856cca73df1d92" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">wedding</a>, Robb apologizes for his slight against them. Lord Walder states that Robb could have had either Sarra or her identical twin sister <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Serra","link":"Serra Frey","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Serra Frey|Serra]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-163828424856cca73df1d92" href="/wiki/Serra_Frey" title="Serra Frey">Serra</a>, or he could have had both for all he cared.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Rains of Castamere (episode)|The Rains of Castamere]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-163828424856cca73df1d92" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
