<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Johnna resides in Hardhome with her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"mother","link":"Karsi","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Karsi|mother]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/Karsi" title="Karsi">mother</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sister","link":"Johnna's sister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Johnna's sister|sister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/Johnna's_sister" title="Johnna's sister">sister</a>.

<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Johnna and her sister are evacuated from Hardhome after Karsi agrees to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tormund","link":"Tormund","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tormund]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/Tormund" title="Tormund">Tormund</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a>'s plan to resettle the wildlings in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Gift","link":"The Gift","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Gift]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/The_Gift" title="The Gift">The Gift</a>, in exchange for military support when the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Walkers","link":"White Walkers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walkers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/White_Walkers" title="White Walkers">White Walkers</a> finally assail <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>. As she loads them into a boat, Johnna and her sister ask Karsi if she'll go with them, to which their mother replies that she'll follow shortly. Karsi is killed shortly thereafter in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Massacre at Hardhome","link":"Massacre at Hardhome","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Massacre at Hardhome]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-104755666056cca6e697bdc" href="/wiki/Massacre_at_Hardhome" title="Massacre at Hardhome">Massacre at Hardhome</a>, but her daughters at that point have reached the safety of the Night's Watch ships.
