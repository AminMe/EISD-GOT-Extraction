<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ghita is a ten year old terminally ill girl.
</p>
<h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164155198056cca69708650" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ghita is brought to the House of Black and White by her father after various treatment attempts have failed. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164155198056cca69708650" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> puts the girl out of her misery by giving her the poisoned water from the pool in the central sanctuary.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Unbowed, Unbent, Unbroken]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164155198056cca69708650" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Arya wears Ghita's face and pretends to be a child prostitute in order to ambush and murder Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meryn Trant","link":"Meryn Trant","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meryn Trant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-164155198056cca69708650" href="/wiki/Meryn_Trant" title="Meryn Trant">Meryn Trant</a> in a brothel.
