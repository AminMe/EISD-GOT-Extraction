<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>

He then found a woman, Marya, kind enough to overlook his trade.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"Lore\">\"[[Robert's Rebellion (Histories & Lore)#Davos Seaworth's perspective|Histories & Lore: Robert's Rebellion \u2013 Davos Seaworth entry]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158130692056cca69952fc0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Salladhor Saan","link":"Salladhor Saan","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Salladhor Saan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158130692056cca69952fc0" href="/wiki/Salladhor_Saan" title="Salladhor Saan">Salladhor Saan</a> attended their wedding feast.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"3.01\">\"[[Valar Dohaeris]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158130692056cca69952fc0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> She gave him a son they named Matthos. <p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Melisandre","link":"Melisandre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Melisandre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158130692056cca69952fc0" href="/wiki/Melisandre" title="Melisandre">Melisandre</a> asks Davos if he loves his wife to which he replies he does. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158130692056cca69952fc0" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Marya is the daughter of a common carpenter. She has seven sons with Davos: Dale, Allard, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Matthos","link":"Matthos Seaworth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Matthos Seaworth|Matthos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158130692056cca69952fc0" href="/wiki/Matthos_Seaworth" title="Matthos Seaworth">Matthos</a>, Marc, Devan, Stannis (named after <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the king","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|the king]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158130692056cca69952fc0" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">the king</a>), and Steffon. While Davos went with Stannis' household to Dragonstone, Marya remained at the family keep in Cape Wrath to raise their two youngest sons. Of Marya's other five sons, only Devan survived after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Blackwater","link":"Battle of Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158130692056cca69952fc0" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Blackwater" title="Battle of Blackwater" class="mw-redirect">Battle of Blackwater</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Marya is described as good-natured, plump woman with sagging breasts and a kindly smile.
</p>
