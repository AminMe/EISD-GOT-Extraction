<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16224564656cca7aceefa1" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Aggar's background is the same. He took part in the assault on Winterfell and the hunt for Bran and Rickon. Alongside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gelmarr","link":"Gelmarr","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gelmarr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16224564656cca7aceefa1" href="/wiki/Gelmarr" title="Gelmarr">Gelmarr</a> and Gynir Rednose, he took part in the abduction and killing of the children whose corpses were passed off as Bran's and Rickon's. He, Gelmarr, and Gynir were secretly killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16224564656cca7aceefa1" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a> at Theon's command, to prevent them from revealing the truth about the slain children. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Farlen","link":"Farlen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Farlen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16224564656cca7aceefa1" href="/wiki/Farlen" title="Farlen">Farlen</a> was falsely accused by Theon of the killing and was executed.
</p>
