Ebbert Whitehill is an unseen character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. He is one of the three eldest sons of Lord Ludd Whitehill and Lady Whitehill, brother of Gryff, Gwyn, Karl and Torrhen. Biography 

 Sons of Winter 

Ebbert is mentioned when Rodrik Forrester visits Highpoint and talks with Ebbert's sister, Gwyn, regarding a tapestry of the Whitehill family. Ebbert, as a second born son, has been sent to The Citadel to train to become a maester. Family Tree
