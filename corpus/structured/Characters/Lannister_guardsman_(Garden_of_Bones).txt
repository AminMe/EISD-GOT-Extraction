<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The unnamed Lannister guardsman is on sentry duty at the camp of the Lannister forces commanded by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stafford Lannister","link":"Stafford Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stafford Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169960059056cca7af4f26b" href="/wiki/Stafford_Lannister" title="Stafford Lannister">Stafford Lannister</a> on the way to reinforce the army of Tywin Lannister in the Riverlands.
</p>
<h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169960059056cca7af4f26b" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>

They discuss who is the greatest swordsman in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169960059056cca7af4f26b" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>.
