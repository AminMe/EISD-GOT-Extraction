<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Biography </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Background </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is a general loyal to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon of Dragonstone","link":"House Baratheon of Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon of Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone" title="House Baratheon of Dragonstone">House Baratheon of Dragonstone</a>, serving <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a>'s army in his campaign to retake the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"North","link":"North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/North" title="North" class="mw-redirect">North</a> from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bolton","link":"House Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/House_Bolton" title="House Bolton">House Bolton</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a> </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Following <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Bolton","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Bolton</a>'s attack on the camp, Stannis orders him to put the guards who were on watch duty in chains and question them to find out if they fell asleep or conspired with the enemy, and then hang them. He is later present during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shireen Baratheon","link":"Shireen Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shireen Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Shireen_Baratheon" title="Shireen Baratheon">Shireen Baratheon</a>'s sacrifice. He looks visibly distressed as he watches her burn alive.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Dance of Dragons]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Later, he informs Stannis that nearly half of his army has deserted him, including all the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sellswords","link":"Mercenary","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mercenary|sellswords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Mercenary" title="Mercenary">sellswords</a> and horses. He stands behind Stannis as they discover <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Selyse Baratheon","link":"Selyse Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Selyse Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Selyse_Baratheon" title="Selyse Baratheon">Selyse Baratheon</a>'s swaying corpse beneath a tree where she hanged herself. The General then tells Stannis that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Melisandre","link":"Melisandre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Melisandre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Melisandre" title="Melisandre">Melisandre</a> was just seen riding out of camp. Stannis then orders him to get the men into marching formation. As the army approaches <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>, they are surprised and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"attacked","link":"Battle of Winterfell","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Winterfell|attacked]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Winterfell" title="Battle of Winterfell">attacked</a> by a large force of Bolton cavalry, where the General is killed.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Mother's Mercy]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-111019793156cca694898e0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
