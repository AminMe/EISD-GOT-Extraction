<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Hugh</b>, called Ser <b>Hugh of the Vale</b>, is a recurring character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first season","link":"Season 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1|first season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">first season</a>. He is played by guest star <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jefferson Hall","link":"Jefferson Hall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jefferson Hall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Jefferson_Hall" title="Jefferson Hall">Jefferson Hall</a> and debuts in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things","link":"Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Cripples,_Bastards_and_Broken_Things" title="Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things">Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things</a>." Ser Hugh is a freshly made knight. He is killed by a splintered lance while jousting with Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Hugh served as Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Arryn","link":"Jon Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Jon_Arryn" title="Jon Arryn">Jon Arryn</a>'s <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"squire","link":"squire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[squire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Squire" title="Squire">squire</a> for four years before Jon Arryn's death. After Arryn's death, Hugh was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knighted","link":"knighthood","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knighthood|knighted]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Knighthood" title="Knighthood">knighted</a> for his loyal service by King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>. When Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lysa Arryn","link":"Lysa Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lysa Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Lysa_Arryn" title="Lysa Arryn">Lysa Arryn</a> returned to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eyrie","link":"Eyrie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eyrie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Eyrie" title="Eyrie" class="mw-redirect">Eyrie</a> with her son, Ser Hugh elected to remain in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> in the King's service.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> sends <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jory Cassel","link":"Jory Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jory Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Jory_Cassel" title="Jory Cassel">Jory Cassel</a> to quiz Ser Hugh about Jon Arryn's death. </p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Silent Sisters.png|thumb|Ser Hugh of the Vale being prepared for transport home to be buried.]]","title":"Silent_Sisters.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Silent Sisters","caption":"Ser Hugh of the Vale being prepared for transport home to be buried.","captionParsed":"Ser Hugh of the Vale being prepared for transport home to be buried."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" alt="" src="http://vignette4.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/a/a2/Silent_Sisters.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20110525160356" width="180" height="107" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Silent Sisters.png" data-image-key="Silent_Sisters.png"  type="image" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Hugh is a reasonably effective warrior but is newly-proud and slightly drunk on his achievement of becoming a knight.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Stark met Ser Barristan at a cart transporting Ser Hugh's remains after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Silent Sisters","link":"Silent Sisters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Silent Sisters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Silent_Sisters" title="Silent Sisters">Silent Sisters</a> had dressed him. Ser Barristan explains that he did stand vigil alone, but Ser Hugh apparently has a mother in the Vale and the remains are being sent to her. Ser Hugh's armor is new since he had it made especially for the tournament and has not been completely paid for, but Lord Stark will deal with the armorer himself and insists to the Silent Sister accompanying the cart that Ser Hugh's mother receive his armor since he knows it will mean something to her.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grand Maester","link":"Grand Maester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grand Maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Grand_Maester" title="Grand Maester">Grand Maester</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pycelle","link":"Pycelle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pycelle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Pycelle" title="Pycelle">Pycelle</a> is being accused by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> of fatally poisoning Jon Arryn, Pycelle denies it while accusing Ser Hugh of doing it and that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Varys","link":"Varys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Varys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Varys" title="Varys">Varys</a> and Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei","link":"Cersei","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-193075336956cca63fa2474" href="/wiki/Cersei" title="Cersei" class="mw-redirect">Cersei</a> will concur with Pycelle about Ser Hugh's guilt.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Upon being knighted, Ser Hugh's sigil is a blue field bordered in crescent moons.
</p>
