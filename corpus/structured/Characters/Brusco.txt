 Background 

Brusco is a bouncer living in Braavos. Season 5 

When Lanna enters the brothel to sell oysters, Brusco tells her to leave, but Lhara convinces him to let her stay. Appearances 

 In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels Brusco doesn't work at a brothel. Instead he's a fishmonger that takes Arya, under her alias of Cat of the Canals, as an employee. Brea, who appears as a prostitute in the episode, is his eldest daughter in the novels. See also (spoilers from the books) References
