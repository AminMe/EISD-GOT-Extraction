<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Brusco is a bouncer living in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196919168856cca74910983" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a>.

<h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196919168856cca74910983" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a> </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lanna","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark|Lanna]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196919168856cca74910983" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Lanna</a> enters the brothel to sell oysters, Brusco tells her to leave, but <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lhara","link":"Lhara","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lhara]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196919168856cca74910983" href="/wiki/Lhara" title="Lhara">Lhara</a> convinces him to let her stay.
