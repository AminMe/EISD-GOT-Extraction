<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Hallyne</b>, sometimes called <b>Hallyne the Pyromancer</b>, is a recurring character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"second season","link":"Season 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2|second season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">second season</a>. He is played by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Roy Dotrice","link":"Roy Dotrice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Roy Dotrice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Roy_Dotrice" title="Roy Dotrice">Roy Dotrice</a>. Hallyne is the head of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Alchemists' Guild","link":"Alchemists' Guild","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Alchemists' Guild]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Alchemists'_Guild" title="Alchemists' Guild">Alchemists' Guild</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Hallyne is the head of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Alchemists' Guild","link":"Alchemists' Guild","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Alchemists' Guild]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Alchemists'_Guild" title="Alchemists' Guild">Alchemists' Guild</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, in charge of the city's stocks of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wildfire","link":"wildfire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wildfire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Wildfire" title="Wildfire">wildfire</a> and concerned with other fire-related matters.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the reign of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys II Targaryen","link":"Aerys II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Aerys_II_Targaryen" title="Aerys II Targaryen">Aerys II Targaryen</a>, in which the Guild returned to prominence, Hallyne was an acolyte of the Guild. Later, as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a> raged on, Hallyne heard rumors that Aerys had commissioned the Guild's Wisdoms, led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rossart","link":"Rossart","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rossart]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Rossart" title="Rossart">Rossart</a>, to create a great weapon to employ against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>. However, the city was <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sacked","link":"Sack of King's Landing","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of King's Landing|sacked]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Sack_of_King's_Landing" title="Sack of King's Landing">sacked</a> before the weapon could be unleashed and many of the Wisdoms assassinated.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/www.youtube.com\/watch?v=E7b-Z2lLtLc Game of Thrones Season Two Blu-ray Feature: History of the Alchemists' Guild]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Queen Regent <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> commissions the Alchemist's Guild to produce wildfire for the defense of the city. Hallyne receives <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Guildhall of the Alchemists","link":"Guildhall of the Alchemists","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Guildhall of the Alchemists]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Guildhall_of_the_Alchemists" title="Guildhall of the Alchemists">Guildhall of the Alchemists</a> after he discovers Cersei's plans. Tyrion recalls an old sailor’s proverb “Piss on wildfire and your cock burns off.” <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a> grins and Wisdom Hallyne says that he has never conducted that particular experiment. Hallyne adds that it could be true because "the substance", as pyromancers call wildfire, burns so hot that it can melt wood, stone, steel, and even flesh, which melts "like tallow", according to the aged pyromancer. Hallyne claims that wildfire was the key to the power of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a> after their dragons died out. However, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a> mocks him and says that he has seen many insane old men pushing carts filled with jars of “pig shit” around war camps making claims about their substances properties. Hallyne wags a finger at Bronn and assures him that their order does not deal in pig shit. Hallyne tells Tyrion that the substance is fire given form and that they have been perfecting it since the reign of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maegor Targaryen","link":"Maegor Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maegor Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Maegor_Targaryen" title="Maegor Targaryen">Maegor Targaryen</a>. Bronn asks what the purpose of wildfire is and Hallyne says that it can be used in catapults.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"E15\">\"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the scenes</h2>
<ul data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><li>Wisdom Hallyne is played by Roy Dotrice, who narrated the audiobook versions of all five novels currently in the <i>A Song of Ice and Fire</i> series (and thus in a sense is "reprising" his role as Hallyne). Dotrice was originally cast to play <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grand Maester","link":"Grand Maester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grand Maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Grand_Maester" title="Grand Maester">Grand Maester</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pycelle","link":"Pycelle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pycelle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Pycelle" title="Pycelle">Pycelle</a>, but fell ill shortly before production and was replaced by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Julian Glover","link":"Julian Glover","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Julian Glover]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Julian_Glover" title="Julian Glover">Julian Glover</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/grrm.livejournal.com\/168109.html George R.R. Martin's blog] explaining Pycelle's recasting<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Hallyne is a self-important man frustrated with the Guild's fall in power and influence since the reign of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mad King","link":"Mad King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mad King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43222173556cca67c24864" href="/wiki/Mad_King" title="Mad King" class="mw-redirect">Mad King</a>. Hallyne is said to be a "third generation alchemist", i.e. his father and his grandfather were also alchemists.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Hallyne is rewarded with the title of "lord" after the Battle of the Blackwater, as a reward for providing all of the wildfire which was used decisively in defense of the city. The title is only nominal, however, as he gains no lands or income.
</p>
