 Kayla is a recurring character in the third and fourth seasons. She is portrayed by contortionist Pixie Le Knot. Kayla is a prostitute in the King's Landing brothel owned by Petyr Littlefinger Baelish. Biography 

 Kayla is a prostitute and a highly skilled contortionist. She is one of only four women in the known world who can bend her body to the extent that she can properly perform a sexual position known as the Meereenese Knot. Season 3

 Behind the Scenes 

The actress who plays Kayla, Pixie Le Knot, is a professional contortionist. In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there is no character named Kayla. The Meereenese Knot is an inside joke to the books fandom about a major plot issue George R. R. Martin had to deal with when writing A Dance With Dragons. See also References
