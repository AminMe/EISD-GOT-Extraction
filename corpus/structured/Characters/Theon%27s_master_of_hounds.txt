Theon's master of hounds is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Steve Wilson and only appears in A Man Without Honor. Biography 

 Season 2 

The master of hounds assists Theon Greyjoy in the search for the escaped Bran and Rickon Stark. They track them to a shepherd's farm where the hounds lose the scent of the boys.
