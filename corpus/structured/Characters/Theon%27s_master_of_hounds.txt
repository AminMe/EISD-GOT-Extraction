<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The master of hounds assists <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a> in the search for the escaped <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark|Bran]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickon Stark","link":"Rickon Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickon Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" href="/wiki/Rickon_Stark" title="Rickon Stark">Rickon Stark</a>. They track them to a shepherd's farm where the hounds lose the scent of the boys. Theon later returns to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> with the charred corpses of two children.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Man Without Honor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Theon's crew turn on him and deliver him to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ramsay Snow","link":"Ramsay Bolton","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ramsay Bolton|Ramsay Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" href="/wiki/Ramsay_Bolton" title="Ramsay Bolton">Ramsay Snow</a> in exchange for being allowed to leave Winterfell alive.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Valar Morghulis]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> However, they are betrayed by Snow, captured and flayed alive.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Mhysa]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-204172083556cca7b26d93e" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
