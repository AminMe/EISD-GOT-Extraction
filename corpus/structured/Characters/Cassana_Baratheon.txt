<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><b>Cassana Baratheon</b>, née <b>Estermont</b>, is an unseen character in <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Game of Thrones","link":"Game of Thrones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Game of Thrones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Game_of_Thrones" title="Game of Thrones" class="mw-redirect">Game of Thrones</a></i>. She is deceased when the events of the series begin and is not expected to appear in the series. She is the mother of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Cassana was born to House Estermont, a noble house from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stormlands","link":"the Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/The_Stormlands" title="The Stormlands">the Stormlands</a>. She became the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady of Storm's End","link":"Lord of Storm's End","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Storm's End|Lady of Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Storm's_End" title="Lord of Storm's End">Lady of Storm's End</a> and the wife of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Steffon Baratheon","link":"Steffon Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Steffon Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Steffon_Baratheon" title="Steffon Baratheon">Steffon Baratheon</a>, the head of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a>, one of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Houses","link":"Great Houses","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Houses]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Great_Houses" title="Great Houses" class="mw-redirect">Great Houses</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. They had three sons, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon|Robert]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>. She lived with her family in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-181345472556cca6c77cf1d" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Cassana comes from House Estermont. She married Steffon Baratheon and bore him three sons as in the series. She accompanied him on a trip to Essos. Their ship, the <i>Windproud</i>, sank in Shipbreaker Bay within sight of Storm's End on their return voyage. Their bodies were never found.
</p>
