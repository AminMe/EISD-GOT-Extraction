<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Mychal Baratheon is an early member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17429838656cca7c9baf57" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a>, the son of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Orys Baratheon","link":"Orys Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Orys Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17429838656cca7c9baf57" href="/wiki/Orys_Baratheon" title="Orys Baratheon">Orys Baratheon</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Argalia Baratheon","link":"Argalia Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Argalia Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17429838656cca7c9baf57" href="/wiki/Argalia_Baratheon" title="Argalia Baratheon">Argalia Baratheon</a>, and a distant ancestor of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17429838656cca7c9baf57" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> and his brothers <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17429838656cca7c9baf57" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon|Renly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17429838656cca7c9baf57" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Mychal Baratheon is mentioned only in the TV adaptation of <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17429838656cca7c9baf57" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> and has no corresponding counterpart in the books. The page Ned Stark reads from <i>The Lineages and Histories of the Great Houses of the Seven Kingdoms</i> specifies that he was the son of Orys Baratheon, founder of House Baratheon. The full family tree of House Baratheon for all three centuries of its existence hasn't been revealed in the books yet, nor has specific mention been made of Orys' son, so it is possible that reference to Mychal Baratheon is based on notes from author George R.R. Martin.
</p>
