In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Lomas Longstrider was a famous scribe who travelled all over the Known World and documented his adventures in the books Wonders and Wonders Made by Man. 

The places he visited and/or wrote about included Qarth, Old Ghis, Norvos, Braavos, Volantis, the Shivering Sea, Yi Ti and Bayasabhad. 

He is long dead when the series takes place. Tyrion Lannister has memorised many passages from his books, and Barristan Selmy is also familiar with his work. References
