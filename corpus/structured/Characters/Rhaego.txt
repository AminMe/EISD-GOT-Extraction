</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Rhaego</b> is a minor character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first","link":"Season 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1|first]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">first</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"second","link":"Season 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2|second]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">second</a> seasons. He was the son of Khal <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogo","link":"Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Drogo" title="Drogo">Drogo</a> and Khaleesi <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen.","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen|Daenerys Targaryen.]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen.</a> He was named in honor of his uncle, Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegar Targaryen","link":"Rhaegar Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegar Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Rhaegar_Targaryen" title="Rhaegar Targaryen">Rhaegar Targaryen</a>. According to a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a> prophecy, he would have been <i>the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stallion Who Mounts the World","link":"Stallion Who Mounts the World","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stallion Who Mounts the World]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Stallion_Who_Mounts_the_World" title="Stallion Who Mounts the World">Stallion Who Mounts the World</a></i>, a great <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"khal","link":"khal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[khal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Khal" title="Khal">khal</a></i> of <i>khals</i> who would have united the Dothraki as one horde and used them to conquer the entire world. He was stillborn after being involved in a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"blood magic","link":"blood magic","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[blood magic]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Blood_magic" title="Blood magic">blood magic</a> ritual performed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mirri Maz Duur","link":"Mirri Maz Duur","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mirri Maz Duur]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Mirri_Maz_Duur" title="Mirri Maz Duur">Mirri Maz Duur</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Daenerys's handmaiden <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Irri","link":"Irri","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Irri]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Irri" title="Irri">Irri</a> realizes that Daenerys is pregnant with Drogo's child. They throw a small feast to celebrate. Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jorah Mormont","link":"Jorah Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jorah Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Jorah_Mormont" title="Jorah Mormont">Jorah Mormont</a> is troubled by the news and sends word of it to his paymaster, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Varys","link":"Varys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Varys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Varys" title="Varys">Varys</a>, in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. Daenerys tells Drogo she is certain the child will be a boy.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Lord Snow]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Daenerys strikes her brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Viserys","link":"Viserys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Viserys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Viserys" title="Viserys" class="mw-redirect">Viserys</a> after he threatens her and her unborn son.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> orders Daenerys, her unborn child and Viserys killed.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> In <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vaes Dothrak","link":"Vaes Dothrak","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vaes Dothrak]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Vaes_Dothrak" title="Vaes Dothrak">Vaes Dothrak</a>, omens are taken and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"crones","link":"Dosh khaleen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dosh khaleen|crones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Dosh_khaleen" title="Dosh khaleen">crones</a> proclaim that Drogo's son will be the Stallion That Mounts the World. Daenerys names him Rhaego for her deceased brother, Rhaegar.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Golden Crown]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">On the borders of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lhazar","link":"Lhazar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lhazar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Lhazar" title="Lhazar">Lhazar</a>, close to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Waste","link":"Red Waste","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Waste]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Red_Waste" title="Red Waste">Red Waste</a>, Rhaego is reportedly stillborn and comes out looking like a monster. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mirri Maz Duur","link":"Mirri Maz Duur","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mirri Maz Duur]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Mirri_Maz_Duur" title="Mirri Maz Duur">Mirri Maz Duur</a> claims that he had scales like a lizard, was blind with bat-like wings, and had innards full of graveworms. She also says that when she touched him, his skin fell away from his bones, and that it was his death that paid for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogo's","link":"Drogo","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogo|Drogo's]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/Drogo" title="Drogo">Drogo's</a> life.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Fire and Blood]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-41838858156cca5d79f80f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, despite being told that her stillborn baby was a monster, Daenerys is visited by images of Rhaego as he would have been had he not been cursed by Mirri Maz Duur and survived. She sees him in her vision in the House of the Undying in Qarth. He appears to her as a young man with his father's physique and tanned skin coloring, with Daenerys' long silver-gold hair, and with almond shaped eyes of the traditional Targaryen purple color. He stands before a burning city, under the banner of a fiery stallion. He holds out his hand to her, but then fire pours from his mouth, and he is consumed by flame.
</p>
