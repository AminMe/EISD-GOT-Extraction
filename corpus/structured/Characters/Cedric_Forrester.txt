Lord Cedric Forrester is an unseen character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. 

He ​was the man responsible for constructing the seat of House Forrester: Ironrath 1500 years ago. He had at least three sons, triplets, who helped him construct Ironrath. 

Because of his decision to build Ironrath he is regarded as one of the most important ancestors of House Forrester, as he built their ancestral home, enabling his descendants to further secure their hold of the Ironwood, for which their house is famous for.
