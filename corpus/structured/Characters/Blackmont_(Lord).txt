 Book series 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, House Blackmont is ruled by Lady Larra Blackmont as the Dornish practice equal primogeniture, allowing firstborn daughters to inherit and to pass on the family name instead of adopting that of their husbands. 

Larra and her children are among the Dornish nobles that accompany Oberyn Martell to King's Landing. See also References
