<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is captured by the forces of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> interrogates him and learns that he has estimated the size of the Stark army as 20,000 men. Robb feigns mercy and lets him go in an effort to trick <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> into thinking 20,000 <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Northmen","link":"Northmen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Northmen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" href="/wiki/Northmen" title="Northmen">Northmen</a> were marching against his army, when in actuality Robb had only sent 2,000 men. This by extension has Robb <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"defeat","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood|defeat]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">defeat</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>'s army at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Whispering Wood","link":"Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" href="/wiki/Whispering_Wood" title="Whispering Wood">Whispering Wood</a>, with Jaime being captured himself.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Pointy End]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-157053391456cca7a908f92" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
