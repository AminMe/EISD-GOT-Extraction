In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, the only character named Godwyn is a guard loyal to House Lannister. He serves the royal family as one of the Red Cloaks station in King's Landing. 

Godwyn accompanies Princess Myrcella Baratheon and Prince Tommen Baratheon in the Red Keep when they encounter Arya Stark, though they do not recognise her and mistake her for a street child. The accompanying septa orders Godwyn to catch Arya, but she manages to evade him and escape. References
