<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Slavery","link":"Slavery","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slavery]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-146722450256cca71a8bfac" href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">Slavery</a> is officially illegal in Pentos, due to a treaty it established a hundred years ago with the Free City of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-146722450256cca71a8bfac" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a>, which abhors slavery. However, wealthy and powerful magisters in Pentos such as Illyrio often flout these restrictions: his bronze-collared "servants" are actually slaves in all but name.
</p>
