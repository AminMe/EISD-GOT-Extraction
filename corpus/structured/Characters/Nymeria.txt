</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Nymeria</b> was the warrior-queen who led the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhoynar","link":"Rhoynar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhoynar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Rhoynar" title="Rhoynar">Rhoynar</a> refugees to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a> a thousand years ago. She is an ancestor of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Martell","link":"House Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/House_Martell" title="House Martell">House Martell</a>, and seen as the founder of Dorne as a unified realm under their rule.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">History</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Nymeria Ten Thousand Ships.png|thumb|200px|left|Nymeria leading the Rhoynar refugee fleet in its migration to Westeros]]","title":"Nymeria_Ten_Thousand_Ships.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Nymeria Ten Thousand Ships","align":"left","caption":"Nymeria leading the Rhoynar refugee fleet in its migration to Westeros","width":200,"captionParsed":"Nymeria leading the Rhoynar refugee fleet in its migration to Westeros"}}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" alt="" src="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/2/20/Nymeria_Ten_Thousand_Ships.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20150410223335" width="200" height="109" class="image thumb alignLeft withCaption" data-image-name="Nymeria Ten Thousand Ships.png" data-image-key="Nymeria_Ten_Thousand_Ships.png"  type="image" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->The Rhoynar once lived in city-states along the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhoyne","link":"Rhoyne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhoyne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Rhoyne" title="Rhoyne">Rhoyne</a> River. About a thousand years ago (perhaps 700 years before the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen Conquest","link":"War of Conquest","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of Conquest|Targaryen Conquest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/War_of_Conquest" title="War of Conquest">Targaryen Conquest</a>), the Rhoynar were on the verge of being conquered by the expanding power of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valyrian Freehold","link":"Valyrian Freehold","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valyrian Freehold]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Valyrian_Freehold" title="Valyrian Freehold">Valyrian Freehold</a> and its <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"Dragons","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dragons|dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a>. Defeated at the end of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhoynish Wars","link":"Rhoynish Wars","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhoynish Wars]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Rhoynish_Wars" title="Rhoynish Wars">Rhoynish Wars</a>, Nymeria rallied the survivors and fled <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Essos","link":"Essos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Essos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Essos" title="Essos">Essos</a> altogether, sailing in a refugee fleet said to include ten thousand ships. After much wandering she eventually led her people to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a>, at the southeastern tip of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. After landing, Nymeria had all of the Rhoynar's 10,000 ships burned, so that none would have second thoughts about staying.
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Nymeria_and_Mors_Martell.png|thumb|200px|Local lord [[Mors Martell]] united his forces with Nymeria and her Rhoynar, to conquer and unify Dorne for the first time.]]","title":"Nymeria_and_Mors_Martell.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Nymeria and Mors Martell","caption":"Local lord [[Mors Martell]] united his forces with Nymeria and her Rhoynar, to conquer and unify Dorne for the first time.","width":200,"captionParsed":"Local lord <a _rte_dataidx=\"0017\" href=\"\/wiki\/Mors_Martell\" title=\"Mors Martell\">Mors Martell<\/a> united his forces with Nymeria and her Rhoynar, to conquer and unify Dorne for the first time."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" alt="" src="http://vignette4.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/9/99/Nymeria_and_Mors_Martell.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20150316165321" width="200" height="112" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Nymeria and Mors Martell.png" data-image-key="Nymeria_and_Mors_Martell.png"  type="image" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->At the time, Dorne was divided into many petty kingdoms and had never been unified. Impressed by Nymeria, local king Mors Martell married her and united her forces with his own. Together, they conquered and unified all of Dorne for the first time, reigning from the Martell's seat at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sunspear","link":"Sunspear","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sunspear]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Sunspear" title="Sunspear">Sunspear</a>. Dorne was the last of the Seven Kingdoms to be unified.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Dornish revere Nymeria as the founder of their realm, and her descendants in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Martell","link":"House Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/House_Martell" title="House Martell">House Martell</a> continued to rule Dorne through the present day. As a result, "Nymeria" actually became a common name in Dorne (comparable to how "Brandon" is a very common name in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"The North","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The North|the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>, after their founder <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brandon Stark the Builder","link":"Brandon Stark (the Builder)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brandon Stark (the Builder)|Brandon Stark the Builder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Brandon_Stark_(the_Builder)" title="Brandon Stark (the Builder)">Brandon Stark the Builder</a>). <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oberyn Martell","link":"Oberyn Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oberyn Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Oberyn_Martell" title="Oberyn Martell">Oberyn Martell</a> - himself a direct descendant of Nymeria, warrior-queen of the Rhoynar - named one of his own bastard daughters <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Nymeria Sand","link":"Nymeria Sand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nymeria Sand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Nymeria_Sand" title="Nymeria Sand">Nymeria Sand</a>. Nymeria's fame also spread across Westeros: even young <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a>, fascinated with legends about past warrior-queens, chose to name her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"direwolf","link":"Direwolf","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Direwolf|direwolf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Direwolf" title="Direwolf" class="mw-redirect">direwolf</a> "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Nymeria","link":"Nymeria (direwolf)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nymeria (direwolf)|Nymeria]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Nymeria_(direwolf)" title="Nymeria (direwolf)">Nymeria</a>".
</p>
<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> In the books </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Nymeria was the leader of the Rhoynar refugees and is seen as the founder of unified Dorne.  Before the Valyrians defeated the coalition of Rhoynar city-states, Nymeria herself was the ruling Princess of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ny Sar","link":"Ny Sar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ny Sar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Ny_Sar" title="Ny Sar">Ny Sar</a>.  
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After Nymeria died she was succeeded by her eldest child, her daughter with Mors Martell.  Within her own lifetime she was able to enforce the gender-blind equal primogeniture laws of the Rhoynar in Dorne, even though she had a younger son by a different husband (after Mors died) who by the laws of the Andals would have inherited rule of Dorne after her.  
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">A minor detail in the books is that after Nymeria married Mors Martell, the formal name of their descendants' House actually became "House Nymeros Martell" (Nymeros meaning "of Nymeria"), combining their two names. The TV series has not mentioned this, though it is rarely mentioned in the books either.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"Nymeria" became a popular name in Dorne in later generations. Even Arya Stark named her direwolf "Nymeria" after her. In the novels, her choice of name for her direwolf is specifically explained in the first novel: in contrast with her refined sister <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a>, who gave her direwolf the elegant name "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady","link":"Lady","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lady]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Lady" title="Lady">Lady</a>", the tomboyish Arya named her direwolf after a famous warrior-queen. The TV series didn't do this in Season 1, though certainly because even Dorne itself had not yet been introduced and it would have taken a long time to explain.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Nymeria is remembered as a warrior-queen, though she actually was not a warrior herself, in the sense of carrying and wielding weapons. She was considered a warrior for being a cunning military strategist and commander of armies (comparable to how in his later years Tywin Lannister was considered a dangerous war-leader, without actually wielding his own sword anymore). That being said, many of the Rhoynar refugees in her army were female, because Rhoyar women were taught to fought alongside their men (and most of the men had died fighting the Valyrians).
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">As the founder of one of the Seven Kingdoms, Nymeria is revered in Dorne much as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brandon Stark the Builder","link":"Brandon Stark (the Builder)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brandon Stark (the Builder)|Brandon Stark the Builder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Brandon_Stark_(the_Builder)" title="Brandon Stark (the Builder)">Brandon Stark the Builder</a> is revered in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"The North","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The North|the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lann","link":"Lann","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lann]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Lann" title="Lann">Lann</a> the Clever in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Westerlands","link":"The Westerlands","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Westerlands|the Westerlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/The_Westerlands" title="The Westerlands">the Westerlands</a>, or <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Garth Greenhand","link":"Garth Greenhand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Garth Greenhand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Garth_Greenhand" title="Garth Greenhand" class="mw-redirect">Garth Greenhand</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"The Reach","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Reach|the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a>.  A major difference however is that these figures all lived over six thousand years ago in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Age of Heroes","link":"Age of Heroes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Age of Heroes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57818181356cca623bc0e5" href="/wiki/Age_of_Heroes" title="Age of Heroes">Age of Heroes</a>, before written accounts, and all of the tales about them are shrouded in legend - many maesters question if "Bran the Builder" or "Lann the Clever" ever truly existed at all, or are just folklore.  In contrast, Nymeria unified Dorne only one thousand years ago, long after the times of mythical heroes had progressed into the era of written history, so Nymeria is fully understood to have been a real person.
</p>
