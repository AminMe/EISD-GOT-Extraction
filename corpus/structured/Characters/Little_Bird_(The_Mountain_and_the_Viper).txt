This leads him to the realization that Jorah has been reporting <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a>'s actions to Varys. When confronted with this revelation, Jorah asks Barristan who brought him the pardon but Barristan doesn't tell him.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Mountain and the Viper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Song of Ice and Fire","link":"Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" href="/wiki/Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="Song of Ice and Fire" class="mw-redirect">Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Varys is known for employing orphans and urchins as his "little birds". However, Jorah's treachery is revealed in a different manner. After saving Daenerys from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"manticore","link":"manticore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[manticore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" href="/wiki/Manticore" title="Manticore" class="mw-redirect">manticore</a>, Barristan comes into her service as Arstan Whitebeard in order to observe her and make sure that she has not inherited <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"her father","link":"Aerys II Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II Targaryen|her father]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" href="/wiki/Aerys_II_Targaryen" title="Aerys II Targaryen">her father</a>'s <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"madness","link":"Targaryen madness","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Targaryen madness|madness]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" href="/wiki/Targaryen_madness" title="Targaryen madness">madness</a>. After saving Daenerys from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mero","link":"Mero","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mero]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" href="/wiki/Mero" title="Mero">Mero</a>, Jorah recognizes him and reveals his true identity. In return, Barristan reveals Jorah's treachery, which he knew about from attending meetings of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Small Council","link":"Small Council","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Small Council]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" href="/wiki/Small_Council" title="Small Council">Small Council</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the TV series, Barristan explains to Jorah in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kissed by Fire","link":"Kissed by Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kissed by Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-125527256356cca78a216b0" href="/wiki/Kissed_by_Fire" title="Kissed by Fire">Kissed by Fire</a>" that Robert never trusted him to do anything more than protect him, hence Barristan was barred from Small Council meetings, though the Lord Commander is usually the Small Council's military attaché. Hence, he had no knowledge of Jorah's treachery prior to reading the message from the little bird.
</p>
