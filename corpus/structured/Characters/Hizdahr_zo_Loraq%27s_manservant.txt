</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meereen","link":"Meereen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meereen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131927766556cca7ee9cffc" href="/wiki/Meereen" title="Meereen">Meereenese</a> man serving the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of Loraq","link":"House of Loraq","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of Loraq]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131927766556cca7ee9cffc" href="/wiki/House_of_Loraq" title="House of Loraq">House of Loraq</a>. His clothing and lack of collar suggest that he is a free man working as a servant, not a slave, even prior to Daenerys's victory.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131927766556cca7ee9cffc" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He's alongside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hizdahr zo Loraq","link":"Hizdahr zo Loraq","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hizdahr zo Loraq]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131927766556cca7ee9cffc" href="/wiki/Hizdahr_zo_Loraq" title="Hizdahr zo Loraq">Hizdahr zo Loraq</a> when the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Masters","link":"Great Masters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Masters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131927766556cca7ee9cffc" href="/wiki/Great_Masters" title="Great Masters">Great Masters</a> gather over the walls of Meereen when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131927766556cca7ee9cffc" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> and her army appear before the city gates to lay <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"siege","link":"Siege of Meereen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Meereen|siege]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131927766556cca7ee9cffc" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Meereen" title="Siege of Meereen">siege</a> to the city.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Breaker of Chains]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131927766556cca7ee9cffc" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
