<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Background </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">This lesser merchant, presumably of Essosi origin, deals in idols and images of different religions, including representations of the fiery heart of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Light","link":"Lord of Light","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Light]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69611041956cca8068e2bf" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Light" title="Lord of Light" class="mw-redirect">Lord of Light</a>.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The merchant is in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69611041956cca8068e2bf" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> when members of the the recently restored <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Faith Militant","link":"Faith Militant","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Faith Militant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69611041956cca8068e2bf" href="/wiki/Faith_Militant" title="Faith Militant">Faith Militant</a> approach him and destroy his post and the idols he sells.
