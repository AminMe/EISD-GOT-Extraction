 A foreign merchant is a character in the fifth season. He is portrayed by Allon Sylvain and appears in Sons of the Harpy. Biography 

 Background 

This lesser merchant, presumably of Essosi origin, deals in idols and images of different religions, including representations of the fiery heart of the Lord of Light. Season 5 

The merchant is in King's Landing when members of the the recently restored Faith Militant approach him and destroy his post and the idols he sells.
