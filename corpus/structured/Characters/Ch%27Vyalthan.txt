 Ch'Vyalthan, is an unseen character mentioned in the second season. He is deceased when the events of the series begin and is not expected to appear. He was an Archmaester of the Citadel and the author of the book An History of the Great Sieges of Westeros. Biography 

 Season 2 

Tyrion Lannister refers to his book when preparing the defense of King's Landing. Varys criticizes him as an uninspired writer.
