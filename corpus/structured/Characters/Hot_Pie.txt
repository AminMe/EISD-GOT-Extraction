<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Hot Pie is an orphan living in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> and working for a baker as an apprentice. He was born and grew up in the city.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Mockingbird]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Hot Pie and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lommy Greenhands","link":"Lommy Greenhands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lommy Greenhands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Lommy_Greenhands" title="Lommy Greenhands" class="mw-redirect">Lommy Greenhands</a> are recruited to join the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. They bully <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> when she joins their group and demand she give up her sword, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Needle","link":"Needle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Needle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Needle" title="Needle">Needle</a>. Hot Pie claims that he killed a boy once by kicking him to death. When Arya refuses and threatens to kill him like she has with other 'fat boys', he backpedals and bumps into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>, who scares him off.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Fire and Blood]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Hot Pie is travelling north on the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsroad","link":"Kingsroad","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsroad]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Kingsroad" title="Kingsroad">Kingsroad</a> with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a>’s recruits.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The North Remembers]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gold Cloaks","link":"Gold Cloaks","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gold Cloaks]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Gold_Cloaks" title="Gold Cloaks" class="mw-redirect">Gold Cloaks</a> from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> come upon them looking for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>. Yoren intimidates them into leaving but they promise to return with more men. Lommy says that they should yield if the gold cloaks return, not wanting to be caught in a battle. Hot Pie claims that he is unafraid of battle and Arya questions his courage. Hot Pie claims to have seen lots of battles and Arya calls him a liar. Hot Pie insists that he saw a man stabbed in the neck outside a tavern in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Flea Bottom","link":"Flea Bottom","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Flea Bottom]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Flea_Bottom" title="Flea Bottom">Flea Bottom</a>. Lommy tells Hot Pie that two men fighting is not a battle. Hot Pie says that they had armor on. Arya questions the relevance of this and Hot Pie says that if the combatants wear armor, it is automatically a battle. Lommy refutes the assertion and Hot Pie wonders what a dyer's apprentice would know about battle. As Gendry returns to refill the pail, Arya tell Hot Pie that Gendry is an armorer's apprentice. She instructs Hot Pie to relay his definition of a battle to Gendry. Gendry asks who told Hot Pie that. Hot Pie claims that he heard it from a knight. Gendry asks how Hot Pie knew he was a knight and Hot Pie again cites armor as deciding the issue. Gendry explains that anyone can buy armor. Hot Pie asks how Gendry knows and Gendry reminds him that he sold armor.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Night Lands]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

They are all captured by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brotherhood without Banners","link":"Brotherhood without Banners","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brotherhood without Banners]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Brotherhood_without_Banners" title="Brotherhood without Banners" class="mw-redirect">Brotherhood without Banners</a>. The Brotherhood takes them to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a> and feeds them, but before they can depart, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sandor \"The Hound\" Clegane","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane|Sandor \"The Hound\" Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">Sandor "The Hound" Clegane</a> is brought in as a prisoner, and in surprise, identifies Arya Stark.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Dark Wings, Dark Words]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Hot Pie is a young recruit for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a> meets in King's Landing. He was a baker's apprentice. He does not appear until the second novel <i>A Clash of Kings</i>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The first encounter between Hot Pie and Arya is much more violent than in the show: Hot Pie threatens Arya and tries to take Needle by force, while Lommy encourages him to. Gendry tells them to leave Arya alone, but they do not listen. Arya does not want to fight and offers Hot Pie her wooden sword, but he demands Needle and tries to take it from her. Using the wooden sword, Arya whacks Hot Pie's donkey, causing him to fall off of it, then when Hot Pie is on the ground Arya breaks his nose. When she turns her attention to Lommy who backs off in fear, Hot Pie attacks her from behind with a jagged rock. Gendry warns Arya of his approach (but he does not intervene in the brawl otherwise). Arya evades the rock and beats Hot Pie so badly that he soils his pants and is forced to spend a short while riding on the back of a wagon due to his injuries. For this, Yoren thrashes Arya to the extent of her being too sore to sit for a short while.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After substantial time as prisoners of the Lannisters at Harrenhal, and their subsequent escape together, Arya starts to think of both Gendry and Hot Pie as her "pack". Just before they meet the Brotherhood Without Banners, Arya notes to herself that Hot Pie is only slowing them down as she tries to make her way to Riverrun, but she won't leave him behind because a wolf pack protects its members.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Hot Pie ends up making bread for a different inn named the Inn of the Kneeling Man. He decides to stay there because he is tired of travelling dangerous roads, exposed to the rain, suffering of saddle sores and constantly frightened. He asks Arya to come back when the war was over, and she promises to, though she has no idea if the war would ever be done. Although Arya thinks Hot Pie is stupid and cowardly, she misses him a lot.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Hot Pie is not mentioned in the books afterwards. He never meets <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne","link":"Brienne of Tarth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne of Tarth|Brienne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Brienne_of_Tarth" title="Brienne of Tarth">Brienne</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Podrick Payne","link":"Podrick Payne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Podrick Payne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-101949759656cca619eeb2d" href="/wiki/Podrick_Payne" title="Podrick Payne">Podrick Payne</a>.
</p>
