<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lyonel Baratheon was a member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>, one of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Great Houses","link":"Great Houses","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Great Houses]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/Great_Houses" title="Great Houses" class="mw-redirect">Great Houses</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>, and rulers of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormlands","link":"Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/Stormlands" title="Stormlands" class="mw-redirect">Stormlands</a>. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lyonel Baratheon appears in <i>The Hedge Knight</i>, one of the stories in the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tales of Dunk and Egg","link":"Tales of Dunk and Egg","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tales of Dunk and Egg]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/Tales_of_Dunk_and_Egg" title="Tales of Dunk and Egg">Tales of Dunk and Egg</a></i> series, which focuses on Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon V Targaryen","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Aegon V Targaryen</a> during Aegon's time as Ser Duncan's squire. Ser Lyonel is known as "the Laughing Storm". He would often break into booming laughter the moment tourney rivals touched his shield, and laugh all the time he was mounting and charging and knocking them from their stirrups. If his challengers wore any sort of crest on their helm, Ser Lyonel would strike it off and fling it into the crowd.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The entry for Lyonel in the <i>Lineages</i> book from Season 1 (<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"see this image","link":"File:Steffon.jpg","wasblank":false,"noforce":false,"wikitext":"[[:File:Steffon.jpg|see this image]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-29537191356cca77cd1403" href="/wiki/File:Steffon.jpg" title="File:Steffon.jpg">see this image</a>) says that he died at the age of 45, but this is unlikely, and possibly a case of the TV writers just filling in the gaps of knowledge for the prop book - in which case it is not strictly TV-canon.  In the novels, he apparently died between 239 AL and 245 AL, and when he took part in Duncan's trial by seven in 209 AL he was already a large and powerful warrior.  Assuming that Lyonel was at the very least 16 years old in 209 AL (like Duncan was, though he was probably older), he would have been born around 193 AL.  Lyonel therefore could have been at most around 52 years old when he died - but from how he is presented in <i>The Hedge Knight</i>, he was <i>apparently</i> somewhat older than Duncan.  More information may be filled in with future <i>Tales of Dunk &amp; Egg</i> novellas.
</p>
