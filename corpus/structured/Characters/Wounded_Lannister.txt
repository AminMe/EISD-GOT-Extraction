<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He was a fisherman from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannisport","link":"Lannisport","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lannisport]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" href="/wiki/Lannisport" title="Lannisport">Lannisport</a> conscripted into the force led by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stafford Lannister","link":"Stafford Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stafford Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" href="/wiki/Stafford_Lannister" title="Stafford Lannister">Stafford Lannister</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He participates in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Oxcross","link":"Battle of Oxcross","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Oxcross]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Oxcross" title="Battle of Oxcross">Battle of Oxcross</a>, in which his leg is wounded. He is treated for an infected wound by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talisa Maegyr","link":"Talisa Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talisa Stark|Talisa Maegyr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" href="/wiki/Talisa_Stark" title="Talisa Stark">Talisa Maegyr</a>. Though he begs for her not to, she amputates the leg, telling him that not doing so would kill him. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>, who had noticed Talisa, helps to hold him down, forcing him to bite down on a piece of leather. Afterward, he is loaded onto a cart and taken away.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Talisa blames Robb for the boy losing his leg, explaining that he isn't responsible for the death of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> and isn't friends with King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a>, and that he was a fisherman from Lannisport before they forced a spear in his hand. Robb tells Talisa that the boy was lucky she was there, but she says he was unlucky Robb was.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-96280795156cca76684660" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
