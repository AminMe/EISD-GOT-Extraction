 Background 

Aemon Blackfyre was the second son of Daemon Blackfyre, a legitimized bastard of King Aegon IV Targaryen and his cousin Daena Targaryen, by Serena Blackfyre. He had two brothers: Aegon, his twin, who had been born first, and Daemon II Blackfyre. Season 1
