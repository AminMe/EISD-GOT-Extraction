<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Background</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"\u200bSeason 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3|\u200bSeason 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">â€‹Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Anguy is among the company of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Thoros","link":"Thoros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thoros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Thoros" title="Thoros">Thoros</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Myr","link":"Myr","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Myr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Myr" title="Myr">Myr</a> when they come across <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hot Pie","link":"Hot Pie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hot Pie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Hot_Pie" title="Hot Pie">Hot Pie</a>. He coerces an unwilling Hot Pie into following them to a nearby <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"inn","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads|inn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">inn</a> by making an impressive trick-shot. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Anguy is a superb archer from the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dornish Marches","link":"Dornish Marches","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dornish Marches]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Dornish_Marches" title="Dornish Marches">Dornish Marches</a>, the mountainous southern region of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stormlands","link":"the Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/The_Stormlands" title="The Stormlands">the Stormlands</a> which borders <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dorne","link":"Dorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Dorne" title="Dorne">Dorne</a>. He won the archery competition during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tourney of the Hand","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|Tourney of the Hand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">Tourney of the Hand</a> and was approached by Alyn, a guardsman on orders fromÂ <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a>Â to offer him a place on the Stark household guard, but Anguy drunkenly declined. Anguy is part of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron Throne","link":"Iron Throne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron Throne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Iron_Throne" title="Iron Throne">Iron Throne</a> host assembled by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> to execute Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> and becomes one of the founders of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brotherhood Without Banners","link":"Brotherhood Without Banners","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brotherhood Without Banners]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Brotherhood_Without_Banners" title="Brotherhood Without Banners">Brotherhood Without Banners</a> after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle at the Mummer's Ford","link":"Battle at the Mummer's Ford","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle at the Mummer's Ford]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Battle_at_the_Mummer's_Ford" title="Battle at the Mummer's Ford">Battle at the Mummer's Ford</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Many characters who later formed the Brotherhood were introduced at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tourney of the Hand","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|Tourney of the Hand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">Tourney of the Hand</a> in the first novel. This tourney did occur in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a> of the TV series, but the characters were not introduced, because they would not really make a significant appearance until Season 3 - they are given cameos in the first novel but only reappear as "the Brotherhood" in the third novel (though as in Season 2 of the TV series, in the second novel characters do mention that something called "the Brotherhood" is harassing Lannister supply lines).
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">At the Tourney of the Hand, Anguy won the archery contest and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Thoros","link":"Thoros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thoros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Thoros" title="Thoros">Thoros</a> of Myr won the melee contest. This is why they arrived in King's Landing, and were available when Ned Stark ordered <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Beric Dondarrion","link":"Beric Dondarrion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beric Dondarrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-121858339356cca66ea531d" href="/wiki/Beric_Dondarrion" title="Beric Dondarrion">Beric Dondarrion</a> to lead out a group of able knights to bring Gregor Clegane to justice - the soldiers who formed the original core of the Brotherhood Without Banners. Thus the connection might be lost on the TV audience that one of the reasons the Brotherhood is such a disproportionately capable fighting force is because so many of its initial members were elite fighters who came to the capital city to participate in the Tourney of the Hand. For example it might a bit contrived in the TV series that, as if by pure chance, the Brotherood's lead archer Anguy just happens to be preternaturally skilled with a bow. The books actually do explain that he is a professional archer so skilled that he actively competes in tournaments, and indeed recently won first place at a <i>royal-level</i> archery tournament. Moreover, he doesn't just happen to be wandering in the Riverlands, but the rational reason was given that he was in the capital for a major tournament, before being sent out with Dondarrion.
</p>
