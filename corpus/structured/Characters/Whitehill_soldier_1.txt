<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Rodrik","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester|Lord Rodrik]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57320780356cca7b0d2b4b" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Lord Rodrik</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talia Forrester","link":"Talia Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talia Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57320780356cca7b0d2b4b" href="/wiki/Talia_Forrester" title="Talia Forrester">Talia Forrester</a> attempt to enter the Great Hall at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57320780356cca7b0d2b4b" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>, this soldier sits on the steps and tells them to go around.
