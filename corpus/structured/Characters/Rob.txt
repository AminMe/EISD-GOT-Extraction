 Rob, also known as Red Rob, is an unseen character in Game of Thrones: A Telltale Games Series. History 

 Background 

Red Rob was a farmer. Rodrik Forrester and his friends Namond and Meghan would play on his farm when they were children. The Sword in the Darkness 

Rodrik travels to Red Rob's abandoned farm to meet in secret with Gwyn Whitehill and reminisces on playing there as a child.
