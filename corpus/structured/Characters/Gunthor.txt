<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145614215856cca797c0962" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Gunthor son of Gurn, along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shagga","link":"Shagga","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shagga]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145614215856cca797c0962" href="/wiki/Shagga" title="Shagga">Shagga</a> son of Dolf, and other clansmen, meet Tyrion and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145614215856cca797c0962" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a> in the Mountains of the Moon. In the novel, Gunthor is the first to introduce himself to Tyrion by saying Shagga's TV-series line: "When you meet your gods, say it was Gunthor son of Gurn of the Stone Crows who sent you to them."  Later, instead of departing with Tyrion to the Lannister camp, Gunthor stays behind at the Mountains to rally the members of the hill tribes.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Later, when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sandor Clegane","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145614215856cca797c0962" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">Sandor Clegane</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145614215856cca797c0962" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> arrive in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale","link":"Vale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145614215856cca797c0962" href="/wiki/Vale" title="Vale" class="mw-redirect">Vale</a>, they are told that hill tribes, including the Stone Crows led by Gunthor, are robbing and killing in the area.
</p>
