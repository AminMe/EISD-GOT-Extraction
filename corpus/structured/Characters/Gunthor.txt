In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Gunthorson of Gurn, along with Shagga son of Dolf, and other clansmen, meet Tyrion and Bronnin the Mountains of the Moon. In the novel, Gunthor is the first to introduce himself to Tyrion by saying Shagga's TV-series line: When you meet your gods, say it was Gunthor son of Gurn of the Stone Crows who sent you to them.Later, instead of departing withTyrionto the Lannistercamp, Gunthor stays behind at the Mountains to rally the members of the hill tribes.
