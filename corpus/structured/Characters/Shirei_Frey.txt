<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159520100756cca6c54e8b1" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Shirei Frey is the - supposed - seventh daughter of Lord Walder Frey. She is the second daughter of Lord Walder Frey and his seventh spouse, Lady Annara Farring - thus making her the youngest of Lord Walder's twenty-nine legitimate children. According to Merrett Frey, however, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Black Walder Frey","link":"Walder Rivers","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Rivers|Black Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-159520100756cca6c54e8b1" href="/wiki/Walder_Rivers" title="Walder Rivers">Black Walder Frey</a> was rumored to be sleeping with Lady Annara, thus casting doubts on the parentage of her offspring. Shirei is six years old.
</p>
