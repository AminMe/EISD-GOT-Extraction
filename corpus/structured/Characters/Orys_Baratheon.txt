</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Orys Baratheon</b> is an unseen character in <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Game of Thrones","link":"Game of Thrones","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Game of Thrones]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Game_of_Thrones" title="Game of Thrones" class="mw-redirect">Game of Thrones</a></i>. He is not expected to appear in the series, being long dead by the time it starts. Orys Baratheon is the founder of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Orys Baratheon is the founder of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon","link":"House Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon" title="House Baratheon">House Baratheon</a> and is the distant ancestor of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a> and his brothers <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon|Renly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Orys_argalia.jpg|thumb|Orys Baratheon took Argalia, daughter of the last Storm King, to cement his rule over the Stormlands]]","title":"Orys_argalia.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Orys argalia","caption":"Orys Baratheon took Argalia, daughter of the last Storm King, to cement his rule over the Stormlands","captionParsed":"Orys Baratheon took Argalia, daughter of the last Storm King, to cement his rule over the Stormlands"}}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" alt="" src="http://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/c/c4/Orys_argalia.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120327154713" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Orys argalia.jpg" data-image-key="Orys_argalia.jpg"  type="image" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of Conquest","link":"War of Conquest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of Conquest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/War_of_Conquest" title="War of Conquest">War of Conquest</a>, he was a commander in the army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon the Conqueror","link":"Aegon I Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon I Targaryen|Aegon the Conqueror]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Aegon_I_Targaryen" title="Aegon I Targaryen">Aegon the Conqueror</a>, and was the Conqueror's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bastard","link":"bastard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bastard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Bastard" title="Bastard" class="mw-redirect">bastard</a> half-brother. When Aegon invaded <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>, he sent Orys to seize <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>, the ancestral seat of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm Kings","link":"Storm Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Storm_Kings" title="Storm Kings" class="mw-redirect">Storm Kings</a> and capital of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stormlands","link":"Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Stormlands" title="Stormlands" class="mw-redirect">Stormlands</a>. Orys defeated the Storm King, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Argilac the Arrogant","link":"Argilac the Arrogant","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Argilac the Arrogant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Argilac_the_Arrogant" title="Argilac the Arrogant" class="mw-redirect">Argilac the Arrogant</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"took","link":"Fall of Storm's End","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fall of Storm's End|took]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Fall_of_Storm's_End" title="Fall of Storm's End" class="mw-redirect">took</a> not only Storm's End but also his sigil - the black stag - and words - "Ours is the Fury" - as well as his daughter <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Argalia","link":"Argalia Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Argalia Baratheon|Argalia]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Argalia_Baratheon" title="Argalia Baratheon">Argalia</a>, to cement his rule over the Stormlands. He became the first Lord Paramount of the Stormlands.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"The Complete Guide to Westeros: House Baratheon\" featurette, Season 1 Blu Ray boxset<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:House Baratheon lineage.jpg|thumb|right|The lineage of Orys Baratheon]]","title":"House_Baratheon_lineage.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"House Baratheon lineage","align":"right","caption":"The lineage of Orys Baratheon","captionParsed":"The lineage of Orys Baratheon"}}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" alt="" src="http://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/a/ae/House_Baratheon_lineage.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20110615180104" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb alignRight withCaption" data-image-name="House Baratheon lineage.jpg" data-image-key="House_Baratheon_lineage.jpg"  type="image" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><i>The World of Ice and Fire</i> reveals that Orys Baratheon was Aegon's closest friend and ally. He served Aegon as the first-ever <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hand of the King","link":"Hand of the King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hand of the King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Hand_of_the_King" title="Hand of the King">Hand of the King</a>. Aegon offered Orys's hand in marriage to the daughter of the Storm King, Argilac Durrandon, but was rebuffed. Angered at the insult of being offered a bastard instead of a king, Argilac cut off the hands of Aegon's envoys.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Aegon landed his army at the mouth of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackwater Rush","link":"Blackwater Rush","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackwater Rush]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Blackwater_Rush" title="Blackwater Rush">Blackwater Rush</a>, he led a lightning campaign to secure the surrounding area, taking <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rosby","link":"Rosby","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rosby]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Rosby" title="Rosby">Rosby</a>, Stokeworth, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maidenpool","link":"Maidenpool","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maidenpool]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Maidenpool" title="Maidenpool">Maidenpool</a> in short order. Orys led the army while Aegon attacked from the skies.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After this victory, Aegon sent Orys and his sister-wife <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaenys","link":"Rhaenys Targaryen (sister of Aegon I)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaenys Targaryen (sister of Aegon I)|Rhaenys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Rhaenys_Targaryen_(sister_of_Aegon_I)" title="Rhaenys Targaryen (sister of Aegon I)">Rhaenys</a> to take Storm's End. Argilac left his castle to give battle in the open, attacking during a torrential thunderstorm to limit the use of Rhaenys's dragon, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meraxes","link":"Meraxes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meraxes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Meraxes" title="Meraxes">Meraxes</a>. However, Meraxes instead fought on foot and was still able to inflict carnage on Argilac's forces. Argilac himself was slain by Orys. Orys negotiated the surrender of Storm's End, with Argilac's daughter Argella delivered to him in chains and naked. Orys treated her kindly, later wedding her.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Orys continued to serve as Hand of the King for his nephew Aenys I, who succeeded Aegon I upon his death. Orys later died during one of the numerous local rebellions against Aenys I: many lords hoped that with Aegon the Conqueror dead and his weak son on the throne, they might be able to throw off Targaryen rule forever. These rebellions were crushed by Aegon's ruthless younger son, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maegor the Cruel","link":"Maegor Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maegor Targaryen|Maegor the Cruel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" href="/wiki/Maegor_Targaryen" title="Maegor Targaryen">Maegor the Cruel</a>, who succeeded Orys as Hand of the King.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/asoiaf.westeros.org\/index.php\/topic\/83614-bubonicon-45\/page__st__40 Bubonicaon 45 GRRM live-read]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-42092599156cca65ac89de" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The relationship between Aegon the Conqueror and Orys Baratheon may be loosely inspired by that of the real-world William the Conqueror of Normandy and Odo of Bayeux, who was the Norman conqueror's half-brother (Orys was rumored to be Aegon's half-brother) and vassal, and upon the conquest of England, was made the ruling earl of an important region of England and was his brother's regent and chancellor in that country.
</p>
