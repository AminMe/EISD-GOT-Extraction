<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is a member of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Thenn","link":"Thenns","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thenns|Thenn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Thenns" title="Thenns">Thenn</a>, a tribe of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Folk","link":"Free Folk","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Folk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Free_Folk" title="Free Folk">Free Folk</a> known for their practices of ritual self-scarification and cannibalism. He is also a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"warg","link":"warg","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[warg]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Warg" title="Warg">warg</a>, capable of entering the minds of animals to see through their eyes and control their actions. He is bound to an owl, allowing him to act as a scout for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Styr","link":"Styr","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Styr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Styr" title="Styr">Styr</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Magnar","link":"Magnar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Magnar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Magnar" title="Magnar">Magnar</a> of Thenn.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Having already scaled <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"raided","link":"Raiding","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raiding|raided]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Raiding" title="Raiding">raided</a> a village in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Gift","link":"the Gift","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Gift]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/The_Gift" title="The Gift">the Gift</a>, the Thenn warg locates <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tormund","link":"Tormund","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tormund]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Tormund" title="Tormund">Tormund</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ygritte","link":"Ygritte","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ygritte]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Ygritte" title="Ygritte">Ygritte</a>'s group through his owl and leads Styr and the rest of his warband to them.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Two Swords]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">On top of the Wall, the warg's owl spots the signal fire created by Mance Rayder. He tells Styr and Tormund that the time has come to attack <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-11974898856cca6666e7f0" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a>.
