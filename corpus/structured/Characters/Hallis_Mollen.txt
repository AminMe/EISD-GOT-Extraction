<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Cassel","link":"Rodrik Cassel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Cassel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Cassel" title="Rodrik Cassel">Rodrik Cassel</a> suggests to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> that if she is heading south to capture <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>, she should take "Hal" and a squad of guardsmen with her. Catelyn turns him down, pointing out that too large of a traveling party will attract unwanted attention. Instead, Catelyn only allows Rodrik himself to accompany her.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsroad]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Hallis Mollen is Jory Cassel's replacement as captain of the guards at Winterfell. He has a tendency to state the obvious.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, Hallis goes south with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>'s army and takes part in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a>. Hallis actually accompanies Catelyn south when she goes to treat with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a>. After Catelyn receives <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a>'s bones at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>, she orders Hallis to escort them back to Winterfell for proper burial in the crypts. In the TV series, Ned Stark's bones are delivered instead by Littlefinger, at Tyrion's request, at Renly's camp in the Stormlands. No mention is made of what happened to Ned Stark's bones after Catelyn flees the camp after Renly's death with only <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne of Tarth","link":"Brienne of Tarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne of Tarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-167105445756cca7e1d19bb" href="/wiki/Brienne_of_Tarth" title="Brienne of Tarth">Brienne of Tarth</a> (though in the TV version, she may have simply sent Hallis away from the camp with Ned's bones before Renly was killed).
</p>
