<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">She is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"slave","link":"slave","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[slave]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Slave" title="Slave" class="mw-redirect">slave</a> forced into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"prostitutution","link":"Prostitution","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Prostitution|prostitutution]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Prostitution" title="Prostitution">prostitutution</a> at a brothel on the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Long Bridge","link":"Long Bridge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Long Bridge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Long_Bridge" title="Long Bridge">Long Bridge</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Volantis","link":"Volantis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Volantis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Volantis" title="Volantis">Volantis</a>. Like all bedslaves in Volantis, she was branded with a slave tattoo shaped like a tear on her cheek. As word of Daenerys Targaryen's exploits spread, the prostitute started dressing like her to entice customers. Exposed buttocks aside, the costume is actually rather accurate, being the exact shade of blue and almost the same style that Daenerys favored during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Liberation of Slaver's Bay","link":"Liberation of Slaver's Bay","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Liberation of Slaver's Bay]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Liberation_of_Slaver's_Bay" title="Liberation of Slaver's Bay">Liberation of Slaver's Bay</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Samantha Bentley","link":"Samantha McEwan","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Samantha McEwan|Samantha Bentley]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Samantha_McEwan" title="Samantha McEwan">Samantha Bentley</a> played two other prostitutes in Season 4 and Season 5, but they are not the same characters. Indeed, within a single episode ("High Sparrow") she played both a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"whore","link":"Whore (The Stranger)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Whore (The Stranger)|whore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Whore_(The_Stranger)" title="Whore (The Stranger)">whore</a> dressed up as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stranger","link":"the Stranger","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stranger|the Stranger]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/The_Stranger" title="The Stranger">the Stranger</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, and hundreds of miles away, a whore dressed up as Daenerys Targaryen in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Volantis","link":"Volantis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Volantis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Volantis" title="Volantis">Volantis</a> - her face was obscured by her hair when she played the Stranger, so it was easy to have her appear twice without anyone noticing.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Costume designer <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Michele Clapton","link":"Michele Clapton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Michele Clapton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-50130614256cca6a71cdc4" href="/wiki/Michele_Clapton" title="Michele Clapton">Michele Clapton</a> explained how the idea to have a prostitute in a Volantis brothel cosplaying as Daenerys, wearing an assless-cutout imitation of Daenerys's costume:  she came up with the design as a joke, a good laugh was had by all, and then they decided it was so funny that they should put the joke costume into the actual TV show.  As Clapton said:
</p>
<dl data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><dd>"I wanted to shock [showrunners] David [Benioff] and Dan [Weiss], because they always ask me to do outrageous things. I just thought, 'Fine, I'm going to do a costume with no ass! Some people said, 'Well, how would they know what she looked like?' - She's this iconic woman so of course people talk and gossip and know what people look like!
