<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is a member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Thirteen","link":"the Thirteen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Thirteen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/The_Thirteen" title="The Thirteen">the Thirteen</a>, the rulers of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qarth","link":"Qarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Qarth" title="Qarth">Qarth</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He receives <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> outside the gates of the city with the rest of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Thirteen","link":"the Thirteen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Thirteen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/The_Thirteen" title="The Thirteen">the Thirteen</a>. They refuse to admit her without seeing her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"dragons","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a>. She is defiant and is eventually admitted under the protection of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Xaro Xhoan Daxos","link":"Xaro Xhoan Daxos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Xaro Xhoan Daxos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos" title="Xaro Xhoan Daxos">Xaro Xhoan Daxos</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He's present at a gathering of Qartheen dignataries attended by Daenerys, accompanied by her protectors, and Xaro Xhoan Daxos.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> He, along with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Spice King","link":"Spice King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Spice King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Spice_King" title="Spice King">Spice King</a> and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Copper King","link":"Copper King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Copper King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Copper_King" title="Copper King">Copper King</a>, turns Dany down when she requests boats. The Silk King will not help her because of his business with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a>, as he doesn't want to offend his best customers.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Old Gods and the New]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He's present at a session of the Thirteen in which Daenerys accuses the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Spice King","link":"Spice King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Spice King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Spice_King" title="Spice King">Spice King</a> of stealing her dragons. He's murdered by one of the doubles of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pyat Pree","link":"Pyat Pree","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pyat Pree]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Pyat_Pree" title="Pyat Pree">Pyat Pree</a> along with the rest of the Qartheen merchant lords after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"warlock","link":"warlock","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[warlock]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" href="/wiki/Warlock" title="Warlock" class="mw-redirect">warlock</a> reveals he stole the dragons and declares Xaro Xhoan Daxos King of Qarth.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Man Without Honor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158575998156cca71781150" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
