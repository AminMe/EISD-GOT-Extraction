</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Jafer Flowers</b> is a background character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first season","link":"Season 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1|first season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">first season</a>. He is played by an unidentified extra and only appears in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Pointy End","link":"The Pointy End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Pointy End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/The_Pointy_End" title="The Pointy End">The Pointy End</a>." He is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ranger","link":"ranger","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ranger]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Ranger" title="Ranger">ranger</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> and is killed while beyond <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>. His body is found and returned to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a>. Later, his body is burned alongside Othor's after a failed attack on the Lord Commander.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Jafer Flowers is a ranger in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. His name, Flowers, suggests that he was a bastard from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a> before joining the Watch. He and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Othor","link":"Othor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Othor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Othor" title="Othor">Othor</a> accompanied <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Benjen Stark","link":"Benjen Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Benjen Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Benjen_Stark" title="Benjen Stark">Benjen Stark</a>'s ranging mission beyond <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>. Both Jafer and Othor are killed while on this mission.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Jafer Flowers' wight attacked the main castle garrison. The wight killed five men, among them Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaremy Rykker","link":"Jaremy Rykker","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaremy Rykker]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Jaremy_Rykker" title="Jaremy Rykker">Jaremy Rykker</a>, and continued to attack even after having its head cut off - it was only subdued after being chopped to pieces by a dozen swordsmen.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">His name indicates that he was originally born as a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bastard","link":"Bastardy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bastardy|bastard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Bastardy" title="Bastardy">bastard</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Unlike in the series, when the bodies of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Othor","link":"Othor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Othor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Othor" title="Othor">Othor</a> and Jafer were brought to Castle Black, both corpses had blue eyes - different from the eye color they had in their lives.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Jafer's severed hand, which was torn by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ghost","link":"Ghost","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ghost]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Ghost" title="Ghost">Ghost</a>, continued to twitch and stir long after Jafer was destroyed. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Alliser Thorne","link":"Alliser Thorne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Alliser Thorne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/Alliser_Thorne" title="Alliser Thorne">Alliser Thorne</a> took it to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-202977810156cca6a200081" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, but it had rotted away while he was waiting for an audience.
</p>
