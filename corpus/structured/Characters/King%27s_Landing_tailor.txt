The King's Landing tailor is a minor characters in the third season. He is played by guest star Joe Cassidy and only appears in Dark Wings, Dark Words. Biography 

 Background 

 He is a royal tailor in King's Landing. Season 3 

King Joffrey is being fitted in his chambers for his upcoming wedding to Margaery Tyrell. The tailor fits Joffrey in his new clothes, but Joffrey finds the flower embroidery insulting and tells him off.
