<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1"> Background </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16694918056cca785a1a31" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>" </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Bezzaq is a slave master who was captured by Asher and Beskha in order to sell him. To Asher and Beskha's surprise, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lost Legion","link":"Lost Legion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lost Legion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16694918056cca785a1a31" href="/wiki/Lost_Legion" title="Lost Legion">Lost Legion</a> enter the tavern instead of the "freed slaves" who supposedly put the bounty on Bezzaq.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Asher and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tazal","link":"Tazal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tazal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16694918056cca785a1a31" href="/wiki/Tazal" title="Tazal">Tazal</a> get into an argument about the payment because they should've received eight hundred dragons instead of four. In continuation, Tazal slits Bezzaq's throat and reveals that he intends to go to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wise Masters","link":"Wise Masters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wise Masters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-16694918056cca785a1a31" href="/wiki/Wise_Masters" title="Wise Masters">Wise Masters</a> and tell that Asher kidnapped Bezzaq and killed him because he wouldn't take less than eight hundred gold dragons and killed him, but Legion were there to enact swift justice.
