<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Mord is the gaoler in charge of the Sky Cells in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Eyrie","link":"the Eyrie","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Eyrie]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-165621264956cca6817ab27" href="/wiki/The_Eyrie" title="The Eyrie">the Eyrie</a>. He is in the service of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Arryn","link":"House Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-165621264956cca6817ab27" href="/wiki/House_Arryn" title="House Arryn">House Arryn</a>. He is slow witted and follows the orders of his "betters."  He is not so slow that he does not recognize opportunity when it is presented and he is the only reason Tyrion was able to make his play for freedom.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Actor Peter Dinklage was not wearing padding, and the scene where Mord beats him with a truncheon, even though it was a prop one, left him with bruises.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-165621264956cca6817ab27" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Mord is a petty, dimwitted, and cruel man who delights in tormenting prisoners and telling them how they are going to fall out of the cells to their deaths on the rocks six hundred feet below the castle. He took <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister|Tyrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-165621264956cca6817ab27" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion</a>'s coat when he first got there and the nights are freezing.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Mord is described as very unattractive person: he has small dark eyes and rotting brown teeth. The left side of his face is scarred where an axe cut off his ear and part of his cheek. He has a large belly and thick, stubby-fingered hands.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Petyr Baelish","link":"Petyr Baelish","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Petyr Baelish]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-165621264956cca6817ab27" href="/wiki/Petyr_Baelish" title="Petyr Baelish">Petyr Baelish</a> kills <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lysa Arryn","link":"Lysa Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lysa Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-165621264956cca6817ab27" href="/wiki/Lysa_Arryn" title="Lysa Arryn">Lysa Arryn</a> and puts the blame on <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Marillion","link":"Marillion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Marillion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-165621264956cca6817ab27" href="/wiki/Marillion" title="Marillion">Marillion</a>, Mord tortures Marillion by plucking his eyes and cutting several of his fingers, till he breaks down and confesses falsely that he killed Lysa. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark|Sansa]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-165621264956cca6817ab27" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa</a> looks at Mord, she notices that he has gold teeth.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"Mord" means "murder" in Norwegian, Danish, German, Swedish and Polish, though it is uncertain if George R. R. Martin had any knowledge of this when he created the name.
</p>
