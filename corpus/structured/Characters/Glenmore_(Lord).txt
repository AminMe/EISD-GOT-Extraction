<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lord Glenmore commands a moderately sized army, and is described by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lady Elissa Forrester","link":"Elissa Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elissa Forrester|Lady Elissa Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Elissa_Forrester" title="Elissa Forrester">Lady Elissa Forrester</a> as being "no coward". His daughter, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Elaena","link":"Elaena Glenmore","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elaena Glenmore|Elaena]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Elaena_Glenmore" title="Elaena Glenmore">Elaena</a>, had been betrothed to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik Forrester","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik Forrester</a> before the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a> broke out, the reason for the two not already being married. He is protected by the Glenmore Elite Guard, a force of twenty men, led by his son, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arthur Glenmore","link":"Arthur Glenmore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arthur Glenmore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Arthur_Glenmore" title="Arthur Glenmore">Arthur Glenmore</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">After Elaena had received word that Rodrik survived the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>, she quickly set out for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>. However, Lord Glenmore advised her to reconsider the betrothal in light of recent events. Depending on player choice, Rodrik can potentially offer half of the Forresters' ironwood to Lord Glenmore so that his worries may be assuaged.
</p>
<h3>"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sons of Winter","link":"Sons of Winter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sons of Winter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Sons_of_Winter" title="Sons of Winter">Sons of Winter</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Elaena and her brother Arthur Glenmore visit Ironrath, on the grounds to help Rodrik take down <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gryff Whitehill","link":"Gryff Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gryff Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Gryff_Whitehill" title="Gryff Whitehill">Gryff Whitehill</a>, because Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ludd Whitehill","link":"Ludd Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ludd Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Ludd_Whitehill" title="Ludd Whitehill">Ludd Whitehill</a> paid Lord Glenmore a visit at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rillwater Crossing","link":"Rillwater Crossing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rillwater Crossing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/Rillwater_Crossing" title="Rillwater Crossing">Rillwater Crossing</a> and threatened him and his house with destruction along with the Forresters if he didn't accept a marriage between Gryff and Elaena. Lord Glenmore accepted the deal. He also believes his elite guard is on a ship bound for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-150091688456cca71564371" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a>, when they are actually with Arthur and Elaena in Ironrath.
</p>
