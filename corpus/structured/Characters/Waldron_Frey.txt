<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196469544156cca7079d2f7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Arya is betrothed to Elmar Frey. The character has been renamed for the television series. Elmar is the twenty-second son of Lord Walder Frey and the fourth born to Lord Walder's seventh wife, Annara Farring. Elmar is the older brother of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shirei Frey","link":"Shirei Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shirei Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196469544156cca7079d2f7" href="/wiki/Shirei_Frey" title="Shirei Frey">Shirei Frey</a>, the youngest child that Lord Walder had with Annara Farring. He is nine years old.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Elmar encounters Arya several times in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196469544156cca7079d2f7" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a> and tries to boss her around, while mentioning proudly that he is trueborn son of lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196469544156cca7079d2f7" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a> and is going to marry a princess - both of them are unaware that Arya is that princess. Arya is not impressed and does not comply to him. After being informed that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196469544156cca7079d2f7" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> breached the pact with the Freys, Elmar tells Arya whiningly that his father told him he had to marry someone else, or to become a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"septon","link":"septon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[septon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-196469544156cca7079d2f7" href="/wiki/Septon" title="Septon">septon</a>. Arya has no sympathy to him.
</p>
