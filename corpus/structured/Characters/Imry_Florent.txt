<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Imry Florent is a member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Florent","link":"House Florent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Florent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/House_Florent" title="House Florent">House Florent</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brightwater Keep","link":"Brightwater Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brightwater Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/Brightwater_Keep" title="Brightwater Keep">Brightwater Keep</a>, a noble house sworn to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Reach","link":"the Reach","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Reach]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/The_Reach" title="The Reach">the Reach</a>. However, due to his sister Selyse's marriage to Stannis, the Florents declare for Stannis in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Imry accompanies Stannis aboard the King's flagship, the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Fury","link":"Fury","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fury]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/Fury" title="Fury">Fury</a></i>. After much of Stannis' fleet is destroyed with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wildfire","link":"wildfire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wildfire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/Wildfire" title="Wildfire">wildfire</a>, Stannis orders his forces to disembark. Ser Imry warns Stannis that hundreds will die trying to reach the shore. The King replies that it will actually be thousands, but is the first to climb down to a ferry boat.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Blackwater (episode)|Blackwater]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">As the King's flagship was not engulfed in wildfire during the battle, Ser Imry likely survived the ensuing conflict and retreated upon the defeat of Stannis' army.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Imry Florent, like the rest of House Florent, declares for Stannis after the death of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>. He's given command of Stannis' fleet as Lord High Captain and leads it blindly into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackwater Rush","link":"Blackwater Rush","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackwater Rush]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/Blackwater_Rush" title="Blackwater Rush">Blackwater Rush</a> instead of sending scouts like Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Davos","link":"Davos Seaworth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Davos Seaworth|Davos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-145291211256cca69ba69e6" href="/wiki/Davos_Seaworth" title="Davos Seaworth">Davos</a> advised. He was killed in the battle when the flagship <i>Fury</i> is engulfed in wildfire.
</p>
