<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Stiv is a former member of the Night's Watch, who abandoned the Wall to live amongst the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Folk","link":"Free Folk","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Folk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Free_Folk" title="Free Folk">Free Folk</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Stiv, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Osha","link":"Osha","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Osha]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Osha" title="Osha">Osha</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wallen","link":"Wallen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wallen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Wallen" title="Wallen">Wallen</a> flee south of the Wall, apparently in fear of the return of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Walkers","link":"White Walkers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walkers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/White_Walkers" title="White Walkers">White Walkers</a>. In the woods near <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a> they stumble across <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a> and decide to steal his horse. Realizing his importance, they ponder taking him prisoner and returning him to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mance Rayder","link":"Mance Rayder","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mance Rayder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Mance_Rayder" title="Mance Rayder">Mance Rayder</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King-Beyond-the-Wall","link":"King-Beyond-the-Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King-Beyond-the-Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/King-Beyond-the-Wall" title="King-Beyond-the-Wall">King-Beyond-the-Wall</a>, for a reward, but decide against it. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> intervenes, and kills Wallen and disables Osha.Â Stiv holds a knife to Bran's throat, and despite Bran's protests not to, Robb lowers his sword as Stiv tells him to. However, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a> shoots him through the back with an arrow, killing him. Osha surrenders to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark|Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">Stark</a> custody.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Golden Crown]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-131981418156cca6d6cf3b3" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
