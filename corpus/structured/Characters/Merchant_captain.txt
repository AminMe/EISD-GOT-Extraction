<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While sailing to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oldtown","link":"Oldtown","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oldtown]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-183207791556cca765b579f" href="/wiki/Oldtown" title="Oldtown">Oldtown</a>, the captain is bribed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-183207791556cca765b579f" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> to smuggle him and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-183207791556cca765b579f" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a> into Dorne and keep the secret. However, he tried to sell this information to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Obara Sand","link":"Obara Sand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Obara Sand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-183207791556cca765b579f" href="/wiki/Obara_Sand" title="Obara Sand">Obara Sand</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Planky Town","link":"Planky Town","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Planky Town]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-183207791556cca765b579f" href="/wiki/Planky_Town" title="Planky Town">Planky Town</a>, she was insulted that he did not offer up such a vital warning for free, so she instead tortured it out of him.  The <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sand Snakes","link":"Sand Snakes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sand Snakes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-183207791556cca765b579f" href="/wiki/Sand_Snakes" title="Sand Snakes">Sand Snakes</a> end up burying him up to his neck in the sand, gagged, and covered by a bucket filled with scorpions. Later, Obara kills the captain by throwing her spear, impaling him through the head.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Sons of the Harpy (episode)|Sons of the Harpy]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-183207791556cca765b579f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
