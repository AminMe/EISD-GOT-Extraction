<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Waymar Royce was a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"Knighthood","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Knighthood|knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Knighthood" title="Knighthood">knight</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Royce","link":"House Royce","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/House_Royce" title="House Royce">House Royce</a>, an ancient and proud noble family of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>, and the youngest son of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yohn Royce","link":"Yohn Royce","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yohn Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Yohn_Royce" title="Yohn Royce">Yohn Royce</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord","link":"Lord of Runestone","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Runestone|Lord]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Runestone" title="Lord of Runestone">Lord</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Runestone","link":"Runestone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Runestone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Runestone" title="Runestone">Runestone</a> and the head of the family. He joined the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> and was placed amongst the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ranger","link":"Ranger","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ranger]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Ranger" title="Ranger">Rangers</a> due to his noble birth and skill at arms.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, House Royce of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Runestone","link":"Runestone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Runestone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Runestone" title="Runestone">Runestone</a> is distantly related to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a>. As is the third and youngest son of "Bronze" Yohn Royce, and therefore having little chance of inheriting lands or wealth, he joined the Night's Watch. Waymar was fast-tracked to being an officer because of his noble blood. Eighteen-year-old Waymar is a skilled warrior, trained by his castle's master-at-arms, but is untested in battle. He is arrogant and has a tendency to underrate the experience of other men due to their lower birth, even though they have been serving on the Wall for longer than he has, but he is not completely stupid.  For example, he realized that if the Wall was "weeping" recently (partially melting), it could not be cold enough for eight wildlings with warm clothes, shelter, and material for a fire to simply freeze to death.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The young Sansa (who was less than 11 years old at the time) developed a massive romantic crush on the handsome young man in the brief time he and his father stayed at Winterfell before he departed to the Wall.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Despite his youthful arrogance and untested nature, when the White Walkers appear he bravely attempts to fight one in single combat.  However, his sword shatters after only a few clashes with the White Walker's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ice blade","link":"White Walker ice blade","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walker ice blade|ice blade]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/White_Walker_ice_blade" title="White Walker ice blade">ice blade</a> due to its extreme cold.  One of the metal shards pierced his left eye, and he was defeated and killed.  <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Will","link":"Will","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Will]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Will" title="Will">Will</a> had been hiding in a nearby tree and witnessed the clash.  After waiting for some time he hoped the White Walkers had left and climbed down:  after examining Waymar's corpse, he went to retrieve his shattered sword-hilt so he could take it back to Castle Black as evidence of what happened.  When Will turned to rise again, Waymar stood over him - now revived as a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wight","link":"Wights","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wights|wight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Wights" title="Wights">wight</a>, his remaining right eye glowing blue.  The wight-Waymar then wrapped its hands around Will's throat and strangled him to death.  The TV version changed this around so that Will, not <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gared","link":"Gared","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gared]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-205770004856cca612ab7f0" href="/wiki/Gared" title="Gared">Gared</a>, is the survivor of Waymar's party who escapes but only to be later beheaded by Ned Stark for desertion:  Waymar is never shown being revived as a wight, and instead Gared is simply beheaded by another White Walker.
</p>
