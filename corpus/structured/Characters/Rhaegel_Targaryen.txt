He was the brother of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Baelor \"Breakspear\"","link":"Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)|Baelor \"Breakspear\"]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/Baelor_Targaryen_(son_of_Daeron_II)" title="Baelor Targaryen (son of Daeron II)">Baelor "Breakspear"</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys","link":"Aerys I Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys I Targaryen|Aerys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/Aerys_I_Targaryen" title="Aerys I Targaryen">Aerys</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maekar","link":"Maekar Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maekar Targaryen|Maekar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/Maekar_Targaryen" title="Maekar Targaryen">Maekar</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aelinor","link":"Aelinor Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aelinor Targaryen|Aelinor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/Aelinor_Targaryen" title="Aelinor Targaryen">Aelinor</a>.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Prince Rhaegel was the third son of King Daeron the Good and Queen Myriah. He was sickly, meek, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"insane","link":"Targaryen madness","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Targaryen madness|insane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/Targaryen_madness" title="Targaryen madness">insane</a>. He was known for dancing naked through the halls of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a>. He was married to Lady Alys Arryn and had three children, twins Aelor and Aelora, and daughter Daenora.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He was named <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Prince of Dragonstone","link":"Prince of Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Prince of Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/Prince_of_Dragonstone" title="Prince of Dragonstone">Prince of Dragonstone</a> when his brother Aerys I ascended the Iron Throne since Aerys had no children of his own. He later died when he choked on a lamprey pie. His son Aelor became the new Prince of Dragonstone, but he himself died in a mishap at the hands of his sister-wife Aelora. This left her made with grief and she later took her own life after being assaulted at a masked ball. Daenora was married to her cousin Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerion Targaryen","link":"Aerion Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerion Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-212918487256cca736bbd97" href="/wiki/Aerion_Targaryen" title="Aerion Targaryen">Aerion Targaryen</a> and had a son named Maegor.
</p>
