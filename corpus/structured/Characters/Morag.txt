<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Months after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"mutiny","link":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mutiny at Craster's Keep|mutiny]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep" title="Mutiny at Craster's Keep">mutiny</a>, in which a group of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"oathbreaking brothers","link":"Betrayers","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Betrayers|oathbreaking brothers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Betrayers" title="Betrayers" class="mw-redirect">oathbreaking brothers</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> seize <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's Keep","link":"Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Craster's_Keep" title="Craster's Keep">Craster's Keep</a>, Morag delivers <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's last son","link":"Craster's last son","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's last son]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Craster's_last_son" title="Craster's last son">Craster's last son</a> to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Karl Tanner","link":"Karl Tanner","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Karl Tanner]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Karl_Tanner" title="Karl Tanner">Karl Tanner</a>, leader of the mutineers, and tells him he's intended to be given to "the gods". Understanding she's referring to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Walkers","link":"White Walkers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walkers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/White_Walkers" title="White Walkers">White Walkers</a>, Karl orders the baby to be abandoned at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Haunted Forest","link":"Haunted Forest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Haunted Forest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Haunted_Forest" title="Haunted Forest">Haunted Forest</a> to be seized by the creatures.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Oathkeeper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After a group of brothers of the Night's Watch <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"raid","link":"Raid on Craster's Keep","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raid on Craster's Keep|raid]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Raid_on_Craster's_Keep" title="Raid on Craster's Keep">raid</a> Craster's Keep and kill all the mutineers, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a> offers Craster's wives protection at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a>. Morag refuses, citing how the 'crows' led by Karl have treated them just like Craster did. Jon asks if they want to remain in Craster's Keep, causing Morag to spit on the ground and demand the place to be burned. Morag and the rest choose to find their own way.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[First of His Name]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels there is no mention of a wife of Craster by the name of Morag. The fate of Craster's wives after the mutineers abandon Craster's Keep hasn't been mentioned yet.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Although her backstory isn't detailed on-screen, writer <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bryan Cogman","link":"Bryan Cogman","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bryan Cogman]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" href="/wiki/Bryan_Cogman" title="Bryan Cogman">Bryan Cogman</a> explained that he had specific ideas for the character:
</p>
<dl data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><dd>"The original idea behind Morag was that she was the first of the daughters who became Craster's wife. So she's the prime wife, if you will, and the matriarchal figure for these women. She knows she can't fight these mutineers and knows that without them, the White Walkers could destroy the wives. So there's a lot going on."<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/www.makinggameofthrones.com\/production-diary\/2014\/4\/28\/interview-with-bryan-cogman MakingGameOfThrones.com]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-69615702456cca6d1c4307" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
