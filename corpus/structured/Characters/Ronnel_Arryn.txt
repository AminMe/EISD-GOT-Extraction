<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109912131656cca720c3970" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ronnel's family was unchanged. Ronnel died around the same time his son Elbert was born, however, Elbert himself died when he was executed by the Mad King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerys II Targaryen","link":"Aerys II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerys II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109912131656cca720c3970" href="/wiki/Aerys_II_Targaryen" title="Aerys II Targaryen">Aerys II Targaryen</a> alongside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickard","link":"Rickard Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickard Stark|Rickard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109912131656cca720c3970" href="/wiki/Rickard_Stark" title="Rickard Stark">Rickard</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brandon Stark","link":"Brandon Stark (Son of Rickard)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brandon Stark (Son of Rickard)|Brandon Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109912131656cca720c3970" href="/wiki/Brandon_Stark_(Son_of_Rickard)" title="Brandon Stark (Son of Rickard)">Brandon Stark</a> at the beginning of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-109912131656cca720c3970" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, extinguishing Ronnel's line.
