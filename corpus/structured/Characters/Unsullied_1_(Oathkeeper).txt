An Unsullied is an unnamed minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Karl Jackson and debuts in Oathkeeper. Biography 

 Background 

He's an Unsullied, enslaved and castrated as a child and submitted to a cruel training by the Good Masters of Astapor. He was made a free man after Daenerys Targaryen bought 8,000 Unsullied and had them sack Astapor. Like the rest of his fellow Unsullied, he chose to fight for her, participating in the liberation of the entire slave population of Yunkai. Season 4 

He is one of the Unsullied who dress themselves up as slaves and infiltrate into Meereen under Grey Worm's lead during the Siege of Meereen.
