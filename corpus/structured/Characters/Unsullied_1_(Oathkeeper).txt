<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He's an <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Unsullied","link":"Unsullied","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Unsullied]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Unsullied" title="Unsullied">Unsullied</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"enslaved","link":"slavery","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[slavery|enslaved]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">enslaved</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"castrated","link":"eunuch","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[eunuch|castrated]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Eunuch" title="Eunuch" class="mw-redirect">castrated</a> as a child and submitted to a cruel training by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Good Masters","link":"Good Masters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Good Masters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Good_Masters" title="Good Masters">Good Masters</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Astapor","link":"Astapor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Astapor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Astapor" title="Astapor">Astapor</a>. He was made a free man after <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> bought 8,000 Unsullied and had them <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sack","link":"Sack of Astapor","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of Astapor|sack]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Sack_of_Astapor" title="Sack of Astapor">sack</a> Astapor. Like the rest of his fellow Unsullied, he chose to fight for her, participating in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"liberation","link":"Battle of Yunkai","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Yunkai|liberation]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Yunkai" title="Battle of Yunkai">liberation</a> of the entire slave population of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yunkai","link":"Yunkai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yunkai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-153488842356cca7ccf274d" href="/wiki/Yunkai" title="Yunkai">Yunkai</a>.
</p>
