<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Duncan was the eldest son and heir of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon V Targaryen","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Aegon V Targaryen</a>. He was named in honor of his father's great friend Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a>, who had also become <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a> of Aegon V's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a>. He died alongside his father and his namesake in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tragedy at Summerhall","link":"Tragedy at Summerhall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tragedy at Summerhall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Tragedy_at_Summerhall" title="Tragedy at Summerhall">Tragedy at Summerhall</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Oathkeeper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Duncan was the eldest son of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon V Targaryen","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Aegon V Targaryen</a>, named after his close friend Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a>. He once defeated the ten-year-old <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a> in a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tourney","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|tourney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">tourney</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackhaven","link":"Blackhaven","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackhaven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/Blackhaven" title="Blackhaven">Blackhaven</a>. Prince Duncan took pity on the small knight and jousted against him when no one else would, giving Barristan the nickname "the Bold". Years later he was defeated by Ser Barristan at a winter tourney in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-67197887756cca6629f868" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Prince Duncan fell in love with a woman known as Jenny of Oldstones and loved her so much he gave up his crown for her and married her against his father's wishes. His love for her inspired a song. He died in the Tragedy at Summerhall alongside his father and his namesake.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Due to the simplification of the Targaryen lineage by removing Jaehaerys II from the line of kings and making Aerys and Rhaella the children of Aegon V rather than his grandchildren, Duncan is Aerys and Rhaella's brother in the TV series though he was their uncle in the books.
</p>
