<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Wallen is a former member of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209167073856cca77358ded" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>, who abandoned the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wall","link":"Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209167073856cca77358ded" href="/wiki/Wall" title="Wall" class="mw-redirect">Wall</a> to live amongst the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wildlings","link":"wildlings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wildlings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209167073856cca77358ded" href="/wiki/Wildlings" title="Wildlings" class="mw-redirect">wildlings</a>.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stiv","link":"Stiv","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stiv]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209167073856cca77358ded" href="/wiki/Stiv" title="Stiv">Stiv</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Osha","link":"Osha","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Osha]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209167073856cca77358ded" href="/wiki/Osha" title="Osha">Osha</a> and Wallen flee south of the Wall, apparently in fear of the return of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Walkers","link":"White Walkers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walkers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-209167073856cca77358ded" href="/wiki/White_Walkers" title="White Walkers">White Walkers</a>.
