<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Frey soldier partook in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>. <img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Rains of Castamere (episode)|The Rains of Castamere]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The soldier is first seen at a campfire with fellow Frey soldiers, mocking the death of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> and spreading stories on how he attached <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Wind","link":"Grey Wind","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Wind]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Grey_Wind" title="Grey Wind">Grey Wind</a>'s head to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>'s body. While <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"one of the other men","link":"Frey soldier 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Frey soldier 2|one of the other men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Frey_soldier_2" title="Frey soldier 2">one of the other men</a> doesn't immediately believe this, saying that many of the Frey men would claim to have done the act, the soldier insists that with help from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Malcolm","link":"Malcolm","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Malcolm]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Malcolm" title="Malcolm">Malcolm</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Talbot","link":"Talbot","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Talbot]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Talbot" title="Talbot">Talbot</a>, he sewed Grey Wind's head onto Robb's body, mentioning how difficult it was to do so.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a>, who passes the Freys along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sandor Clegane","link":"Sandor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sandor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Sandor_Clegane" title="Sandor Clegane">Sandor Clegane</a>, jumps off the horse and walks up to the Freys, engaging in a conversation. She asks for a meal, whereas one of the soldiers simply orders her to "fuck off". Arya states that she is hungry, and the Frey soldier asks her what part of "fuck off" she doesn't understand. Arya claims to have money, showing the coin of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a> that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a> gave her. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Frey soldier seems interested, reaching his hand out for the coin. Arya, however, drops the latter by 'accident' and apologizes. The soldier calls her a "little shit" and leans down to retrieve the coin. Before even grasping it, Arya pulls the man off of the wooden log and repeatedly stabs him in the neck. As the other Frey soldiers join the battle, Clegane intervenes and protects the Stark girl, eliminating each and every one of them as the stabbed Frey soldier bleeds profusely.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After the battle is over, Sandor walks over to Arya, who is coldly staring down at the corpse of the Frey soldier she stabbed. He asks her if this was the first man she'd ever killed. After she confirms that it was the "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first <i>man<\/i>","link":"Stableboy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stableboy|first ''man'']]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Stableboy" title="Stableboy">first <i>man</i></a>", he asks where she retrieved the knife. She reveals that she pocketed it off of Clegane himself. Mildly annoyed, he grabs his knife back from her, and declares that the next time she plans on doing something like this, she should <i>tell</i> him first. She retrieves Jaqen's coin and mumbles the words <i>Valar Morghulis</i>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Mhysa]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the whole scene does not occur in the books. However, the trick with the coin Arya uses on the soldier is based on the way she tricks and kills a guard when she flees <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a> after the castle is seize by the Northern army led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Roose Bolton","link":"Roose Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Roose Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/Roose_Bolton" title="Roose Bolton">Roose Bolton</a>. Also, Arya stabs <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Tickler","link":"the Tickler","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Tickler]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203571193856cca7a6a3228" href="/wiki/The_Tickler" title="The Tickler">the Tickler</a> multiple times, but that happens later in the book.
</p>
