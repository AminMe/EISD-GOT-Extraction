<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lollys Stokeworth is a member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stokeworth","link":"House Stokeworth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stokeworth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/House_Stokeworth" title="House Stokeworth">House Stokeworth</a>, a noble family of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Crownlands","link":"the Crownlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Crownlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/The_Crownlands" title="The Crownlands">the Crownlands</a>, and the second daughter of Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tanda Stokeworth.","link":"Tanda Stokeworth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tanda Stokeworth|Tanda Stokeworth.]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Tanda_Stokeworth" title="Tanda Stokeworth">Tanda Stokeworth.</a> She is considered dim-witted by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> arranges for a marriage between Lollys and Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bronn","link":"Bronn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bronn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Bronn" title="Bronn">Bronn</a> of the Blackwater, to sway the ambitious ex-<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sellsword","link":"Mercenary","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mercenary|sellsword]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Mercenary" title="Mercenary">sellsword</a> from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>'s service. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, her background remains the same. However, she is a fairly regular background character during the course of the first three books. She is morbidly obese and is considered <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"simple","link":"lackwit","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[lackwit|simple]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Lackwit" title="Lackwit">simple</a>. In addition, she is second in line for the inheritance of House Stokeworth's lands and titles, so there are no suitors for her hand; her mother, Lady Tanda, is desperate to find a match for her, even relentlessly pursuing Tyrion for a time.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lollys is a victim in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riot of King's Landing","link":"Riot of King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riot of King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Riot_of_King's_Landing" title="Riot of King's Landing">Riot of King's Landing</a>, where she is ambushed and gang-raped by fifty men in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Flea Bottom","link":"Flea Bottom","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Flea Bottom]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Flea_Bottom" title="Flea Bottom">Flea Bottom</a>, which results in her pregnancy with a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bastard","link":"bastard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bastard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Bastard" title="Bastard" class="mw-redirect">bastard</a> child. After this, she is considered defiled and is left a social pariah at court. This makes her an ideal match for Bronn, who as an "upjumped cutthroat" is also considered too low for suitable noble matches, yet now their circumstances have met in the middle.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shae","link":"Shae","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shae]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Shae" title="Shae">Shae</a>, while acting as secret mistress to Tyrion during his stint as Hand of the King, served a time as hand-maiden to Lollys, before coming into <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a>'s service following her marriage to Tyrion. She tells Tyrion discontentedly that Lollys sleeps and eats all day, and sometimes she falls asleep while she’s eating, then the food falls under the blankets and Shae has to clean her. Shae knows that Lollys was gang-raped, but has no sympathy for her.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>, Lollys stays with her mother and sister in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a>. They are invited by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> to seek refuge with her in the Queen's Ballroom at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maegor's Holdfast","link":"Maegor's Holdfast","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maegor's Holdfast]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Maegor's_Holdfast" title="Maegor's Holdfast">Maegor's Holdfast</a>. When they are about to cross the drawbridge, Lollys becomes frightened and refuses to cross, clutches at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shae","link":"Shae","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shae]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-26222233056cca609ac9a8" href="/wiki/Shae" title="Shae">Shae</a> and sobbing, "I don't want to, I don't want to, I don't want to." Sansa notices that Shae looks as if she wants to shove Lollys into the dry moat, onto the iron spikes. Sansa speaks softly to Lollys and tries to calm her, but to no avail. Finally, Shae and Falyse grab Lollys by her elbows, and drag her across the bridge. Sansa feels sorry for Lollys, knowing about her rape and pregnancy.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lollys's presence at the Riot of King's Landing, her rape in the riot, and her subsequent pregnancy, were all omitted from the TV series continuity. She is furthermore portrayed as being in better physical and mental condition, although she is implied to be a bit dim.
</p>
