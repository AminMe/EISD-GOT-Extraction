<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-184452776356cca63ccdc69" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Matthos is the third of Davos' seven sons, and serves his father proudly on the ship, the <i>Black Betha</i>. Four of Davos' sons serve in Davos army - Dale, Allard, Matthos, and Marc - and all four are killed in the Battle of the Blackwater. In the TV series, these four sons have been functionally condensed into Matthos. In <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Histories & Lore","link":"Histories & Lore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Histories & Lore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-184452776356cca63ccdc69" href="/wiki/Histories_&_Lore" title="Histories &amp; Lore">Histories &amp; Lore</a>: <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion (Histories & Lore)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion (Histories & Lore)|Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-184452776356cca63ccdc69" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion_(Histories_&_Lore)" title="Robert's Rebellion (Histories &amp; Lore)">Robert's Rebellion</a>, Davos states that Matthos is his only son, which indicates that his three youngest sons from the book - Devan, Stannis and Steffon - do not exist in the TV series.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"Matthos" is pronounced "Math-os", not "Mat-ohs".
