<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Paxter Redwyne is the Lord of the Arbor and the head of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Redwyne","link":"House Redwyne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Redwyne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/House_Redwyne" title="House Redwyne">House Redwyne</a>, a vassal family of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Highgarden","link":"Highgarden","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Highgarden]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/Highgarden" title="Highgarden">Highgarden</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, no mention is made of his sexual preferences – if this was indeed the "Lord Redwyne" that Lord Varys was referring to, and not some distant cousin.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Paxter Redwyne commands one of the largest fleets in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a> which anchors at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Arbor","link":"the Arbor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Arbor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/The_Arbor" title="The Arbor">the Arbor</a>. During <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>, he participated in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Siege of Storm's End","link":"Siege of Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Storm's_End" title="Siege of Storm's End">Siege of Storm's End</a>; his fleet prevented the castle from being resupplied by sea. It is mentioned in passing that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> still bears a grudge against Lord Redwyne for this.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He is the first cousin and oldest friend of his liege lord, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mace Tyrell","link":"Mace Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mace Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/Mace_Tyrell" title="Mace Tyrell">Mace Tyrell</a>. He married Lord Mace's elder sister, Lady Mina of House Tyrell, and they have two sons, Ser Horas and Ser Hobber, and one daughter, Desmera.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Redwyne is described as "stoop-shouldered and thin, his bald head fringed by tufts of orange hair."
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Redwyne doesn't side with Renly Baratheon in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>, as Cersei held his twin sons hostage at the Red Keep. Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-161847103456cca72b6b1c7" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>, he is made an advisor on the Small Council alongside his liege Lord Mace Tyrell and Lord Mathis Rowan. At the breakfast prior to King Joffrey's wedding ceremony, he unveils a scale model of a war galley with 200 oars being built in the shipyards of the Arbor, which will be named <i>King Joffrey's<b> </b>Valor</i>, though it's currently unknown if the name was changed following Joffrey's death.
</p>
