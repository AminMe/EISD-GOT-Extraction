Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Barristan Selmy","link":"Barristan Selmy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Barristan Selmy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-198376318456cca6a577c28" href="/wiki/Barristan_Selmy" title="Barristan Selmy">Barristan Selmy</a> advises her to exercise caution and give him a fair trial as a way of maintaining the equality among the citizens, to which she agrees. Before the trial can take place, Mossador enters the Son's cell and murders him. Mossador dumps his corpse in public, his face covered by his harpy mask and writes "kill the masters" on a wall in his blood.
