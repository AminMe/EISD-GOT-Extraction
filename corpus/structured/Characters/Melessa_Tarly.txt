<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lady Melessa of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Florent","link":"House Florent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Florent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/House_Florent" title="House Florent">House Florent</a> is the wife Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Randyll Tarly","link":"Randyll Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Randyll Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Randyll_Tarly" title="Randyll Tarly">Randyll Tarly</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Horn Hill","link":"Horn Hill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Horn Hill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Horn_Hill" title="Horn Hill">Horn Hill</a> and mother of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Samwell Tarly","link":"Samwell Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Samwell Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Samwell_Tarly" title="Samwell Tarly">Samwell Tarly</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. She is a cousin of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Selyse Florent","link":"Selyse Florent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Selyse Florent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Selyse_Florent" title="Selyse Florent" class="mw-redirect">Selyse Florent</a>, the wife of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a>, and Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Imry Florent","link":"Imry Florent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Imry Florent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Imry_Florent" title="Imry Florent">Imry Florent</a>.

<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Before leaving <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's Keep","link":"Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Craster's_Keep" title="Craster's Keep">Craster's Keep</a>, Samwell Tarly gives <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gilly","link":"Gilly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gilly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Gilly" title="Gilly">Gilly</a> a thimble that once belonged to Lady Melessa. He explains that he used to read to her while she was sewing but that his father ended the practice when he found out. He reveals that the thimble is the only possession he has from her and that it was a gift given when he left for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>.

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Melessa Florent is a daughter of Lord Alester Florent, head of House Florent and Lord of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brightwater Keep","link":"Brightwater Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brightwater Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Brightwater_Keep" title="Brightwater Keep">Brightwater Keep</a>, and Lady Melara Crane. She's the mother of Samwell and Dickon Tarly.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Melessa is a first cousin of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Selyse Baratheon","link":"Selyse Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Selyse Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Selyse_Baratheon" title="Selyse Baratheon">Selyse Baratheon</a>, as Melessa's father Alester is the older brother of Selyse's father Ryam. This also makes Sam the second cousin of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a>'s daughter, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Shireen Baratheon","link":"Shireen Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Shireen Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-152087033356cca68251e0f" href="/wiki/Shireen_Baratheon" title="Shireen Baratheon">Shireen Baratheon</a>.
</p>
