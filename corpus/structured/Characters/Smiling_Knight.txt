<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">While checking <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Book of Brothers","link":"The Book of Brothers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Book of Brothers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/The_Book_of_Brothers" title="The Book of Brothers">The Book of Brothers</a></i>, King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a> passingly reads the entry on Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arthur Dayne","link":"Arthur Dayne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arthur Dayne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/Arthur_Dayne" title="Arthur Dayne">Arthur Dayne</a>, which recounts his deeds and slayings, including his killing of the Smiling Knight.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, novels the Smiling Knight was an infamous outlaw reputed to be insane as well as a deadly swordsman. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> describes him "a madman, cruelty and chivalry all jumbled up together, but he did not know the meaning of fear." Jaime compares him to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a>, calling him "The Mountain of my boyhood". At some point during his career he killed Ser Victor Tyrell, cousin of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mace Tyrell","link":"Mace Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mace Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/Mace_Tyrell" title="Mace Tyrell">Mace Tyrell</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Highgarden","link":"Highgarden","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Highgarden]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/Highgarden" title="Highgarden">Highgarden</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">During the combat between the Brotherhood and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171902460456cca6c8dfee2" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a>, the Smiling Knight briefly crossed swords with Jaime Lannister, but Jaime held him off. Arthur Dayne next fought an extended exchange with him until the Smiling Knight's sword broke. Dayne paused the fight and allowed the outlaw to take a fresh sword. The Smiling Knight did so, but remarked that it was Dayne's famous sword Dawn that he truly desired. In the second exchange, the Smiling Knight was killed.
</p>
