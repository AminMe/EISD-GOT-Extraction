 Walda Freyis an unseen character in Game of Thrones. She is a grandniece of Lord Walder Frey. Biography 

 Background 

Waldais a grandniece of Walder Frey, the Lord of the Crossing. Season 1 

While waiting outside the Twins, Robb Stark has Theon Greyjoy shoot down any ravens sent out to prevent Lord Walder from alerting the House Lannister of his army's movements.
