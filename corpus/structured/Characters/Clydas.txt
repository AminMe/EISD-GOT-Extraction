<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55935047956cca78907a9c" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Clydas is described as ugly: short, bald, chinless, hunched, with pink eyes and rounded shoulders. Jon Snow estimates him to be about 60-years-old. A few white hairs still cling to his scalp. Alongside Chett he tends to Aemon's needs and the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ravens","link":"ravens","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ravens]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55935047956cca78907a9c" href="/wiki/Ravens" title="Ravens">ravens</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-55935047956cca78907a9c" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a>.
