<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Finn originates from the area around <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Raventree Hall","link":"Raventree Hall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raventree Hall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Raventree_Hall" title="Raventree Hall">Raventree Hall</a>, controlled by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Blackwood","link":"House Blackwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Blackwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/House_Blackwood" title="House Blackwood">House Blackwood</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Riverlands","link":"the Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/The_Riverlands" title="The Riverlands">the Riverlands</a>. House Blackwood is one of the few major houses outside of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a> that continues to worship the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Old Gods of the Forest","link":"Old Gods of the Forest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Old Gods of the Forest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Old_Gods_of_the_Forest" title="Old Gods of the Forest">Old Gods of the Forest</a>, and Finn does so.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>https:\/\/www.telltalegames.com\/community\/discussion\/comment\/1952518\/#Comment_1952518<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Finn's Knife.png|thumb|200px|The knife Finn acquired from the lording.]]","title":"Finn's_Knife.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Finn's Knife","caption":"The knife Finn acquired from the lording.","width":200,"captionParsed":"The knife Finn acquired from the lording."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" alt="" src="http://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/7/7e/Finn's_Knife.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/200?cb=20150919033853" width="200" height="113" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Finn&#39;s Knife.png" data-image-key="Finn's_Knife.png"  type="image" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Finn had an affair with a woman who was married to a lord's son. After the lordling found out about the affair between Finn and his wife, he attempted to murder Finn with a dagger. But Finn easily overpowered the lordling and killed him with his own dagger. He kept the dagger as a trophy. To avoid the death sentence of a common man murdering a nobleman, he went to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Wall","link":"The Wall","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Wall|Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">Wall</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">At the Wall he began bullying his fellow brothers-in-training. He is trained under <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Frostfinger","link":"Frostfinger","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Frostfinger]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Frostfinger" title="Frostfinger" class="mw-redirect">Frostfinger</a>, but talks when he addresses the recruits, bragging about his sexual exploits and his murder of a lordling to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gared Tuttle","link":"Gared Tuttle","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gared Tuttle]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Gared_Tuttle" title="Gared Tuttle">Gared Tuttle</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cotter","link":"Cotter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cotter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Cotter" title="Cotter">Cotter</a>. He then takes part in training. He proves himself to be a competent sword-fighter, but lacks restraint and wins through brute strength. He refuses to pick up a crossbow, considering it a coward's weapon, and laughs when Cotter accidentally shoots himself in the toe. He helps lift barrels, and does so with great ease, hefting it over his shoulder, but knocks into Gared in an attempt to make him drop the barrel he's carrying. As he's boasting that he doesn't need to fire a crossbow because he can hit the target with his dagger, he realizes it's missing. He soon works out Cotter has pick-pocketed it and a fight breaks out. Frostfinger breaks up the fight and takes Finn away for punishment, when Finn refuses, Frostfinger smacks him across the face for his insolence and leads him away.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Lost Lords]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Sword in the Darkness","link":"The Sword in the Darkness","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Sword in the Darkness]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/The_Sword_in_the_Darkness" title="The Sword in the Darkness">The Sword in the Darkness</a>" </h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Finn is made a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"ranger","link":"ranger","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[ranger]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Ranger" title="Ranger">ranger</a> and goes to the weirwood tree to say his vows with Cotter and Gared, on the way Gared has the option to patch things up with Finn if he stood up for Cotter where he admits the only reason he got so mad at Gared was because he was the closest thing he had to a friend. When they return to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a>, Finn stands with Cotter, waiting for Gared to finish talking to his uncle, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan","link":"Duncan Tuttle","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan Tuttle|Duncan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Duncan_Tuttle" title="Duncan Tuttle">Duncan</a>. As they stand around Finn and Cotter discuss whether pissing off the Wall could kill a man standing below. Finn and Cotter ask Gared what his uncle came to Castle Black for, Gared lies and Finn comments that he's lying to his brothers already. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a> decides to go to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's Keep","link":"Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Craster's_Keep" title="Craster's Keep">Craster's Keep</a> to kill their <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"treacherous","link":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mutiny at Craster's Keep|treacherous]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep" title="Mutiny at Craster's Keep">treacherous</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"brothers","link":"Raid on Craster's Keep","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raid on Craster's Keep|brothers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Raid_on_Craster's_Keep" title="Raid on Craster's Keep">brothers</a>, both Finn and Cotter agree to go. Cotter reveals he stole Gared's map of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"North Grove","link":"North Grove","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[North Grove]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/North_Grove" title="North Grove">North Grove</a>, Finn laughs at Gared for believing in a rumour told to children and leaves.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Finn is on duty on the Wall when Gared approaches him next, Finn talks about how he doesn't trust Cotter. Finn waits for his relief, complaining to Frostfinger that they're late. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Frostfinger","link":"Frostfinger","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Frostfinger]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Frostfinger" title="Frostfinger" class="mw-redirect">Frostfinger</a> is unmoved. Finn leaves and Gared is attacked <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Britt","link":"Britt Warrick","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Britt Warrick|Britt]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Britt_Warrick" title="Britt Warrick">Britt</a>, he returns to witness Gared standing over Britt's Body and flees.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Sword in the Darkness]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

After the fight, Finn and Gared carry a wounded Cotter through the Northern Forest, to an abandoned wilding camp, where they encounter <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sylvi","link":"Sylvi","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sylvi]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" href="/wiki/Sylvi" title="Sylvi">Sylvi</a>, Cotter's sister.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Sons of Winter]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-128505959456cca67141ee2" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
