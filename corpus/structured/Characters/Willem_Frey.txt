<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171574373056cca7123ea8f" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, there is a character named Willem Frey but he is a young grandson of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Walder Frey","link":"Walder Frey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Walder Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171574373056cca7123ea8f" href="/wiki/Walder_Frey" title="Walder Frey">Walder Frey</a> and the second son of Ser Cleos Frey (the analogous character of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Alton Lannister","link":"Alton Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Alton Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171574373056cca7123ea8f" href="/wiki/Alton_Lannister" title="Alton Lannister">Alton Lannister</a>) and Jeyne Darry. This "Willem Frey" is only ten years old, and thus was never married. He serves as a page at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ashemark","link":"Ashemark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ashemark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-171574373056cca7123ea8f" href="/wiki/Ashemark" title="Ashemark">Ashemark</a>. He does not make an appearance in the books so far, and is only mentioned in the appendix.
</p>
