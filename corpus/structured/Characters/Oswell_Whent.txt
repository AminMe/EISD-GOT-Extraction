<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-137677467156cca6fb9657a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Oswell Whent was a younger brother of Lord Whent, known and remembered for his dark humor. He wore a distinctive helmet emblazoned with a black bat with its wings spread, symbolizing House Whent. Oswell was present at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tourney","link":"Tourney","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tourney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-137677467156cca6fb9657a" href="/wiki/Tourney" title="Tourney" class="mw-redirect">Tourney</a> at Harrenhal where <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-137677467156cca6fb9657a" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> became a Kingsguard.
</p>
