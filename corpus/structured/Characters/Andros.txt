<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Lost Lords","link":"The Lost Lords","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Lost Lords]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/The_Lost_Lords" title="The Lost Lords">The Lost Lords</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Andros is sent along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Morgryn","link":"Rickard Morgryn","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickard Morgryn|Morgryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Rickard_Morgryn" title="Rickard Morgryn">Morgryn</a> by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Ludd Whitehill","link":"Ludd Whitehill","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ludd Whitehill|Lord Ludd Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Ludd_Whitehill" title="Ludd Whitehill">Lord Ludd Whitehill</a> to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> in order to negotiate a sell of ironwood with the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Master of Coin","link":"Master of Coin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Master of Coin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Master_of_Coin" title="Master of Coin">Master of Coin</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>. After an hour of waiting in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Keep","link":"Red Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Red_Keep" title="Red Keep">Red Keep</a>, the two ambassadors are introduced to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mira Forrester","link":"Mira Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mira Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Mira_Forrester" title="Mira Forrester">Mira Forrester</a>. Andros is very unhappy with this, claiming that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Forrester","link":"House Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/House_Forrester" title="House Forrester">House Forrester</a> has no business selling ironwood to the crown, and demanding that any sale of ironwood go through House Whitehill. Morgryn tries to calm Andros down, but Tyrion ends his meeting with them in order to deal directly with Mira. After attempting to threaten Mira, Andros leaves the Red Keep angry.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Lost Lords]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sons of Winter","link":"Sons of Winter","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sons of Winter]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Sons_of_Winter" title="Sons of Winter">Sons of Winter</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Andros attends <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tommen Baratheon's","link":"Tommen Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tommen Baratheon|Tommen Baratheon's]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Tommen_Baratheon" title="Tommen Baratheon">Tommen Baratheon's</a> coronation festival. He converses with another man about how he pushed Morgryn out of the ironwood deal and is now Ludd Whitehill's exclusive contact in King's Landing. He later notices Mira Forrester conversing with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lyman Lannister","link":"Lyman Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lyman Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Lyman_Lannister" title="Lyman Lannister">Lyman Lannister</a>, his new business partner, and berates him for speaking with the enemy. However, Mira convinces Lyman to reveal that Andros is hiring an army of sellswords for Ludd, large enough to march against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ironrath","link":"Ironrath","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ironrath]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Ironrath" title="Ironrath">Ironrath</a>. Lyman then confronts Andros for revealing his addiction to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"milk of the poppy","link":"Milk of the poppy","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Milk of the poppy|milk of the poppy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Milk_of_the_poppy" title="Milk of the poppy">milk of the poppy</a>, and angrily ends their partnership. Figuring that Mira was the cause, Andros threatens the handmaiden before walking off, visibly red.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Sons of Winter]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Nest of Vipers","link":"A Nest of Vipers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Nest of Vipers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/A_Nest_of_Vipers" title="A Nest of Vipers">A Nest of Vipers</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a> learns of Mira's confrontation with Andros at the coronation festival, and mentions it during her meeting with the handmaiden. She later offers to "take care" of Mira's problem with Andros if she can discover who Tyrion plans to call as witnesses during his trial, heavily implying that she will have the merchant lord killed. 
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">If <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rodrik","link":"Rodrik Forrester","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rodrik Forrester|Rodrik]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" href="/wiki/Rodrik_Forrester" title="Rodrik Forrester">Rodrik</a> later lets the traitor live, he reveals that Mira's actions in King's Landing prevented Andros' attempt to gather an army for the Whitehills from succeeding.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Sons of Winter]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-158487164156cca6dfe4eaa" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
