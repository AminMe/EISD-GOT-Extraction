<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the books, Ulf, son of Umar, is also the leader of the Moon Brothers. He fights for the Lannisters during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Green Fork","link":"Battle of the Green Fork","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Green Fork]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-166638816356cca793c65c0" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Green_Fork" title="Battle of the Green Fork">Battle of the Green Fork</a> and perishes.
