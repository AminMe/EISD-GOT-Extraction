<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>'s army sneaks through the mountains bordering the Westerlands, and ambushes Stafford's army at night in their own camp at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oxcross","link":"Oxcross","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oxcross]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Oxcross" title="Oxcross">Oxcross</a>. After the Lannister defeat at the Battle of Oxcross, King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a> torments <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a> before the royal court; Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lancel Lannister","link":"Lancel Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lancel Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Lancel_Lannister" title="Lancel Lannister" class="mw-redirect">Lancel Lannister</a> reports that Robb Stark employed sorcery and wolves to massacre the Lannister army and that after the battle, the Northmen feasted on the flesh of the slain.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> In the books </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Stafford Lannister is the brother of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joanna Lannister","link":"Joanna Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joanna Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Joanna_Lannister" title="Joanna Lannister">Joanna Lannister</a>. He and his sister were the children of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tytos Lannister","link":"Tytos Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tytos Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Tytos_Lannister" title="Tytos Lannister">Tytos Lannister</a>'s younger brother. Thus, both Joanna and Stafford were first cousins of Tywin and the main branch of the family.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In his youth, Stafford and two more Lannisters were captured by Lady Tarbeck in retaliation for the imprisonment of her lord husband by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tytos Lannister","link":"Tytos Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tytos Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Tytos_Lannister" title="Tytos Lannister">Tytos Lannister</a>. She threatened to kill the captives if her husband would be harmed. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister|Tywin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin</a> suggested his father to send Lord Tarbeck back to his wife in three pieces, but the soft-hearted Tytos complied and returned Tarbeck unharmed in exchange for the three Lannisters.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Stafford dies in the Battle of Oxcross, killed by Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickard Karstark","link":"Rickard Karstark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickard Karstark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Rickard_Karstark" title="Rickard Karstark">Rickard Karstark</a>. Both <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brynden Tully","link":"Brynden Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brynden Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Brynden_Tully" title="Brynden Tully">Brynden Tully</a> consider him a poor commander as he didn't scout the area believing Robb Stark was far away. Robb Stark had been led by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Wind","link":"Grey Wind","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Wind]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Grey_Wind" title="Grey Wind">Grey Wind</a> along a goat path and had thus bypasses fortified positions in order to attack undetected.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The Lannister survivors regrouped at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannisport","link":"Lannisport","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lannisport]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-43422437256cca697b6f56" href="/wiki/Lannisport" title="Lannisport">Lannisport</a> under Ser Daven Lannister, Stafford's son and widely considered to be a more intelligent commander than his father.
</p>
