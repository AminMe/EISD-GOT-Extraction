 Background 

He is an elderly warrior and raider and one of the prominent leaders in the Free Folk army gathered by Mance Rayder. Following the defeat of the wildling army in the Battle of Castle Black, he leads his followers to the relative safety of Hardhome. Season 5 

This wildling elder attends the meeting where Jon Snow explains why he has arrived at Hardhome to recruit the wildlings.
