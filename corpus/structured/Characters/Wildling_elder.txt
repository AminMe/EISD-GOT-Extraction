<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is an elderly warrior and raider and one of the prominent leaders in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Folk","link":"Free Folk","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Folk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192385911556cca7c1e32f7" href="/wiki/Free_Folk" title="Free Folk">Free Folk</a> army gathered by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mance Rayder","link":"Mance Rayder","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mance Rayder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192385911556cca7c1e32f7" href="/wiki/Mance_Rayder" title="Mance Rayder">Mance Rayder</a>. Following the defeat of the wildling army in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Castle Black","link":"Battle of Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192385911556cca7c1e32f7" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Castle_Black" title="Battle of Castle Black">Battle of Castle Black</a>, he leads his followers to the relative safety of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hardhome","link":"Hardhome","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hardhome]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-192385911556cca7c1e32f7" href="/wiki/Hardhome" title="Hardhome">Hardhome</a>.
</p>
