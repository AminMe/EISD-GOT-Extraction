<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Originally born a slave in Essos, Melisandre is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Priest","link":"Red Priest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Priest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Red_Priest" title="Red Priest">Red Priestess</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Light","link":"Lord of Light","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Light]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Light" title="Lord of Light" class="mw-redirect">Lord of Light</a>, a deity that is not widely worshiped in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>. Hailing from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Asshai","link":"Asshai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Asshai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Asshai" title="Asshai">Asshai</a>, a country located in the far east of the continent of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Essos","link":"Essos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Essos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Essos" title="Essos">Essos</a>, she claims to wield powerful magical abilities, particularly the power of prophecy. Melisandre wears a large ruby necklace that seems to glow whenever she performs her magic. Some years ago she crossed the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Narrow Sea","link":"Narrow Sea","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Narrow Sea]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Narrow_Sea" title="Narrow Sea">Narrow Sea</a> and came to the court of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> on the island stronghold of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dragonstone","link":"Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Dragonstone" title="Dragonstone" class="mw-redirect">Dragonstone</a>, to preach her faith. Stannis and the majority of his household have now converted to her religion, and she has become a close adviser to Stannis himself.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/guide\/houses\/sbaratheon\/melisandre\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 guide to houses, House Baratheon of Dragonstone - Melisandre entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

She was asked to come back to audition during shooting of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a>, and was cast as Melisandre. <img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[http:\/\/www.imdb.com\/title\/tt0944947\/trivia]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Melisandre is a priestess of the red god, R'hllor, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Light","link":"Lord of Light","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Light]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Light" title="Lord of Light" class="mw-redirect">Lord of Light</a>, whose worship involves the idolization of fire and light. R'hllor is a popular deity in several of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Free Cities","link":"Free Cities","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Free Cities]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Free_Cities" title="Free Cities">Free Cities</a> (particularly <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lys","link":"Lys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Lys" title="Lys">Lys</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Myr","link":"Myr","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Myr]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Myr" title="Myr">Myr</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Volantis","link":"Volantis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Volantis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Volantis" title="Volantis">Volantis</a>) but is almost unheard of in Westeros. Although the series has altered some of Melisandre's actions, van Houten's portrayal is overall consistent with Melisandre as described in the books.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Very little has been revealed about Melisandre's earlier life, other than that she is originally from the city of Asshai in the distant east. Melisandre recalls that as a small child, she was a slave named "Melony". At the slave auction block, she was known as "Lot Seven". She was sold at a young age to the temple of the Lord of Light in Asshai, eventually rising to become a priestess, and thus has spent most of her life in the service of the religion.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In <i>A Clash of Kings</i>, Melisandre "gives birth" to two shadows, the first against Renly and the second - being the one that Davos bears witness to - against Ser Cortnay Penrose, the castellan of Storm's End. He ferries Melisandre into the caves beneath Storm's End because the castle is an ancient stronghold, and guarded against magic. To accomplish this he employs the same route, and "smuggler's tricks" he used when smuggling onions and dried fish into the castle during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Siege of Storm's End","link":"Siege of Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Siege of Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Siege_of_Storm's_End" title="Siege of Storm's End">Siege of Storm's End</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. The TV series condensed this into just one incident.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">After Melisandre arrives at Castle Black, she oddly continues to wear light clothing not well suited to the sub-arctic cold at the Wall - her magical powers apparently give her all the heat she needs. Her powers also allow her to only sleep for one hour a night: she dislikes sleeping more than the bare minimum she has to, because she has nightmares about her time as a slave. She takes an interest in Jon Snow, and whenever she looks into the flames for visions of Azor Ahai, who she believes to be Stannis, all she sees are visions of Jon.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Melisandre does not travel with Stannis's host to Winterfell but stays in Castle Black. She repeatedly warns Jon of "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"daggers in the dark","link":"Mutiny at Castle Black","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mutiny at Castle Black|daggers in the dark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Mutiny_at_Castle_Black" title="Mutiny at Castle Black">daggers in the dark</a>", but he refuses to listen, especially after she tells him of a vision she has had of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> fleeing through the North and finding Jon at the Wall, and the girl turns out to be Alys Karstark.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Melisandre's name did not appear in the Season 1 <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pronunciation guide","link":"Pronunciation guide","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pronunciation guide]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" href="/wiki/Pronunciation_guide" title="Pronunciation guide">Pronunciation guide</a>, but the phonetic pronunciation of her name was later seen written out in a behind-the-scenes short video: "meh-liss-AHN-dreh".<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[https:\/\/www.youtube.com\/watch?v=L2p9dRl0yYo \"Long Story Short: The creation of Game of Thrones\"]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207865160856cca5835a007" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
