<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 5","link":"Season 5","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">Season 5</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"his comrade","link":"Dwarf hunter 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dwarf hunter 1|his comrade]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Dwarf_hunter_1" title="Dwarf hunter 1">his comrade</a> hunt for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> when a price is placed on his head. They mistake <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"another dwarf","link":"Dwarf 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dwarf 1|another dwarf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Dwarf_1" title="Dwarf 1">another dwarf</a> for Tyrion and kill him, bring his head before Queen <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cersei Lannister","link":"Cersei Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cersei Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Cersei_Lannister" title="Cersei Lannister">Cersei Lannister</a>. She reveals that the head is not Tyrion's, and Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meryn Trant","link":"Meryn Trant","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meryn Trant]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Meryn_Trant" title="Meryn Trant">Meryn Trant</a> accuses them of trying to deceive their queen and offers to throw them in a cell. Cersei advises against it, as she does not wish to disuade the other hunters. Both men thank her for her mercy and she orders them to take the head with them, before <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qyburn","link":"Qyburn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qyburn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Qyburn" title="Qyburn">Qyburn</a> suggests that he keep the head as it may be useful for his work.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The House of Black and White]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Cersei is presented with the head of a different dwarf, whom Brienne met on the road in the Riverlands.  
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Many other dwarf heads are also presented to Cersei off-screen, however, one of which is indeed from one of the dwarfs who performed at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Purple Wedding","link":"Purple Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Purple Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Purple_Wedding" title="Purple Wedding">Purple Wedding</a>.  The actor-dwarf's name is Oppo.  He is killed by sailors from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a> who spot him in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Volantis","link":"Volantis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Volantis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-25431301056cca7fe42e0a" href="/wiki/Volantis" title="Volantis">Volantis</a> and mistake him for Tyrion.
