<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Robar Royce is a knight of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Royce","link":"House Royce","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/House_Royce" title="House Royce">House Royce</a>, a proud and ancient noble family of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vale of Arryn","link":"Vale of Arryn","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vale of Arryn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Vale_of_Arryn" title="Vale of Arryn" class="mw-redirect">Vale of Arryn</a>, and a son of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yohn Royce","link":"Yohn Royce","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yohn Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Yohn_Royce" title="Yohn Royce">Yohn Royce</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord","link":"Lord of Runestone","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Runestone|Lord]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Runestone" title="Lord of Runestone">Lord</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Runestone","link":"Runestone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Runestone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Runestone" title="Runestone">Runestone</a>. At the onset of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>, he pledged his allegiance to Renly Baratheon, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord","link":"Lord of Storm's End","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Storm's End|Lord]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Storm's_End" title="Lord of Storm's End">Lord</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Storm's End","link":"Storm's End","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Storm's End]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Storm's_End" title="Storm's End">Storm's End</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Paramount","link":"Lord Paramount","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Paramount]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Lord_Paramount" title="Lord Paramount">Lord Paramount</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Stormlands","link":"the Stormlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Stormlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/The_Stormlands" title="The Stormlands">the Stormlands</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>'s camp is a hive of activity as his troops prepare for the coming battle with the forces of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> in darkness. The camp is beset by strong winds. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> meets with King Renly in his command tent. Ser Robar and his sworn brother, Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Emmon Cuy","link":"Emmon Cuy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Emmon Cuy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Emmon_Cuy" title="Emmon Cuy">Emmon Cuy</a>, are outside while <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne of Tarth","link":"Brienne of Tarth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne of Tarth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Brienne_of_Tarth" title="Brienne of Tarth">Brienne of Tarth</a> stands watch inside the tent. Hearing Brienne scream the guards rush into the tent with swords in hand. They see Brienne kneeling over Renly’s corpse and assume that she is responsible. They threaten her life. Catelyn tries to intervene but they push her aside. Brienne fights both of them off, killing them.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Robar Royce is a member of Renly's Rainbow Guard as "Robar the Red", the second son of Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yohn Royce","link":"House Royce","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Royce|Yohn Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/House_Royce" title="House Royce">Yohn Royce</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Runestone","link":"Runestone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Runestone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Runestone" title="Runestone">Runestone</a> after Ser Andar Royce and older brother of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Waymar Royce","link":"Waymar Royce","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Waymar Royce]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Waymar_Royce" title="Waymar Royce">Waymar Royce</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. He is first seen in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> at the Tourney of the Hand with his father and Ser Andar where he wears steel armor with ancient bronze runes that are supposed to have mystical powers of protection. He unhorses freerider <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lothor Brune","link":"Lothor Brune","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lothor Brune]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Lothor_Brune" title="Lothor Brune">Lothor Brune</a>, but is then defeated by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Loras Tyrell","link":"Loras Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Loras Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Loras_Tyrell" title="Loras Tyrell">Loras Tyrell</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> sends Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Beric Dondarrion","link":"Beric Dondarrion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beric Dondarrion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Beric_Dondarrion" title="Beric Dondarrion">Beric Dondarrion</a> after Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a>, who is terrorizing the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>, Ser Robar is sent to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingswood","link":"Kingswood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingswood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Kingswood" title="Kingswood">Kingswood</a> to inform the King who is hunting with others, including Lord Yohn Royce.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In Renly's camp he tells Catelyn Stark as he escorts her to the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sept","link":"sept","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[sept]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Sept" title="Sept">sept</a> that the reason that he had joined Renly was to seek the glory he would never find as the second son of a noble house and he was weary of being in tournaments. Catelyn Stark guesses him to be about 21 years old. When Renly names him to his Rainbow Guard he names him the red knight Robar the Red. At the feast at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bitterbridge","link":"Bitterbridge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bitterbridge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Bitterbridge" title="Bitterbridge">Bitterbridge</a>, Ser Bryce Caron dares him to juggle daggers.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Renly is murdered, Brienne's scream causes Ser Robar and Ser Emmon Cuy and two guards to rush into the tent. They arrive just after Renly's assassination to find Brienne bending over his body and covered in his blood. Cuy blames Brienne for the death and he and the other two guards attack her. Catelyn Stark convinces Robar that Brienne is innocent. He tells Lady Catelyn to get her away and he fights off the men-at-arms that follow Cuy, while Catelyn uses an iron brazier to knock Emmon Cuy unconscious, then she and Brienne escape. Ser Robar and Ser Emmon are subsequently killed in a fit of rage by their Lord Commander, Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Loras Tyrell","link":"Loras Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Loras Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93665885956cca69c782d5" href="/wiki/Loras_Tyrell" title="Loras Tyrell">Loras Tyrell</a>, for their failure to protect their king.
</p>
