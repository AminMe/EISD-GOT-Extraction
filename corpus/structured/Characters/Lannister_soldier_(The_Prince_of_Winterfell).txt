A Lannister Soldier is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Ciaran McMahon and only appears in The Prince of Winterfell. Biography 

 Background 

This soldier is fighting for House Lannister in the War of the Five Kings. Season 2 

He bumps into Arya Stark while crossing the courtyard at Harrenhal, making her waste the water she is carrying.
