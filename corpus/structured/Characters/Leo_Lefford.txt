<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Leo Lefford is the Lord of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Golden Tooth","link":"Golden Tooth","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Golden Tooth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Golden_Tooth" title="Golden Tooth">Golden Tooth</a> and the head of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lefford","link":"House Lefford","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lefford]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/House_Lefford" title="House Lefford">House Lefford</a>, a vassal house of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Leo Lefford has dinner with Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> and his other commanders the night before their victory in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Green Fork","link":"Battle of the Green Fork","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Green Fork]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Green_Fork" title="Battle of the Green Fork">Battle of the Green Fork</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Baelor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /> He is present for a strategy meeting with Tywin following the defeat in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a>. He wears a blue and yellow cloak over his armor. Also present are <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kevan Lannister","link":"Kevan Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kevan Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Kevan_Lannister" title="Kevan Lannister">Kevan Lannister</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Addam Marbrand","link":"Addam Marbrand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Addam Marbrand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Addam_Marbrand" title="Addam Marbrand">Addam Marbrand</a>, another bannerman, Tywin's servant and several Lannister officers. He reports that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Wind","link":"Grey Wind","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Wind]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Grey_Wind" title="Grey Wind">Grey Wind</a> killed a dozen men and as many horses during the battle. He believes that they should try to ransom Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>. He also suggests returning to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Casterly Rock","link":"Casterly Rock","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Casterly Rock]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Casterly_Rock" title="Casterly Rock">Casterly Rock</a> to raise levies. Tywin dismisses his bannermen and then announces plans to relocate to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Fire and Blood]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the bannermen present for the meeting are Leo Lefford, Harys Swyft, and Addam Marbrand. It is Lefford who suggests ransoming Jaime but several of Lefford's other lines from the passage in the book are given to Kevan in the episode. Swyft suggests returning to Casterly Rock but several of Swyft's other lines are also given to Kevan.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Leo Lefford is the bannerman in charge of Tywin Lannister's baggage train. He later participates in the Battle of the Fords - called the "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Stone Mill","link":"Battle of Stone Mill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Stone Mill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-57731832856cca74682ade" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Stone_Mill" title="Battle of Stone Mill">Battle of Stone Mill</a>" in the TV series, in which he drowns.
</p>
