<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">A <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dwarf","link":"Dwarfism","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dwarfism|dwarf]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Dwarfism" title="Dwarfism">dwarf</a>, he makes a living by performing for other peoples' amusement.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is one of five dwarfs hired by King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey","link":"Joffrey","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Joffrey" title="Joffrey" class="mw-redirect">Joffrey</a> to re-enact the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Purple Wedding","link":"Purple Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Purple Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Purple_Wedding" title="Purple Wedding">Purple Wedding</a>. He dresses as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> and rides a mount resembling <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Melisandre","link":"Melisandre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Melisandre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Melisandre" title="Melisandre">Melisandre</a>. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> and many other guests find the spectacle in poor taste, and he instructs his squire <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Podrick Payne","link":"Podrick Payne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Podrick Payne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Podrick_Payne" title="Podrick Payne">Podrick Payne</a> to pay each of the dwarves 20 Gold Dragons afterwards.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Lion and the Rose]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2>In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, there are only two dwarfs jousting. They ride live animals, a dog and a sow. The one meant as a mockery of Stannis bears a shield depicting the black stag on yellow of House Baratheon. Stannis is also vilified in a song by Galyeon of Cuy, which depicts Stannis as an envious and evil man, bordering on an stereotypical fantasy evil overlord, planning on usurping the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-65760016856cca70da6e9e" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>.
