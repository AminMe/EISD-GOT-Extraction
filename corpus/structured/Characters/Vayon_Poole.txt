</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Vayon Poole</b> is a minor character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first season","link":"Season 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1|first season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">first season</a>. <p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maester","link":"Maesters","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maesters|Maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" href="/wiki/Maesters" title="Maesters">Maester</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Luwin","link":"Luwin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Luwin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" href="/wiki/Luwin" title="Luwin">Luwin</a> approaches <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> to talk about the costs of the royal visit to Winterfell and the need to replace the members of the household that accompanied Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark|Eddard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard</a> to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>, she, grieving for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark|Bran]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran</a>, tells Luwin to have Poole see to the task. Luwin notes that Poole also went south with Lord Stark.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsroad]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

He dies while he and his men are packing for an evacuation Ned had ordered in anticipation of his move against Joffrey.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Pointy End]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-118773814156cca67fe837f" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
