<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91197154856cca7181465b" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Rylene Florent is not Shireen Baratheon's cousin, but Shireen's mother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Selyse Baratheon","link":"Selyse Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Selyse Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91197154856cca7181465b" href="/wiki/Selyse_Baratheon" title="Selyse Baratheon">Selyse Baratheon</a>'s paternal aunt, which makes her Shireen's great aunt, as well as the aunt of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Imry Florent","link":"Imry Florent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Imry Florent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91197154856cca7181465b" href="/wiki/Imry_Florent" title="Imry Florent">Imry Florent</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Melessa Tarly","link":"Melessa Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Melessa Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-91197154856cca7181465b" href="/wiki/Melessa_Tarly" title="Melessa Tarly">Melessa Tarly</a>. Davos describes her as Shireen's "cousin" in the Season 3 finale, but he may have just been speaking loosely. Rylene Florent is married to Ser Rycherd Crane.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Rylene is only mentioned in the novels' appendix, and does not make an appearance.
</p>
