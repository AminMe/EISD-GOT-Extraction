 Eglantine is a recurring background character in the second season. She is played by an unidentified extra and debuts in The North Remembers. Eglantine is the septa of Princess Myrcella Baratheon. Biography 

 Background 

Eglantine is a septa of the Faith of the Seven. She serves at the Red Keep in King's Landing as governess and companion to Princess Myrcella Baratheon. Season 2

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Septa Eglantine is a loyal servant of the royal family and Princess Myrcella Baratheon. She accompanies Myrcella as part of her retinue when she is sent to Dorne as part of the marriage alliance arranged by Tyrion Lannister with House Martell.
