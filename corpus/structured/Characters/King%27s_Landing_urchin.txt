A King's Landing urchin is an unnamed minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Stephen Grech and only appears in Baelor. Biography 

 Season 1 

While Arya Stark is in Flea Bottom, she hears a commotion and sees people starting to run past. She calls to a small ragged child and asks him where the crowds are going. As he is running past her, he tells her that they are taking him to the Great Sept of Baelor.
