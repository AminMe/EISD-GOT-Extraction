</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Haggo</b> is a recurring background character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"first season","link":"Season 1","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1|first season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">first season</a>. He is played by an unidentified extra and debuts in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"series premiere","link":"Winter is Coming (episode)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winter is Coming (episode)|series premiere]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Winter_is_Coming_(episode)" title="Winter is Coming (episode)" class="mw-redirect">series premiere</a>. Haggo is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bloodrider","link":"bloodrider","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bloodrider]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Bloodrider" title="Bloodrider">bloodrider</a> to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Khal","link":"Khal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Khal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Khal" title="Khal">Khal</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogo","link":"Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Drogo" title="Drogo">Drogo</a>.
</p>
<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Haggo is a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bloodrider","link":"bloodrider","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bloodrider]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Bloodrider" title="Bloodrider">bloodrider</a> in the service of Khal Drogo. He is one of Drogo's closest allies and a stalwart warrior who favors the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arakh","link":"Arakh","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arakh]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Arakh" title="Arakh">Arakh</a>. Unlike <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cohollo","link":"Cohollo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cohollo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Cohollo" title="Cohollo">Cohollo</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qotho","link":"Qotho","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qotho]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Qotho" title="Qotho">Qotho</a>, Haggo wears a leather vest as protection.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Haggo, alongside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qotho","link":"Qotho","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qotho]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Qotho" title="Qotho">Qotho</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cohollo","link":"Cohollo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cohollo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Cohollo" title="Cohollo">Cohollo</a>, accompanies Khal <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogo","link":"Drogo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Drogo" title="Drogo">Drogo</a> to view his bride, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Winter is Coming (episode)|Winter is Coming]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Haggo is present at the feast in which a drunken <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Viserys","link":"Viserys Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Viserys Targaryen|Viserys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Viserys_Targaryen" title="Viserys Targaryen">Viserys</a> threatens Daenerys and her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"unborn child","link":"Rhaego","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaego|unborn child]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Rhaego" title="Rhaego">unborn child</a>. He doesn't take part when Drogo orders Qotho and Cohollo to restrain him while Drogo pours molten gold over his head, killing him.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Golden Crown]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Following the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"raid","link":"Raiding","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raiding|raid]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Raiding" title="Raiding">raid</a> of a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lhazareen","link":"Lhazareen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lhazareen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Lhazareen" title="Lhazareen">Lhazareen</a> village in Lhazar, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mago","link":"Mago","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mago]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Mago" title="Mago">Mago</a> defies Drogo. Haggo, along with his fellow <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bloodrider","link":"bloodrider","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bloodrider]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Bloodrider" title="Bloodrider">bloodriders</a>, steps forward to defend Drogo's honor, only for the Khal to take matters in his own hands.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Pointy End]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Pointy End]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Drogo's wound festers and he becomes seriously ill. He falls from his horse in front of the Khalasar, a sign of great weakness. While Daenerys and then Cohollo kneel to the sides of the khal to check on him, Haggo remains mounted alongside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Qotho","link":"Qotho","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Qotho]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Qotho" title="Qotho">Qotho</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Baelor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Haggo is another of Drogo's bloodriders. During the wedding of Daenerys and Drogo, Haggo gives them a large leather whip with a silver handle. When the three bloodriders attempt to stop the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mirri Maz Duur","link":"Mirri Maz Duur","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mirri Maz Duur]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Mirri_Maz_Duur" title="Mirri Maz Duur">Mirri Maz Duur</a>'s <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"blood magic","link":"blood magic","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[blood magic]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Blood_magic" title="Blood magic">blood magic</a> ritual to heal Drogo, Haggo is killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rakharo","link":"Rakharo","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rakharo]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17291469356cca6cf5ea94" href="/wiki/Rakharo" title="Rakharo">Rakharo</a> and Jhogo.
</p>
