<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127742537456cca75f1b866" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, the only Duncan mentioned as part of the Night's Watch is Duncan Liddle, known as Big Liddle, the eldest son of Torren Liddle, the head or clan chief of the Liddles, one of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"northern","link":"The North","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The North|northern]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127742537456cca75f1b866" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">northern</a> mountain clans.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Duncan Liddle is first mentioned in the Appendix of <i>A Dance with Dragons</i>. He is not mentioned as being part of the expedition to Hardhome.
