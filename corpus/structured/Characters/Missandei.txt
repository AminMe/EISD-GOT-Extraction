</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><b>Missandei</b> is a major character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fifth","link":"Season 5","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 5|fifth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Season_5" title="Season 5">fifth</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"sixth","link":"Season 6","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 6|sixth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Season_6" title="Season 6">sixth</a> seasons. She initially appeared as a recurring character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"third","link":"Season 3","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3|third]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">third</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"fourth","link":"Season 4","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4|fourth]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">fourth</a> seasons. She is played by starring cast member <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Nathalie Emmanuel","link":"Nathalie Emmanuel","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Nathalie Emmanuel]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Nathalie_Emmanuel" title="Nathalie Emmanuel">Nathalie Emmanuel</a> and debuts in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valar Dohaeris","link":"Valar Dohaeris","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valar Dohaeris]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Valar_Dohaeris" title="Valar Dohaeris">Valar Dohaeris</a>".
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">A slave who served as an interpreter to the masters of Astapor, Missandei was freed when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> took the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Unsullied","link":"Unsullied","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Unsullied]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Unsullied" title="Unsullied">Unsullied</a> army and used it to overthrow the slavers. Missandei now serves Daenerys as her trusted advisor and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Handmaiden","link":"Handmaiden","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Handmaiden]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Handmaiden" title="Handmaiden">Handmaiden</a>. After Daenerys flees Meereen on the back of her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragon","link":"dragon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon" class="mw-redirect">dragon</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogon","link":"Drogon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Drogon" title="Drogon">Drogon</a>, Missandei remains in the city as part of her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Small Council","link":"Small Council","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Small Council]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Small_Council" title="Small Council">Small Council</a> along with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Worm","link":"Grey Worm","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Worm]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Grey_Worm" title="Grey Worm">Grey Worm</a> while <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jorah Mormont","link":"Jorah Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jorah Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Jorah_Mormont" title="Jorah Mormont">Jorah Mormont</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daario Naharis","link":"Daario Naharis","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daario Naharis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Daario_Naharis" title="Daario Naharis">Daario Naharis</a> search for the missing queen.

She does not interpret faithfully, offering Kraznys commentary on Daenerys' words and leaving out Kraznys' insults to the Khaleesi.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Valar Dohaeris]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Daenerys meets with Kraznys and other Good Masters and announces her intention to buy all of the eight thousand Unsullied for sale, but Kraznys says she cannot afford them. The slaver grows increasingly insulting and condescending - which makes Missandei struggle to interpret politely. Daenerys then adds she also wants to buy the hundreds of young men still in training. In exchange she offers one of her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"dragons","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a>. The Good Masters agree, but Daenerys also asks for Missandei as a gift and a show of good faith for the upcoming transaction. He agrees, and Missandei follows Daenerys.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Daenerys takes missandei.png|thumb|left|Missandei joins Daenerys.]]","title":"Daenerys_takes_missandei.png","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Daenerys takes missandei","align":"left","caption":"Missandei joins Daenerys.","captionParsed":"Missandei joins Daenerys."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" alt="" src="http://vignette3.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/d/d5/Daenerys_takes_missandei.png/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20140624175159" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb alignLeft withCaption" data-image-name="Daenerys takes missandei.png" data-image-key="Daenerys_takes_missandei.png"  type="image" />Daenerys asks the young slave for her name and if she has any living family, but she says she does not. Daenerys warns her that she is heading into war and she may be killed or may fall sick and die, to which Missandei simply recites the Valyrian aphorism: "Valar morghulis". Daenerys responds by correctly interpreting, "all men must die" but also adds that "we are not men", making Missandei grin.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Walk of Punishment]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Missandei, sporting a new dress to match Daenerys's outfit, is present when Kraznys and Daenerys conduct their exchange. She interprets their exchange until Daenerys fluidly begins issuing orders to the Unsullied in perfectly accented <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Low Valyrian","link":"Low Valyrian","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Low Valyrian]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Low_Valyrian" title="Low Valyrian">Low Valyrian</a>, at which Missandei stares in shock. Missandei survives the subsequent sack of Astapor and marches out of the city with Daenerys.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[And Now His Watch is Ended]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Missandei serves as Daenerys's primary aide-de-camp during the march to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yunkai","link":"Yunkai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yunkai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Yunkai" title="Yunkai">Yunkai</a>. On her queen's orders, she gathers the Unsullied officers together and orders them to select a single leader from among themselves. When the new leader, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Grey Worm","link":"Grey Worm","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Grey Worm]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Grey_Worm" title="Grey Worm">Grey Worm</a>, is presented, Missandei explains that all Unsullied are given names consisting of drab colors and vermin to remind them of their station. Daenerys promptly orders the Unsullied to select their own names, though Grey Worm elects to keep his.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Kissed by Fire]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Missandei is a slave native to the island of Naath, located off the northwestern coast of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sothoryos","link":"Sothoryos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sothoryos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Sothoryos" title="Sothoryos">Sothoryos</a>. She acts as an interpreter to Kraznys mo Nakloz, one of the Good Masters of Astapor.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">As is typical for the peoples of Sothoryos, Missandei is dark-skinned. She also possesses the typical Naathi features of a flat face and golden eyes.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Missandei's three brothers were also captured along with her by the slaver-raid which brought all four of them to Astapor. Her three brothers were made into eunuchs and trained as Unsullied, though one of them did not survive the brutal training regimen. Her two surviving brothers, named Marselen and Mossador, did survive, and when Daenerys freed the Unsullied they were happily reunited with Missandei. There is no indication so far that Missandei's Unsullied brothers will appear in the TV series.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">While actress Nathalie Emmanuel was 23 years old when she was cast in the role, in the books Missandei is only 10 years old when she is introduced, the same age as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17781251156cca5d39e876" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> at the time. In spite of her age, she is incredibly intelligent well beyond her years and serves as a key advisor and aide to Daenerys, although in the TV series, she is shown to be reluctant to counsel her without prompting. Due to this age-lift, her interactions with Grey Worm starting in Season 5 have no counterpart in the novels.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Grey Worm is never attacked by Sons of the Harpy, though a loved one of Missandei, her own brother Mossador, is not only attacked but killed by them.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Missandei remains loyal to Daenerys after her departure from Meereen. After Hizdahr takes over, he dismisses her like he does with other Daenerys's loyalists (who wouldn't serve him anyway), claiming that it is undignified for a king to use a child or a former slave as a herald. Missandei is confident that Daenerys will return.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Once Ser Barristan Selmy is convinced that Hizdahr tried to poison Daenerys and that he collaborates with the Yunkai and the Sons of the Harpy, he performs a <i>coup d'etat </i>and imprisons Hizdahr. Then he and other loyal subordinates of Daenerys form a ruling council to oversee Meereen till their queen returns, and Missandei assists them. One of her ideas is to offer the Wise Masters a ransom for the hostages they hold (Daario, Jhogo the bloodrider and Hero the Unsullied) in order to drive a wedge between them and their mercenaries. Ser Barristan admires Missandei's wise advice, that reminds him of the tactics Littlefinger and Varys used at Westeros in order to form a division in the enemy ranks. He thinks that although Missandei is only eleven years old, she is as clever as half the men at the council and wiser than all of them.
