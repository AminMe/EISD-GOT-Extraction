 A street tough is an unnamed minor character in the fifth season. He is played by guest star Winston Davis and only appears in The House of Black and White. Biography 

 Season 5 

He and his comrade see Arya Stark cutting the heads off pigeons with her sword Needle on the streets of Braavos.  In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, there is no parallel to this character. He and his companions are perhaps based loosely on two Bravosnamed Terro and Orbelo, who pick on someone at night. Arya spots them, intervenes and threatens the Bravos with her knife, and they go away. References
