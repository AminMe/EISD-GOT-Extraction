 Orphan kid 2 is a background character in the third season who debuts in Valar Dohaeris. Biography 

 Background 

This child is an orphan living at an orphanage in King's Landing. Season 3 

The orphanage he is living at is visited by Margaery Tyrell as she passes through the streets of Flea Bottom. Margaery asks him if his father was a soldier, in which he replies yes.
