<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Norren's family faithfully has served House Forrester for centuries. His squire is <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bowen","link":"Bowen (Squire)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bowen (Squire)|Bowen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" href="/wiki/Bowen_(Squire)" title="Bowen (Squire)">Bowen</a>, who views serving him with little joy.
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->Norren serves in the Northern army, and fought at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a>, where he claims to have been an integral part in the capture of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime Lannister","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime Lannister</a>.
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Iron From Ice]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Iron From Ice","link":"Iron From Ice","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Iron From Ice]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" href="/wiki/Iron_From_Ice" title="Iron From Ice">Iron From Ice</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Shortly before the events of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Red Wedding","link":"Red Wedding","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Red Wedding]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" href="/wiki/Red_Wedding" title="Red Wedding">Red Wedding</a>, Norren is seen outside <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Twins","link":"The Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">The Twins</a> boasting to House Forrester's men that he captured Jaime Lannister at the Battle of the Whispering Wood. This was then reduced to an integral part, and then a minor part, though Norren is still proud of it. The men joke at his expense. Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Forrester","link":"Gregor Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" href="/wiki/Gregor_Forrester" title="Gregor Forrester">Gregor Forrester</a> emerges from his tent and explains some of Norren's history. Later, he runs out of wine, and has Bowen fetch some more. Gared joins him, and Bowen reveals his disdain for serving Norren.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Once the slaughter begins, Norren fights the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Frey","link":"House Frey","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Frey|Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-53688036956cca6eb775aa" href="/wiki/House_Frey" title="House Frey">Frey</a> men, killing several with his axe and a Forrester shield. If Gared leaves behind Bowen, he will ask about his squire's whereabouts, showing concern for him. Seeing an opportunity, Lord Forrester and Gared flee, while Norren charges back into the fray. It is left uncertain as to whether Norren got out alive or was killed by the Frey men.
</p>
