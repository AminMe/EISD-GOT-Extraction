<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Among the captives that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-20439220156cca764eb1b2" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> took to Harrenhal there was wizened old woman. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-20439220156cca764eb1b2" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> overheard her complaining one night "It's not just, it's not. We never did no treason, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the others","link":"Brotherhood Without Banners","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brotherhood Without Banners|the others]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-20439220156cca764eb1b2" href="/wiki/Brotherhood_Without_Banners" title="Brotherhood Without Banners">the others</a> come in and took what they wanted, same as this bunch." Her friend disagreed, saying "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Beric","link":"Beric Dondarrion","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beric Dondarrion|Lord Beric]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-20439220156cca764eb1b2" href="/wiki/Beric_Dondarrion" title="Beric Dondarrion">Lord Beric</a> did us no hurt, though, and that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"red priest","link":"Thoros","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Thoros|red priest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-20439220156cca764eb1b2" href="/wiki/Thoros" title="Thoros">red priest</a> with him, he paid for all they took". The old woman answered disdainfully "Paid? He took two of my chickens and gave me a bit of paper with a mark on it. Can I eat a bit of raggy old paper, I ask you? Will it give me eggs?". She looked about to see that no guards were near, and spat three times. "There's for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tullys","link":"House Tully","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully|Tullys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-20439220156cca764eb1b2" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">Tullys</a> and there's for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannisters","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister|Lannisters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-20439220156cca764eb1b2" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">Lannisters</a> and there's for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Starks","link":"House Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark|Starks]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-20439220156cca764eb1b2" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">Starks</a>."
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The old woman is not mentioned afterwards.
