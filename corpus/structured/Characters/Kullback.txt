<p data-rte-fromparser="true">After the fight night of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of Castle Black","link":"Battle of Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56786893556cca7db899f4" href="/wiki/Battle_of_Castle_Black" title="Battle of Castle Black">Battle of Castle Black</a><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Watchers on the Wall]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56786893556cca7db899f4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56786893556cca7db899f4" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a> enters the main wildling camp to treat with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King-Beyond-the-Wall","link":"King-Beyond-the-Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King-Beyond-the-Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56786893556cca7db899f4" href="/wiki/King-Beyond-the-Wall" title="King-Beyond-the-Wall">King-Beyond-the-Wall</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mance Rayder","link":"Mance Rayder","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mance Rayder]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56786893556cca7db899f4" href="/wiki/Mance_Rayder" title="Mance Rayder">Mance Rayder</a>. Inside Mance's tent, Mance asks Kullback to pour both of them drinks, and later to get them some food. As Kullback finishes, he leaves his knife sticking in a table, which Jon eyes the entirety of the parley. Soon after, the camp comes under attack from riders of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon of Dragonstone","link":"House Baratheon of Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon of Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56786893556cca7db899f4" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone" title="House Baratheon of Dragonstone">House Baratheon of Dragonstone</a>, and Kullback joins Jon, Mance, and the several other wildlings outside. He is ordered taken into custody by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56786893556cca7db899f4" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a> after the fighting ends.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Children]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-56786893556cca7db899f4" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
