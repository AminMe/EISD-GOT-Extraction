A Yunkai'i slave is a minor unnamed character first appearing in Season 3. He's played by Marin Tudor and only appeared in Mhysa. Biography 

 Season 3 

He is one of the slaves that are freed by Daenerys Targaryen following the Sack of Yunkai. When Daenerys greets the slaves of Yunkai and tells them that it is their own choice to reach for their freedom, the slave stretches his hand towards Daenerys and calls out mhysa. After a moment, another follows suit and soon the entire crowd is shouting mhysa.
