<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">She is apparently a granddaughter of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Olenna Tyrell","link":"Olenna Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Olenna Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Olenna_Tyrell" title="Olenna Tyrell">Olenna Tyrell</a>, one of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Margaery","link":"Margaery Tyrell","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Margaery Tyrell|Margaery]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Margaery_Tyrell" title="Margaery Tyrell">Margaery</a>'s cousins from a lesser branch of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">She is seated in the gardens with Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Olenna Tyrell","link":"Olenna Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Olenna Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Olenna_Tyrell" title="Olenna Tyrell">Olenna Tyrell</a> and another girl embroidering a golden Tyrell rose with their motto, "Growing Strong", which prompts Olenna to complain about their family having the dullest sigil and motto in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Seven Kingdoms","link":"Seven Kingdoms","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Seven Kingdoms]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Seven_Kingdoms" title="Seven Kingdoms">Seven Kingdoms</a>. Olenna spooks her by boldly quoting the mottos for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Greyjoy","link":"House Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/House_Greyjoy" title="House Greyjoy">House Greyjoy</a>, thinking of her House's words as being inferior, before observing that there is "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"a spider in the garden","link":"Varys","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Varys|a spider in the garden]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Varys" title="Varys">a spider in the garden</a>". When Varys approaches, Olenna shoos them away with the mocking advice to "grow strong".
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Margaery Tyrell arrives in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wed","link":"Marriage","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Marriage|wed]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Marriage" title="Marriage">wed</a> King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Joffrey Baratheon","link":"Joffrey Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Joffrey Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Joffrey_Baratheon" title="Joffrey Baratheon">Joffrey Baratheon</a> accompanied by three handmaidens, Elinor Tyrell, Megga Tyrell, and Alla Tyrell. The girls are all daughters of different <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"cadet branches","link":"Cadet branch","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Cadet branch|cadet branches]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Cadet_branch" title="Cadet branch">cadet branches</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a>. Elinor Tyrell is the only one of the three to have reached puberty. Both of the ladies seen in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"And Now His Watch is Ended","link":"And Now His Watch is Ended","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[And Now His Watch is Ended]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/And_Now_His_Watch_is_Ended" title="And Now His Watch is Ended">And Now His Watch is Ended</a>", as well as the two attending Lady Margaery in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Valar Dohaeris","link":"Valar Dohaeris","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Valar Dohaeris]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-64213498156cca6d900542" href="/wiki/Valar_Dohaeris" title="Valar Dohaeris">Valar Dohaeris</a>" are obviously adults, so that particular detail appears to have been ignored. Their relationship to Margaery might be the same, however, since these two refer to Olenna as "nana", this presumably means she is their grandmother.
</p>
