<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140532065256cca7d34b04b" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Kaeth was made Grand Maester at the age of eighty, and sent to the court of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon V Targaryen","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140532065256cca7d34b04b" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Aegon V Targaryen</a>. He died within a year of his appointment and was succeeded by Grand Maester Ellendor.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Kaeth's <i>Lives of Four Kings</i> is considered a literary classic, despite scanting <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Viserys II Targaryen","link":"Viserys II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Viserys II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140532065256cca7d34b04b" href="/wiki/Viserys_II_Targaryen" title="Viserys II Targaryen">Viserys II Targaryen</a>, and his hand-drawn illuminations to be a masterpiece; only four original copies of his book survived, until Joffrey senselessly destroyed one at his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wedding feast","link":"Purple Wedding","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Purple Wedding|wedding feast]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-140532065256cca7d34b04b" href="/wiki/Purple_Wedding" title="Purple Wedding">wedding feast</a>.
</p>
