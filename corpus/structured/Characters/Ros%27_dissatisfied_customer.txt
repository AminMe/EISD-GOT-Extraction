 Ros' dissatisfied customer is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Derek Reid and only appears in The Night Lands. Biography 

 Season 2 

He pays for the services of the prostitute Ros in the brothel owned by Petyr Baelish. She begins to cry as they are just beginning. He walks out, dissatisfied.
