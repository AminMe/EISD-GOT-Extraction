<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Lord Whent's first is never given and comes from the companion book <i>The World of Ice and Fire</i>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Old Lord Whent hosted a great <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tourney","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|tourney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">tourney</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a> in 281 AC, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Year of the False Spring","link":"Year of the False Spring","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Year of the False Spring]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Year_of_the_False_Spring" title="Year of the False Spring">Year of the False Spring</a>. The tourney was held to honor his maiden daughter, who held the title of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Queen of Love and Beauty","link":"Queen of Love and Beauty","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Queen of Love and Beauty]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Queen_of_Love_and_Beauty" title="Queen of Love and Beauty">Queen of Love and Beauty</a> when the tourney began. Her crown was defended by her four brothers and her uncle, Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oswell Whent","link":"Oswell Whent","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oswell Whent]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Oswell_Whent" title="Oswell Whent">Oswell Whent</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a>. Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rhaegar Targaryen","link":"Rhaegar Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rhaegar Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Rhaegar_Targaryen" title="Rhaegar Targaryen">Rhaegar Targaryen</a> won the tourney and named <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lyanna Stark","link":"Lyanna Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lyanna Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Lyanna_Stark" title="Lyanna Stark">Lyanna Stark</a> the Queen of Love and Beauty rather than his wife Princess <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Elia Martell","link":"Elia Martell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Elia Martell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Elia_Martell" title="Elia Martell">Elia Martell</a>, to the shock of all of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westeros","link":"Westeros","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westeros]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Westeros" title="Westeros">Westeros</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jorah Mormont","link":"Jorah Mormont","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jorah Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Jorah_Mormont" title="Jorah Mormont">Jorah Mormont</a> states that he defended a Lord Whent at a tourney at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lannisport","link":"Lannisport","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lannisport]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-160664709656cca7a15bb7b" href="/wiki/Lannisport" title="Lannisport">Lannisport</a> in 289 AC, though is unclear if this was Lord Walter or a descendant of his.
</p>
