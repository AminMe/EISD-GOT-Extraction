<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206476424856cca757d2587" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Todder is the son of a winesink owner. He is described as a short, ugly boy with an unpleasant voice. He has pig eyes, small and shiny. Instead of a Builder, Toad is made a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ranger","link":"Ranger","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ranger]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-206476424856cca757d2587" href="/wiki/Ranger" title="Ranger">Ranger</a>.
