 Gared is a minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Dermot Keaney and only appears in the series premiere. In the unaired pilot he was played by Richard Ridings, but the role was recast. Gared is a ranger in the Night's Watch. He is killed by a White Walker while on a ranging north of the Wall.  In the books 

In the A Song of Ice and Fire novels, Gared is past fifty and has lost both ears, his little finger and three toes to frostbite. He has been on the Wall for over forty years. Some of Gared and Will's roles have been switched around from the books; in the book it is Gared who escapes and is executed for desertion. See also References
