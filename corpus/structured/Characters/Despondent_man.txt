<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is a man who wants to die and seeks a soothing end to his despair at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of Black and White","link":"House of Black and White","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of Black and White]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22196495056cca7795eddc" href="/wiki/House_of_Black_and_White" title="House of Black and White">House of Black and White</a>.
</p>

<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The man sits with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22196495056cca7795eddc" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a> at a fountain in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House of Black and White","link":"House of Black and White","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House of Black and White]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22196495056cca7795eddc" href="/wiki/House_of_Black_and_White" title="House of Black and White">House of Black and White</a>. Shortly afterwards, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22196495056cca7795eddc" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> finds his corpse and two <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Faceless Men","link":"Faceless Men","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Faceless Men]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-22196495056cca7795eddc" href="/wiki/Faceless_Men" title="Faceless Men">Faceless Men</a> carry it out of the room.
