</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the Scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">According to costume designer <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Michele Clapton","link":"Michele Clapton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Michele Clapton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" href="/wiki/Michele_Clapton" title="Michele Clapton">Michele Clapton</a>, Quaithe's mask was designed to display a stylistic link to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Melisandre","link":"Melisandre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Melisandre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" href="/wiki/Melisandre" title="Melisandre">Melisandre</a>, because both of them are from the region of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Asshai","link":"Asshai","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Asshai]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" href="/wiki/Asshai" title="Asshai">Asshai</a>. Melisandre wears an ornate neck piece featuring a repeated design motif of elongated hexagons. Quaithe's mask was constructed using the same hexagonal shapes as links, though filled in with metal. Further, the dresses of both Melisandre and Quaithe have the same hexagonal design motif (elongated so they are taller than they are wide), though the pattern on Melisandre's dress is solid red and more difficult to see.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"A Man Without Honor\" HBO featurette<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Quaithe is an enigmatic shadowbinder, preferring to speak in riddles and prophecies. She hides her true features behind a wooden, lacquered mask, as do many people from the Shadow Lands.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, Quaithe first meets Daenerys in the Red Waste. When Daenerys' scout Jhogo returns from Qarth, Quaithe, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Xaro Xhoan Daxos","link":"Xaro Xhoan Daxos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Xaro Xhoan Daxos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" href="/wiki/Xaro_Xhoan_Daxos" title="Xaro Xhoan Daxos">Xaro Xhoan Daxos</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Pyat Pree","link":"Pyat Pree","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Pyat Pree]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" href="/wiki/Pyat_Pree" title="Pyat Pree">Pyat Pree</a> accompany him to meet Daenerys, and invite her to the city. Quaithe then continues with Daenerys' group to Qarth itself. The TV series simplifies this so Daenerys simply meets Quaithe when she actually arrives at Qarth. Daenerys encounters Quaithe several more times during her stay in Qarth, during which the shadowbinder explains that magic has made an unexpected return in the weeks since the dragons' birth. Quaithe also urges Daenerys to leave Qarth as soon as possible and visit Asshai, saying cryptically: <i>"To go north, you must journey south. To reach the west, you must go east. To go forward you must go back, and to touch the light you must pass beneath the shadow"</i>. Daenerys is reluctant to trust her, however, recalling her unpleasant experience with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mirri Maz Duur","link":"Mirri Maz Duur","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mirri Maz Duur]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" href="/wiki/Mirri_Maz_Duur" title="Mirri Maz Duur">Mirri Maz Duur</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Daenerys is in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Astapor","link":"Astapor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Astapor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-103923674056cca5f176860" href="/wiki/Astapor" title="Astapor">Astapor</a>, Quaithe appears in her cabin seemingly in a dream, repeats her mysterious words, then disappears.
</p>
