 A stableboy is an unnamed minor character in the first season. He is played by guest star Hugo Culverhouse and only appears in The Pointy End. Biography 

 Background 

He's a lowborn servant attending the stables in the Red Keep. Season 1 

During the massacre of the Stark household, he tries to stop Arya Stark as she retrieves Needle from a baggage cart in the Red Keep.
