<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Humfrey Beesbury was a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"Knighthood","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Knighthood|knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Knighthood" title="Knighthood">knight</a> of House Beesbury during the reign of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King","link":"King of the Andals and the First Men","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King of the Andals and the First Men|King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/King_of_the_Andals_and_the_First_Men" title="King of the Andals and the First Men">King</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daeron II Targaryen","link":"Daeron II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daeron II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Daeron_II_Targaryen" title="Daeron II Targaryen">Daeron II Targaryen</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">He challenged his brother-in-law Ser Humfrey Hardyng at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ashford","link":"Ashford","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ashford]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Ashford" title="Ashford">Ashford</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tourney","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|Tourney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">Tourney</a>, breaking twelve lances in what was considered the best fought battle of the day, that the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"smallfolk","link":"smallfolk","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[smallfolk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Smallfolk" title="Smallfolk">smallfolk</a> soon began calling “the Battle of the Humfrey's.” However Ser Humfrey Hardyng won the struggle and remained a champion.  Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerion \"Brightflame\" Targaryen","link":"Aerion Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerion Targaryen|Aerion \"Brightflame\" Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Aerion_Targaryen" title="Aerion Targaryen">Aerion "Brightflame" Targaryen</a> subsequently used dirty tactics in a tilt against Hardyng, however, deliberately impaling Hardyng's horse through the neck, and as it fell Hardyng's leg was crushed underneath.  
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Humfrey Hardyng was therefore eager to join the side of Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duncan the Tall","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Duncan the Tall</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Trial of Seven","link":"Trial by seven of Duncan the Tall","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Trial by seven of Duncan the Tall|Trial of Seven]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Trial_by_seven_of_Duncan_the_Tall" title="Trial by seven of Duncan the Tall">Trial of Seven</a>, to take revenge on Prince Aerion.  Hardyng's leg had been crushed but he insisted that he could still fight well as long as he remained mounted.  His brother-in-law Humfrey Beesbury was similarly eager to join Duncan's side, for payback against Aerion.   
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Humfrey Beesbury was slain in the first pass of the trial by seven, by Ser Donnel of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-113615500956cca7ede4819" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a>.  Beesbury was impaled in the groin by Ser Donnel's iron-tipped lance - the first several feet of the lance snapped off and remained protruding from his corpse as it lay in a pool of bloodstained mud.  His brother-in-law Humfrey Hardyng was also mortally wounded in the trial and died of his injuries soon afterwards.  The artwork in the Season 4 "Histories &amp; Lore" featurette in which Humfrey Beesbury appears inaccurately depicts him fighting on foot.
</p>
