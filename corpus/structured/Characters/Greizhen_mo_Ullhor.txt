<h2 data-rte-spaces-before="1" data-rte-spaces-after="1" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"> Biography </h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Greizen mo Ullhor is of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Good Masters","link":"Good Masters","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Good Masters]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" href="/wiki/Good_Masters" title="Good Masters">Good Masters</a>, the ruling elite of the city. He is an immensely wealthy <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"slaver","link":"Slavery","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Slavery|slaver]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" href="/wiki/Slavery" title="Slavery">slaver</a> who has grown rich from the training and trading of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Unsullied","link":"Unsullied","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Unsullied]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" href="/wiki/Unsullied" title="Unsullied">Unsullied</a> warrior-eunuchs.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Season 3</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Greizhen accompanies fellow Good Master <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kraznys mo Nakloz","link":"Kraznys mo Nakloz","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kraznys mo Nakloz]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" href="/wiki/Kraznys_mo_Nakloz" title="Kraznys mo Nakloz">Kraznys mo Nakloz</a> and a third Good Master for negotiations with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daenerys Targaryen","link":"Daenerys Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daenerys Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" href="/wiki/Daenerys_Targaryen" title="Daenerys Targaryen">Daenerys Targaryen</a> and is amused by Kraznys' comments about her. When Daenerys states that she wants to buy not just the fully-trained Unsullied but the slaves in training as well, Greizhen opposes, concerned that if these half-trained warriors fail on the battlefield it would shame Astapor.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Walk of Punishment]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:KraznysGreizhen.jpg|thumb|right|250px|Greizhen and Kraznys about to deliver the Unsullied]]","title":"KraznysGreizhen.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"KraznysGreizhen","align":"right","caption":"Greizhen and Kraznys about to deliver the Unsullied","width":250,"captionParsed":"Greizhen and Kraznys about to deliver the Unsullied"}}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" alt="" src="http://vignette2.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/6/65/KraznysGreizhen.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/250?cb=20140121191704" width="250" height="140" class="image thumb alignRight withCaption" data-image-name="KraznysGreizhen.jpg" data-image-key="KraznysGreizhen.jpg"  type="image" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Greizhen is present at one of Astapor's plazas for the conclusion of the transaction with Daenerys: the Unsullied and the half-trained boys in exchange for her <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragon","link":"dragon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" href="/wiki/Dragon" title="Dragon" class="mw-redirect">dragon</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Drogon","link":"Drogon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Drogon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" href="/wiki/Drogon" title="Drogon">Drogon</a>. He is killed when Daenerys orders to Unsullied to kill all the Good Masters, slave-drivers and soldiers in the city, sparing only the children, and liberate all the slaves.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[And Now His Watch is Ended]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-34347461156cca7041388b" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
