'''Aegon Targaryen''', the son of Prince [[Rhaegar Targaryen]], is an unseen character in ''[[Game of Thrones]]''. He is deceased when the events of the series begin and is not expected to appear in the series.

==Biography==
===Background===
[[File:Slaughter.png|thumb|left|Aegon is killed by Gregor Clegane]]Aegon Targaryen was the son of Prince Rhaegar Targaryen and [[Elia Martell]]. He had an older sister, [[Rhaenys Targaryen (daughter of Rhaegar)|Rhaenys Targaryen]].<ref>[http://viewers-guide.hbo.com/game-of-thrones/#!/guide/houses/targaryen/ HBO Viewers Guide House Targaryen Family Tree]</ref> [[House Targaryen]] was deposed as the ruling house of [[Westeros]] during [[Robert's Rebellion]]. Rhaegar was killed during the fighting and Elia and both their children were killed soon afterwards during the [[Sack of King's Landing]].<ref name="Elia viewers guide">[http://viewers-guide.hbo.com/game-ofthrones/season2/#!/guide/houses/targaryen/elia-targaryen/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 guide to houses, House Targaryen - Elia Targaryen entry]</ref>

Had he lived and ascended the throne, he would have ruled as "Aegon VI".
