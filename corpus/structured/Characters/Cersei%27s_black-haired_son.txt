<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Cersei's black-haired son is her first-born child, and the only of her children whose father is King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert Baratheon","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert Baratheon</a>. Very little is known about him: that he had black hair; resembled Robert a lot; and died as a result of a fever. His name, year of birth and age at the point of his demise are unknown.
</p>
<h3><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> watches over <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark|Bran]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran</a>, who has not woken up after having fell from the tower, Cersei comes to visit. She tells Catelyn that years ago she has lost her first boy, a little black-haired beauty. She and Robert were nearly driven mad by the child's death.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsroad]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Robert and Cersei talk about the imminent <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dothraki","link":"Dothraki","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dothraki]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/Dothraki" title="Dothraki">Dothraki</a>'s invasion and their unhappy marriage. Cersei says that she has felt something for Robert once, even after they lost their first boy, for quite a while.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Wolf and the Lion]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Cersei never gave birth to any of Robert's children. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Eddard Stark","link":"Eddard Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Eddard Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/Eddard_Stark" title="Eddard Stark">Eddard Stark</a> confronts her about the identity of the father of her children, she admits that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime</a> is their father, and that she loathes Robert since their first night, for coming to their bed drunk and calling her by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lyanna","link":"Lyanna Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lyanna Stark|Lyanna]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-127894043256cca6027b640" href="/wiki/Lyanna_Stark" title="Lyanna Stark">Lyanna</a>'s name. She also reveals that Robert got her pregnant once, and her brother found a woman that could end the pregnancy. Robert was unaware of both the pregnancy and the abortion.
</p>
