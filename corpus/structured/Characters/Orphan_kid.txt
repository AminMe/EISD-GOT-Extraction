<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is an orphan living at an orphanage in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-30297175756cca7bdcc7dc" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. His father was a soldier who died helping to defend <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-30297175756cca7bdcc7dc" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a> against <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis Baratheon","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-30297175756cca7bdcc7dc" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis Baratheon</a>'s forces during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-30297175756cca7bdcc7dc" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a>. His mother died giving birth to him.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-30297175756cca7bdcc7dc" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The orphanage he is living at is visited by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Margaery Tyrell","link":"Margaery Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Margaery Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-30297175756cca7bdcc7dc" href="/wiki/Margaery_Tyrell" title="Margaery Tyrell">Margaery Tyrell</a> as she passes through the streets of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Flea Bottom","link":"Flea Bottom","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Flea Bottom]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-30297175756cca7bdcc7dc" href="/wiki/Flea_Bottom" title="Flea Bottom">Flea Bottom</a>. She talks to him about his parents and gives him a toy wooden knight to remember his father by. When he explains that his father was only a soldier not a knight, she says that his father did what's knights swear to do and that he should be proud. She then promises to look after him and his fellow orphans.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Valar Dohaeris]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-30297175756cca7bdcc7dc" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
