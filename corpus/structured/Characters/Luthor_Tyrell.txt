<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Luthor Tyrell is the father of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mace Tyrell","link":"Mace Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mace Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" href="/wiki/Mace_Tyrell" title="Mace Tyrell">Mace Tyrell</a>, the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Highgarden","link":"Lord of Highgarden","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Highgarden]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Highgarden" title="Lord of Highgarden">Lord of Highgarden</a> and the head of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tyrell","link":"House Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" href="/wiki/House_Tyrell" title="House Tyrell">House Tyrell</a>. He is survived by his widow <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Olenna Tyrell","link":"Olenna Tyrell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Olenna Tyrell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" href="/wiki/Olenna_Tyrell" title="Olenna Tyrell">Olenna Tyrell</a>, known as the "Queen of Thorns".<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/#!\/guide\/houses\/tyrell\/ HBO viewers guide, season 1 guide to houses, House Tyrell entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"House Tyrell viewers guide\">[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/guide\/houses\/tyrell\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 guide to houses, House Tyrell entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In his youth, Luthor was expected to marry <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Viola Redwyne","link":"Viola Redwyne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Viola Redwyne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" href="/wiki/Viola_Redwyne" title="Viola Redwyne">Viola Redwyne</a>. The night before he was due to propose to her, however, Viola's sister Olenna "stumbled" upon his bedchamber after allegedly getting lost after her embroidery lesson. The next morning, Luthor did not propose to Viola; in fact, he never left his chamber, as he couldn't walk, and could only think of what Olenna had given him the night before.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Oathkeeper]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lord Luthor's widow, Lady Olenna, describes her late husband to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sansa Stark","link":"Sansa Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sansa Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" href="/wiki/Sansa_Stark" title="Sansa Stark">Sansa Stark</a> as "an oaf".  She explains that her husband died when he rode over a cliff while hawking. He had been looking up and not paying attention to where his horse was going. She used the incident as a metaphor for the Tyrells' alliance with the Lannisters.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>[[Dark Wings, Dark Words]]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 4","link":"Season 4","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 4]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" href="/wiki/Season_4" title="Season 4">Season 4</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">After Joffrey is poisoned and dies right in front of Margaery, she is left somewhat shaken. Days later, wearing mourning clothes, Olenna recalls to her that Luthor was an oaf she had to sit through boring banquets with and produce heirs with - but it was still horrifying after he died in his hunting accident, when they brought him back and she saw his lifeless corpse laid out on a table. Olenna seems uncharacteristically a little upset, though she tries to hide it, as she clearly didn't <i>rejoice</i> when Luthor died.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Breaker of Chains]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-58600417356cca66f6a3c5" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
