He delivered all five of Lady <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark|Catelyn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77145233556cca600d7347" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn</a>'s children. <h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77145233556cca600d7347" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, Luwin's role as the maester to House Stark is unchanged. Luwin has forged several links of his maester's chain, showing areas where he has interest and expertise, such as herblore and astronomy. He is also one of the few maesters to have studied the so-called 'higher mysteries', meaning magic. This study is theoretical, and meant to demonstrate to those who show an interest in it that magic, if it ever existed in the first place, is dead and gone from the world. Luwin lives in the maester's tower in Winterfell.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When Bran tells Maester Luwin about his dream that his father was in the crypts, it is Luwin who takes Bran down into the crypts to show him that Lord Eddard is not there rather than Osha. Shaggydog surprises and bites him. Bran and Rickon go back with him to his tower and are there when word comes that their father is dead. They are talking about the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Children of the Forest","link":"Children of the Forest","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Children of the Forest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77145233556cca600d7347" href="/wiki/Children_of_the_Forest" title="Children of the Forest">Children of the Forest</a> when the wolves start to howl and the raven arrives with the letter.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Luwin is fatally injured during the sack of Winterfell, but not by Dagmer (who is not in Winterfell but in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Torrhen's Square","link":"Torrhen's Square","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Torrhen's Square]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77145233556cca600d7347" href="/wiki/Torrhen's_Square" title="Torrhen's Square">Torrhen's Square</a> at that point). Before Theon is knocked unconscious, he sees a Bolton knight on a warhorse planting a spear between Luwin's shoulders. The Stark boys find him near death on the edge of the black pool, beneath the shelter of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"heart tree","link":"heart tree","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[heart tree]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-77145233556cca600d7347" href="/wiki/Heart_tree" title="Heart tree">heart tree</a>.
</p>
