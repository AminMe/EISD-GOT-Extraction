<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Behind the Scenes</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">This White Walker was played by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ian Whyte","link":"Ian Whyte","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ian Whyte]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79777456156cca6d8422f6" href="/wiki/Ian_Whyte" title="Ian Whyte">Ian Whyte</a>, who also played the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Walker","link":"White Walker (Winter is Coming)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walker (Winter is Coming)|White Walker]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79777456156cca6d8422f6" href="/wiki/White_Walker_(Winter_is_Coming)" title="White Walker (Winter is Coming)">White Walker</a> in the series premiere, "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winter is Coming","link":"Winter is Coming (episode)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winter is Coming (episode)|Winter is Coming]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79777456156cca6d8422f6" href="/wiki/Winter_is_Coming_(episode)" title="Winter is Coming (episode)" class="mw-redirect">Winter is Coming</a>". There's no indication that they're meant to be the same White Walker, though it is possible. A <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"different","link":"White Walker (Oathkeeper)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Walker (Oathkeeper)|different]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79777456156cca6d8422f6" href="/wiki/White_Walker_(Oathkeeper)" title="White Walker (Oathkeeper)">different</a> White Walker, played by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ross Mullan","link":"Ross Mullan","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ross Mullan]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79777456156cca6d8422f6" href="/wiki/Ross_Mullan" title="Ross Mullan">Ross Mullan</a>, retrieved <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's last son","link":"Craster's last son","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's last son]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79777456156cca6d8422f6" href="/wiki/Craster's_last_son" title="Craster's last son">Craster's last son</a> in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Oathkeeper","link":"Oathkeeper","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Oathkeeper]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-79777456156cca6d8422f6" href="/wiki/Oathkeeper" title="Oathkeeper">Oathkeeper</a>, though again, it is possible that the same White Walker has been tasked with retrieving all Craster's sons.
</p>
