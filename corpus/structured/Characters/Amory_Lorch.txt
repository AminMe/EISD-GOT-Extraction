<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Amory Lorch is a landed <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight" class="mw-redirect">knight</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lorch","link":"House Lorch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lorch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/House_Lorch" title="House Lorch">House Lorch</a>, a knightly house sworn to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Lannister","link":"House Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/House_Lannister" title="House Lannister">House Lannister</a>. He has been a loyal vassal of the Lannisters for many years. The sigil of his House is a black <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"manticore","link":"manticore","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[manticore]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Manticore" title="Manticore" class="mw-redirect">manticore</a> on a red field.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref name=\"House Lannister viewers guide\">[http:\/\/viewers-guide.hbo.com\/game-of-thrones\/season2\/#!\/guide\/houses\/lannister\/ HBO viewers guide, season 2 guide to houses, House Lannister entry]<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Lorch is raiding in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a> when he is approached by a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gold Cloak","link":"Goldcloak on the Kingsroad","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Goldcloak on the Kingsroad|Gold Cloak]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Goldcloak_on_the_Kingsroad" title="Goldcloak on the Kingsroad">Gold Cloak</a> with a warrant for the royal bastard <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>. They have been run off by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a> and his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> recruits who are harboring the boy. Lorch confronts Yoren's group and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"attacks","link":"Raid by the Gods Eye","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Raid by the Gods Eye|attacks]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Raid_by_the_Gods_Eye" title="Raid by the Gods Eye">attacks</a> them when they refuse to give up Gendry. He kills <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Yoren","link":"Yoren","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Yoren]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Yoren" title="Yoren">Yoren</a> personally after his men have subdued him and then imprisons the survivors, ordering them to be taken to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>. He does not realize that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a> are among them. He questions them about Gendry and is tricked into believing that the slain <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lommy Greenhands","link":"Lommy Greenhands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lommy Greenhands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Lommy_Greenhands" title="Lommy Greenhands" class="mw-redirect">Lommy Greenhands</a> was his target because he was carrying Gendry's bull's head helm.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[What is Dead May Never Die]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lorch and his men deliver the prisoners to Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a> at Harrenhal. Ser Gregor oversees the torture of the captives, killing several of them. When Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> returns to Harrenhal he ends the interrogations and orders the prisoners put to work, criticizing his men for wasting valuable manpower.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Garden of Bones]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lorch attends a war council held by Lord Tywin. Tywin criticizes his men for underestimating <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> and demands that they devise a new strategy. Lorch reports rumors from spies that there is discontent in Robb's camp. Tywin is dismissive, saying that discontent is a part of war and that the very same spies would report the same from the Lannister side.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Amory is chastised by Lord Tywin during a war council after the knight's illiteracy means that a letter meant for Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Damon","link":"Damon Marbrand","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Damon Marbrand|Damon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Damon_Marbrand" title="Damon Marbrand">Damon</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Marbrand","link":"House Marbrand","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Marbrand]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/House_Marbrand" title="House Marbrand">House Marbrand</a> is instead accidentally sent to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Marlin","link":"Marlin Dormund","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Marlin Dormund|Lord Marlin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Marlin_Dormund" title="Marlin Dormund">Lord Marlin</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Dormund","link":"House Dormund","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Dormund]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/House_Dormund" title="House Dormund">House Dormund</a>, a vassal of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a>. Exasperated, Tywin remarks that "my <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"cupbearer","link":"cupbearer","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[cupbearer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Cupbearer" title="Cupbearer">cupbearer</a> reads better than you!" Later, Amory catches <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Arya Stark","link":"Arya Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Arya Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Arya_Stark" title="Arya Stark">Arya Stark</a> with a stolen letter and tries to seize her, and then heads to inform Tywin. Frantically, Arya seeks out the assassin <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaqen H'ghar","link":"Jaqen H'ghar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaqen H'ghar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Jaqen_H'ghar" title="Jaqen H'ghar">Jaqen H'ghar</a> and names Amory as her next kill. Ser Amory is killed with a poisoned dart dipped in <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wolfsbane","link":"wolfsbane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wolfsbane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Wolfsbane" title="Wolfsbane">wolfsbane</a> just as he enters Lord Tywin's chambers. Tywin mistakes it for an assassination attempt on himself.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Old Gods and the New]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

reputation of both.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lorch killed Rhaenys by stabbing her about fifty times, even Tywin considering the manner of her death to be overly brutal. He asked Lorch why killing a four year old girl required so many thrusts, and Lorch answered that she kicked him and would not stop screaming. Tywin commented that if Lorch had half the brain a turnip has, he would have calmed Rhaenys with a few sweet words and used a soft silk pillow to smother her. In the TV series, it is not mentioned that he was involved in the killing of Rhaenys, her murder instead being attributed to Gregor Clegane.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Considering he is not responsible for the killing of Princess Rhaenys and his backstory is cut from the TV series, Lorch is generally perceived as being less cruel than his book counterpart.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books, Ser Amory comes into conflict with Yoren while hunting down the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brotherhood Without Banners","link":"Brotherhood Without Banners","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brotherhood Without Banners]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Brotherhood_Without_Banners" title="Brotherhood Without Banners">Brotherhood Without Banners</a> instead of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gendry","link":"Gendry","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gendry]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Gendry" title="Gendry">Gendry</a>. Although Yoren assures Lorch multiple times that his caravan has nothing to do with the Brotherhood, Lorch picks a fight with him anyway without provocation or reason.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">There is no mention in the books that Lorch is illiterate.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lorch is not one of the names Arya gives to Jaqen, and was not killed by him. After <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Roose Bolton","link":"Roose Bolton","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Roose Bolton]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Roose_Bolton" title="Roose Bolton">Roose Bolton</a> took <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harrenhal","link":"Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Harrenhal" title="Harrenhal">Harrenhal</a>, he and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vargo Hoat","link":"Locke","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Locke|Vargo Hoat]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-70202414156cca60cb93af" href="/wiki/Locke" title="Locke">Vargo Hoat</a> had Lorch thrown into the bear pit, where he was torn apart. Arya considered that poetic vengeance for Yoren's murder, as she had thought of Yoren as hairy and tough, like a bear.
</p>
