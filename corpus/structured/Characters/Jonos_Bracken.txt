<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Jonos Bracken is the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord of Stone Hedge","link":"Lord of Stone Hedge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord of Stone Hedge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Lord_of_Stone_Hedge" title="Lord of Stone Hedge">Lord of Stone Hedge</a> and head of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bracken","link":"House Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/House_Bracken" title="House Bracken">House Bracken</a>, a vassal family of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Tully","link":"House Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/House_Tully" title="House Tully">House Tully</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>. He is considered a loyal bannerman to Lord <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hoster Tully","link":"Hoster Tully","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hoster Tully]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Hoster_Tully" title="Hoster Tully">Hoster Tully</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kurleket","link":"Kurleket","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kurleket]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Kurleket" title="Kurleket">Kurleket</a>, a man-at-arms sworn to Lord Jonos, is among the men <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Catelyn Stark","link":"Catelyn Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Catelyn Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Catelyn_Stark" title="Catelyn Stark">Catelyn Stark</a> summons to help her apprehend <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Inn at the Crossroads","link":"Inn at the Crossroads","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Inn at the Crossroads]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Inn_at_the_Crossroads" title="Inn at the Crossroads">Inn at the Crossroads</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Cripples, Bastards and Broken Things]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Jonos Bracken, having presumably joined <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a>'s army after the victory at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","link":"Battle of the Whispering Wood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Whispering Wood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Whispering_Wood" title="Battle of the Whispering Wood">Battle of the Whispering Wood</a>, advocates joining forces with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Renly Baratheon","link":"Renly Baratheon","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Renly Baratheon]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Renly_Baratheon" title="Renly Baratheon">Renly Baratheon</a>. Robb points out that Renly is the late King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert","link":"Robert Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert Baratheon|Robert]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Robert_Baratheon" title="Robert Baratheon">Robert</a>'s youngest brother and he cannot become king before <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stannis","link":"Stannis Baratheon","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stannis Baratheon|Stannis]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Stannis_Baratheon" title="Stannis Baratheon">Stannis</a>. Bracken asks if Robb means to join Stannis instead. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Umber","link":"Jon Umber","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Umber]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Jon_Umber" title="Jon Umber">Jon Umber</a> then suggests they become independent altogether, and declares Robb as the King in the North. Bracken and the other lords present, even the river lords who have never been ruled from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>, draw their swords and swear fealty to Robb as the King in the North.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Fire and Blood]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i>, Jonos Bracken is the ruling lord of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bracken","link":"House Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/House_Bracken" title="House Bracken">House Bracken</a> and its castle, Stone Hedge. He is a loyal bannerman to Lord Hoster. However, he has an enmity towards <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Blackwood","link":"House Blackwood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Blackwood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/House_Blackwood" title="House Blackwood">House Blackwood</a>, part of an old feud going back a thousand years and which continues to cause unrest in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverlands","link":"Riverlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-207866623356cca6d285d67" href="/wiki/Riverlands" title="Riverlands" class="mw-redirect">Riverlands</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Lord Jonos has fathered five daughters: Barbara, Jayne, Catelyn, Bess, and Alysanne, but no trueborn sons, leading some to question his masculinity. Although he claims Harry Rivers - "The Bastard of Bracken" - as his natural born son, there are those who doubt whether Jonos truly sired the boy, as Harry is fair haired and handsome, and Jonos is neither.
</p>
