<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->A <b>Winterfell shepherd</b> is an unnamed recurring character in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"second season","link":"Season 2","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2|second season]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">second season</a>. He is played by guest star <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Donagh Deeney","link":"Donagh Deeney","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Donagh Deeney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Donagh_Deeney" title="Donagh Deeney">Donagh Deeney</a> and only appears in "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Ghost of Harrenhal","link":"The Ghost of Harrenhal","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/The_Ghost_of_Harrenhal" title="The Ghost of Harrenhal">The Ghost of Harrenhal</a>." He is a shepherd on the lands of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. He appeals to Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a> for help tending his flock and is given two orphan boys. Both the shepherd and the orphans are later murdered by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dagmer","link":"Dagmer","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dagmer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Dagmer" title="Dagmer">Dagmer</a>.
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He and his family live in the lands of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. His sons are fighting in the army of King <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the great hall of Winterfell, Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bran Stark","link":"Bran Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bran Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Bran_Stark" title="Bran Stark">Bran Stark</a> holds court for his people. His brother <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickon Stark","link":"Rickon Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickon Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Rickon_Stark" title="Rickon Stark">Rickon Stark</a> sits beside him, shelling nuts loudly with a rock. The shepherd reports being unable to tend to his flock with his three sons fighting in the south. He reassures Bran of the loyalty of his sons but says that there are growing numbers of wolves in the north and that he cannot protect his sheep from them alone. Bran offers to assign two orphans, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Billy","link":"Billy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Billy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Billy" title="Billy">Billy</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jack","link":"Jack","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jack]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Jack" title="Jack">Jack</a>, from Winterfell's outlying Winterstown to aid him if he will give them room and board. The shepherd gratefully accepts, saying that his wife always wanted more children.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Ghost of Harrenhal]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} --><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"image","wikitext":"[[File:Farmer.jpg||thumb|Theon asks the shepherd about Bran and Rickon.]]","title":"Farmer.jpg","params":{"thumbnail":true,"alt":"Farmer","caption":"Theon asks the shepherd about Bran and Rickon.","captionParsed":"Theon asks the shepherd about Bran and Rickon."}}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" alt="" src="http://vignette1.wikia.nocookie.net/gameofthrones/images/5/5b/Farmer.jpg/revision/latest/scale-to-width-down/180?cb=20120524081219" width="180" height="101" class="image thumb withCaption" data-image-name="Farmer.jpg" data-image-key="Farmer.jpg"  type="image" />
<!-- RTE::{"spaces":0,"type":"LINE_BREAK"} -->Winterfell is later <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"seized","link":"Fall of Winterfell","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Fall of Winterfell|seized]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Fall_of_Winterfell" title="Fall of Winterfell">seized</a> by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a>. Bran and Rickon escape and pass close by to the shepherd's farm. Theon tracks them there and brutally questions the shepherd who denies having seen them. Dagmer finds nutshells and tells Theon to send <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Maester","link":"Maesters","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Maesters|Maester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Maesters" title="Maesters">Maester</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Luwin","link":"Luwin","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Luwin]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Luwin" title="Luwin">Luwin</a> back to Winterfell. Theon later returns to Winterfell with the charred corpses of two boys, pretending that they are the Stark princes.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[A Man Without Honor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Theon tries to give Dagmer a pouch of gold for the shepherd, to compensate him for his trouble, but <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dagmer","link":"Dagmer","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Dagmer]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" href="/wiki/Dagmer" title="Dagmer">Dagmer</a> reveals to Theon that he killed the shepherd and his wife to prevent them from revealing that the two corpses were those of the orphans.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Prince of Winterfell]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-38227848556cca7671ca27" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i>A Clash of Kings</i>, in which most of Season 2 is based, there's no shepherd that adopts two orphans. Nevertheless, there's a miller and his wife from Winterstown whose two sons are killed to facilitate an identical ruse.
