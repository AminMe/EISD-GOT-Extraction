<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He is one of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Rickard Karstark","link":"Rickard Karstark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Rickard Karstark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169360284556cca77d8dba1" href="/wiki/Rickard_Karstark" title="Rickard Karstark">Rickard Karstark</a>'s men.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 3","link":"Season 3","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 3]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169360284556cca77d8dba1" href="/wiki/Season_3" title="Season 3">Season 3</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He acts as a lookout when Rickard and his men murdered <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Martyn","link":"Martyn Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Martyn Lannister|Martyn]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169360284556cca77d8dba1" href="/wiki/Martyn_Lannister" title="Martyn Lannister">Martyn</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Willem Lannister","link":"Willem Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Willem Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169360284556cca77d8dba1" href="/wiki/Willem_Lannister" title="Willem Lannister">Willem Lannister</a>. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robb Stark","link":"Robb Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robb Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169360284556cca77d8dba1" href="/wiki/Robb_Stark" title="Robb Stark">Robb Stark</a> orders that Karstark's accomplices be hanged, he protests that he was only the lookout. Robb, on the basis that the man knew perfectly well what Rickard intended to do and did nothing to stop it, then orders him to be executed last so that he may watch the others die.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Kissed by Fire]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-169360284556cca77d8dba1" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
