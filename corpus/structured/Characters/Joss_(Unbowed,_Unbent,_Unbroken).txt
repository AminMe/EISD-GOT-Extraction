<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18178685856cca79b69c58" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, there are several characters named Joss. The only one who is a resident of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Braavos","link":"Braavos","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Braavos]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-18178685856cca79b69c58" href="/wiki/Braavos" title="Braavos">Braavos</a> is a mummer from "The Ship" playhouse, dubbed "Joss the Gloom". However, many people go to the House of Black and White to seek and end to their misery or that of their loved ones.
</p>
