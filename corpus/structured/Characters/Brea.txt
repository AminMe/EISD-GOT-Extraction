'''Brea''' is a minor character in the [[Season 5|fifth]] season. She is played by [[Gemita Samarra]] and appears in "[[The Dance of Dragons]]".<ref>[http://www.imdb.com/title/tt3866826]</ref><ref>[http://instagram.com/p/vjcGUXmrB3/]</ref>

==Biography==
===Background===
Brea is a [[prostitute]] living in [[Braavos]].

===[[Season 5]]===
Brea is brought to Ser [[Meryn Trant]] by the brothel's [[Braavosi Madam|madam]] after he dismisses [[Braavosi Prostitute|another whore]] for being too old. Meryn, however, also finds Brea too old and dismisses her as well.

==In the books==

In the ''[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]'' novels, Brea is not a prostitute but the eldest daughter of [[Brusco]], who is not a brothel bouncer but a fishmonger that briefly takes [[Arya]] in.
