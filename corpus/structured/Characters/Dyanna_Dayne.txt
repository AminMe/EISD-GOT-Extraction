<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154559366656cca70fcee42" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Dyanna Dayne was the wife of Maekar Targaryen and the mother of Daeron, Aerion, Aemon, Aegon, Rhae and Daella Targaryen.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">She is mentioned in "The Hedge Knight", the first of the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tales of Dunk and Egg","link":"Tales of Dunk and Egg","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tales of Dunk and Egg]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154559366656cca70fcee42" href="/wiki/Tales_of_Dunk_and_Egg" title="Tales of Dunk and Egg">Tales of Dunk and Egg</a></i> novellas. When <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Egg","link":"Aegon V Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon V Targaryen|Egg]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154559366656cca70fcee42" href="/wiki/Aegon_V_Targaryen" title="Aegon V Targaryen">Egg</a> offers to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"squire","link":"squire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[squire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154559366656cca70fcee42" href="/wiki/Squire" title="Squire">squire</a> for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Dunk","link":"Duncan the Tall","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duncan the Tall|Dunk]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154559366656cca70fcee42" href="/wiki/Duncan_the_Tall" title="Duncan the Tall">Dunk</a> at the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tourney","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|tourney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154559366656cca70fcee42" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">tourney</a> at <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ashford","link":"Ashford","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ashford]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-154559366656cca70fcee42" href="/wiki/Ashford" title="Ashford">Ashford</a>, Dunk asks what his mother would say of it. Egg replies that she would not have anything to say, because she is dead. Her name is revealed in the companion book <i>The World of Ice and Fire</i>.
</p>
