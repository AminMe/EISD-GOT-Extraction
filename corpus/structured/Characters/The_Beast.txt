<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">He seemed to know <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Beskha","link":"Beskha","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beskha]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-40453569956cca6f7c7a21" href="/wiki/Beskha" title="Beskha">Beskha</a> as he call her "Beskha the Basilisk".
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">"<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Nest of Vipers","link":"A Nest of Vipers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Nest of Vipers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-40453569956cca6f7c7a21" href="/wiki/A_Nest_of_Vipers" title="A Nest of Vipers">A Nest of Vipers</a>"</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The Beast is first seen talking to another pit fighter. He has little patience for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Asher Forrester","link":"Asher Forrester","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Asher Forrester]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-40453569956cca6f7c7a21" href="/wiki/Asher_Forrester" title="Asher Forrester">Asher Forrester</a>, and threatens to kill him. However, after Asher defeats <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Bloodsong","link":"Bloodsong","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Bloodsong]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-40453569956cca6f7c7a21" href="/wiki/Bloodsong" title="Bloodsong">Bloodsong</a> he comes to respect the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westerosi","link":"Westerosi","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westerosi]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-40453569956cca6f7c7a21" href="/wiki/Westerosi" title="Westerosi" class="mw-redirect">Westerosi</a>, and follows him to Westeros.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">When the Forresters and the pit fighters are ambushed by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Whitehills","link":"House Whitehill","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Whitehill|Whitehills]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-40453569956cca6f7c7a21" href="/wiki/House_Whitehill" title="House Whitehill">Whitehills</a> upon Asher's arrival in Westeros, The Beast is hit by several arrows while trying to lift the gate. He is killed by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gryff Whitehill","link":"Gryff Whitehill","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gryff Whitehill]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-40453569956cca6f7c7a21" href="/wiki/Gryff_Whitehill" title="Gryff Whitehill">Gryff Whitehill</a>'s right-hand man, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Harys","link":"Harys","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Harys]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-40453569956cca6f7c7a21" href="/wiki/Harys" title="Harys">Harys</a>, with a spear thrown through his side.
</p>
