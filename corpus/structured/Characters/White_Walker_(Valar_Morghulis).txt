<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">This White Walker and others of his race lead an army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wights","link":"Wights","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Wights|wights]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/Wights" title="Wights">wights</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Beyond the Wall","link":"Beyond the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Beyond the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/Beyond_the_Wall" title="Beyond the Wall">Beyond the Wall</a>.
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the <i>A Song of Ice and Fire</i> books, Sam slays a White Walker that attacks him after he falls back from the main column during the march to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster's Keep","link":"Craster's Keep","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster's Keep]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/Craster's_Keep" title="Craster's Keep">Craster's Keep</a>. Sam gains the nickname of "Sam the Slayer" both in admiration by those who believe him and in mockery by those who don't. After the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"murders","link":"Mutiny at Craster's Keep","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mutiny at Craster's Keep|murders]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/Mutiny_at_Craster's_Keep" title="Mutiny at Craster's Keep">murders</a> of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Craster","link":"Craster","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Craster]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/Craster" title="Craster">Craster</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mormont","link":"Jeor Mormont","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jeor Mormont|Mormont]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/Jeor_Mormont" title="Jeor Mormont">Mormont</a>, Sam and Gilly take refuge at a village that Sam believes to be <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"White Tree","link":"White Tree","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[White Tree]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/White_Tree" title="White Tree">White Tree</a>, in which they are attacked by the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"wight","link":"wight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[wight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-123620233056cca6a49eeea" href="/wiki/Wight" title="Wight" class="mw-redirect">wight</a> of Small Paul, who had been killed by the White Walker.
</p>
