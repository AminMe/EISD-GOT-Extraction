</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">However, Aegon unleashed his three <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"dragons","link":"dragons","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[dragons]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" href="/wiki/Dragons" title="Dragons">dragons</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Vhagar","link":"Vhagar","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Vhagar]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" href="/wiki/Vhagar" title="Vhagar">Vhagar</a>, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Meraxes","link":"Meraxes","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Meraxes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" href="/wiki/Meraxes" title="Meraxes">Meraxes</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Balerion","link":"Balerion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Balerion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" href="/wiki/Balerion" title="Balerion">Balerion</a> the Black Dread against the combined might of the Kingdoms of the Reach and the Rock. Four thousand men perished, consumed in a "<a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"field of fire","link":"Field of Fire","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Field of Fire|field of fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" href="/wiki/Field_of_Fire" title="Field of Fire">field of fire</a>," including King Mern. Seeing he had no chance of victory, King Loren bent the knee to Aegon and gave up his crown. He was named Lord Paramount of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Westerlands","link":"Westerlands","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Westerlands]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" href="/wiki/Westerlands" title="Westerlands" class="mw-redirect">Westerlands</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Warden","link":"Warden","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Warden]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" href="/wiki/Warden" title="Warden">Warden</a> of the West.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Field of Fire (Complete Guide to Westeros)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[House Lannister (Complete Guide to Westeros)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" /><img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[House Tyrell (Histories & Lore)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-129815553456cca7729cae9" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
