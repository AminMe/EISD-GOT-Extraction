Scolera is a character in the fifth Season. She is played by Mary Jordan and appears in The Gift. Biography 

 Background 

Scolera is a septa of the Faith of the Seven serving at the Great Sept of Baelor in King's Landing Season 5 

Scolera is present along with SeptasUnella and Moelle during the arrest ofCersei Lannister.
