<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">The founder of House Frey lived about six hundred years before the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"War of the Five Kings","link":"War of the Five Kings","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of the Five Kings]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" href="/wiki/War_of_the_Five_Kings" title="War of the Five Kings">War of the Five Kings</a>, three hundred years before the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen Conquest","link":"War of Conquest","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[War of Conquest|Targaryen Conquest]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" href="/wiki/War_of_Conquest" title="War of Conquest">Targaryen Conquest</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Baelor]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">The founder of House Frey looked at a map and noticed that <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Trident","link":"the Trident","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Trident]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" href="/wiki/The_Trident" title="The Trident">the Trident</a> speared <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Neck","link":"the Neck","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Neck]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" href="/wiki/The_Neck" title="The Neck">the Neck</a>, cutting off <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the North","link":"the North","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the North]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" href="/wiki/The_North" title="The North">the North</a> from the South. He realized that a river of gold would flow to the man who owned the right bridge across the water. He set in motion the building of this bridge, but did not see it to completion as this took three generations. Eventually, the Frey family obtained their much desired <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"lordship","link":"lordship","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[lordship]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" href="/wiki/Lordship" title="Lordship">lordship</a>, and the castle-bridge their founder began construction on as finished as <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"The Twins","link":"The Twins","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[The Twins]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" href="/wiki/The_Twins" title="The Twins">The Twins</a>.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[House Frey (Histories & Lore)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-47145158456cca7454f62a" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
