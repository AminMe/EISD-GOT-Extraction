<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114083764856cca5d900ecc" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Meera Reed is the only daughter of Lord Howland Reed of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Greywater Watch","link":"Greywater Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Greywater Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114083764856cca5d900ecc" href="/wiki/Greywater_Watch" title="Greywater Watch">Greywater Watch</a>, the southernmost of the vassal houses sworn to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Winterfell","link":"Winterfell","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Winterfell]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114083764856cca5d900ecc" href="/wiki/Winterfell" title="Winterfell">Winterfell</a>. Sixteen-year-old Meera is a noted hunter and trapper. Meera and Jojen are guests at Winterfell when it is taken by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Theon Greyjoy","link":"Theon Greyjoy","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Theon Greyjoy]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114083764856cca5d900ecc" href="/wiki/Theon_Greyjoy" title="Theon Greyjoy">Theon Greyjoy</a>. Theon looked at her once or twice and wondered if she was still a maiden, but other than that paid no attention to her and Jojen. When the Stark boys escape with their wolves, <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hodor","link":"Hodor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hodor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114083764856cca5d900ecc" href="/wiki/Hodor" title="Hodor">Hodor</a>, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Osha","link":"Osha","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Osha]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-114083764856cca5d900ecc" href="/wiki/Osha" title="Osha">Osha</a>, the Reeds join them.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Meera is described as near Robb's age, slim as a boy, with long brown hair knotted behind her head and only the barest suggestion of breasts.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Unlike in the series, Meera is armed with net and a three prong frog spear, not bow and arrows.
</p>
