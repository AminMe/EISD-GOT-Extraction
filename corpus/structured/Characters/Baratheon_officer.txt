 A Baratheon Officer is an unnamed minor character in the second season. He is played by guest star Kieran Cunningham and only appears in Blackwater. Biography 

 Background 

This man is an officer in the army of House Baratheon of Dragonstone. Season 2 

During the Battle of the Blackwater he commands troops attacking the Mud Gate. He yells to his soldiers to get up the ladders.
