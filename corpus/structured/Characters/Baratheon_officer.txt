<p data-rte-fromparser="true">This man is an officer in the army of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Baratheon of Dragonstone","link":"House Baratheon of Dragonstone","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Baratheon of Dragonstone]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93307238256cca80857a48" href="/wiki/House_Baratheon_of_Dragonstone" title="House Baratheon of Dragonstone">House Baratheon of Dragonstone</a>.

<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93307238256cca80857a48" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">During the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Battle of the Blackwater","link":"Battle of the Blackwater","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Battle of the Blackwater]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93307238256cca80857a48" href="/wiki/Battle_of_the_Blackwater" title="Battle of the Blackwater">Battle of the Blackwater</a> he commands troops attacking the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Mud Gate","link":"Mud Gate","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Mud Gate]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-93307238256cca80857a48" href="/wiki/Mud_Gate" title="Mud Gate">Mud Gate</a>.
