<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Willem Wylde was a member of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a> during the reign of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King","link":"King of the Andals and the First Men","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King of the Andals and the First Men|King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/King_of_the_Andals_and_the_First_Men" title="King of the Andals and the First Men">King</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daeron II Targaryen","link":"Daeron II Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daeron II Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Daeron_II_Targaryen" title="Daeron II Targaryen">Daeron II Targaryen</a>. When a place opened in the Kingsguard, Daeron gave the position to Ser Willem. Daeron either did not know or willfully ignored his father <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegon IV","link":"Aegon IV Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegon IV Targaryen|Aegon IV]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Aegon_IV_Targaryen" title="Aegon IV Targaryen">Aegon IV</a>'s promise to Ser Quentyn Ball that he would be raised to the Kingsguard. This was one of the contributing factors to Ser Quentyn siding with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Daemon Blackfyre","link":"Daemon Blackfyre","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Daemon Blackfyre]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Daemon_Blackfyre" title="Daemon Blackfyre">Daemon Blackfyre</a> during the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Blackfyre Rebellion","link":"Blackfyre Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Blackfyre Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Blackfyre_Rebellion" title="Blackfyre Rebellion">Blackfyre Rebellion</a>.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Willem, along with his two fellow Kingsguard members Ser Roland Crakehall and Ser Donnel of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Duskendale","link":"Duskendale","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Duskendale]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Duskendale" title="Duskendale">Duskendale</a>, escorted the royal <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen|Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">Targaryen</a> <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"princes","link":"Prince","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Prince|princes]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Prince" title="Prince">princes</a> to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Ashford","link":"Ashford","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Ashford]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Ashford" title="Ashford">Ashford</a> for the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"tourney","link":"Tournament","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tournament|tourney]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Tournament" title="Tournament">tourney</a>. After Prince <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aerion Targaryen","link":"Aerion Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aerion Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-203259698056cca79556af7" href="/wiki/Aerion_Targaryen" title="Aerion Targaryen">Aerion Targaryen</a> invoked the Trial of Seven all three Kingsguard members present were made part of his seven. Ser Willem was carried off the field unconscious at the end of the trial.
</p>
