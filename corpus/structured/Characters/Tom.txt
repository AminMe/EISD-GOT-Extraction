<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Tom is a soldier for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Stark","link":"House Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/House_Stark" title="House Stark">House Stark</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 2","link":"Season 2","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 2]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/Season_2" title="Season 2">Season 2</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Brienne","link":"Brienne of Tarth","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Brienne of Tarth|Brienne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/Brienne_of_Tarth" title="Brienne of Tarth">Brienne</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jaime","link":"Jaime Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jaime Lannister|Jaime]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/Jaime_Lannister" title="Jaime Lannister">Jaime</a> come inland to see three tavern wenches hanged from a tree. She plans to cut them down and bury them but is interrupted by three Stark soldiers. Tom is leading the trio and asks what they are doing there. Brienne tells them she is moving a prisoner, and they laugh at her because she is a woman. They question her further and Brienne says that she is taking him to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Riverrun","link":"Riverrun","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Riverrun]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/Riverrun" title="Riverrun">Riverrun</a>, and the Stark soldiers let them leave. Tom asks her what she thinks of the wenches they hanged. Brienne tells him she hopes they were given quick deaths and Tom says two of them did, implying that they made the last suffer. <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tom's friend","link":"Stark soldier (Valar Morghulis)","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stark soldier (Valar Morghulis)|Tom's friend]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/Stark_soldier_(Valar_Morghulis)" title="Stark soldier (Valar Morghulis)">Tom's friend</a> recognizes Jaime and says that he's the Kingslayer. Brienne denies it, and then Tom orders them to say his name simultaneously on the count of three. Brienne pushes Jaime away, and kills Tom's friends. She then gives Tom a slow death.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[Valar Morghulis]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, there's no Stark soldier named Tom. Brienne, Jaime, and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Cleos Frey","link":"Alton Lannister","wasblank":false,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Alton Lannister|Cleos Frey]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/Alton_Lannister" title="Alton Lannister">Cleos Frey</a> do not run into any Stark soldiers after they escape from Riverrun. They do see the women hanging in the tree with the same sign, who were hanged for "laying with lions" close to the lands of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Bracken","link":"House Bracken","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Bracken]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/House_Bracken" title="House Bracken">House Bracken</a>, which had previously been occupied by Lannister forces led by Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Gregor Clegane","link":"Gregor Clegane","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Gregor Clegane]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-135136267156cca6c5e332b" href="/wiki/Gregor_Clegane" title="Gregor Clegane">Gregor Clegane</a>. Brienne pulls the boat over, climbs the tree, and begins to cut the bodies down. However, when she gets high enough she sees the mast of the boat from Riverrun that is pursuing them, so they leave the women unburied.
</p>
