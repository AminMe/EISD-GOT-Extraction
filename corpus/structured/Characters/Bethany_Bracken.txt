<h2>Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>History</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Bethany Bracken was a mistress of King Aegon IV, the Unworthy. She was caught abed with Ser <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Terrence Toyne","link":"Terrence Toyne","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Terrence Toyne]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/Terrence_Toyne" title="Terrence Toyne">Terrence Toyne</a>, a <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"knight","link":"knight","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[knight]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/Knight" title="Knight" class="mw-redirect">knight</a> of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Kingsguard","link":"Kingsguard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Kingsguard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/Kingsguard" title="Kingsguard">Kingsguard</a>. King Aegon had Ser Terrence fed to his dogs.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[The Kingsguard (Histories & Lore)]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>
<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Bethany is indirectly referred to. Her identity was revealed by GRRM in an interview, which established her as Aegon IV's seventh mistress, and her backstory expaned in <i>The World of Ice and Fire</i>. Bethany Bracken was groomed from childhood by her father, who had been Aegon's <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Hand of the King","link":"Hand of the King","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Hand of the King]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/Hand_of_the_King" title="Hand of the King">Hand of the King</a>, and her older sister, Barbra Bracken, to seduce King Aegon and replace Melissa Blackwood, which had in turn replaced Barbra as Aegon's mistress, thus restoring the king's favor upon House Bracken.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Bethany caught Aegon's eye, as his father and sister intended, when he visited <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Stone Hedge","link":"Stone Hedge","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Stone Hedge]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/Stone_Hedge" title="Stone Hedge">Stone Hedge</a> to visit <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Aegor Rivers","link":"Aegor Rivers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Aegor Rivers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/Aegor_Rivers" title="Aegor Rivers">Aegor Rivers</a>, one of his <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"bastard","link":"bastard","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[bastard]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/Bastard" title="Bastard" class="mw-redirect">bastard</a> sons, and took her with him to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"King's Landing","link":"King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-17329163356cca808db415" href="/wiki/King's_Landing" title="King's Landing">King's Landing</a>. However, Bethany found his "royal embraces" distressing, and turned to Ser Terrence Toynce for comfort and the two became lovers. They were caught abed by Aegon himself, resulting in the death by torture of Ser Terrence and the execution of Bethany and her father.
</p>
