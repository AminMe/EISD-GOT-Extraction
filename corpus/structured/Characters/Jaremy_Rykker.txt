<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Biography</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-filler="true"></p><h3>Background</h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Jaremy Rykker is an officer in the rangers of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a> and a member of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Rykker","link":"House Rykker","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Rykker]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/House_Rykker" title="House Rykker">House Rykker</a>. He is acting <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"First Ranger","link":"First Ranger","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[First Ranger]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/First_Ranger" title="First Ranger">First Ranger</a> when <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Benjen Stark","link":"Benjen Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Benjen Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Benjen_Stark" title="Benjen Stark">Benjen Stark</a> is away from <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a>.
</p>
<h3 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1"><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Season 1","link":"Season 1","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Season 1]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Season_1" title="Season 1">Season 1</a></h3>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">Ser Jaremy attends the induction of new recruits into the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. He is the acting first ranger in the absence of <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Benjen Stark","link":"Benjen Stark","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Benjen Stark]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Benjen_Stark" title="Benjen Stark">Benjen Stark</a> and summons the recruits assigned to the rangers after the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Lord Commander","link":"Lord Commander","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Lord Commander]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Lord_Commander" title="Lord Commander">Lord Commander</a>'s speech. He leads an escort for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jon Snow","link":"Jon Snow","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jon Snow]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Jon_Snow" title="Jon Snow">Jon Snow</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Samwell Tarly","link":"Samwell Tarly","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Samwell Tarly]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Samwell_Tarly" title="Samwell Tarly">Samwell Tarly</a> when they go <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"north of the Wall","link":"north of the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[north of the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/North_of_the_Wall" title="North of the Wall" class="mw-redirect">north of the Wall</a> to take their vows in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Godswood","link":"Godswood","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Godswood]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Godswood" title="Godswood">Godswood</a>. He embraces his newly sworn brothers after they take their vows. They discover the bodies of rangers <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Othor","link":"Othor","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Othor]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Othor" title="Othor">Othor</a> and <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jafer Flowers","link":"Jafer Flowers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jafer Flowers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Jafer_Flowers" title="Jafer Flowers">Jafer Flowers</a> near the Godswood and return them to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Castle Black","link":"Castle Black","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Castle Black]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Castle_Black" title="Castle Black">Castle Black</a>. The dead men had accompanied Benjen on his scouting mission beyond the Wall.<img data-rte-meta="{"type":"ext","placeholder":1,"wikitext":"<ref>\"[[You Win or You Die]]\"<\/ref>"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" class="placeholder placeholder-ext" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw==" type="ext" />
</p>

<h2 data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">In the books</h2>
<p data-rte-fromparser="true">In the <i><a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"A Song of Ice and Fire","link":"A Song of Ice and Fire","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[A Song of Ice and Fire]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/A_Song_of_Ice_and_Fire" title="A Song of Ice and Fire">A Song of Ice and Fire</a></i> novels, Ser Jaremy is an officer in the rangers of the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Night's Watch","link":"Night's Watch","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Night's Watch]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Night's_Watch" title="Night's Watch">Night's Watch</a>. Ser Jaremy attends the officers meeting with <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tyrion Lannister","link":"Tyrion Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tyrion Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Tyrion_Lannister" title="Tyrion Lannister">Tyrion Lannister</a> and recalls being sent to <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"the Wall","link":"the Wall","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[the Wall]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/The_Wall" title="The Wall">the Wall</a> by <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Tywin Lannister","link":"Tywin Lannister","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Tywin Lannister]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Tywin_Lannister" title="Tywin Lannister">Tywin Lannister</a> after fighting for <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"House Targaryen","link":"House Targaryen","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[House Targaryen]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/House_Targaryen" title="House Targaryen">House Targaryen</a> in the <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Sack of King's Landing","link":"Sack of King's Landing","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Sack of King's Landing]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Sack_of_King's_Landing" title="Sack of King's Landing">Sack of King's Landing</a> during <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Robert's Rebellion","link":"Robert's Rebellion","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Robert's Rebellion]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Robert's_Rebellion" title="Robert's Rebellion">Robert's Rebellion</a>. It is Ser Jaremy rather than Ser Alliser who challenges Sam about his decision to take his vows in the Godswood. He is not named as being part of the escort to the Godswood. He identifies the bodies when they are returned to Castle Black.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Ser Jaremy and four other men were killed by the wight <a data-rte-meta="{"type":"internal","text":"Jafer Flowers","link":"Jafer Flowers","wasblank":true,"noforce":true,"wikitext":"[[Jafer Flowers]]"}" data-rte-instance="130814-90655978156cca6c0145dc" href="/wiki/Jafer_Flowers" title="Jafer Flowers">Jafer Flowers</a>. Jaremy managed to hack the wight's head off, but was killed when the headless corpse pulled his own dagger from its sheath and buried it in his bowels.
</p><p data-rte-fromparser="true" data-rte-empty-lines-before="1">Give that Ser Jaremy is not shown to be killed in the episode, it is possible that he simply wasn't killed in the TV continuity.
</p>
