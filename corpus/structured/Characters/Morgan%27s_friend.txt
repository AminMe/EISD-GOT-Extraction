Morgan's friend is an minor character in the fourth season. He is played by guest star Stuart Martin and appears in Two Swords. Biography 

 Season 4 

He visits Littlefinger's Whore house with his friend, Morgan. While there, they are confronted by the foreigner, Oberyn Martell, who threatens Morgan and then stabs a dagger through his hand.
